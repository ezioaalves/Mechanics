# Creature Types (d20 Modern SRD)

*Source: d20 Modern SRD, p149–156 (`Sourcebooks/OSR/modernsrd.pdf`). The 15 creature types and their shared traits. Each type's per-size physical-ability and natural-attack-damage advancement table (Str/Dex/Con/minimum HD/Slam/Bite/Claw/Gore by size) is **left in the SRD** — those numeric grids extract with column misalignment, so transcribing them risks injecting wrong values; consult the PDF directly when advancing a creature's size. BAB and save progressions come from the Creature Saves and Base Attack Bonuses table.*

## Aberration
Bizarre anatomy, strange abilities, or an alien mindset.
- **Hit Die:** d8. **BAB:** ¾ HD. **Good Save:** Will. **Skill Points:** 2 × Int score, +2 per HD beyond 1. **Feats:** 1, +1 per 4 HD beyond 1.
- Bonus feat: Archaic or Simple Weapons Proficiency; proficient with natural weapons and any weapon in its entry (+Armor Proficiency if noted as armored). Darkvision 60 ft.

## Animal
A nonhumanoid creature, usually a vertebrate, with no magic, language, or culture.
- **Hit Die:** d8. **BAB:** ¾ HD. **Good Saves:** Fortitude and Reflex (some vary). **Skill Points:** 10–15. **Feats:** 1, +1 per 4 HD beyond 1.
- Proficient with natural weapons only; not with armor. Intelligence 1 or 2 (nothing with Int 3+ is an animal). Low-light vision.

## Construct
An animated object or artificially constructed creature.
- **Hit Die:** d10. **BAB:** ¾ HD. **Good Saves:** none. **Skill Points:** none. **Feats:** none.
- Proficient with natural weapons only; not with armor. No Constitution score and usually no Intelligence. Extra hit points by size. Darkvision 60 ft.
- **Immunities:** mind-influencing effects, poison, sleep, paralysis, stunning, disease, necromancy, and any effect requiring a Fortitude save (unless it also works on objects or is harmless); not subject to critical hits, nonlethal damage, ability damage, ability drain, energy drain, or massive damage.
- **Repairable:** can't heal on its own; a Repair check (DC 30, 1 hour) heals 1d10. Reduced to 0 hp = immediately destroyed and irreparable. Cannot be raised from the dead.

## Dragon
A reptilian, usually winged creature with magical or unusual abilities.
- **Hit Die:** d12. **BAB:** = total HD. **Good Saves:** Fortitude, Reflex, Will. **Skill Points:** 6 + Int modifier per HD beyond 1. **Feats:** 1, +1 per 4 HD beyond 1.
- Proficient with natural weapons only; not with armor. Darkvision 60 ft. Immune to sleep, hold, and paralysis.

## Elemental
A being composed of air, earth, fire, or water.
- **Hit Die:** d8. **BAB:** ¾ HD. **Good Saves:** vary by element — Fortitude (earth, water) or Reflex (air, fire). **Skill Points:** 2 × Int score, +2 per HD beyond 1. **Feats:** Int modifier (min 0), +1 per 4 HD beyond 1.
- Proficient with natural weapons only; not with armor. Darkvision 60 ft. Immune to poison, sleep, paralysis, stunning; not subject to critical hits, flanking, or massive damage. Cannot be raised from the dead.

## Fey
A creature with supernatural abilities and ties to nature or another force/place; usually human-shaped.
- **Hit Die:** d6. **BAB:** ½ HD. **Good Save:** Will. **Skill Points:** 3 × Int score, +2 per HD beyond 1. **Feats:** 1, +1 per 4 HD beyond 1.
- Bonus feat: Archaic or Simple Weapons Proficiency (+entry weapons, +Armor Proficiency if noted). Low-light vision.

## Giant
A humanoid creature of Large size or larger, known for great strength.
- **Hit Die:** d8. **BAB:** ¾ HD. **Good Save:** Fortitude. **Skill Points:** 6 + Int modifier (min +1), +2 per HD beyond 1. **Feats:** 1 + Int modifier (min 0), +1 per 4 HD beyond 1.
- Must be Large or larger. Bonus feat: Archaic or Simple Weapons Proficiency (+natural and entry weapons, +Armor Proficiency if noted). Low-light vision.

## Humanoid
Two arms, two legs, one head (or a humanlike torso/arms/head); few or no supernatural abilities.
- **Hit Die:** d8. **BAB:** ¾ HD. **Good Save:** choose one (usually Reflex). **Skill Points:** 6 + Int modifier, +1 per HD beyond 1. **Feats:** 1, +1 per 4 HD beyond 1.
- Must be Medium-size or smaller. With more than 1 HD, gets one bonus feat from: Archaic Weapons Proficiency, Armor Proficiency (light), or Simple Weapons Proficiency. Unarmed attacks count as slam attacks dealing nonlethal damage. Keen Sight: underground humanoids may have darkvision 60 ft., low-light vision, or both (per entry).

## Magical Beast
Similar to an animal but can have Int 3+, and may have supernatural/extraordinary abilities or a bizarre form.
- **Hit Die:** d10. **BAB:** = total HD. **Good Saves:** Fortitude, Reflex. **Skill Points:** 2 × Int score, +1 per HD beyond 1 (or 10–15 if Int is 1 or 2). **Feats:** 1 + Int modifier (min 0), +1 per 4 HD beyond 1.
- Proficient with natural weapons only; not with armor. Darkvision 60 ft. and low-light vision (unless noted).

## Monstrous Humanoid
A humanoid with monstrous or animalistic features, often with supernatural abilities.
- **Hit Die:** d8. **BAB:** = total HD. **Good Saves:** Reflex, Will. **Skill Points:** 2 × Int score, +2 per HD beyond 1. **Feats:** 1 + Int modifier (min 0), +1 per 4 HD beyond 1.
- Bonus feat: Archaic or Simple Weapons Proficiency (+natural and entry weapons, +Armor Proficiency if noted). Darkvision 60 ft.

## Ooze
An amorphous or mutable creature.
- **Hit Die:** d10. **BAB:** ¾ HD. **Good Saves:** none. **Skill Points:** none. **Feats:** none.
- Proficient with natural weapons only; not with armor. No Intelligence score. No natural armor but gains extra hit points by size.
- **Immunities:** mind-affecting effects, poison, sleep, paralysis, stunning, gaze attacks, visual effects, illusions, and other sight-dependent attacks; not subject to critical hits, flanking, or massive damage. Blindsight 60 ft.

## Outsider
A nonelemental creature from another dimension, reality, or plane.
- **Hit Die:** d8. **BAB:** = total HD. **Good Saves:** Fortitude, Reflex, Will. **Skill Points:** 8 + Int modifier per HD. **Feats:** 1, +1 per 4 HD beyond 1.
- Bonus feat: Archaic or Simple Weapons Proficiency (+natural and entry weapons, +Armor Proficiency if noted). Darkvision 60 ft. Cannot be raised from the dead.

## Plant
A vegetable creature.
- **Hit Die:** d8. **BAB:** ¾ HD. **Good Save:** Fortitude. **Skill Points:** none. **Feats:** none.
- Proficient with natural weapons only; not with armor. Immune to sleep, paralysis, stunning, and mind-affecting effects; not subject to critical hits or massive damage. Low-light vision (with visual organs) or Blindsight 60 ft. (without).

## Undead
A once-living creature animated by spiritual or supernatural forces.
- **Hit Die:** d12. **BAB:** ½ HD. **Good Save:** Will. **Skill Points:** 3 × Int score, +2 per HD beyond 1. **Feats:** 1 + Int modifier, +1 per 4 HD beyond 1.
- Bonus feat: Archaic or Simple Weapons Proficiency (+natural and entry weapons, +Armor Proficiency if noted). No Constitution score (uses Charisma modifier for Concentration). Darkvision 60 ft.
- **Immunities:** poison, sleep, paralysis, stunning, disease, necromantic and mind-affecting effects; not subject to critical hits, nonlethal damage, ability damage, ability drain, energy drain, massive damage, or any Fortitude-save effect (unless it works on objects or is harmless).
- **Healing:** cannot heal on its own if it has no Int score; healed by negative energy. Most are destroyed at 0 hp or less. Cannot be raised from the dead.

## Vermin
Insects, arachnids, other arthropods, worms, and similar invertebrates.
- **Hit Die:** d8. **BAB:** ¾ HD. **Good Save:** Fortitude. **Skill Points:** none. **Feats:** none.
- Proficient with natural weapons only; not with armor. No Intelligence score (mindless; immune to mind-affecting effects). Darkvision 60 ft. (with visual organs).
- **Potent Venom:** Medium-size or larger poisonous vermin add to their poison's save DC by size — Medium +2, Large +4, Huge +6, Gargantuan +8, Colossal +10.
