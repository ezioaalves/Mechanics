# Equipment — General Gear (d20 Modern SRD)

*Source: d20 Modern SRD, p91–101 (`Sourcebooks/OSR/modernsrd.pdf`). Base-layer reference covering the campaign-useful adventuring gear. **Modern-only categories are intentionally omitted** — Computers & Consumer Electronics, Surveillance Gear, firearm Weapon Accessories, and modern Services (auto repair, bail, airfare); consult the SRD directly if ever needed. Lifestyle/housing is covered in [`../Equipment/General/Lifestyle.md`](../Equipment/General/Lifestyle.md). Purchasing uses the Wealth system — see [`05_Wealth.md`](05_Wealth.md).*

> **Size** affects concealment and whether an object needs one or two hands. **Purchase DC** is the base Wealth-check DC (before any black-market restriction modifier). **Restriction** gives the rating and its black-market purchase-DC modifier: Lic (+1), Res (+2), Mil (+3), Illegal (+4).
