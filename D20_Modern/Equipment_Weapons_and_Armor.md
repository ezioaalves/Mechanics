## Improvised Weapons

A character takes a –4 penalty when wielding or throwing an improvised weapon (weapon-proficiency feats don't offset it). An improvised thrown weapon has a range increment of 10 ft. (Large 15, Huge 30, Gargantuan 60, Colossal 120). They threaten a critical only on a natural 20, deal lethal damage by size, and have a 50% chance to break each time they deal damage or strike a hard surface. Strength modifier applies to damage from Tiny or larger improvised weapons. Fine objects deal no damage.

## Armor

- **Equipment Bonus:** adds to the wearer's Defense.
- **Nonproficient Bonus:** maximum equipment bonus applicable if you lack the matching Armor Proficiency feat.
- **Maximum Dex Bonus:** caps the Dexterity bonus to Defense while worn.
- **Armor Penalty:** applies to Balance, Climb, Escape Artist, Hide, Jump, Move Silently, and Tumble.
- **Speed (30 ft.):** the wearer's speed in this armor assuming a 30-ft. base speed.
