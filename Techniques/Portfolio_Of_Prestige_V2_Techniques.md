# Portfolio of Prestige Volume II Techniques

## Daisei no Jutsu (Turbocharged Technique)
**Taijutsu** (Strike; Requires Frenzied Rampage (a), Cleave (f) and base attack bonus +9)
**Rank:** 10 (A-Class); **Learn DC:** 25, 4 success; **Perform requirements:** 13 ranks (DC 27); **Time:** 1 full-round action; **Components:** M, E; **Range:** Charge; **Target:** One creature (see text); **Duration:** Instantaneous; **Chakra Cost:** 5.

The user charges one creature. If the attack hits, it deals an additional 3d6 points of damage.

**Empower**
If the attack triggers an extra attack from *Cleave*, the user spend 2 chakra to move up to the remaining movement available in his charge to make that attack. This movement provokes attacks of opportunities from moving through a creature's threatened area as usual, doesn't deal any additional damage and must still be executed in a straight line unless the user is able to turn during his charge.
If multiple extra attacks are triggered from *Great Cleave*, the user can continue to make attacks by spending 2 chakra per attack, up to a number of times equal to one-half his character level provided enough enemies are within the maximum range of his charge.

## Kyujutsu: Geisen (Bow Art: Trick Shot)
**Taijutsu** (Strike; requires base attack bonus +7) [Armed]
**Rank:** 10 (A-Class); **Learn DC:** 25, 4 success; **Perform requirements:** 13 ranks (DC 27); **Time:** 1 attack action; **Components:** M, F, E; **Range:** or Medium (20 ft. + 10 ft./2 levels); **Target:** One creature (see text); **Duration:** Instantaneous; **Chakra Cost:** 6.

The user can ricochet attacks to hit multiple enemies. He makes a ranged attack with any bow against an enemy. If it hits, the attack ricochets and allows the user to make a second attack roll with the same attack bonus at a –2 penalty against any enemy within 30 feet of the last target hit.
If the user has the adept marksman class feature, all ricochet attacks ignore cover and allows the user to ricochet attacks against a creature that has already been hit once per use of this technique.

**Empower**
The user can spend 2 chakra to increase the number of times an attack can cause a ricochet, up to the number of iterative attacks the user gains from base attack bonus. These ricochets cannot target the same creature twice unless the user has the adept marksman class feature. Each time an attack ricochets, the penalty to attack rolls increases by –2.

## Ninpou: Dokuton (Ninja Art: Noxious Release)
**Ninjutsu** (Requires base Fortitude save +5) [Poison]
**Rank:** 8 (B-Class); **Learn DC:** 22, 3 success; **Perform requirements:** 10 ranks (DC 23); **Time:** 1 attack action; **Components:** H, X; **Range:** Personal (see text); **Target:** You (see text); **Duration:** See text; **Saving Throws:** See text; **Chakra Cost:** 4.

The user injects or ingests a single dose of injury or ingested poison, and either secretes it through his skin or blows it out as a cloud of noxious gas.
When secreted through the skin, it acts as a contact poison that affects the next creature the user makes skin contact with (either a touch attack, natural attack or unarmed attack, or when he is struck by a natural or unarmed attack) within 1 round. When released as a toxic cloud, it acts as an inhaled poison that affects creatures in a 15-foot-long cone-shaped spread.
The save DC of the poison is equal to the poison's base save DC minus 2.

**Defensive Maneuver:** If the character has the master poisoner class feature, he can use this ability as a defensive maneuver whenever under its effect after being hit by a melee or ranged attack. Toxic blood jets out of the wound at the attacker, allowing the user to make a ranged touch attack with a range of 30 feet. If it hits, the target is affected by the poison the user used to trigger master poisoner, with a save DC equal to the poison's save DC minus 2.

**Empower**
The user can spend 1 chakra to increase the save DC of the poison by 1, up to the poison's save DC or the technique's save DC, whichever is higher.

**Expendable Component:** One dose of injury or ingested poison.

## Ninpo: Kibaku Retsu (Ninja Art: Explosive Array)
**Ninjutsu** (Requires Demolitionist (a), Demolitions 6 ranks)
**Rank:** 5 (C-Class); **Learn DC:** 17, 2 success; **Perform requirements:** 6 ranks (DC 18); **Time:** 1 full-round action; **Components:** C, X; **Range:** Close (10 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels); **Target:** X; **Duration:** X; **Saving Throws:** X (X); **Chakra Resistance:** X; **Chakra Cost:** 2 (see text).

The user charges and links two or more paper bombs using kunai and iron wire, and applies them to a surface within range. The user can charge one paper bomb per round, plus an additional bomb for each attack he gains from his base attack bonus (minimum 2), and gains a +4 bonus to his Chakra Control check to do so as well as to a Demolitions check to deal greater damage to structures.
This technique can be used multiple times in succession to add more bombs to a charged array, but this must be done in successive rounds. The user cannot exceed the maximum number of bombs allowed in an array per bomb type using this technique.
The chakra cost of this technique is equal to 2 plus the cost of charging the paper bombs.

**Expendable Components:** Two or more kunai and paper bombs, linked with at least 20 feet of iron wire.

## Shugoki no Jutsu (Guardian Spirit Technique)
**Ninjutsu** (Requires Listen 6 ranks, Hide 9 ranks, Move Silently 9 ranks, Spot 6 ranks and base Will save +4) [Hijutsu]
**Rank:** 12 (S-Class); **Learn DC:** 28, 6 success; **Perform requirements:** 17 ranks (DC 32); **Range:** 10 feet (see text); **Target:** One willing creature; **Duration:** Concentration (up to 1 minute/level); **Chakra Cost:** 6 per minute.

This technique functions as *Shintenshin no Jutsu*, except as above and as follow. The user does not control the actions of the subject, but instead shares the subject's senses. Whenever the subject must make a Listen, Hide, Move Silently or Spot check, it can choose to use the user's modifier on those checks instead of its own. If the user can sense chakra, the subject gains that ability as well. Concentrating on this technique is a full-round action, and the user returns to his body when he stops concentrating on the technique.
If the user has the erase presence class ability, he can confer the benefits to the subject by increasing the perform time to 1 minute. The normal limitations otherwise apply to the subject as if it was the source of the ability, and it cannot be reapplied.
