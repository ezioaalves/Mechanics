# Puppetry Techniques (Ao Higi)

These techniques require the use of puppets and often specific components to function. They are also known as the **Blue Secret Techniques (Ao Higi)**.

## Rank 4 (C-Class)

### Ao Higi: Sangeki Daidan (Three-hit Destruction)
**Type:** Ninjutsu (requires Puppetry, Vise Grip component)
**Rank:** 4 (C-Class); **Learn DC:** 17; **Perform DC:** 18; **Time:** 1 attack action; **Chakra Cost:** 6.
**Description:** The puppet latches onto the target with its vise grip and delivers three rapid-fire strikes.
**Effect:** Deals 3d8+Str damage. Target must succeed at a Fortitude save (DC 15) or be stunned for 1 round.

### Ao Higi: Saegenshou (Explosive Punch)
**Type:** Ninjutsu (requires Puppetry, Blast Punch component)
**Rank:** 4 (C-Class); **Learn DC:** 17; **Perform DC:** 18; **Time:** 1 attack action; **Chakra Cost:** 5.
**Description:** The puppet delivers a high-impact strike using its explosive charges.
**Effect:** Deals 4d6+Str damage and knocks the target back 10 feet (Fortitude DC 15 to resist knockback).

## Rank 7 (B-Class)

### Ao Higi: Kaigeki Taihouken (High Power Bodyblow)
**Type:** Ninjutsu (requires Puppetry, Blast Punch component)
**Rank:** 7 (B-Class); **Learn DC:** 22; **Perform DC:** 24; **Time:** 1 attack action; **Chakra Cost:** 10.
**Description:** An advanced version of the explosive punch that delivers a shattering blow.
**Effect:** Deals 8d6+Str damage. Target must succeed at a Fortitude save (DC 20) or be incapacitated for 1d3 rounds.

## Style-Specific Techniques

### Kairai Engeki: Shichihenge (Puppet Theater: Costume Change)
**Type:** Ninjutsu (requires Puppetry)
**Rank:** 3 (D-Class); **Learn DC:** 15; **Perform DC:** 15; **Time:** 1 move action; **Chakra Cost:** 3.
**Description:** Changes the appearance of the puppet into that of a humanoid or animal.
**Effect:** Cosmetic transformation. +10 bonus to Disguise.

### Kairai Engeki: Kengakuryokou (Puppet Theater: Field Trip)
**Type:** Fuinjutsu (requires Puppetry)
**Rank:** 6 (C-Class); **Learn DC:** 20; **Perform DC:** 21; **Time:** 1 full-round action; **Chakra Cost:** 1 per 2 levels.
**Description:** Applies a seal that allows anyone with the same chakra signature to animate the puppet as a swift action.
**Effect:** Increases Puppetry duration by 30 mins per point.

## Hidden Secret Techniques (Sasori Style)

### Akahigi: Hyakki no Souen (Red Secret Technique: Performance of a Hundred Puppets)
**Type:** Ninjutsu [Kinjutsu] (requires Puppeteer 10, Living Puppeteer 5)
**Rank:** 14 (Super S-Class); **Learn DC:** 35; **Perform DC:** 40; **Time:** 1 full-round action; **Chakra Cost:** 50.
**Description:** The ultimate puppetry technique, controlling a massive swarm of puppets simultaneously.
**Effect:** Control up to 100 puppets (sum of HD <= Character Level x 10). The puppeteer can make a swarm attack with all puppets in a 60-ft radius.
