# Portfolio of Prestige Volume IV Techniques

## Fuinjutsu: Shintaiyo no In (Sealing Technique: Seal of the Forgotten Secrets)
**Fuinjutsu** (Requires Thought Reform (a)) [Kinjutsu]
**Rank:** 10 (A-Class); **Learn DC:** 25, 6 success; **Perform requirements:** 13 ranks (DC 27); **Time:** 1 attack action; **Components:** C, H, X; **Range:** Personal; **Target:** You; **Duration:** Until expended; **Saving Throws:** None (see text); **Chakra Resistance:** No; **Chakra Cost:** 4.

The user scribes a seal onto their own skin which contains his own lifeforce. When using this technique, the user can feed his life force into the seal one point at a time, to a maximum number of points equal to half his level. Feeding a point into the seal deals 1 point temporary Constitution damage to the user. 
As an attack action, his seal can be transferred to the corpse of a dead humanoid. The seal bonds to the creature and awakens it with a sense of limited consciousness for 1 minute per point fed into the seal. Each minute, the corpse can answer one question, limited to the knowledge it possessed in life. If the target would be opposed to telling the user this information, it can make a Will save against the technique's DC. If the save is successful, it can attempt to lie. Otherwise, the creature must answer truthfully. 
An individual can only be awakened with a *Shintaiyo no In* seal once, and its whole body does not have to be present. However, the individual's head and spinal cord must be undamaged for the creature to awaken.

**Expendable Components:** Materials to scribe a seal. Scribing the seal requires a Fuinjutsu check (DC 27), does not have to be done by the user and takes one seal slot. An attempt to craft the seal takes 5 minutes, and a failed check will result in the sealing process failing without the user noticing until the end. 
A Fuinjutsu check with the same difficulty class can be attempted to confirm the seal was crafted successfully (cannot be retried).

## Mugoteisha no Jutsu (Cruel Interruption Technique)
**Taijutsu** (Requires Swarm Fighter (a))
**Rank:** 8 (A-Class); **Learn DC:** 23, 4 success; **Perform requirements:** 11 ranks (DC 25); **Time:** 1 instant action; **Components:** C, M, Mas; **Range:** 5 feet and Personal; **Target:** One target and You; **Duration:** Instantaneous; **Saving Throws:** None; **Chakra Resistance:** No; **Chakra Cost:** 8.

The user grabs a nearby creature and drags it in front of an incoming attack.
**Defensive Maneuver:** This technique can only be used when the user is attacked and has a creature other than his attacker within reach. Once per day as an instant action, the user can use this technique to defend against an melee or ranged incoming attack. Skill threshold does not apply to defensive maneuvers.
To defend against the attack, the user must make a Perform check (mastery counts) opposed to the attack roll of the attack. Treat this perform check as a Grapple attempt against the secondary target. To succeed against the attempt, the user must succeed on the opposed check and Grapple attempt. Success causes the secondary target to be moved to intercept the attack cause it to miss the user. If the attack roll is higher than the secondary target's Defense, it also suffers the full damage from the attack.
Defensive maneuver must be declared before knowing the result of the attack. Failure by more than 10 means that the chakra spent trying to avoid the attack is wasted, and the technique fails.
This defensive maneuver allows the user to defend against an attack from an opponent with a Challenge Rating equal to his level or 1, whichever is lower.
Defensive Maneuvers do not count as Avoiding an Attack and is usable a separate number of times a day. Each secondary targets can only be used to intercept an attack once per day.

**Mastery**
Every step of mastery in this technique increases the number of times per day it can be used by 1.
The first step of mastery in this technique increases the Challenge Rating of attacks the user can defend against to his level or 5, whichever is lower.
Every step of mastery after the first increases the Challenge Rating of attack he can defend by 5 or up to his level, whichever is lower.

## Ninpo: Chakra Yuurei no Jutsu (Ninja Secrets: Chakra Wraith Technique)
**Ninjutsu** (Requires Null Chakra State (a) and Dense Chakra (f))
**Rank:** 8 (S-Class); **Learn DC:** 24, 5 success; **Perform requirements:** 12 ranks (DC 28); **Time:** 1 attack action; **Components:** C, H, Mas; **Range:** Personal (see text); **Target:** You; **Duration:** 1 round/level; **Saving Throws:** None; **Chakra Resistance:** See text; **Chakra Cost:** 10.

Used only during a wild chakra state, this technique causes the user to become as a wraith, a singularity that draws in and traps life energy. 
This technique can only be used during wild chakra state. It grants the user an aura extending 5 feet around him, causing all plant life within to quickly wither and die. For the duration of the technique, the user gains chakra resistance 8 plus one-half his character level. Any creature that begins its turn inside the user's aura also suffers 1d8 points of negative energy damage. If the creature has chakra resistance, it applies to this damage.
Each time the user's chakra resistance negates a technique, he gains temporary hit points equal to half his character level. These temporary hit points stack up to the user's level.

**Mastery**
The first step of mastery in this technique increases the damage dealt by the aura to 2d6.
The second and fourth steps of mastery in this technique increase the chakra resistance gained by 1.
The third and fifth steps of mastery in this technique increase the range of the aura by 5 feet.

## Sarusen (Gorilla Warfare)
**Taijutsu** (Stance; requires Speed rank 1 (a) and Tumble 6 ranks)
**Rank:** 5 (C-Class); **Learn DC:** 18, 2 success; **Perform requirements:** 6 ranks (DC 18); **Time:** 1 swift action; **Components:** C, M; **Range:** Personal; **Target:** You; **Duration:** Stance; **Chakra Cost:** 4.

While in this stance, the user is not impeded by difficult terrain and halves any speed penalty that might affect him (rounded down to the nearest multiple of 5 feet). 
If the user has the Mobile Defense class feature, he gains double that bonus against grapple checks. In addition, the user can spend 4 chakra as an attack action while in this stance to become immune to the effects of grapple and entanglement for 1 round. The user can use this property only once per day.

## Senjutsu Hiken: Saigo no Gimu (Tactical Secret: Last Duty)
**Taijutsu** (Strike; requires Intuitive Weapon Supremacy (a) and base attack bonus +10)
**Rank:** 12 (S-Class); **Learn DC:** 28, 5 success; **Perform requirements:** 16 ranks (DC 32); **Time:** 1 full-attack action; **Components:** M; **Range:** Personal; **Target:** You; **Duration:** Instantaneous; **Chakra Cost:** 12.

This technique was developed by ancient sohei for a specific and very narrow purpose. Its true intention is for a sohei to end a fight swiftly and with prejudice.
This technique can only be used while under the effects of *Intuitive Weapon Supremacy*. Upon successful completion of this technique, the user makes a full-attack action and ends the effects of *Intuitive Weapon Supremacy* at the end of his turn. In addition to making multiple attacks, this technique has additional effects based on the type of weapon the user is wielding. 
— **Unarmed or One-handed Melee Weapons:** All attacks are made at the user's highest attack bonus.
— **Two-Handed Melee Weapons:** All attacks are made against their target's touch Defense and add twice the user's Strength modifier to damage. 
— **Thrown and Ranged Weapons:** All attacks are made at the user's highest attack bonus and ignore range increment penalties.
Furthermore, if the technique is performed within the first or second round of activating *Intuitive Weapon Supremacy*, it can be used as an attack action instead of a full-attack action. When performed this way, the user still makes a full-attack action when using the technique.
