# Movement Rules

Shinobi benefit from exceptional mobility. This section covers tactical, local, and overland movement modes.

## Movement Scales
*   **Tactical:** For combat, measured in feet (or squares) per round.
*   **Local:** For exploring an area, measured in feet per minute.
*   **Overland:** For long distances, measured in miles per hour or miles per day.

## Modes of Movement

### Walk
Unhurried but purposeful movement at 3 miles per hour (for an unencumbered human).

### Hustle
A jog at about 6 miles per hour.
*   Moving speed twice in a single round.
*   Moving speed in the same round as an attack or move action.

### Land Hop
A shinobi-specific movement scale.
*   Speed: Approximately 9 miles per hour (at level 10, base speed is 40 ft).
*   Duration: Can be maintained for 4 hours without a problem.
*   Cost: 1 chakra per hour.
*   Penalty: Performing for a 5th hour deals 1 damage, with each additional hour doubling the damage.
*   Terrain: Jump check (DC 15 for highway, DC 20 for trackless) halves terrain penalties for that hour. Add 5 to DC to ignore penalties.
*   **Note:** Cannot be performed in combat.

### Run
*   **Run (x3):** Moving 3x speed (running pace in heavy armor/o-yoroi). ~9 mph.
*   **Run (x4):** Moving 4x speed (running pace in light/medium/no armor). ~12 mph.
*   **Run (x5):** Optional rule for shinobi in light/medium/no armor. ~15 mph.

## Forced March
*   Normal walking: 8 hours/day.
*   Beyond 8 hours: Constitution check (DC 10, +2 per extra hour).
*   Failure: 1 point of nonlethal damage and becomes fatigued. Eliminated only when damage is healed. Mounts suffer lethal damage from forced marches.
