# Creature Special Qualities

This section details the special qualities and abilities inherent to various creatures and NPCs in the Naruto d20 system.

## Generic Qualities

### Alternate Form
A creature can assume one or more alternate forms.
*   **Reversion:** Reverts to natural form when killed.
*   **Type/Subtype:** Retains original.
*   **Size:** Gains size of new form.
*   **Statistics:** Loses natural weapons/armor/movement of original. Gains those of new form.
*   **Abilities:** Gains Physical (Str, Dex, Con) of new form. Retains Mental (Int, Wis, Cha) of original.
*   **Hit Points:** Retains original HP regardless of Constitution change.
*   **Techniques:** Retains ability to perform techniques (requires hands/mouth as appropriate).
*   **Disguise:** +10 bonus on Disguise checks to look like the form.

### Banish Resistance (Su)
Creature gains a bonus to saves against effects that would banish it (e.g., *Tsuihou no Jutsu*). If the effect targets HD, add bonus to effective HD.

### Blindsight / Blindsense (Ex)
Use non-visual senses (chakra, scent, vibration) to locate creatures.
*   **Blindsight:** Invisibility/concealment irrelevant (requires line of effect). No Spot/Listen needed.
*   **Blindsense:** Notice things but cannot pinpoint precision. 50% miss chance against target. Denial of Dex bonus to Defense.

### Chakra Damage / Drain (Su)
*   **Damage:** Deals damage directly to Chakra Pool. Heals during rest.
*   **Drain:** Treated as damage, but grants creature 1 temporary chakra. Excess drain grants 2 temp HP per point (lasts 1 hour).

### Chakra Immunity (Ex)
Works like resistance but cannot be overcome or lowered.

### Chakra Resistance (Ex)
Level check (1d20 + character level) vs. CR to affect creature. Success means the technique works normally.

### Change Shape
Assume appearance of a specific creature but retain most physical qualities.
*   **Retention:** Physical ability scores, HD, HP, skill ranks, feats, BAB, and save bonuses.
*   **Loss:** Natural weapons and movement modes of original. Gains those of new form.

### Damage Reduction (Ex or Su)
Ignores a set amount of damage from weapons and natural attacks. 
*   **Bypassing:** Noted after the slash (e.g., 10/chakra). Energy damage always bypasses DR.
*   **Materials:** Cold Iron, Dark Iron, Silver, Thorium.
*   **Chakra-Enhanced:** Weapons enhanced by techniques or seals overcome DR/chakra.
*   **Epic:** Vulnerable only to +4 weapons, legendary seals, or 21+ level creatures.

### Energy Drain and Negative Levels (Su)
Bestows negative levels on a melee attack.
*   **Drain Effect:** Creature gains 5 HP (10 on crit) per level bestowed.
*   **Penalties:** -1 to all checks, -5 HP, -1 effective level, loss of highest spell/technique slot.
*   **Recovery:** Attempt Fortitude save after 24 hours. Failure results in permanent level loss.
*   **Death:** If negative levels equal current level, character is instantly slain.

### Evasion / Improved Evasion (Ex)
*   **Evasion:** No damage on successful Reflex save.
*   **Improved:** Half damage on failed save.

### Fast Regeneration (Ex or Su)
*   **Non-lethal:** Treats all damage as non-lethal (unless vulnerable to that type).
*   **Recovery:** Recovers HP per round. Cannot be killed unless hit by vulnerability or CDG while unconscious.
*   **Regrowth:** Can regrow limbs in 1 round by concentrating.

### Frightful Presence (Ex)
Automatic intimidation during dramatic actions. Opponents within 30 ft with fewer HD must save (Will) or become shaken for 5d6 rounds.

### Incorporeal (Ex or Su)
No physical body.
*   **Immunity:** Nonmagical physical attacks.
*   **Miss Chance:** 50% chance to ignore damage from corporeal techniques (except force or soul flayer seals).
*   **Movement:** Pass through solid objects. No weight (no traps). No scent or footprints.
*   **Defense:** Ignores natural armor/shields.

### Invisibility
Visually undetectable.
*   **Combat:** +2 attack vs. those relying on sight. Total concealment (50% miss chance).
*   **Detection:** Spot DC 20 (active within 30 ft), Listen (beats Move Silently). Beatty Spot by 20 pinpoints location.
*   **Tracks:** Still leaves tracks in sand/mud/water.

### Low-Light Vision (Ex)
See twice as far in poor light. Distinguish colors.

### Modes of Movement
*   **Burrow:** Tunnel through dirt (not rock).
*   **Climb:** +8 racial bonus to Climb. Take 10 anytime.
*   **Fly:** Maneuverability (Perfect, Good, Average, Poor, Clumsy). Dive attacks deal double damage.
*   **Swim:** +8 racial bonus. Take 10 anytime. Run in straight line.
*   **Teleport:** Move without provoking AOOs. Requires footing and Line of Sight.

### Natural Weapons
Physical attacks (Bite, Claw, Gore, Slam, Sting, Tentacle).
*   **Primary:** Full BAB.
*   **Secondary:** -5 penalty (-2 with Multiattack).
*   **Critical:** Usually x2.

### Nonabilities
*   **Strength:** Automatically fails Str checks. Uses Dex for melee BAB.
*   **Dexterity:** Cannot move. Uses Int for Initiative. Automatically fails Reflex/Dex checks.
*   **Constitution:** No metabolism. Immune to Fort saves (unless object-affecting). No Chakra (usually). Immune to drain/energy damage.
*   **Intelligence:** Mindless. Immune to mind-affecting. No feats/skills.
*   **Wisdom/Charisma:** Lack of Wisdom implies an object. Lack of Charisma implies no self-awareness.

### Possession (Su)
Take control of a subject's body.
*   **Changes:** Gains vessel's Str/Dex/Con and Size. Retains own Int/Wis/Cha.
*   **Subtypes:** Gains vessel's subtypes/templates/bloodline (except *Moujuu Aishou*).
*   **Classes:** Retains own class levels/abilities. Does not gain vessel's.
*   **Signature:** Overwrites vessel's chakra signature.

### Powerful Charge (Ex)
Bonus damage on charge attacks.

### Regeneration (Ex)
Impervious to damage except specific types.
*   **Heal Rate:** Heals fixed amount every round.
*   **Massive Damage:** If damage doesn't match vulnerability and is below threshold, it heals. If above threshold and fails save, becomes dazed.
*   **Vulnerability:** Damage from vulnerabilities cannot be regenerated.

### Remote Sensing (Su)
Project a probe up to 400 ft per level.
*   **Probe:** Can move 200 ft/round. Sense chakra 50 ft.
*   **Status:** User is unmoving and unaware of surroundings.
*   **Trace:** Probe has signature 2 steps lower. Can be tracked back.

### Rend (Ex)
Hitting with natural attacks allows creature to latch on and deal extra damage (Atk + 1.5x Str).

### Scent (Ex)
Detect foes within 30 ft (60 upwind, 15 downwind).
*   **Tracking:** Use Survival (Wis) to track by smell. DC 10 (fresh). Masked by water or strong odors (DC 20).

### Suppress Chakra (Ex or Su)
Hide chakra signature as per the skill.

### Trample (Ex)
Move over smaller opponents as full-round action. Deals slam + 1.5x Str damage. Reflex half (DC 10 + 1/2 HD + Str).

### Tremorsense (Ex or Su)
Sense location of anything in contact with the ground (or water for aquatic).

### Vulnerability to Energy
Takes +50% damage from a specific energy type.
