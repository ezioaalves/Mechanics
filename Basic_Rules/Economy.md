# Economy and Wealth

This section details the shinobi economy, currency conversion, and mission compensation.

## Currency
The standard unit of currency is **Ryo**.

### Purchase DC to Ryo Equivalence
| Purchase DC | Ryo Equivalence | Purchase DC | Ryo Equivalence |
| :---: | :--- | :---: | :--- |
| 2 | 50 | 20 | 20,000 |
| 5 | 300 | 25 | 90,000 |
| 10 | 1,200 | 30 | 350,000 |
| 15 | 5,000 | 40 | 6,500,000 |

*Note: For a specific DC, the value lies between that DC and the DC 1 point above.*

## Mission Compensation
Ninja missions provide a wealth reward equal to a **Profession check** with a bonus based on mission rank.

| Mission Rank | Profession Bonus |
| :---: | :---: |
| D-Rank | +0 |
| C-Rank | +1 |
| B-Rank | +2 |
| A-Rank | +3 |

## Hiring Ninja Costs
Base price for a 3-day mission with 3 ninjas.

| Mission Rank | Purchase DC |

| D-Rank | 13 |

| B-Rank | 25 |

### Cost Modifiers
*   **Additional Time:** +0.5 per day, +2 per week, or +4 per month (choose one).
*   **Additional Ninja:** +2 per ninja.

## Wealth Awards from Treasure
*   Typically, treasure has a Wealth bonus equal to its Challenge Rating (CR).
*   Characters gain wealth equal to a successful purchase of an object with Purchase DC = Treasure Wealth Bonus + 10.
*   Dividing treasure among allies reduces the effective Wealth bonus by 2 per additional share.
*   Wealth bonus increases by 2 per multiplier for hoard-style treasure.

### XP Costs (Casting/Crafting)
*   Experience costs for performing techniques or crafting items scale with rank.

| Rank | XP Multiplier |
| :---: | :---: |
| 1-3 | x1 |
| 4-6 | x2 |
| 7-9 | x4 |
| 10-14 | x10 |
| 15+ | x20 |

*   For items: Level 1-5 (x1), 6-10 (x2), 11-15 (x4), 16+ (x10).
