# Character Creation Guide

This guide provides the step-by-step process for creating a character in the **Naruto: Rokugan Rising** campaign. It combines standard d20 mechanics with a narrative-focused "20 Questions" system designed to anchor your character in the world's rich lore.

## Overview

1.  **Ability Scores:** 36-Point Buy.
2.  **The 20 Questions (Narrative Build):** Determines Race, Class, Occupation, and flavor traits.
3.  **Core Stats:** Hit Points, Chakra, Saving Throws.
4.  **Feats & Skills:** Selecting abilities and proficiencies.
5.  **Equipment & Jutsu:** Outfitting your shinobi.

---

## Step 1: Ability Scores

Generate your ability scores using a **36-point buy** system.
*   **Base:** All scores start at 8.
*   **Costs:**
    *   8 to 14: 1 point per increase.
    *   15 to 16: 2 points per increase.
    *   17 to 18: 3 points per increase.

*Example Array:* 16, 14, 14, 14, 14, 10 (Costs: 10 + 6 + 6 + 6 + 6 + 2 = 36)

## Step 2: The 20 Questions (Narrative & Mechanical Build)

Answer the following questions to define your character's place in the world. Your answers will determine your starting mechanical choices.

### Part I: Core Roots
**1. Which Great Village does your character come from?**
*   *Context:* The Great Villages are the military arms of the Great Clans. Your choice defines your culture and loyalty.
*   *Mechanic:* This counts as your **Race** selection (Human of [Clan/Nation]).
    *   **Benefit:** Gain +1 Minor Benefit and +2 Class Skills related to the nation's culture.

**2. Which Clan does your character descend from?**
*   *Context:* Clans hold specific traditions, secret arts (Hiden), and bloodlines (Kekkei Genkai). If you are clanless, describe how you joined the academy.
*   *Mechanic:* This determines your **Starting Occupation**.
    *   **Benefit:** Gain the bonus class skills and starting wealth modifier associated with your clan or background occupation.

### Part II: Path & Potential
**3. What kind of Shinobi did you train to be?**
*   *Context:* Did you focus on taijutsu, ninjutsu, genjutsu, or a specialized support role?
*   *Mechanic:* Select your **Starting Class** (Strong, Fast, Tough, Smart, Dedicated, or Charismatic).

**4. What are you Nature Affinity?**
*   *Context:* What was the one thing you were naturally good at?
*   *Mechanic:* This determines your **Primary Elemental Affinity** (Fire, Water, Earth, Wind, or Lightning).

### Part III: Shinobido (Caminho Ninja) & Desire (Seu Jeito Ninja! Tô certo!)
**5. What is your Shinobido (Caminho Ninja)?**
*   *Context:* A specific obligation to your lord, clan, or family. It must be something that can conflict with your personal desires.
*   *Examples:* "Protect the heir," "Retrieve the lost scroll," "Clear my father's name."

**6. What is your Desire (Seu Jeito Ninja! Tô certo!)?**
*   *Context:* Your personal code or ambition. It is the source of your internal fire.
*   *Examples:* "I will never go back on my word," "I will become the strongest," "I will prove that hard work beats genius."

**7. What is your relationship with your village?**
*   *Mechanic:*
    *   **Positive:** Gain **+1 Reputation**.
    *   **Negative/Friction:** Gain **+1 Class Skill** (representing self-reliance or alternative learning).

**8. What does your character think of the Shinobi Code (Shinobido)?**
*   *Mechanic:*
    *   **Adherent:** Gain **+2 Action Points** (Conviction).
    *   **Sceptic/Pragmatist:** Gain **4 Skill Points** to invest in **Craft** or **Profession** (Worldly knowledge).

### Part IV: Strengths & Weaknesses
**9. What is your greatest achievement so far?**
*   *Mechanic:* Select your **Level 1 Feat**. It should reflect this achievement.

**10. What does your character resent or regret?**
*   *Mechanic:* (Optional) Take a **Character Flaw (Community Compendium)** to gain a **Bonus Feat**.

**11. What brings your character peace?**
*   *Context:* A hobby, a person, or a place.
*   *Mechanic:* Flavor trait (e.g., "Animal Bond", "Tea Ceremony").

**12. What frightens or disturbs your character?**
*   *Context:* A phobia or deep-seated worry.
*   *Mechanic:* Narrative Flaw (e.g., "Fear of Thunder", "Distrust of Doctors").

**13. Who was your mentor?**
*   *Mechanic:* Choose one **Rank 2 Technique** taught by them and gain one related **Class Skill**.

### Part V: Personality
**14. What do people first notice about you?**
*   *Mechanic:* Define a distinctive **Physical Trait** or **Mannerism**.

**15. How do you react under stress?**
*   *Mechanic:* Narrative guidance for roleplay (e.g., "Becomes cold and analytical," "Gets loud and reckless").

**16. What are your prior relationships?**
*   *Context:* Family, rivals, childhood friends.
*   *Mechanic:* Gain **1 Restricted Item** (non-sealed) given to you by this connection.

### Part VI: Legacy
**17. Who are your parents?**
*   *Mechanic:* Choose a skill you have **0 Ranks** in. Place **2 Ranks** in it (representing parental influence you may have ignored).

**18. Who were you named to honor?**
*   *Mechanic:* Roll on the **Heritage Table** for a minor flavor benefit or omen.

**19. What is your name?**
*   *Context:* Shinobi names often have meanings related to nature, virtues, or clan traditions.

### Part VII: The End
**20. How does your character wish to die?**
*   *Context:* A true shinobi accepts death. Envisioning it defines how you live.

## Step 3: Core Combat Stats

*   **Hit Points:** Max at 1st level. (Hit Die + CON Mod).
*   **Chakra Pool:** `(2 + CON Mod) * Level` (Doubled at 1st Level).
*   **Chakra Reserve:** `2 * Level`.
*   **Base Attack Bonus (BAB) & Saves:** As per Class Table.
*   **Defense:** `10 + Class Bonus + DEX Mod + Equipment`.
*   **Initiative:** `DEX Mod + Misc Mod`.

### Universal Combat Rules (House Rules)
*   **CMB (Combat Maneuver Bonus):** `BAB + STR/DEX Mod + Size`.
*   **CMD (Combat Maneuver Defense):** `10 + BAB + STR + DEX + Size`.
*   **Free Feats:** All characters receive **Combat Martial Arts**, **[Universal: Risky Strike]**, **[Universal: Defensive Stance]**, **Piranha Attack**, **Nin-Weapon Proficiency** and the armor proficiency listed on the class picked at level 1.
*   **Merged Feats:**
    *   *Deft Maneuvers* covers Trip, Disarm, Feint, etc.
    *   *Powerful Maneuvers* covers Bull Rush, Powerful Maneuvers, Overrun.
    *   *Precise Shot* includes *Precise Shot*.

---

## Step 4: Skills

Calculate Skill Points: `(Class Base + INT Mod) * 4` at 1st level.

**Consolidated Skill List:**
*   **Acrobatics:** (Balance + Jump + Tumble)
*   **Stealth:** (Hide + Move Silently)
*   **Perception:** (Spot + Listen + Search)
*   **Chakra Control:** (Concentration + Chakra Control)
*   **Disable Device:** (Open Lock + Disable Device)
*   **Diplomacy:** (Diplomacy + Gather Info)
*   **Linguistics:** (Decipher Script + Speak Language)

---

## Step 5: Equipment & Techniques

1.  **Wealth:** Roll `2d4` wealth points + Occupation bônus + 1 if Profession ranks 
2.  **Starting Techniques:**
    *   You know **1d4 + 1** techniques.
    *   **Plus:** If you have ranks in *Ninjutsu*, you automatically know the "Academy Three": *Bunshin* (Clone), *Henge* (Transform), *Kawarimi* (Substitution).
