# Epic Hero Classes

Basic classes (10 levels) can progress into Epic versions.

## Epic Strong Hero
*   **Action Points:** 8 + 1/2 level.
*   **Skill Points:** 3 + Int.
*   **Talents:** Gained at 11th, 13th, 15th, 17th, 19th.
*   **Trees:** *Extreme Effort, Ignore Hardness, Melee Smash, Smite Allegiance.*

## Epic Fast Hero
*   **Action Points:** 8 + 1/2 level.
*   **Skill Points:** 5 + Int.
*   **Talents:** Gained at 11th, 13th, 15th, 17th, 19th.
*   **Trees:** *Outstanding Speed, Deflect, Increase Speed.*

## Epic Tough Hero
*   **Action Points:** 8 + 1/2 level.
*   **Skill Points:** 3 + Int.
*   **Talents:** Gained at 11th, 13th, 15th, 17th, 19th.
*   **Trees:** *Damage Reduction, Energy Resistance, Toughness.*

## Epic Smart Hero
*   **Action Points:** 8 + 1/2 level.
*   **Skill Points:** 9 + Int.
*   **Talents:** Gained at 11th, 13th, 15th, 17th, 19th.
*   **Trees:** *Research, Strategy.*

## Epic Dedicated Hero
*   **Action Points:** 8 + 1/2 level.
*   **Skill Points:** 5 + Int.
*   **Talents:** Gained at 11th, 13th, 15th, 17th, 19th.
*   **Trees:** *Empathy, Healing, Insight.*

## Epic Charismatic Hero
*   **Action Points:** 8 + 1/2 level.
*   **Skill Points:** 7 + Int.
*   **Talents:** Gained at 11th, 13th, 15th, 17th, 19th.
*   **Trees:** *Fast-talk, Leadership.*
