# Epic Level Progression (21+)

Once a character reaches 20th level, they may progress into Epic levels.

## General Rules
*   **Epic Attack and Defense Bonus:** +1 cumulative bonus to attack and defense every even-numbered level (22, 24, etc.).
*   **Epic Saves:** +1 cumulative bonus on all saving throws at every odd-numbered level (21, 23, etc.).
*   **Epic Reputation:** +1 cumulative bonus to reputation every level.

## Epic Chakra Signatures
| Source | Legendary | Godlike |
| :--- | :---: | :---: |
| Chakra Signature (Pool) | 150 - 299 | 300+ |

*   **Legendary:** Can be located without sight/light. Counts as *Strong* to dormant senses.
*   **Godlike:** Overwhelms the senses. Sensing a Godlike source requires a Will save (DC 18) each round or become dazed. Counts as *Overwhelming*.
