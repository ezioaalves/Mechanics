# Epic Prestige Class Progressions

Prestige classes with 10 levels have epic progressions.

*   **Epic Ninja Scout:** Sneak attack +1d6 every 4 levels (starting at 12th). Speed rank increases.
*   **Epic Puppeteer:** Gained *Epic Puppetry Skills* (Epic Defense, Flight, Grapple, Puppet, Range, Sneak Attack).
*   **Epic Sacred Fist:** Damage Reduction 5/chakra (improves at 19th). Sacred Fist Stance damage increases (up to 2d10).
*   **Epic Samurai:** Gained *Armor Adaptation, Supreme Path Mastery, Armor Supremacy.*
*   **Epic Shinobi Swordsman:** Gained *Hide in Plain Sight* (even while observed, within 10 ft of shadow).
*   **Epic Taijutsu Master:** Gained *Epic Taijutsu Mastery* (Greater Weapon Mastery, Stance Mastery, etc.). Unarmed damage increases to 2d10 (Medium).
