# Missions and Bounties

## Missions
Missions are the lifeblood of a shinobi village.

### Ranks
*   **D-Rank:** Genins. Non-lethal manual labor. Minimal pay.
*   **C-Rank:** Genins/Chuunins. Bandits, document recovery. Outside village.
*   **B-Rank:** Chuunins/Jounins. Espionage, bodyguard, ninja battles. Dangerous.
*   **A-Rank:** Jounins. High-risk, assassination, renegade ninjas.
*   **S-Rank:** Elite Jounin/ANBU. Top-secret, high lethality.

### Bounty Hunting
The warning level system on bounty posters:
*   **-:** Pay Class E/D. No lethal force permitted.
*   **C:** Armed but unskilled. Handled with caution.
*   **B:** Armed and proficient. Lethal force permitted.
*   **A:** Extremely dangerous. Lethal force encouraged.
*   **AA:** Highly skilled. Kill on sight (DOA status).
*   **AAA:** Highest warning. Long-standing threat. Double/Triple rewards.
*   **(S):** Suspected Shinobi skills.
*   **(BBA):** Bring Back Alive.
*   **(DOA):** Dead or Alive.
