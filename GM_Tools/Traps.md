# Mechanical Traps

Traps are defined by their location, trigger, reset, and effect.

## Elements of a Trap
*   **Trigger:** Location, Proximity (Detect 20 ft), Sound (+15 Listen), Touch, Timed.
*   **Reset:** No Reset, Repair, Manual, Automatic.
*   **Bypass:** Lock (Disable DC 30), Hidden Switch (Search DC 25).
*   **Detection:** Search DC 20 for simple traps.

## Damage and Effects
*   **Pits:** 1d6 damage per 10 feet fallen.
*   **Spikes:** Atk +10 melee. 1d4 spikes per target (1d6+1 each).
*   **Kunai Shower:** Ranged attack vs target.

## Designing Traps (CR Modifiers)
| Feature | Modifier |
| :--- | :---: |
| Search DC 15 / 25 / 30 | -1 / +1 / +2 |
| Disable DC 15 / 25 / 30 | -1 / +1 / +2 |
| Reflex DC 15 / 25 / 30 | -1 / +1 / +2 |
| Attack Bonus +1 to +5 | -1 |
| Attack Bonus +15 to +19 | +1 |
| Liquid (Drowning) | +5 |
| Onset delay 1 round | +3 |

## Trap Cost
*   **Base Cost:** Purchase DC 20.
*   **Crafting:** DC varies by CR (CR 1-3: DC 20; CR 11+: DC 35).
