# Tiger (Tora)
World's finest ambush predators. prideful and strong of heart.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast
**Hit Dice:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian (4+summon level), Noble (7+summon level), Champion (11+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—2/level; Protector—3/level plus 5; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—3.5/level plus 18; Champion—6/level plus 20.

### Abilities
*   **Pounce (Ex):** Full attack on charge.
*   **Rake (Ex):** Extra attacks on grapple/pounce (Tiger 1d8+3, Giant 2d6+4).
*   **Improved Grab (Ex):** Establish hold and rake.
