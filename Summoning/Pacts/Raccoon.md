# Raccoon (Araiguma)
Proud, unusual creatures that can grow enormously large.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (BAB 3/4)
**Hit Dice:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (6+summon level), Champion (10+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—3/level plus 15; Champion—5/level plus 20.

### Abilities
*   **Natural Weapons:** 2 claw and 1 bite attack. Opposable thumbs.
*   **Scent (Ex):** Detect approaching enemies.
*   **Skill Bonuses:** +4 racial bonus on Climb and Move Silently.
