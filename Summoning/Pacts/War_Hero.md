# War Hero (Gunshin)
Materialized spirits of fallen heroes. robed and masked humans with fine weaponry.

**Creature Type:** Humanoid (Good Fortitude, Reflex, Will; BAB Total)
**Hit Dice:** 1d10
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian (4+summon level), Noble (6+summon level), Champion (10+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—3/level; Protector—3/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—3/level plus 15; Champion—5/level plus 20.

### Abilities
*   **Battle Arsenal:** Summoned with a random melee weapon/armor (enhancement scales by rank).
*   **Battle Senses (Ex):** Insight bonus to Defense, cannot be flanked.
*   **Nittoryu:** Noble/Champion level can wield two weapons.
