# Hare (Usagi)
A hare is a swift and agile creature that has been known to elude even the most tenacious of pursuers. Excellent messenger.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Reflex and Will; BAB 3/4)
**Hit Dice:** 1d6
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (4+summon level), Champion (8+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 7; Noble—3/level plus 12; Champion—3.5/level plus 15.

### Abilities
*   **Darkvision (Ex):** 60 feet.
*   **Scent (Ex):** Detect approaching enemies, sniff out hidden foes.
*   **Skill Bonuses:** +8 racial Hide, Jump, Listen, Spot; +4 racial Move Silently.
