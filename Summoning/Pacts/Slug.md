# Slug (Namekuji)
Knowledgeable and kind-hearted. Often looked down upon but grow to massive size.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Fortitude, Will; BAB 1/2)
**Hit Dice:** 1d6
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (4+summon level), Champion (8+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 7; Noble—3/level plus 12; Champion—4/level plus 18.

### Abilities
*   **Acid Spit (Su):** 60-ft range. Damage scales by rank (Soldier 2d8, Champion 8d8).
*   **Escape Artist (Su):** Escape grapple by breaking into smaller slugs.
*   **Immunities:** Mind-affecting, massive damage. Cannot be knocked prone.
*   **Slow:** Cannot run or charge.
