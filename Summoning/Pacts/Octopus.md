# Octopus (Tako)
Bottom-dwelling hunter. Aggressive and dangerous.

**Creature Type:** Animal (Aquatic)
**Hit Dice:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian (4+summon level), Noble (7+summon level), Champion (11+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—3/level plus 15; Champion—5/level plus 20.

### Abilities
*   **Constrict (Ex):** 2d6 + 1.5x Str damage on successful grapple.
*   **Improved Grab (Ex):** Free grapple attempt on tentacle hit.
*   **Ink Cloud (Ex):** Total concealment.
*   **Jet (Ex):** Move 200 ft as full-round action.
*   **Tentacles:** Bite and 8 tentacle attacks.
