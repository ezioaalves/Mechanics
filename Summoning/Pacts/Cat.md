# Cat (Neko)
The cat is a proud, capricious animal. Clever and intuitive. Prefers a summoner who will lavish it with attention and understands the value of their pact.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Reflex; BAB 3/4)
**Hit Dice:** 1d6
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (5+summon level), Champion (9+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 7; Noble—3/level plus 12; Champion—4/level plus 18.

### Abilities
*   **Catfall (Ex):** Treat fall as 40 feet shorter.
*   **Natural Weapons:** 2 claw attacks and 1 bite attack.
*   **Scent (Ex):** Detect approaching enemies.
*   **Skill Bonuses:** +4 racial bonus to Climb, Hide and Move Silently; +8 racial to Jump and Balance.
