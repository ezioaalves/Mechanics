# Boar (Osu-buta)
A boar may not be the smartest of creatures but it does possess one redeeming quality, its almost single-minded dedication to a task or ideal.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Fortitude; BAB 3/4)
**Hit Dice:** 1d12
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (6+summon level), Champion (10+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—2.5/level; Protector—3/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—3/level plus 15; Champion—4.5/level plus 20.

### Abilities
*   **Darkvision (Ex):** 60 feet.
*   **Scent (Ex):** Detect approaching enemies, sniff out hidden foes.
*   **Trample (Ex):** Damage equal to gore damage.
