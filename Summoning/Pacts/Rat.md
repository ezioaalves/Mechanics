# Rat (Nezumi)
Stealthy and insidious. Known for sneaking and spreading disease.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (BAB 3/4)
**Hit Dice:** 1d6
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (4+summon level), Champion (8+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 7; Noble—3/level plus 12; Champion—4/level plus 18.

### Abilities
*   **Disease Carrier (Ex):** Bite inflicts disease (DC 15, 1 Con damage).
*   **Skill Bonuses:** +8 racial Balance and Move Silently; +8 racial Hide at night.
