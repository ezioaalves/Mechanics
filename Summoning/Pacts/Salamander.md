# Salamander (Hanzaki)
Aquatic, steam-breathing lizard. Loyal and flocks towards strong personalities.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Fortitude, Aquatic subtype)
**Hit Dice:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (5+summon level), Champion (10+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—3.5/level plus 15; Champion—5.5/level plus 20.

### Abilities
*   **Breath Weapon (Su):** 2d4/level (half fire, half water). 30-ft cone. DC 10+level+Con.
*   **Regeneration (Ex):** Regrow lost limbs (not head).
*   **Swallow Whole (Ex):** Large+ only.
