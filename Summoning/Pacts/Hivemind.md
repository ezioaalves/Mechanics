# Hivemind (Mushigokoro)
Mindless soldiers that follow orders without question. Includes Wasps, Centipedes, Giant Beetles, Scorpions, and Mantises.

**Creature Type:** Vermin
**Hit Dice:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian (4+summon level), Noble (6+summon level), Champion (10+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—2/level; Protector—3/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—3/level plus 18; Champion—4.5/level plus 20.

### Abilities
*   **Armored Chitin (Ex):** +1 natural armor every 2 summon levels.
*   **Darkvision (Ex):** 60 feet.
*   **Telepathy (Su):** 30 feet (Noble+ sentient, Champion 100 ft).

*Note: Specific variants (Wasp, Centipede, etc.) have distinct stat blocks.*
