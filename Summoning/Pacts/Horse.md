# Horse (Uma)
Creatures of determination. Can be deadly in combat with their kicks.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Fortitude and Will)
**Hit Dice:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (5+summon level), Champion (9+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—2/level; Protector—3/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—3/level plus 18; Champion—4.5/level plus 20.

### Abilities
*   **Bonus Feat:** Endurance.
*   **Increased Carrying Capacity (Ex):** Treated as one size larger for load.
*   **Scent (Ex):** Detect approaching enemies.
