# Snake (Hebi)
Treacherous and sneaky. Serve only those who show equal power.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Reflex, Will)
**Hit Dice:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (6+summon level), Champion (10+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—2/level; Protector—2/level plus 5; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—3.5/level plus 15; Champion—5.5/level plus 25.

### Abilities
*   **Constrict (Ex):** Damage equal to bite (Giant: 1d6+6).
*   **Poison (Ex):** 1d4/1d4 Str damage (Giant: DC 12).
*   **Swallow Whole (Ex):** Large+ only.
