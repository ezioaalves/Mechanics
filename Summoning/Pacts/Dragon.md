# Dragon (Ryuu)
These mythical creatures are thought to be nothing but legends. There are six types: Earth, Fire, Ice, Lightning, Water, and Wind.

**Creature Type:** Dragon
**Hit Dice:** 1d10
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector (4+summon level), Guardian (6+summon level), Noble (8+summon level), Champion (12+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—4/level; Protector—5/level plus 5; Guardian—5/level plus 15; Noble—6/level plus 20; Champion—7/level plus 30.

### Abilities
*   **Breath Weapon (Su):** 2d4 damage per summon level (40-ft cone or 60-ft line). Usable once per 1d6 rounds.
*   **Darkvision (Ex):** 90 feet.
*   **Energy Resistance (Ex):** 5 + 2 per summon level against its given type.
*   **Flight (Ex):** All dragons are capable of flight.

*Note: Stats vary significantly by type (Earth, Fire, etc.). See core rulebook for full tables.*
