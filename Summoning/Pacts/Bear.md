# Bear (Kuma)
This powerful animal is one of the strongest of mother nature's creation. Bane of hunters and even fierce woodland creatures, its pride forces it to charge head first and tear apart any enemy that stands in its way.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Fortitude)
**Hit Dice:** 1d10
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian (4+summon level), Noble (7+summon level), Champion (11+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—3/level; Protector—3/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—3.5/level plus 18; Champion—5.5/level plus 20.

### Abilities
*   **Darkvision (Ex):** 60 feet.
*   **Improved Grab (Ex):** To succeed, hit with two claw attacks.
*   **Scent (Ex):** Detect approaching enemies, sniff out hidden foes.
