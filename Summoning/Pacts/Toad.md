# Toad (Gama)
Intelligent and patient. appreciate an easy life but respect the knowledgeable.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Reflex, Will, Aquatic subtype)
**Hit Dice:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (6+summon level), Champion (10+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—3/level plus 15; Champion—5/level plus 20.

### Abilities
*   **Tongue (Ex):** Grapple at double reach (Champion: 4x reach).
*   **Salt Water Aversion:** -4 penalty when exposed to salt water.
*   **Swallow Whole (Ex):** Large+ only.
