# Rhinoceros (Sai)
Headstrong and dangerous animal. Only accept summoners who show pride.

**Creature Type:** Animal (Good Fortitude)
**Hit Dice:** 1d10
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (6+summon level), Champion (10+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—3/level; Protector—3/level plus 5; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—4/level plus 15; Champion—5.5/level plus 20.

### Abilities
*   **Powerful Charge (Ex):** Double gore damage on charge.
*   **Trample (Ex):** Damage equal to gore (DC 10+level+Str).
*   **Natural Weapons:** 1 gore attack.
