# Demon Clown (Onidoke)

All scholars agree that it is best not to delve too deep into the origins of the fabled onidoke, as one shudders to contemplate the depths of depravity that must have preceded their unholy creation. Demon clowns are tall and, strangely, closely resemble clowns, but with twisted, nightmarish features. They can understand and produce speech, a sound anyone should be glad to go their entire lives not hearing.

**Creature Type:** Fey (Good Fortitude and Will; BAB 3/4)
**HD:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (5+summon level), Champion (9+summon level), Celestial (Epic Summoner and Celestial Pact).
**Summoning Cost:** Soldier—2/level; Protector—3/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—3/level plus 18; Champion—4.5/level plus 20.

### Abilities
*   **Inscrutable Darkness (Su):** Any area of dim light in a 5-ft. radius emanation around a demon clown is considered total darkness instead.
*   **Natural Weapons:** 2 claw attacks.
*   **Sinister Stare (Su):** Guardian+ rank can shoot a stare at any sentient creature within 30 feet. The creature suffers 1d4 damage per summon level, halved with a Will save DC 10+summon level+Cha modifier. Fear effect. If in dim/total darkness, allies of target must save or be shaken for 1d4 rounds.
*   **Vanish (Su):** Champion+ rank can hide as a swift action when it reduces a creature to –1 HP or scores a critical hit. Must be within 5 feet of cover.
