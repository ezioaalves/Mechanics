# Wolf (Okami)
Fast, enduring and dangerous. Pack animals, strongest together.

**Creature Type:** Animal
**Hit Dice:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (6+summon level), Champion (10+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—3/level plus 15; Champion—5/level plus 20.

### Abilities
*   **Scent (Ex):** Detect approaching enemies.
*   **Strength of the Pack (Su):** +2 bonus to attack, damage, trip, saves vs fear within 50 ft of 2+ wolves/summoners.
*   **Trip (Ex):** Free trip attempt on bite hit.
*   **Natural Weapons:** 1 bite attack.
