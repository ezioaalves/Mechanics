# Elephant (Zou)
Large, passive animal with exceptional bulk and physical strength.

**Creature Type:** Animal (Good Fortitude and Will)
**Hit Dice:** 1d10
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian (4+summon level), Noble (7+summon level), Champion (11+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—3/level; Protector—3.5/level plus 5; Guardian—4/level plus 10; Noble—4.5/level plus 15; Champion—6/level plus 20.

### Abilities
*   **Trumpet Call (Ex):** Stun/Deafen in radius (Will DC 10+level+Con).
*   **Trample (Ex):** Damage equal to gore.
*   **Natural Weapons:** Gore or slam and 2 stamps.
