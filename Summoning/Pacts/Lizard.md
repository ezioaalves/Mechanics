# Lizard (Tokage)
Patient and combat-able. Can be both bipedal and quadrupedal.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Reflex; BAB 3/4)
**Hit Dice:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (6+summon level), Champion (10+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 7; Noble—3/level plus 12; Champion—4.5/level plus 18.

### Abilities
*   **Posture (Ex):** Switch between biped (weapons) and quadruped (speed/load).
*   **Poison (Ex):** 1d3 Str damage (Giant: 1d8).
*   **Skill Bonuses:** +8 racial Balance, +4 racial Hide/Move Silently/Survival.
