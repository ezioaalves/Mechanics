# Hawk (Taka)
Swift, silent, and deadly. Known for their keen eyesight and ability to strike without warning.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Reflex; BAB 3/4)
**Hit Dice:** 1d6
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (5+summon level), Champion (9+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 7; Noble—3/level plus 12; Champion—4/level plus 18.

### Abilities
*   **Darkvision (Ex):** 180 feet.
*   **Fling (Ex):** Can drop or fling a smaller grabbed creature (30 feet, 3d6 damage).
*   **Skill Bonuses:** +8 racial Spot and Survival in daylight.
