# Raven (Karasu)
Clever and sturdy. Proficient at detecting foes and taking hits.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Fortitude, Reflex)
**Hit Dice:** 1d6
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (5+summon level), Champion (9+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 7; Noble—3/level plus 12; Champion—4/level plus 18.

### Abilities
*   **Darkvision (Ex):** 60 feet.
*   **Skill Bonuses:** +2 racial Listen/Spot; +4 racial Hide at night.
