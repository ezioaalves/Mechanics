# Dog (Inu)
A dog is a loyal and dedicated animal. It is a kind, loving, and protective soul that will defend its friends and family when the situation calls for it.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Reflex; BAB 3/4)
**Hit Dice:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (5+summon level), Champion (9+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 7; Noble—3/level plus 12; Champion—4/level plus 18.

### Abilities
*   **Darkvision (Ex):** 60 feet.
*   **Scent (Ex):** Detect approaching enemies, sniff out hidden foes.
*   **Skill Bonuses:** +2 racial to Jump, +6 racial to Survival when tracking by scent.
