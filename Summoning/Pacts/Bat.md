# Bat (Koumori)
The bat is a nocturnal animal with very acute senses. Quick and capable of avoiding dangerous situations.

**Creature Type:** Animal (Good Reflex and Will)
**Hit Dice:** 1d6
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (5+summon level), Champion (9+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 7; Noble—3/level plus 12; Champion—3.5/level plus 15.

### Abilities
*   **Blindsense (Su):** Detect things using echolocation.
*   **Natural Weapons:** 1 bite attack.
*   **Skill Bonuses:** +4 racial bonus to Listen and Spot check.
