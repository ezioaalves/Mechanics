The fox is a creature known above all else, for its quick 

wit and razor -sharp cunning. It's with these two things 

that let it stand out against more legendary creatures, 

and often marks its place in tales and folklore as a 

prankster and a wily foe. The fox favours a master who 

enjoys elaborate plans and ruses, and will not answer to 

one who is blunt in their actions.

ability.
