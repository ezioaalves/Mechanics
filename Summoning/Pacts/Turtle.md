# Turtle (Kame)
Known for endurance and wisdom. Slow but hardy.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Fortitude, Aquatic subtype; BAB 3/4)
**Hit Dice:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (5+summon level), Champion (9+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 7; Noble—3/level plus 12; Champion—4.5/level plus 18.

### Abilities
*   **Shell:** +2 natural armor each size category increase.
*   **Slow:** Cannot run, but can still charge.
*   **Skill Bonuses:** +8 racial to Concentration and Swim.
