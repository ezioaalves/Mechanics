# Ram (O-hitsuji)
Headstrong and brash. Often charge head-first into obstacles.

**Creature Type:** Magical Beast (Good Fortitude; BAB 3/4)
**Hit Dice:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble (5+summon level), Champion (9+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—1.5/level; Protector—2/level plus 3; Guardian—3/level plus 10; Noble—3/level plus 15; Champion—5/level plus 20.

### Abilities
