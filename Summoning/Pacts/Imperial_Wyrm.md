# Imperial Wyrm (Tenryuu)
Serpentine, wingless dragon with astonishing healing abilities. Symbols of luck and integrity.

**Creature Type:** Dragon
**Hit Dice:** 1d8
**Rank Requirements:** Soldier, Protector (4+summon level), Guardian (6+summon level), Noble (8+summon level), Champion (12+summon level).
**Chakra Cost:** Soldier—4/level; Protector—5/level plus 5; Guardian—5/level plus 15; Noble—6/level plus 20; Champion—7/level plus 30.

### Abilities
*   **Fast Healing (Su):** Fast healing 1+.
*   **Flight (Su):** Wingless flight.
*   **Mending Touch (Su):** Heal another with touch (1 chakra per 2 HP).
*   **Sense Hostility (Su):** Sense hostile intent within 60 feet.
