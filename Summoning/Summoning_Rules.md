# Summoning Rules

Summoning is yet another handy tool at the disposal of ninja lucky enough to have signed a blood pact with a summon tribe, allowing for him to call upon its power in times of need. A blood pact is typically signed between the summoner and a tribe, and often constitutes an alliance between the tribe and the summoner's clan or village, or simply an oath of loyalty between the two.

## Rank Requirements
A summoned creature belongs to a specific rank: **Soldier, Protector, Guardian, Noble and Champion.** Each rank has different level requirements to be summoned, individual to each blood pact. These requirements are specified parenthetically after each rank (e.g., Noble (8+summon level)).

*   **Soldier (Bushi):** Often called for mundane tasks. They are compelled to obey the summoner.
*   **Protector (Hogosha):** More efficient but harder to summon. Capable of speech. Can summon a blood pact contract at will as a full-round action or dismiss it as a free action.
*   **Guardian (Shugorei):** Strong, typically larger summons with even greater abilities. Compelled to obey.
*   **Noble (Seihei):** Rarely encountered. They have free will but are partial to their summoner's orders.
*   **Champion (Shingen):** A pillar of strength. Very difficult and costly to summon. They can share energy with their summoner (Full-round action, 2 damage/chakra to self to grant 1 temporary chakra to summoner). They always have free will and may ask for compensation/tribute.

## Summon Leveling
A summon's level determines its hit dice, skill points, and chakra cost. Every level, a summon gains 2 hit dice, 2+Int modifier (min 1) skill, and its bonus attack/saves increase. A summon cannot exceed level 10 (20 HD).

### Death and Injury
When a summon is defeated (0 or lower hp), it does not die but simply returns to its original plane. It cannot rest or heal until it returns.

### Chakra Pool and Duration
Summons have the same chakra pool as a heroic character but lose 1 point of chakra every 10 minutes summoned (Elites: 20m, Paragons: 30m). If the pool hits 0, they immediately unsummon.

### Multiple Summons
A character can summon up to five creatures whose total summon level does not exceed his total level or hit dice.

## Elites and Paragons
There is a 10% chance to summon a special version. 1-90 results in an **Elite**, 91-100 results in a **Paragon**.

*   **Elite (+1 chakra/level):** Gained 1 bonus feat, +1 skill point/level, and one secondary elemental affinity.
*   **Paragon (+2 chakra/level):** Gained 1 bonus feat, feat every 3 HD, +2 skill points/level, and one primary or two secondary elemental affinities.
*   **Technique:** Sages can summon these versions at will with an increased rank technique (Rank 4 + 1/2 summon level +1 for Elite/+2 for Paragon).

***

## Signing a Blood Pact
Contracts are obtained in three ways:
1.  A Protector+ rank summon of the type summons a copy for the character to sign.
2.  Finding an original scroll in the world.
3.  Making a new contract with a tribe directly.

### Blood Pact Bonuses
When a character signs a blood pact, they gain a specific bonus based on the creature type.

| Creature Type | Requirements | Blood Pact Bonus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ape** | Str 15, Climb 6 ranks | +4 competence bonus to Climb checks |
| **Bat** | Listen and Spot 4 ranks | +2 morale bonus to Listen and Spot checks |
| **Cat** | — | +2 morale bonus to Hide and Jump checks |
| **Crocodile** | Str 15 or Con 15 | Hold breath for 4 x Con score rounds |
| **Elephant** | Str 15 or Con 15 | Wake any sleeping creature within 100 feet with a shout |
| **Octopus** | Swim 6 ranks | +4 competence bonus to Swim checks |
| **Raccoon** | Int 13 or Wis 13 | +2 competence bonus to Escape Artist and Move Silently |
| **Rhinoceros** | Con 15 | Powerful charge +1d6 |
| **Tyrant** | Str/Cha 17, Intimidate 9 ranks | +4 morale bonus to Will saves against fear |
| **Wolf** | — | Trip attempts do not provoke AOOs (2/day) |

Only **Unique Creatures** (Elite/Paragon versions) belong to a specific tribe or clan. Normal summons can be summoned by anyone with the pact.
