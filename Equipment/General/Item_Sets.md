# Item Sets

SUBSECTION II–A: ITEM SETS
The two aforementioned item sets are contained in this small subsection. The complete
rules for item sets, as well as more item sets, can be found within the free web
enhancement Relic Repository.
Jungle Stalker's Gear
Highly prised and sought-after by hunters and nightstalkers, this collection of items can
turn even a novice into a credible threat.
Item Set (5 pieces)
Power (1/day): 2 pieces set bonus. Pounce on a foe and make a full-attack action as part
of a charge.
Power (1/day, attack action): 5 pieces set bonus. Transform into a Cat or Tiger for 30
minutes.
Special: To craft a piece of the jungle stalker's gear, you must possess at least one piece
of the set.

Raiment of the Lady Samurai
The raiment of the Lady Samurai has been an ancestral relic of many a samurai family,
passed down from mother to daughter every generation of warrior.
Item Set (4 pieces, uncraftable)
Property: 2 pieces set bonus. You gain a +2 resistance bonus to Will saves against fear.
Power (3/day): 4 pieces set bonus. Use when performing a technique with the Chakra
Weaving class ability. You gain a +1 bonus to the saving throw difficulty class of that
technique. This ability does not stack with other abilities that increase the saving throw
difficulty, like Heighten Technique.
Special: Items of this set cannot be crafted.

Mark of Honor [Armor, Sealed Item]
Sealed Item (ironium samurai armor)
Greater (Lv 16)
+3 DC 39
Enhancement: Defense
Power (2/day): Grants a +2 circumstance bonus to skill checks, ability checks and attack
rolls towards any action taken in accordance to the Honor allegiance. A wearer without
the allegiance suffers a -4 penalty to all ability scores for 1 hour or until he removes the
armor or performs an honorable action.
Set: This item is part of a set of four: Raiment of the Lady Samurai.
Blade of Ironheart [Sealed Item, Weapon]
Sealed Item (thorium katana)
Superior (Lv 11)
+2 DC 28
Enhancement: Attack and weapon damage rolls
Property: This weapon is always treated as a chakra state weapon by a wielder with the
ability.
Power (At-Will, attack action): Use when making a successful attack with this weapon.
Discharges the stored technique as though it had been performed normally. A technique
requiring a touch attack automatically hits. Can be used as part of a full-attack action.
Power (3/day, free action): 1 chakra. You can store a single chakra control, genjutsu or
ninjutsu technique in the weapon as long as it is performed in 3 rounds of activating this
seal. The technique must have a perform time of "1 attack action." 
The technique is held for 3 days or until discharged. The technique must be used
normally to be stored, and can be empowered or affected by meta-chakra feats as normal.
The seal can contain up to 15 chakra.
Set: This item is part of a set of four: Raiment of the Lady Samurai.
