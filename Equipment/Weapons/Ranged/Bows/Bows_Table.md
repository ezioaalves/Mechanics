## Daikyuu

This weapon can be loaded or reloaded as part of the firing process, which requires no additional action. The wielder applies his Strength score to damage rolls with this weapon (maximum +4).

The japanese longbow has a rate of fire of 1, meaning that it cannot fire two shots with one attack even with feats that would normally allow it.

---

## Greatbow

The japanese greatbow is used at shorter range to deliver deadlier attack. The wielder adds his Strength modifier to damage rolls. It otherwise functions the same as a daikyuu.

---

## Hankyuu

The wielder does not add his Strength modifier to damage rolls. The hankyuu otherwise functions the same as daikyuu.
