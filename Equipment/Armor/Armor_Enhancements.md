# Way of the Master Armor Enhancements

## Warding [Armor]
*   Superior (Lv 11): +1 DC 26 
*   Greater (Lv 18): +2 DC 38

**Enhancement:** Defense
**Item Slot:** Armor (light or medium)
**Property:** The armor counts as one category larger to defend against Kyushojutsu techniques (light becomes medium, medium becomes heavy).
*Level 18:* The armor counts as two category larger.
