## Core Descriptions

### Armor Puppet
A unique Medium construct that can be worn by the puppeteer as armor (occupying the Chest slot). It provides a +4 armor bonus to Defense. As a move action, the puppeteer can "eject" from the puppet, which then becomes a controlled entity. If the puppet is destroyed while worn, the puppeteer takes 50% of the damage.

### Transforming Puppet
A puppet capable of shifting between two different forms (e.g., Humanoid and Animal). Switching forms is a move action. Each form can have a different set of components, but the total number of components cannot exceed the larger form's slots.

### Tauric Puppet
A centaur-like puppet with a humanoid upper body and a quadrupedal lower body. It gains a +4 bonus to Stability checks and has increased carrying capacity.

### Quadruped Puppet
A four-legged animal puppet. It gains a +10 ft bonus to land speed and a +4 bonus to Jump checks.
