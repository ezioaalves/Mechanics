# Advanced Puppet Crafting

## The Repair Skill
The Repair skill is foundational for any puppeteer. It is used for:
*   **Reloading:** DC 15 + component complexity to reload spent charges (Kunai, Dragon's Breath).
*   **Maintenance:** Fixing a disabled puppet (0 HP). Recovery takes 1 hour and a DC 20 Repair check.
*   **Installation:** Installing a new component takes 10 minutes and a DC 15 Repair check.

## Base Puppet Crafting
Crafting a puppet core requires a Craft (mechanical) check.

## Human Puppets
Crafted from a humanoid, monstrous humanoid or giant corpse.
*   **Spot DC:** 5 to recognize as a puppet.
*   **Threads:** Requires B-Class or better (D-Class imposes -4 penalty).
*   **Hardness:** Small (2), Medium (3), Large+ (5).
*   **Ability Scores:** Strength/Dexterity based on base creature. Max modifiers depend on size (Small: Str+3/Dex+7, Medium: 5/5, Large: Str+7/Dex+3).
*   **Bonus Chakra:** 1 per 10 points of original creature's pool (max +5). Recovers every 24h.

## Animal Puppets
Crafted from an animal, magical beast or vermin.
*   **Hardness:** Same as human puppets.
*   **Natural Attacks:** Retains up to 2 of each original natural attack (max 4 total).
*   **Bonus Chakra:** 1 per 20 points of original pool (max +5).
