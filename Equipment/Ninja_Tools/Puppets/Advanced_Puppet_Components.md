# Advanced Puppet Components

## Structural
*   **Additional Limbs:** +1 slam attack. Weapon cost +1 per instance.
*   **Adept Body:** No penalty for using skills through the puppet.
*   **Armor Plating:** Light (+2 Def), Medium (+4 Def, -2 A CP, -10 ft speed), Heavy (+8 Def, -4 ACP, -10 ft speed).
*   **Auto-Repair Kit:** Removes -1 equipment penalty.
*   **Light Frame:** +2 Dex, -2 Str. Weight reduced by 10 lbs (min half).

## Weapons
*   **Blades:** 1d8 damage (19-20 crit). Hidden variants require Spot vs Atk.
*   **Elemental Cannon:** 2d6 damage (requires Elemental Scroll). Superior variant deals 1d6 splash.
*   **Dragon's Breath (Flamethrower):** 20-ft cone (3d8 fire). Greater variant (40-ft cone, 6d8).
*   **Kunai Shower:** 15-ft cone (2d4+2). Marks II and III increase damage.
*   **Poison Cloud:** Fills a 10-ft radius with inhaled poison (1 charge). Reloading takes 5 mins.
*   **Porcupine Trap:** 20-ft burst (3d6+3 piercing). Can be poisoned.
*   **Vise Grip:** Deals increased slam damage when grappling.

## Special
*   **Chakra Accelerator:** +1 HP per 2 HD (Greater: 1 HP per HD).
*   **Enhanced Machinery:** Grants Speed and Strength ranks 1-3.
*   **Hollow Body:** Store items (1 item 1 size larger, or 2 same size, etc).
*   **Torso Tank:** Swallow Whole ability (inside torso).
*   **Sea of Open Hands:** Medium entity for 15-ft range grappling.
