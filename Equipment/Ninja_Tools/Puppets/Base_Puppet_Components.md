# Base Puppet Components (Redux Ch13)

*Source: NarutoD20 Redux v1.3, Chapter 13 – Ninja Tools, Puppet Components (p402–406). This is the complete base component catalog. For puppet cores, component points and slot rules see [[Puppet_Crafting]]; [[Advanced_Puppet_Components]] is a condensed GM-helper summary, and [[Playwrights_Primer_Components]] adds supplement/epic components.*

## Reading a component entry

Components are added to puppet cores via the **Repair** skill. If a component has an attack or effect requiring a save, the DC is **10 + half the puppet's HD + puppeteer's Intelligence modifier**.

- **Name (Requirements) [Activated]:** If a component must be activated by the puppeteer, it carries the **[Activated]** descriptor. Activating it is an attack action unless otherwise noted.
- **Slot:** The slot type the component fits into (Head, Limb, Chest, Any or None).
- **Points:** Weapon or Utility points the component uses.
- **Cost:** Purchase DC for buying the component.
- **Difficulty:** If listed, the rank of Chakra threads the puppet must be animated by (per *Ninpou: Chakra no Ito*) to activate or use it.
- **Weight:** Added to the puppet's core.
- **Installing a Component:** Make a Repair check with DC equal to the component's Cost.

## Components

### Additional Limbs
Slot: Limbs; Points: 1 Weapon; Difficulty: C-Class; Cost: 20; Weight: 4 lbs.
The puppet gains an additional slam attack. You can add multiple of this component; each time, the weapon point cost to add another increases by 1. This component can be added at half its normal cost (rounded up) to living puppets whose bodies have multiple limbs, up to the number of limbs their body has. Such limbs must be identical to its original limbs in function.

### Adept Body
Slot: Chest; Points: 1 Utility; Cost: 20; Weight: reduced by 5 lbs.
The puppet can be used to perform skills without the usual −4 penalty.

### Armor Plating, Heavy
Slot: Chest; Points: 2 Utility; Cost: 25; Weight: 50 lbs.
Integrated heavy armor: +8 armor bonus to Defense, −4 armor check penalty, +1 maximum Dexterity bonus to Defense, and movement speed reduced by 10 ft. Cannot be sundered or removed.

### Armor Plating, Light
Slot: Chest; Points: 1 Utility; Cost: 15; Weight: 15 lbs.
Integrated light armor: +2 armor bonus to Defense. Cannot be sundered or removed.

### Armor Plating, Medium
Slot: Chest; Points: 1 Utility; Cost: 20; Weight: 30 lbs.
Integrated medium armor: +4 armor bonus to Defense, −2 armor check penalty, +4 maximum Dexterity bonus to Defense, and movement speed reduced by 10 ft. Cannot be sundered or removed.

### Auto-Repair Kit
Slot: Chest; Points: 2 Utility; Cost: 19; Weight: 5 lbs.
Activates when the puppet gains an equipment penalty from being defeated, removing −1 from the penalty after 1 round. Once activated, the component is removed; it cannot be repaired or reused.

### Blades
Slot: Limbs; Points: 1 Weapon; Cost: 15; Weight: 1 lbs.
The puppet's main hand holds a weapon equivalent to a Longsword made for its size. Cannot be disarmed. A puppet cannot combine multiple blades in the same limb/hand.

### Blades, Hidden (Requires Blades (any))
Slot: None; Points: 1 Weapon; Cost: 20; Weight: 0 lbs.
Attached to a Blade component, allowing the weapon to be held inside the puppet's limb or body, drawn or released as a free action. It can be released as part of an attack, in which case the target must make a Spot check (DC equal to the attack roll) or be denied its Dexterity bonus to Defense against the attack. The blade can be sheathed as a full-round action.

### Blades, Huge
Slot: Limbs; Points: 3 Weapon; Cost: 25; Weight: 5 lbs.
As Blades, but the weapon deals 2d8 damage (2d6 for a Small puppet, 3d6 for a Large one).

### Blades, Large
Slot: Limbs; Points: 2 Weapon; Cost: 20; Weight: 3 lbs.
As Blades, but the weapon deals 2d6 damage (1d8 Small, 2d8 Large).

### Blades, Hollow (Requires Venomous or Blades (any))
Slot: None; Points: 1 Weapon; Cost: 15; Weight: none.
Cannot be used with Blades, Hidden. Allows a Blade or Venomous component to hold one extra dose of poison.

### Bomb [Activated]
Slot: Any; Points: 1 Weapon; Cost: 15; Weight: 10 lbs.
Lets the puppet self-destruct, dealing 1d6 damage for each 2 Chakra it has (maximum 10d6), halved with a successful Reflex save. Blast radius 10 ft (Small), 20 ft (Medium) or 30 ft (Large or larger). When used, the puppet is immediately destroyed and each component has a 10% chance of becoming irreparably destroyed (roll for each separately).

### Caltrops Trap [Activated]
Slot: Any; Points: 1 weapon, 2 utility (head or limb) or 2 weapon, 1 utility (chest); Cost: 14; Weight: 2 lbs.
The puppet covers a 10 ft square with caltrops, as though throwing a grenade. Using it costs a charge; the component carries 1 charge as a limb/head component, or 2 as a chest component. A charge is replaced with a Repair check (DC 13, Cost DC 5, Time 1 minute).

### Cannon
Slot: Head or Limb; Points: 1 Weapon; Cost: 20; Weight: 5 lbs.
Holds five throwing weapons of Small or smaller size (including smoke bombs, grenades, etc.) hidden within its body, which can be thrown normally without being drawn. Replacing the projectiles requires a Repair check (DC 12) and takes one round per projectile.

### Chakra Accelerator
Slot: Chest; Points: 2 Utility; Cost: 24; Weight: 15 lbs.
The puppet's body circulates Chakra to strengthen it, granting +1 HP per 2 HD.

### Chakra Accelerator, Greater
Slot: Chest; Points: 3 Utility; Difficulty: B-Class; Cost: 28; Weight: 15 lbs.
As Chakra Accelerator, but more efficient, granting +1 HP per HD.

### Detachable Limbs
Slot: Limbs; Points: 2 Utility; Difficulty: C-Class; Cost: 15; Weight: 10 lbs.
The puppet's limbs can be detached and re-attached easily. It gains +4 to Escape Artist checks, and can free itself from being Entangled with an Escape Artist check (DC 20) as a full-round action.

### Dragon's Breath [Activated]
Slot: Head; Points: 1 Weapon, 1 Utility; Difficulty: C-Class; Cost: 18; Weight: 2 lbs.
The puppet can spend 2 Chakra to release a 20 ft cone of fire, dealing 3d8 fire damage (Reflex halves). Used once before reloading (Repair DC 18, Cost DC 4).

### Dragon's Breath, Greater [Activated]
Slot: Head; Points: 2 Weapon, 1 Utility; Difficulty: B-Class; Cost: 18; Weight: 4 lbs.
As Dragon's Breath, but costs 4 Chakra, creates a 40 ft cone dealing 6d8 fire damage, and reloads with a Repair check (DC 23, Cost DC 7).

### Elemental Cannon
Slot: Limb; Points: 2 Weapon; Cost: 19; Weight: 2 lbs.
Cannot be installed alongside a Blades component. 'Charged' by an Elemental Scroll (bought or made with Craft (Calligraphy)); the scroll's element determines the damage type. Loading a scroll is an attack action requiring a Repair check (DC 13). The puppet makes ranged weapon attacks firing one elemental 'bullet' per attack: range increment 20 feet (max 10 increments), 2d6 energy damage, attack roll modified by the puppeteer's Intelligence modifier. Each bullet consumes one charge from the scroll; each round used costs the puppet 1 Chakra.

### Elemental Cannon, Superior
Slot: Limb; Points: 2 Weapon; Difficulty: C-Class; Cost: 22; Weight: 2 lbs.
As Elemental Cannon, but each 'bullet' deals 1d6 splash damage (same element) to all creatures adjacent to the target(s).

### Elemental Cannon Holster (Requires Elemental Cannon (Any))
Slot: None; Points: 1 Utility; Cost: 14; Weight: 3 lbs.
Installed with an Elemental Cannon, this can carry up to four Elemental Scrolls, allowing the puppet's Elemental Cannon to be reloaded as a move-equivalent action.

### Enhanced Machinery
Slot: Chest or Limb; Points: 1 Weapon, 1 Utility; Difficulty: C-Class; Cost: 22; Weight: 10 lbs.
A puppet carrying Armor Plating, Medium or Heavy cannot receive this component. The puppet uses its own Chakra to amplify its machinery, gaining Speed Rank 1 and Strength Rank 1.

### Enhanced Machinery Mk II
Slot: Chest or Limb; Points: 2 Weapon, 1 Utility; Difficulty: B-Class; Cost: 27; Weight: 10 lbs.
As Enhanced Machinery, but the puppet gains Speed and Strength Rank 2.

### Enhanced Machinery Mk III
Slot: Chest or Limb; Points: 3 Weapon, 2 Utility; Difficulty: A-Class; Cost: 32; Weight: 10 lbs.
As Enhanced Machinery, but the puppet gains Speed and Strength Rank 3.

### Extendable Limbs
Slot: Limbs; Points: 3 Utility; Cost: 16; Weight: 4 lbs.
Attacks with the puppet's limb (a Slam attack or a weapon attack with an installed weapon) gain a 5 ft increase to their reach. This does not increase the puppet's threatened area.

### Force Shield
Slot: Limb; Points: 1 Utility; Difficulty: C-Class; Cost: 13; Weight: 1 lbs.
The puppet creates a shield of Chakra from one limb (which cannot then hold a weapon or object), granting a +2 shield bonus to Defense. Costs 2 Chakra and lasts 1 minute.

### Force Shield, Greater
Slot: Limb; Points: 3 Utility; Difficulty: A-Class; Cost: 20; Weight: 2 lbs.
As Force Shield, but the Chakra cost is 4, the shield bonus improves to +4, and it incurs a −2 armor check penalty.

### Force Shield, Minor
Slot: Limb; Points: 1 Utility; Cost: 10; Weight: 1 lbs.
As Force Shield, but the shield grants a +1 shield bonus to Defense.

### Force Shield, Superior
Slot: Limb; Points: 2 Utility; Difficulty: B-Class; Cost: 16; Weight: 1 lbs.
As Force Shield, but the Chakra cost is 3, the shield bonus improves to +3, and it incurs a −1 armor check penalty.

### Hollow Body
Slot: Chest; Points: 2 Utility; Cost: 16; Weight: reduced by 25%.
The puppet's body is hollow, allowing it to store one item a size larger than it, 2 items the same size, 4 items one size smaller, etc. The puppet's hardness is reduced by 1.

### Improved Servos
Slot: Limb; Points: 2 Utility; Cost: 24; Weight: 10 lbs.
The puppet gains a +2 equipment bonus to Strength, and its natural attacks deal damage as though it were one size category larger.

### Inconspicuous (Living Puppets only)
Slot: None; Points: 1 Utility; Cost: 16; Weight: 5 lbs.
The puppet is made to look more similar to its living form. The Spot check DC to identify it as a puppet increases by 10. The DC for Repair checks to repair it, install components or reload them also increases by 5.

### Iron Maiden [Activated] (Requires Torso Tank)
Slot: None; Points: 2 Weapon; Cost: 20; Weight: 10 lbs.
The torso tank contains an iron maiden trap, which can be activated or deactivated as a move action. Each round a creature spends inside the torso tank while the iron maiden is active, it suffers piercing damage equal to the puppet's Slam attack (using half its Strength modifier).

### Light Frame (Small or Medium size only)
Slot: Chest; Points: 1 Utility; Cost: 12; Weight: reduced by 10 lbs (minimum half).
The puppet gains a +2 bonus to Dexterity, but suffers a −2 penalty to Strength.

### Locking Gauntlets
Slot: Limbs; Points: 1 Utility; Cost: 14; Weight: 1 lbs.
The puppet can wield weapons as though it had the same proficiencies as the puppeteer, and gains a +4 bonus to checks to resist being disarmed.

### Gore
Slot: Head; Points: 1 Weapon; Difficulty: C-Class; Cost: 14; Weight: 2 lbs.
The puppet gains a gore attack as a secondary natural attack. A puppet can only use a single secondary natural attack in a Full Attack.

### Kunai Shower [Activated]
Slot: Any; Points: 1 Weapon; Cost: 16; Weight: 2 lbs.
The puppet releases a flurry of kunai in a 15 ft cone, dealing 2d4+2 piercing damage (Reflex halves). Once used, must be reloaded (Repair DC 14, Cost DC 3, 5 rounds).

### Kunai Shower Mk II [Activated]
Slot: Any; Points: 2 Weapon; Difficulty: C-Class; Cost: 18; Weight: 4 lbs.
As Kunai Shower, but the damage is 3d4+3. The DC to reload increases to 16 (Cost DC 5).

### Kunai Shower Mk III [Activated]
Slot: Any; Points: 2 Weapon; Difficulty: B-Class; Cost: 20; Weight: 6 lbs.
As Kunai Shower, but the damage is 5d4+5. The DC to reload increases to 18 (Cost DC 6).

### Net Trap [Activated]
Slot: Chest; Points: 2 Utility; Cost: 18; Weight: 4 lbs.
The puppet fires a large net that entangles a Medium-size or smaller target within 20 ft on a successful ranged touch attack. The target can try to escape once per round as a move action that provokes attacks of opportunity by making an Escape Artist check (DC 14 + puppeteer's Intelligence modifier). Must be reloaded after use (Repair DC 14, Cost DC 5, 5 minutes).

### Porcupine Trap [Activated]
Slot: Limbs and Chest; Points: 2 Utility; Difficulty: C-Class; Cost: 20; Weight: 5 lbs.
The puppet fires a torrent of needles from its body, dealing 3d6+3 piercing damage in a 20 ft radius burst (Reflex halves). The needles can be poisoned by applying 5 doses when loading the component, though the poison's save DC decreases by 2 if used this way. Must be reloaded after use (Repair DC 15, Cost DC 8, excluding any poison, 1 minute).

### Sea of Open Hands [Activated]
Slot: Limb; Points: 1 Weapon, 2 Utility; Difficulty: B-Class; Cost: 23; Weight: 2 lbs (50 lbs while active).
Releases a large quantity of puppet limbs, treated as a Medium-sized creature attached to the puppet, occupying an adjacent square. While active, the puppet cannot make regular attacks, but can use the Sea of Open Hands to grapple a creature up to 15 ft away. It uses the puppet's grapple bonus but counts as three sizes larger (applying the proper bonuses), and deals damage as though the puppet were three sizes larger. Dismissing this component is a free action.

### Shields
Slot: Limbs; Points: 2 Utility; Cost: 13 + 1 per limb; Weight: 1 lb per limb.
Special plating is applied on every limb of the puppet, granting a +2 shield bonus to Defense.

### Tail Whip
Slot: Limb; Points: 1 Weapon; Difficulty: C-Class; Cost: 12; Weight: 10 lbs.
The puppet gains a tail whip secondary natural attack, dealing damage as a slam attack of its size. A puppet can only use a single secondary natural attack during a Full Attack.

### Torso Tank
Slot: Chest; Points: 2 Utility; Cost: 25; Weight: 10 lbs.
The puppet gains the Swallow Whole ability (see D20 Modern p228), except the creature is trapped inside the puppet's torso rather than swallowed by its mouth. Alternatively, the puppeteer can sit inside the Torso Tank while controlling it, gaining total cover. A puppet counts as one size larger when determining what size creature it can 'swallow'. A creature inside can be released as a move-equivalent action, forced open with a Strength check (DC 25), or the tank temporarily disabled by dealing 2 damage for each HD the puppet has. Creatures inside can only wield light weapons.

### Venomous (Animal Puppet only)
Slot: None; Points: 1 Weapon; Cost: 14; Weight: 1 lbs.
Can only be applied to a Living puppet whose original form had a natural attack that delivered poison. The puppet can now hold a dose of poison, applied by the same attack.

### Vise Grip
Slot: Limb; Points: 2 Weapon; Cost: 18; Weight: 2 lbs.
When dealing damage to a creature it is grappling or pinning, the puppet's slam attack deals damage as though it were one size larger.

### Wire
Slot: Limb; Points: 1 Weapon; Cost: 10; Weight: 10 lbs.
The puppet has garrotte wires hidden in its hand, which can be drawn as a free action even during a grapple. Storing them requires a full-round action.
