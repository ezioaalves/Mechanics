# Necklace of the Urban Survivor [Sealed Item]

This necklace is nothing more than a worn coin dangling on a leather cord.

**Sealed Item (necklace)**
*   Minor (Lv 5): +1 DC 16
*   Superior (Lv 10): +2 DC 27

**Enhancement:** Intimidate checks (competence), saving throws against fear (resistance)
**Property:** If you have the Prey Spotting class feature, you gain a competence bonus to those checks equal to the necklace's enhancement bonus.
