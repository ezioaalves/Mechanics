# Urn of the Hungry Ghosts [Sealed Item]

This small funeral urn holds the ashes of a legendary warrior. It cannot be opened.

**Sealed Item (totem, uncraftable)**
*   Greater (Lv 12): DC 35

**Power (1/day, free action):** 8 chakra. Use when summoning a creature with *Kuchiyose no Jutsu* or a variant. The creature gains a +3 enhancement bonus to speed and strength ranks for 1 minute after it is summoned.
**Special:** This item cannot be crafted. As such, they are very rare.
