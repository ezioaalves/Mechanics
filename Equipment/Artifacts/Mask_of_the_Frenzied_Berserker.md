# Mask of the Frenzied Berserker [Sealed Item]

This featureless white mask takes the shape of the wearer's face when put on, twisted permanently in a rictus of rage.

**Sealed Item (mask)**
*   Superior (Lv 9): DC 26
*   Greater (Lv 18): DC 41

**Power (1/day, free action):** Use when scoring a critical hit against a creature. You make an Intimidate check against all enemies within 30 feet, and gain a +4 circumstance bonus to that check when using the rage or frenzy class feature (or +6 when using both at once).
*   *Level 18:* As above, but 3/day.
**Property:** When using the Frenzied Rampage class feature, the wearer treats his class level as being 1 higher.
*   *Level 18:* As above, but the wearer treats his level as being 2 higher.
