# Shadowreach Blade

Shadowreach Blade [Sealed Item, Weapon]
Sealed Item (any bladed weapon)
Minor (Lv 5)
+0 DC 16
Greater (Lv 13)
+2 DC 36
Superior (Lv 9)
+1 DC 26
Enhancement: Attack and damage rolls
Property: The weapon's enhancement bonus to attack and damage rolls increases by 1 in
total darkness.
Power (3/encounter, free action): Deliver an attack through your shadow with a range
of 20 feet. Attack is resolved as a weapon attack.
Level 9: As above, but 30 feet and the attack deals an extra 1d6 damage.
Level 13: As above, but 50 feet and the attack deals an extra 2d6 damage.
