# Armor of the Stalwart Defender [Sealed Item]

This black-and-gold o-yoroi is imposing and affords great protection, even against friendly fire.

**Sealed Item (o-yoroi)**
*   Superior (Lv 11): +2 DC 28
*   Greater (Lv 15): +3 DC 38

**Enhancement:** Armor
**Power (At-Will, instant action):** Use when beginning your turn in the middle of a swarm. You gain the armor's enhancement bonus as a bonus to saves against the swarm's distraction ability.
**Property:** You gain a +1 luck bonus to saving throws against area effects that originate from an ally. If you have the Bottleneck class feature, the bonus is equal to the armor's enhancement bonus instead.
