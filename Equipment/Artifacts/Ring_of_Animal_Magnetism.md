# Ring of Animal Magnetism

Ring of Animal Magnetism [Sealed Item]
Sealed Item (ring)
Greater (Lv 16)
+2 DC 39
Legendary (Lv 26)
+6 DC 59
Epic (Lv 21)
+4 DC 46
Enhancement: Charisma score
Power (1/day, free action): Use when making a Charisma check or Charisma-based skill
check to change the attitude of another creature. On a successful check, you improve the
attitude of the creature one additional step in your favor, if possible.
Level 21: As above, but 2/day.
Level 26: As above, but 2/day and still improve the creature's attitude one step on your
favor on a check that fails by 5 or less.
