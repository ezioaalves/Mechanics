# Moccasins of the Steppe Walker [Sealed Item]

These hide mocassins bear motifs usually worn by steppe tribes.

**Sealed Item (feet)**
*   Superior (Lv 7): DC 24

**Power (2/day, 1 swift action):** Use after killing an enemy or rendering an enemy unconscious. Gain 1d10 temporary hit points for 1 hour.
**Property:** When using the Rage class feature, your movement speed increases by 5 feet. If you have the Greater Rage class feature, it increases by 10 feet instead.
