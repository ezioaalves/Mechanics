# Jewel of Seven Seas [Armor, Sealed Item]

This ordinary sapphire mounted on a unremarkable silver necklace has the power to contain nature energy.
**Sealed Item (neck, uncraftable)**
*   Greater (Lv 19): +2 DC 42
**Enhancement:** Chakra Control checks to convert nature energy
**Power (1/day, attack action):** The necklace begins gathering nature energy for 5 minutes. The energy decays after 1 hour.
The necklace gathers at a rate of 3 points per minute in rural or natural areas, or 2 points per minute in a small town or village. This power cannot be used in cities, metropolis or polluted areas.
**Power (At-Will, free action):** Absorb some or all of the nature energy contained by the necklace.
**Special:** This object cannot be crafted.
