# Mask of Terror

Mask of Terror [Sealed Item]
This white, four-eyed demon mask grins eerily at you.
Sealed Item (sample)
Epic (Lv 21)
+5 DC 46
Enhancement: Intmidate checks (competence) and Will saves vs. fear (resistance)
Property: The wearer gains frightful presence (DC 10+one-half wearer's level+Cha
modifier), which lasts for 2d6 rounds and activates whenever he takes a threatening
action.
