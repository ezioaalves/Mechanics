# Summoning Scrambler [Sealed Item]

This small length of prayer beads has the ability to disrupt the summoning rituals of others.

**Sealed Item**
*   Superior (Lv 11): DC 28

**Power (1/day, attack action):** 6 chakra. As *Fuinjutsu: Kaiyaku Fuuin*, but affects any creature within 30 feet and only prevents the target from summoning creatures from a blood pact. Lasts for 1 hour (Will DC 21 negates).
