# Deadshot Goggles [Sealed Item]

These simple leather goggles have faded crosshairs over the lenses, and can be adjusted with several dials and switches.

**Sealed Item (goggles)**
*   Superior (Lv 11): +1 DC 28
*   Greater (Lv 20): +2 DC 43

**Enhancement:** Attack rolls with bows, crossbows and firearms (circumstance)
**Power (1/day, swift action):** Use when making an attack with the Deadly Shot class feature. The attack suffers no penalty from range increments and deal additional damage equal to your Dexterity modifier, not multiplied on a critical hit.
*   *Level 20:* As above, 2/day.
**Property:** The goggles can be adjusted as a full-round action to function as binoculars.
