# Ghost Spear

Ghost Spear [Sealed Item, Weapon]
This metal quarterstaff has a hollow half-sphere weighing down each ends.
Sealed Item (quarterstaff)

Greater (Lv 17)
+3 DC 40
Enhancement: Attack and damage rolls
Power (At-Will, free action): Activate the ghost spear after drawing it. The activated
spear will create a larger, much wider blue-colored blade of light. The ghost spear
produces bright light in a 60 foot radius, but this glow can be dulled until it is no stronger
than a candle as a move action.
The activated ghost spear is wielded as a quarterstaff, but deals damage as a naginata and
can be wielded with two hands. Abilities that improve the proficiency of either the
quarterstaff or the naginata also apply to the ghost spear, but not both. The ghost spear
deals half slashing and half force damage on a successful hit and can be sundered
(hardness 10, 30 hit points), but the wielder can reform it as a free action on his next turn.
Special: This item cannot be crafted.
