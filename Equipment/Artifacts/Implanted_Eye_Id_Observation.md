# Implanted Eye: Id Observation

Implanted Eye: Id Observation [Sealed Item]
This implanted eye has a white sclera and a orange iris, but looks clearly artificial.
Sealed Item (implanted eye)
Superior (Lv 9)
DC 26
Greater (Lv 13)
DC 36
Power (3/day, attack action): You gain the ability to see moods as colors surrounding a
person and detect basic emotions, such as anger, frustration, contentment, relief or
happiness. Lasts 1 minute.

Power (1/day, full-round action): Level 12: As above, but functions on a single creature
within 30 feet for as long as you concentrate or 5 rounds, whichever is shorter. You can
read the target's surface as rapidfire images, as though with the Detect Emotion ability
(Will DC 18 negates).
