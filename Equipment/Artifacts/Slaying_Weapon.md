# Slaying Weapon [Sealed Item, Weapon]

This simple weapon doesn't appear out of the ordinary but is perfectly balanced.
**Sealed Item (any weapon)**
*   Greater (Lv 15): +3 DC 38
**Enhancement:** Attack and damage rolls
**Property:** The weapon's enhancement bonus increases by +2 when making an attack with the executioner's mark ability of the keirigan.
**Power (2/day, attack action):** Use when making an attack with the executioner's mark ability. Deal an extra 2d6 points of damage.
