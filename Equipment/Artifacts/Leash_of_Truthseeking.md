# Leash of Truthseeking [Sealed Item]

This intricate collar is connected to a segmented metal rope that ends with a curiously shaped domed handle.

**Sealed Item**
*   Superior (Lv 8): -5 DC 25
*   Greater (Lv 12): -10 DC 35

**Enhancement:** Bluff checks against the character holding the handle
**Property:** This item can only be attached to a helpless creature and can be physically removed as an attack action by that creature or any adjacent creature. 
**Property:** If you have the Interrogator's frightful presence, interrogation, master interrogator or thought reform ability, you gain a +2 bonus to check when using those abilities.
*   *Level 12:* As above, but +4 to checks and and the saving throw DC of those abilities increases by 1.
