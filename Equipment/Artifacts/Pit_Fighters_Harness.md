# Pit Fighter's Harness [Armor, Sealed Item]

This sturdy leather harness offers deceptivelty good protection and makes enables the wearer to slip out of a grapple at a moment's notice.

**Sealed Item (reinforced suit)**
*   Superior (Lv 11): +2 DC 28
*   Greater (Lv 16): +3 DC 39

**Enhancement:** Defense (armor)
**Power (1/day, instant action):** Use when caught in a grapple or a pin. You may immediately attempt to break the grapple, or reverse a pin back into a grapple.
**Property:** If you have the Exceptional Wrestler class feature, you gain double the bonus when defending against Grapple and Pin attempts.
