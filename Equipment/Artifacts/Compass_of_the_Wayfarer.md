# Compass of the Wayfarer

Compass of the Wayfarer [Sealed Item]
This simple compass always points in the same direction—and it isn't magnetic north.
Sealed Item (compass)
Minor (Lv 6)
Property: This compass always points in the direction of the place it was attuned to, and
grants a +5 equipment bonus to checks made to find the way or plot a trip to that
destination.
Special: This compass must be attuned to a specific location before it can be used, a
ritual performed by the crafter that takes 5 minutes.
