# Samehada, the Monster Blade [Major Relic]

This bandage-wrapped greatsword hides something monstrous beneath.

**Major Relic (greatsword, uncraftable)**
*   Damage: +4 (Legendary DC 67)
**Property:** Functions as a *greater soulbound weapon*, but can change attunement at will.
**Property:** Has a will of its own; can transform into its Monster Blade form and slither away.
**Property:** Absorbs and stores half the chakra from disrupted techniques. Stores up to 5 chakra per tail of bijuu power.
**Power (At-Will, free):** 1d6 chakra damage vs techniques (DC 25+rank to disrupt).
**Power (At-Will, full-round):** Draw stored chakra to gain temporary HP/Chakra for 1 hour (max 2 points per level).
