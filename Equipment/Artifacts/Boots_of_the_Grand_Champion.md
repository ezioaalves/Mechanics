# Boots of the Grand Champion [Sealed Item]

These black leather boots are comfortable to wear and hardly make a sound.

**Sealed Item (boots)**
*   Minor (Lv 5): +1 DC 16
*   Superior (Lv 9): +2 DC 26

**Enhancement:** Balance, Move Silently and Tumble checks (competence)
**Power (2/day, free action):** Use this power when moving before and after making a melee attack. You gain the boots' enhancement bonus as a circumstance bonus to the attack roll. If you have the Improved Spring Attack class feature, the circumstance bonus also applies to weapon damage rolls.
