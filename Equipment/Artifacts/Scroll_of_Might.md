# Scroll of Might [Sealed Item]

This small scroll has the power to empower a summoned creature.

**Sealed Item (totem)**
*   Superior (Lv 11): DC 23

**Power (1 charge, free action):** Use this scroll when summoning a creature. The summoned creature is empowered as though you had used the Empower Summoning. The scroll reverts to a blank scroll after used.
