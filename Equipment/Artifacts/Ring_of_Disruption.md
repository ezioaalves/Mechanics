# Ring of Disruption [Armor, Sealed Item]

This simple iron band has no decorations save for a small carving of a triangle.

**Sealed Item (ring)**
*   Superior (Lv 10): DC 25

**Power (1/encounter, swift action):** Use when performing a Kyushojutsu technique. If the technique hits, the target takes 1d6 tenketsu damage and 2 chakra damage.
