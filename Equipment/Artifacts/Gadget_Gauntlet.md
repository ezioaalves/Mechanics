# Gadget Gauntlet

Gadget Gauntlet [Sealed Item]
Sealed Item (gauntlet)
Superior (Lv 10)
DC 27
Greater (Lv 15)
DC 38
Property: This gauntlet can be used to attack with as a held weapon that does 1d4 points
of bludgeoning damage and threatens a critical hit on a natural roll of 20 to deal double
damage. The hand wearing the gadget gauntlet cannot be used to form hand seals or
perform fine manipulations.
Property: This gauntlet can be animated as a puppet with up to 1 hit dice per item level.
Each gauntlet has 1 limb slot, 3 weapon points and 1 utility point.
Level 15: As above, but 4 weapon points and 2 utility points.
The gauntlet is stationary and possesses no quality a puppet might other than the ability to
use a component.
