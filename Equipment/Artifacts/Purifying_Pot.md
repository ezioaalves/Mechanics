# Purifying Pot [Sealed Item]

This large sealing jar created by the God of Shinobi and strongest bearer of the Rinnegan, the Sage of the Six Paths, simply bears a stylized character for 'lightning'.
**Sealed Item (uncraftable)**
*   Legendary (Lv 35): DC 67
**Property:** This sealing jar can contain a single creature with 1,000 chakra or less.
**Power (1 charge, special):** Extract the sealed creature with a Fuinjutsu technique either to free it or seal it into another creature.
The purifying pot is recharged when another creature is sealed inside.
**Power (1/week, 1 round):** 100 chakra. As Fuinjutsu: Seishin Fuuin Shiki, but the range is 30 feet and requires no particular physical condition. Will save negates, DC 12 + one-half character's level + Int modifier.
When using this ability, you can make a Chakra Control check to reduce the cost to 50 (DC 35).
**Special:** This item cannot be crafted and cannot be destroyed.
