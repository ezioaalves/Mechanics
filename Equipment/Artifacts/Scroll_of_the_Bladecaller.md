# Scroll of the Bladecaller

Scroll of the Bladecaller [Sealed Item]
Sealed Item (large scroll)
Superior (Lv 8)
DC 25
Greater (Lv 15)
DC 38
Epic (Lv 20)
DC 45
Property: This scroll can be used as material component for Yaibaki no Kuchiyose, but is
not expended with use. Any weapon summoned when using this scroll as a component
gains a +1 enhancement bonus to attack and damage rolls and can overcome chakra
damage reduction.
Level 15: As above, but +2.
Level 20: As above, but +3.
Power (1/day—Summoning, attack action): Use this power to summon a weapon into
your hand, rolled randomly from the War Hero's battle arsenal ability, with a bonus to
attack and damage rolls depending on the strength of the scroll. The weapon lasts 1
minute.
Level 15: As above, 2/day and the weapon lasts 5 minutes.

Level 20: As above, 3/day and the weapon lasts 10 minutes.
