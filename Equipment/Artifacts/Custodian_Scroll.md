# Custodian Scroll [Sealed Item]

This scroll contains seal that will enable anyone who possesses it to summon the Seven Swords of the Mist.
**Sealed Item (uncraftable)**
*   Greater (Lv 14): DC 37
**Property:** The custodian scroll has seven Fuinjutsu: Shuntori Fuuin seals placed on it attuned to each of the seven swords of the mist. The seals are permanent but still lose power after being used, and can only be recharged using the scroll's power.
Both components of Kabutowari and Kiba are summoned simultaneously.
**Power (1/week, full-round action):** 20 chakra. Recharge all used shuntori fuuin seals.
**Special:** This item cannot be crafted.
