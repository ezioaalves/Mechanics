# Kongou Nyoi [Major Relic]

This masterwork quarterstaff is in fact the Monkey King Enma's transformed form.

**Major Relic (quarterstaff)**
*   Attack/Damage: +2 (masterwork)
**Power:** Once per round, Enma may lash out and extend his arm from the staff to attack twice (Atk +17, 1d4+3 slashing).
**Property:** Staff may freely expand to grant 10-foot reach. Can collapse to Tiny size.
**Property:** Enma is aware of his surroundings and can speak/hear.
