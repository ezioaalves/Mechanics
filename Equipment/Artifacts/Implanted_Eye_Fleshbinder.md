# Implanted Eye: Fleshbinder

Implanted Eye: Fleshbinder [Sealed Item]
This implanted eye has a black sclera and red iris with three small pupils arranged in a
triangle formation.
Sealed Item (implanted eye)
Greater (Lv 12)
DC 35
