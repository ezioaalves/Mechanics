Sealed Item (headband)
Greater (Lv 16)
+2 DC 39
Legendary (Lv 26)
+6 DC 59
Epic (Lv 21)
+4 DC 46
Enhancement: Intelligence score
Power (1/day, free action): Use when making a Knowledge check. You are treated as
though having rolled 15 on a d20, and as though you were trained in the skill even if you
are not.
Level 21: As above, but 2/day.
