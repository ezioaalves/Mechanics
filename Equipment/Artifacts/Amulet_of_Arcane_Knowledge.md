# Amulet of Arcane Knowledge [Sealed Item]

This amulet consists of a ruby trapped in an intricate brass cage, and glows softly when worn.

**Sealed Item (necklace)**
*   Superior (Lv 11): +1 DC 28
*   Greater (Lv 15): +1 DC 38

**Enhancement:** Knowledge and Research checks, checks to identify techniques
**Property:** *Level 12.* You gain a single Grand Scholar discovery for as long as you wear this item. The discovery gained is selected when the amulet is created, and must be chosen from this list: secrets of true stamina, secrets of celerity, secrets of inner strength, offensive knack, defensive trick.
If you already have the discovery, you gain any other discovery you qualify for.
