# Diluted Hero Water

Diluted Hero Water [Sealed Item]
This small crystal phial contains the fabled Hero Water diluted with other shinobi drugs.
Sealed Item
Superior (Lv 8)
DC 25
Greater (Lv 15)
DC 33
Power (1 charge, attack action): Consume the 1/25th dose of hero water. You gain 5
temporary hit points and chakra per round for 3 rounds.
While the effect is active, you ignore fatigue, exhaustion and nausea, and gain a +2 bonus
to Speed and Strength ranks. You will be fatigued until the end of the encounter or 5
minutes after the effects fade, whichever is longer.
Level 15: As above, except the dose is 1/10th. You gain 10 temporary hit points and
chakra per round, and a +3 bonus to Speed and Strength ranks instead.
