# Poisoner's Ring [Sealed Item]

This ornate silver ring is mounted with a circle-cut emerald with an almost invisible hole cut in its center.

**Sealed Item (ring)**
*   Superior (Lv 10): DC 27

**Power (At-Will, 1 minute):** Use this power to store one dose of contact or injury poison inside the ring.
**Power (1/day, see text action):** Use this power when making a touch attack against a creature that has no damage reduction and is wearing light armor or no armor. You deliver the dose of stored poison as part of the touch attack.
If you have the Master Poisoner class feature, this ring allows you to apply the dose of poison to yourself and use the ability as a swift action.
