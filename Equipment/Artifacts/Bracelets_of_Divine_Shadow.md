# Bracelets of Divine Shadow [Sealed Item]

These intricate bracelets, one white and one black, are engraved with zen koans and the symbol of onmyouji.

**Sealed Item (bracers)**
*   Superior (Lv 8): DC 25
*   Greater (Lv 16): DC 39

**Power (1/day):** Use this ability to Suppress Chakra.
*   *Level 16:* As above, but 2/day.
**Power (2/day):** When using the Erase Presence class feature, use this ability when spending chakra to avoid disrupting the ability.
*   *Level 16:* As above, 3/day.
