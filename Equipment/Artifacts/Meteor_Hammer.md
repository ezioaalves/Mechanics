# Meteor Hammer

Meteor Hammer [Sealed Item, Weapon]
This weighted chain has two hollow, heavy balls at each ends.
Sealed Item (dark iron chain)
Epic (Lv 22)
+3 DC 54
Enhancement: Attack and damage rolls
Property: This chain can be used as a double weapon, but the wearer does not gain

reach. Each head of the weapon gain the benefits of the enhancement bonus to damage
rolls and any abilities of the weapon.
Power (At-Will—Fire, attack action): 6 chakra. The weapon deals +1d6 points of fire
damage on a successful hit. Lasts 1 minute.
Power (5/day—Fire, swift action): Use when making an attack to blast the target
regardless of the result of the attack roll. The target takes 6d6 points of fire damage,
halved on a successful Reflex save (DC 8 + wielder's base attack bonus).
Using this ability against an adjacent target has a 50% chance of affecting the wielder as
well.
