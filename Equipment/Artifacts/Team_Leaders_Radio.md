# Team Leader's Radio [Sealed Item]

This black radio looks plain and is easily concealed by simply wearing a dark outfit.

**Sealed Item**
*   Superior (Lv 8): DC 25

**Power (1/day, 1 attack action):** You can use this ability to grant up to 4 allies within 60 feet who can both see you and hear you on a radio the benefits of any tactic you know for 1 minute.
**Property:** If you have the Team Leader class feature, you can issue commands to your allies and provide your Team Leader bonus to all allies within 60 feet for as long as they can see you.
