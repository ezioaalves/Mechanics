# Shades of Overkill [Sealed Item]

These sunglasses completely obscure the eyes of the wearer.

**Sealed Item (glasses)**
*   Superior (Lv 11): +2 DC 28

**Enhancement:** Saves against gaze attacks.
**Properties:** Any technique seen performed through the glasses has an identifiable glow when modified by a meta-chakra feat.
**Power (1/day, free action):** If you have the Null Entity ability, whenever you incur a wild surge, you can use this ability and gain 11 on the 1d20 result (next enemy slain explodes violently) instead of rolling.
