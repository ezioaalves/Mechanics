# Ring of the Specialist [Sealed Item]

This gold ring is plain except for a lengthy inscription inside the band.

**Sealed Item (ring)**
*   Minor (Lv 5): +1 DC 16

**Enhancement:** Attack rolls and skill checks made with explosives (competence)
**Property:** When you use the Specialist's Cunning class feature, you gain the benefits of the Weapon Focus feat for that weapon.
