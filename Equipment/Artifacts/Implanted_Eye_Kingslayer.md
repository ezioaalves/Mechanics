# Implanted Eye: Kingslayer

Implanted Eye: Kingslayer [Sealed Item]
This implanted eye has black sclera and a slitted, vertical purple pupil.
Sealed Item (implanted eye)
Greater (Lv 15)
DC 38
Power (1/day—Evil, attack action): 10 chakra. Target a creature with a gaze attack
within 30 feet and deal 8d6 points of negative energy damage. You can spend 1 chakra to
increase the damage by 1 die, maximum to 1d6 per level or 16d6 damage (Fortitude save
DC 22 halves). This does not provoke an attack of opportunity.
