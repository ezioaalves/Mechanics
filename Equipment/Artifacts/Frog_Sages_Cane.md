# Frog Sage's Cane [Sealed Item, Weapon]

This seemingly ordinary white oak club is in fact an ancient artifact passed down from generations of toad sages and mentors.
**Sealed Item (club, uncraftable)**
*   Epic (Lv 21): DC 45
**Power (At-Will, free action):** Use when dealing non-lethal damage with the cane. Heals 1 point of Charisma damage taken as drawback.
**Special:** This object cannot be crafted.
