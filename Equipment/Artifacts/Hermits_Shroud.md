# Hermit's Shroud [Sealed Item]

This simple white shroud covers the head and the face, leaving only the eyes exposed.

**Sealed Item (head)**
*   Superior (Lv 10): +2 DC 27

**Enhancement:** Wisdom checks with the intuitive weapon proficiency class feature
**Power (At-Will, 1 attack action):** As *Kagekomu no Jutsu*, lasts 1 hour (Will save DC 17).
**Property:** When you use the Intuitive Weapon Proficiency class feature, choose a single opponent within 30 feet. Treat your Wisdom check to use the ability as a feint attempt against that opponent. If successful, the feint persists until your first attack against any opponent or until 3 rounds of combat have passed, whichever is sooner.
