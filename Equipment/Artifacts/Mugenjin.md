# Mugenjin [Major Relic]

This mastercraft katana was wielded by the master assassin Shishio Makoto.

**Major Relic (katana)**
*   Damage: +5 (mastercraft)
**Property:** Wielder gains a +4 bonus to checks to perform *kaengiri, homura dama*, and *tsui no hiken - kaguzuchi* techniques.
**Property:** Counts as an epic weapon for bypassing damage reduction.
