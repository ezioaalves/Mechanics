# Weapon Enhancement Seals

## Ashen
*   **Minor (3):** Fills 5-ft square with ash (Haisekishou).

## Acid Burst
*   **Superior (8):** Half damage is acid.
*   **Greater (14):** +1d6 acid damage.

## Armor Piercing
*   **Superior (7):** +2 attack vs armor/natural armor. Ignore 5 hardness.

## Chakra Current
*   **Superior (9):** Imbue *Chakra Nagashi* without mastery. Duration +5m.

## Chilling
*   **Superior (7):** Half damage is cold.
*   **Greater (13):** 1d8 cold damage (3 rounds).

## Returning
*   **Minor (3):** Weapon returns to hand.

## Soulbound Weapon
*   **Minor (6):** -1 negative level if not bonded. Weapon hardness +5.
