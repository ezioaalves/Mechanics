# Enhancement Seal Mechanics

The art of scribing enhancement seals is a rare practice that grants mundane equipment powerful properties.

## Ranks
| Rank | Level |
| :--- | :---: |
| Minor | 3 - 6 |
| Superior | 7 - 11 |
| Greater | 12 - 19 |
| Epic | 20 - 25 |
| Legendary | 26+ |

## Slots
*   **Armor:** Can hold 1 enhancement seal.
*   **Weapon:** Can hold up to 2 enhancement seals (only one can be Greater+).
*   **Sealed Items:** Generally do not occupy a slot but require specific feats.

## Crafting
*   **XP Cost:** Seal Level x Purchase DC x 5. (Minor seals cost 1/4 normal).
*   **Time:** 1 day + 1/2 day per DC over 10. Add 1-4 days based on rank (Superior to Legendary).

## Item Slots
*   **Armor:** Torso.
*   **Back:** Cloaks, capes.
*   **Feet:** Boots, sandals.
*   **Head:** Glasses, goggles, headbands.
*   **Finger/Ear:** Rings, earrings.
*   **Neck:** Amulets, necklaces.
*   **Wrist:** Bracers, gauntlets.
