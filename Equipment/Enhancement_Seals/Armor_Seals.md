# Armor Enhancement Seals

## Chakra Armor
*   **Minor (6):** DR 1/chakra.
*   **Superior (11):** DR 3/chakra.
*   **Greater (16):** DR 5/chakra.

## Chakra Resistance
*   **Minor (6):** +13 chakra resistance (1 round, 3/day).
*   **Superior (11):** +16 resistance.
*   **Greater (16):** +19 resistance.
*   **Epic (21):** +24 resistance.
*   **Legendary (31):** +32 resistance.

## Changing
*   **Minor (5):** Armor shifts to mundane clothing.

## Featherweight
*   **Superior (7):** Armor treated as one category lighter. Weight halved.

## Flickering
*   **Superior (10):** Teleport up to 30 ft. (2/day). Greater (15): 60 ft.

## Void
*   **Superior (7):** Chakra signature -1 step. Greater (13): -2 steps. Epic (21): Suppressed.
