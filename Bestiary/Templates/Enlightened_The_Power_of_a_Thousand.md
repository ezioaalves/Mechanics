# Enlightened: The Power of a Thousand (Template)

*Source: NarutoD20 Redux v1.3, Chapter 14 – Going Beyond Level 20 (Epics), Epic Monsters (p448–450).*

Sometimes, you just need a foe or ally of such staggering power that even existing ways of improving their power don't quite do enough. For those times, there are **Power Ranks (PR)**. It is highly recommended that you never let players gain access to this mechanic.

A character with any amount of Power Ranks gains the following benefits:

- **Hit Dice:** Gain 1 HP per level or HD per PR. Gains a number of HD (your choice, up to 10).
- **Chakra Pool:** The creature's Chakra shines in a bright, vivid color and can be made visible at will. The creature gains a +15 divine bonus to Chakra Pool per PR. It recovers Chakra at twice the normal speed.
- **Speed:** All modes of movement have their movement speed doubled.
- **Defense:** Gain a +1 divine bonus to Defense per PR. At 2 or more PR, gain a +5 natural armor bonus to Defense.
- **Attack:** Gain a +1 divine bonus to attack rolls per PR, and overcome epic damage reduction.
- **Saves:** Gain a +1 divine bonus to saves per PR. At 6 or more PR, no longer automatically fails a save with a natural roll of 1.
- **Ability Scores:** Gain +2 divine bonus to a single ability score per PR (up to +20 per ability score). When building a humanoid NPC, have it start with one of the following ability score arrays: Uncommon (19, 17, 15, 12, 12, 12), Immortal (23, 20, 18, 15, 13, 12) or Legendary (28, 23, 18, 15, 15, 15).
- **Age:** The character still ages, but does not suffer from age penalties. Each PR increases maximum lifespan by 25% (if not immortal).
- **Immunities:** Gain immunity to ability damage, massive damage, stun, sleep, paralysis, death, compulsion and charm effects. At 5 or more PR, also gain immunity to ability drain, disease, energy drain and poison.
- **Damage Reduction:** A character with PR gains Damage Reduction, as shown below.
- **Elemental Resistances:** Gain 5 resistance against two types of energy, or 10 resistance against one type of energy. Each PR beyond the first allows it to increase one resistance by 5 or gain 5 resistance to a new energy type.
- **Special Abilities:** Gains the special abilities listed below.
- **Disciplines:** Gain two Disciplines, plus one per PR after the first. The creature must meet any prerequisites the Disciplines have.

## Special Abilities

- **Senses:** The character gains either Sense Chakra or Detect Emotions as an activated ability (as normal), and adds 50 ft to its Sense Chakra range per PR.
- **Block Senses:** As an attack action, the character becomes undetectable by Sense Chakra and Detect Emotions used by creatures without any PR. Lasts as long as the character concentrates, plus 1d4 rounds.
- **Enlightened Halo:** The character gains Fast Healing 1 per PR, active as long as they have at least 1 Chakra.
- **Power Aura:** Gain up to two of the following auras. These are Extraordinary and mind-affecting abilities. Creatures with fewer PR than the character coming within range (up to 20 ft per PR, adjustable as a free action) must make a Will save (DC 10 + 2 per PR + Cha modifier) or succumb to its effects. A successful save makes a creature immune to the character's aura's effects for 24 hours. The auras only work if the character is consciously projecting them, but require no action or concentration. At minimum range, it can only affect those touching the character. The character can consciously choose to only affect (or not affect) certain creatures within range. The character does not enjoy the benefits of their own aura.
  - **Daze Aura:** Affected creatures are dazed, staring at the character in fascination. They can attempt an additional save each round to break the effect, which does not make them immune to the aura.
  - **Fright Aura:** Affected creatures are shaken, and will also begin cowering if the character even glances or gestures at them unless they succeed at another Will save (which will not remove the shaken effect).
  - **Pressure Aura:** Affected creatures are utterly overwhelmed by the character's aura, as if it were weighing them down, causing them to take a −4 penalty to attack rolls, skill checks and Defense.
  - **Resolve Aura:** The character's allies receive a +1 morale bonus to attack rolls, saves and checks per PR. The character's enemies suffer a −1 morale penalty to attack rolls, saves and checks per 2 PR. Maximum +5 and −5.

## Disciplines

- **Action Surge** — *Prerequisite:* PR 2. When spending an action point, the character rolls one additional die.
- **Celerity** — *Prerequisite:* PR 1, Dex 19. The character gains Speed Rank 2, or its existing Speed Rank increases by 1. The character can use Shunpo and Mugen Shunpo as though 15 levels higher, if it has these techniques.
- **Detection** — *Prerequisite:* PR 1, Wis 19. The character's Sense Chakra range increases by 50 feet, and their Sense Blocking ability lasts for an additional 2 rounds.
- **Elemental Dominance** — *Prerequisite:* PR 3, Endurance, Chakra Pool 30, Elemental Spirit (chosen element). The character gains near-perfect control over the chosen element within a range of 20 ft per PR. It can learn techniques related to the chosen element in half the time, and ignores any material components such techniques may require.
  - *Earth (Doton):* Prevents the use of all techniques using earth, sand, mud or rock by opponents within range, unless they can ignore the material components. The character can create objects out of earth as long as its Chakra Pool is at least half full.
  - *Fire (Katon):* The character can prevent any creature within range being harmed by fire from any source, and can increase the size of flames within range or extinguish them at will as long as its Chakra pool is at least half full.
  - *Ice (Hyouton):* The character can walk on ice without penalties, and can strengthen ice enough to walk over a millimeter-thin sheet without it breaking, or weaken even a foot-thick wall of ice to crumble with a weak punch. Prevents the use of all techniques using ice or snow by opponents within range, unless they can ignore the material components.
  - *Lightning (Raiton):* The character can prevent any creature within range being harmed by lightning or electricity from any source.
  - *Water (Suiton):* The character can walk on water and water-like substances. Prevents the use of all techniques using water by opponents within range, unless they can ignore the material components. The character can create objects out of water as long as its Chakra Pool is at least half full.
  - *Wind (Fuuton):* The character can freely manipulate wind as though using a fully mastered Kuuryuusan no Jutsu with the range of this ability. Can prevent the use of Fuuton techniques and use strong winds (120 mph or stronger) to gain a fly speed equal to half its normal speed with clumsy maneuverability as long as its Chakra Pool is full.
  - Dominance is active only when the character is conscious and mentally capable (not dazed, stunned or nauseated). It can be used even when it's not the character's turn. Only one Dominance can be activated per round. *Special:* Can be taken multiple times, applying to a different element each time.
- **Elemental Spirit** — *Prerequisite:* PR 1, Int 19. Choose Doton, Fuuton, Hyouton, Katon, Raiton or Suiton. The character deals 1 additional point of damage per damage die when using techniques of the chosen type, and their save DC increases by 1. In addition, the character gains a +5 enhancement bonus to its skill threshold and a +10 bonus to Perform checks when performing techniques of the chosen type. *Special:* Can be taken multiple times, applying to a different element each time.
- **Endurance** — *Prerequisite:* PR 1, Con 19. The character gains a +10 bonus to Constitution checks, its HP increases by 20, and its PR counts as one higher for the purpose of calculating bonuses to Chakra Pool and Damage Reduction.
- **Enlightened Spirit** — *Prerequisite:* PR 2, Wis 21. The character counts as trained in all skills, and can take 10 on any skill check if the skill allows for it, even while stressed or distracted.
- **Illusionist** — *Prerequisite:* PR 1, Cha 19. The character gains a +2 bonus to the save DCs of their Genjutsu, may always take 10 when performing a genjutsu (even when threatened), and gains a +10 bonus to identify and analyse genjutsu.
- **Potence** — *Prerequisite:* PR 1, Str 19. The character gains Strength Rank 2, or its existing Strength Rank increases by 1. The character counts as one size larger for the purpose of grapple, bull rush and overrun actions, as well as for its carrying capacity.
- **Proficiency** — *Prerequisite:* PR 1. The character gains a +10 divine bonus to one skill it has ranks in. *Special:* Can be selected multiple times, applying to a different skill each time.
- **Summoner** — *Prerequisite:* PR 1. The character counts as 5 levels higher when determining the available Rank and maximum HD of creatures they want to summon using Kuchiyose no Jutsu.
- **Transportation** — *Prerequisite:* PR 1, Int 19. The character can perform Jigen Ugoku no Jutsu and Mugen Ugoku no Jutsu with half-seals rather than hand seals, and does not provoke attacks of opportunity when doing so. When performing either of those techniques, they count as ten levels higher.
