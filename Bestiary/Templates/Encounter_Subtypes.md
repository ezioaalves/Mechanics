# Encounter Subtypes

These templates/subtypes are applied to creatures to adjust their difficulty and role in an encounter.

## Minion Subtype
Designed to be destroyed easily in large waves.
*   **Hit Dice:** Gains only 1 HP per HD (plus Con modifier once). Toughness feat grants only 1 HP.
*   **Damage:** Deals half damage from attacks and techniques. (Explosives/external effects not modified).
*   **Challenge Rating:** Worth 1/4 normal XP. 4 minions = 1 normal creature for EL.

## Elite Subtype
More powerful and resilient than peers.
*   **Hit Dice:** Maximum HP per level (plus Con).
*   **Defense:** +1 luck bonus to Defense.
*   **Extra Actions:** +1 swift, move, or attack action per round (cannot be used for spells/techniques).
*   **Challenge Rating:** Worth +50% normal XP. EL treated as normal creature.

## Boss Subtype
Incredible power; dwarf surroundings by presence. Usually fights alone.
*   **Hit Dice:** Double maximum HP per level (plus Con every level). Toughness grants 5 HP.
*   **Defense:** +2 luck bonus to Defense.
*   **Saves:** +1 luck bonus to all saves.
*   **Extra Actions:** +1 swift, move, and attack action each turn (not full-round). Cannot use spells/SAs.
*   **Power Play:** Twice per encounter, ignore action restrictions OR trade extra actions for +1 attack at highest BAB and +1 at -5 (stacks with other effects).
*   **Immunities:** Immune to up to 4: ability damage, daze, death, paralysis, stunning.
*   **Challenge Rating:** Worth double XP. EL counts as 2 creatures.

## Solo Subtype
Force of nature, nigh godlike. Entire armies fall before them.
*   **Hit Dice:** Four times maximum HP per level (plus Con every level). Toughness grants 10 HP.
*   **Defense:** +3 luck bonus to Defense.
*   **Saves:** +2 luck bonus to all saves.
*   **Extra Actions:** Same as Boss, but more flexible.
*   **Multiple Turns:** Rolls initiative twice; use highest roll divided by half. Acts on both initiative counts.
*   **Immunities:** Immune to death effects, loss of turn, and up to 5: ability damage, drain, daze, fear, nausea, negative levels, paralysis, stunning.
*   **Challenge Rating:** Worth triple XP. EL counts as 3 creatures.
