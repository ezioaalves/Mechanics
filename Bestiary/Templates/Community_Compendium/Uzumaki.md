UZUMAKI (TEMPLATE)
Those with Uzumaki blood are said to be a distant
branch of the mighty Senju clan, and as such they have
been blessed with an incredible life force.
Ability Scores: +2 Constitution
Saves:
None
Skills: The character gains a +1 bonus to Chakra Control
checks to tap reserves.
Special Qualities: Overwhelming Chakra
Level Adjustment: +1
OVERWHELMING CHAKRA (SU)
An Uzumaki character is granted an immediate 5
points of bonus chakra to his chakra pool at 1st level and
2 additional points to his chakra pool and reserves each
level after.
