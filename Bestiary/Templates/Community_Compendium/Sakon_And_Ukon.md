SAKON AND UKON (TEMPLATE)
This is a very rare template that allows twin siblings to
break themselves into molecular level and share the
same body, or even invade other people's body.
Special Qualities: Dark twin, Souma no Kou, Kisei Kikai.
Level Adjustment: +4
DARK TWIN (EX)
This ability gives the character (now referred to as
main character) a perfect twin of himself as his ally. The
twin has the same levels, special abilities and ability
scores than the main character, but suffers a penalty of 2 on Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom and Charisma
scores. The two brothers share the same skill ranks (but
not the skill total modifier), feats (If the twin does not
fulfil a prerequisite for a feat possessed by the main
brother, he can't use said feat) and techniques. The twin
is considered to be 3 levels lower (Minimum 1) to
determine the effect of certain special abilities, CR, and
the maximum rank of technique he can use.
The brother has his own personality and may or may
not share the allegiances or objectives of the main
character, at the GM's discretion. Also, the two brothers
can use the Detect Emotions ability on each other at will.
If one of the twins perishes, the remaining brother must
succeed at a Will Saving throw (DC 25) to avoid 1d4
points of permanent wisdom damage, due to the trauma
of losing someone bonded to himself.
SOUMA NO KOU (EX)
This ability allows the twins to share the same body.
As a move equivalent action, the twin may enter the
body of the main character and remain in there for as
long as he wishes, along with clothes and equipment’s.
When sharing the same body, the two brothers recover
from hit point damage, ability damage and chakra loss
twice as fast as they normally would. In addition, when
the main character makes a full attack action, the twin
can also sprout his arms and legs to make another full
attack action himself (unarmed or with any held
weapon). If the main character is reduced to 0 or less hit
points with the twin inside his body, the twin must
succeed at a Fortitude Saving Throw (DC 20). Success
means that the twin is expelled without further damage,
while failure means that the twin is reduced to 0 hit
points and disabled. Any ill effect that requires a
Fortitude Saving Throw that affects the main character
while the twin is still inside his body also affects the
twin. When merged with his twin, the main character
receives a bonus of +2 to all saving throws, attack rolls
and skill checks.
KISEI KIKAI (EX)
This ability can be used by one of the brothers when
the main character is 8th level or higher, and by both of
them when the main character is 16th level or higher.
By activating this power as an attack action (And
consuming 3 points of chakra per round that can't be
converted), the character can break down his body at a
molecular level and fuse into the body of his victim. This
allows him to strategically rip apart the cells of his target
from the inside. To insert himself into someone's body,
the user must succeed at a melee touch attack on a
humanoid creature with no more than twice the main
character's HD. When inside a body, the user can spend
a full-round action to cause 1d4 points of constitution
damage (That can be negated by a Fortitude Save, DC 10
+ number of rounds sharing the body with the user). The
user can freely leave the body as a move action,
appearing at an adjacent square. Unfortunately, because
this fusion makes him and the ninja share the same body,
any damage suffered by the victim that is not caused by
this ability is also dealt to the user. Certain abilities, like
Overwhelming Chakra, Chakra Presence, certain kinds
of Genjutsu and others could expel the intruder from a
body, at GM's discretion.
