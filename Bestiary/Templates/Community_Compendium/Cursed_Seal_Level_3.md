CURSED SEAL LEVEL 3 (TEMPLATE)
The Cursed Seal can be improved further thanks to
advancements in genetics and drug treatment therapy.
Only the most skilled and intelligent of doctors and
geneticists are able to provide the skills necessary to
unlock a Cursed Seal to the next stage. See the Core
Handbook pg. 805 for more details on the Cursed Seal
template.
Skills: Control Cursed Seal as a class skill.
Special Qualities: Cursed Seal Level 3.
Level Adjustment: +2.
When manifested, this ability grants the creature a +4
bonus to Defense, Reflex saves, attack and weapon
damage rolls, a +6 bonus to opposed disarm, trip, grapple,
bull rush or overrun checks and Climb, Jump and Swim
checks. He gains 36 temporary chakra and his carrying
capacity triples. Every consequent use within a 24 hours
period reduces the amount of chakra gained by any level
by 12, minimum 0. The temporary chakra fades
immediately after the Cursed Seal is deactivated.
Like the level 1 Cursed Seal, manifesting or
deactivating this ability is difficult for an unwilling
creature (see Cursed Seal Level 1).
Each round spent under the influence of the level 3
Cursed Seal grants 4 CSP. The level 3 Cursed Seal can be
used a number of times every 24 hours equal to the
creature's Constitution modifier. Each time after that
deals 2 points of temporary Constitution damage upon
activation, and puts the creature into a fatigued
condition when deactivated (or exhausted if it was
already fatigued).
The effects of any Cursed Seal Level do not stack with
another.
CURSED MANIFESTATIONS
Special manifestations occur when a person reaches
Level 3 of the Cursed Seal. The character picks one of the
following features, they can never change it. The bonus
the Manifestation grants is only available while in the
Cursed Seal Level 3 form.
CURSED BLESSING
Your raw potential has been improved thanks to the
Cursed Seal.
Benefit: The character gains a temporary +2 to one
Ability Score or +1 to two Ability Scores. The Ability
Score(s) chosen cannot be changed after the first
selection.
BIZARRE SINEW
The muscle fibers in your body have changed and
become more flexible.
Benefit: The character gains a +4 bonus to Reflex saves.
CELLULAR REGENERATION
The Cursed Seal has amplified the rate at which you
recover from wounds.
Benefit: The character gains fast healing 3.
INCREDIBLE SKILL
The Cursed Seal has vastly improved your skill in some
areas.
Benefit: The character picks 2 different skills to that
get miscellaneous +5 bonuses.

ENDURING CURSE
Your body has grown used to the effects of the Cursed
Seal, you’re now able to lessen the negative effects it has
on you, and you can even utilize the Level 1 form
without penalty.
Prerequisite: 12 ranks Control Cursed Seal
Benefit: The character takes one less CSP per round
(minimum 0).
WILLING CURSE
Prerequisite: Will Over Flesh
Benefit: The character is able to transform a number
of times per day equal to their Wisdom modifier, though
any damage for exceeding this number is still dealt to the
user’s Constitution. The character also receives a +1 to
all bonuses from the Cursed Seal template at all levels,
they also gain a +4 to Control Cursed Seal checks.
