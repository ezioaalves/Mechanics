HAKERU INOCHIRYOKU (TEMPLATE)
Although rare and dissociated with any specific clan,
certain individuals are cursed with a dangerous
bloodline that can suck the life force out of those they
touch. Many of these special individuals attempt to stay
hidden or keep their bloodline a secret, for the lore
behind the bloodline is that of a curse that plagues the
wicked. While the ability is powerful, it comes at a costly
price.
Saves: The character gains a +2 to Fortitude saves against
draining effects.
Skills: None.
Special Qualities: Life Absorption, Endless Fatigue.
Level Adjustment: +0.
LIFE ABSORPTION (SU)
The character has an innate ability that allows him to
drain the life out of other creatures. It is a very deadly
ability that manifests itself within the character at 2nd
level. As the ability surfaces, the character’s hands will
become particularly veiny and pale for the rest of his life.
When in combat, the character can make a melee
touch attack that does not provoke an attack of
opportunity. If he is successful, he deals normal unarmed
damage that does not add his strength modifier. All
damage dealt is drained from the target’s body and may
be converted into health for the user. Despite having
multiple attacks available, the character may not use this
ability multiple times as part of a full attack action.
Similarly, the character can attempt to grapple his
opponent. If successful, the character may drain health
from the grappled enemy at a rate of 1 point of health
per round. This continues until the opponent can break
free of the grapple or the user disengages from the
enemy. The amount of health the user can drain per
round increases at 10th and 15th levels to 2 and 4
respectively.
The character cannot exceed his normal total of health
by more than 1 point per level.
ENDLESS FATIGUE (SU)
Also at 5th level, the character’s body becomes more
and more worn from the bloodline manifesting itself.
The character must make a Fortitude save every 4 hours
(DC 20) to avoid becoming fatigued. Consecutive failures
cause the character to become exhausted. Only sleeping
will recover from this exhausted or medical ninjutsu.
After 4 hours from either sleep or jutsu, the character
will need to resume making saves.
