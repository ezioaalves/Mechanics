HASHIRAMA’S CELLS (TEMPLATE)
Either in part or your entirety, you hold the genetic
makeup of the legendary First Hokage, Hashirama Senju.
This grants you an increased vitality, as well as access to
the First’s kekkei genkai, the Mokuton release.
However, this power comes at an unknown cost, not
least of all the dangers should you ever lose control of
the power.
This template comes in 2 types; Artificial or Natural.
The Artificial Template is one typically grafted onto a
limb of a character, such as an arm, leg, but can be
grafted to anywhere on the body. The limb is selected
upon gaining this template. Characters that can Sense
Chakra may get a “odd feeling” about that particular
limb, while those that can See Chakra may see a stark
difference in the chakra flow of that limb (Spot Check:
DC 15). Characters that can See Through Chakra do not
need to make a Spot check, and can see that the chakra
flow there is a bright, forest-green and clearly not the
characters’ own.
The Innate Template is one the character has gained at
birth; a kekkei genkai, and can only be taken at first level
with the GM’s permission.
Type: As the base Creature.
Abilities: +2 Constitution
Saves: +2 to all Will saves.
Special Qualities: Vitality of the First Hokage, Mokuton
Release, Curse of the First’s Cells.
Level Adjustment: +2 Artificial, +3 Innate
ARTIFICIAL
VITALITY OF THE FIRST HOKAGE
Hit Points: You gain +1 hit points per Hit Dice. At the
GM’s discretion, this may include all your Hit Dice, even
those before you gained the Template “Hashirama’s
Cells”
Chakra: You count as one level higher to determine
the amount of bonus chakra you gain from levels. This
does not increase any bonus chakra or reserves gained
from class levels, only character levels.
Talents: You count as though having selected the
Tough Hero talent “Stamina”, even if you do not meet
the prerequisites. If you already have this talent, then
this grants no further effects other than those listed.
Mokuton Affinity: You gain a Mokuton nature
transformation. With your GM’s permission, this may
become your Primary affinity, at which point your
original Primary affinity downgrades to a Secondary
nature transformation. Note that this doesn't include the
composite elements, Suiton and Doton, although you are
not prevented from acquiring them later through other
means.
CURSE OF THE FIRST’S CELLS
The power of the First Hokage is difficult to control,
and as such, poses a danger on those without sufficient
power to reign in the effects, lest he become consumed
by the power. Whenever your chakra reserves drops to
10% its maximum or below, you must begin making
Fortitude saves (DC 25), which increases by 1 each
round. The character must consecutively pass two
Fortitude saves before he fails 3 consecutive times. If he
fails 3 consecutive times, the First Hokage’s cells attempt
to consume the characters’ body.
If the character fails a Reflex save (DC 33), his body
will be completely consumed, and after 1d3 rounds, a
tree will be left in place of the now dead character. If he
passes the Fortitude saves, the character manages to
reign in the power, and merely suffers a -5 to all Chakra
Control checks until the end of the encounter. Passing
the Reflex save means the character manages to tear off
the part of his body the cells were grafted to, and suffers
all the associated penalties for missing a limb. If the part
of his body is necessary for survival (e.g his head or
torso), then the characters acts as though he failed his
Reflex save, and suffers the above fate.
The tree created occupies a fighting space of a creature
one size category larger than the character, and is
centered on the square the character occupies. Any
creature that finds itself within this space while a
character is being consumed must make a Reflex save
(DC 23) or suffer 1d6 Strength ability damage and
become pinned. A natural 1 means the creature has
become trapped inside the tree, and takes 3d6 Strength
damage, make a massive damage save to avoid being
reduced to -1 hit points and dying, and begin holding his
breath, as per suffocation rules.
Should the character ever completely deplete his
chakra pool and reserves, he is immediately consumed
and turned into a tree, without getting to make a
Fortitude save.

MOKUTON (INNATE) ELEMENTAL AFFINITY
You gain a Primary Mokuton affinity, and your
Primary affinity selected at first level is downgraded to
a secondary nature transformation. You also gain Suiton
and Doton as secondary nature transformations.
In addition, the character is not affected by Curse of
the First’s Cells, and does not ever risk being consumed
by the cells for completely reducing his chakra pool and
chakra reserves to 0.
Special: The GM may, at his discretion, allow a
character who has possessed the “Artificial” version of
this template for a long time, and with sufficient
training, upgrade it to an “Innate” template by paying
the additional ECL cost.
