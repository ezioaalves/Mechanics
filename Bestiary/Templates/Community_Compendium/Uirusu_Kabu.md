UIRUSU KABU (TEMPLATE)
The plagued lands of the Five Great Nations holds a
virus that science at this point cannot even comprehend.
It has left all life in the area devastated, and the humans
were transformed into zombie-like beings that have
enhanced strength but no intelligence. However, some
children born to virus-bearing parents have started
showing signs of a genetic mutation; one that only
surfaces in times of turbulent strife.
Type: Base creature’s type remains unchanged, but gains
Mutant subtype.
Hit Dice: The character gains an additional 2 hit points
per level.
Abilities: See below.
Saves: See below.
Skills: +1 to all Strength-based skill checks. -1 to all
Concentration checks.
Feats: The character gains the Improved Chakra Pool
feat two times for free.
Special Qualities: Accursed Rage, Virus Strain,
Overwhelming Presence.
Level Adjustment: +2
Challenge Rating: +1
ACCURSED RAGE
If the character falls below 25% of his maximum hit
points, he instantly blacks out and goes into a blinding
frenzy unless he succeeds a Will save (DC 25). He must
succeed this save each round until either the rage takes
effect, combat ends, or the character falls unconscious or
dies. During the frenzy, the character’s body goes
through several changes. His size category increases by
1, and he gains a temporary +4 bonus to Strength and a
+2 bonus to Constitution. He also gains a +10 bonus to
his base movement speed.
However, he suffers a temporary -2 penalty to
Dexterity, Intelligence, Wisdom and Charisma. The
character cannot perform actions that require
concentration nor can he perform techniques. Any
suppressed Speed and Strength ranks the user possesses
become active and cannot be suppressed until the
accursed rage ends. The character will attack the most
notable threat, but may attack allies if no other entities
are present during the frenzy. The Accursed Rage lasts
2d6 rounds or until the character’s hit points fall below
0. When the rage ends regardless of his current hit points
or chakra pool, the character will fall unconscious until
awoken or healed (minimum 1 minute).
VIRUS STRAIN
The abilities from this subtype are directly granted by
the strain of virus from The Scar that plagues the Five
Great Nations. However, there is a drawback to having
this virus housed in your system. The character affected
by this virus, while greatly enhanced at times, will often
seem fatigued. He recovers chakra at half the normal rate
per 8 hours of sleep. In addition, his life expectancy
decreases by 25 years due to the virus slowly eating at
his body and mind. Finally, once per day, the character
must succeed a Fortitude save (DC 15) or else suffer 4
points of temporary Constitution damage.
OVERWHELMING PRESENCE
While the character is enveloped in rage, his chakra
signature goes through the roof and his appearance
becomes frightening to say the least. Anyone within 30
feet of the character must make a Will save (DC 18) or
else become shaken for the duration of the frenzy.
