# Portfolio of Prestige Volume IV Feats

## Aftershock
**Prerequisite:** Intimidate 12 ranks, Interrogation class feature.
**Benefit:** Any victim of your successful interrogation, master interrogator or thought reform abilities must succeed a Will save (DC 10 + your Interrogator level) when discussing the details of your session. A failed save means the victim is unable to share any detail regarding the session for 24 hours. It can retry this save afterwards once each day. The difficulty of this save decreases by 1 after each attempt.

## Ambush Charge
**Prerequisite:** Tumble 12 ranks, Run, Mobile Defense class feature.
**Benefit:** You no longer provoke attacks of opportunity when making a charge attack.

## Armor Mastery
**Prerequisite:** Con 15, Armor Training class feature, base attack bonus +15.
**Benefit:** When wearing medium armor, your suffer no penalty to movement or speed ranks. When wearing heavy armor, you gain damage resistance 3/—.

## Army of One
**Prerequisite:** Intimidate 6 ranks, Swarm Fighter class feature, base attack bonus +7.
**Benefit:** Whenever facing a group of creatures with the unit template, the maximum number of attacks it can make against you is reduced by one.

## Back to Back
**Prerequisite:** Bottleneck class feature.
**Benefit:** You can choose one adjacent ally at the start of your turn. As long as that ally remains adjacent to you, they benefit from your Bottleneck ability.

## Battlefield Control
**Prerequisite:** Dodge, Improved Spring Attack class feature, base attack bonus +9. 
**Benefit:** Each time an enemy is hit by your Spring Attack, it loses one attack of opportunity until the start of your next turn. An enemy can only be affected by this feat once per round, and can still make other attacks of opportunities if has abilities that grant it more (such as the Combat Reflexes feat).

## Chakra Ground
**Prerequisite:** Any 2 meta-chakra feat, Delay Surge class feature.
**Benefit:** When you use the Delay Surge class feature to delay a wild surge, you can spend a meta-chakra charge and 2 chakra to negate it entirely instead.

## Controlled Chaos
**Prerequisite:** Chakra Control 9 ranks, Wild Tenacity, Purgative Transference class feature.
**Benefit:** You gain the following uses for the Chakra Control skill: Convert Null Chakra and Haywire.
*   **Check (Convert Null Chakra):** You can expend chakra to increase your null chakra pool by the same amount. Performed as a move action (DC 10) or swift action (DC 15). Temporary chakra gained (per point) +1. No seals (DC modifier -0), half-seal (-2), hand seal (-5).
*   **Check (Haywire):** You can attempt a Chakra Control check (DC 20) to make your chakra go haywire for an encounter. Anyone who drains at least 1 chakra from you while your chakra is haywire incurs a wild surge and automatically ends this ability.

## In Your Head
**Prerequisite:** Attentive, Commanding Presence class feature.
**Benefit:** You can apply the benefits of the Commanding Presence class feature to Gamble and Sense Motive checks as well as Charisma-based skill checks.

## Jack of All Blades
**Prerequisite:** Intuitive Weapon Proficiency class feature.
**Benefit:** You deal a minimum of 1d6 points of damage when using your Intuitive Weapon Proficiency class feature with a Tiny or larger improvised weapon.

## Pinpoint Intuition
**Prerequisite:** Wis 13, Intuitive Weapon Proficiency class feature, base attack bonus +7.
**Benefit:** You can select a single melee or ranged weapon with which you've used the Intuitive Weapon Proficiency class feature. You are always considered to be using your ability with that weapon.
**Special:** Each time you reach a level where you would gain a new feat, you can change the weapon this feat applies to, but you cannot select this feat more than once.

## Savage Glee
**Prerequisite:** Delay Surge class feature.
**Benefit:** Whenever you enter a wild chakra state, you become immune to fear effects.

## Us Versus Them
**Prerequisite:** Cha 13, Back to Back, Improved Bottleneck class feature.
**Benefit:** Your designated ally gains the benefit of Improved Bottleneck as long as they remain adjacent to you, or Bottleneck when within 30 feet.

## Wild Tenacity
**Prerequisite:** Improved Chakra Pool, Null Entity class feature.
**Benefit:** Your chakra pool improves by 4, and you are fatigued instead of exhausted when suffering from chakra depletion.
