# Portfolio of Prestige Volume II Feats

## Compelling Mayhem
**Prerequisite:** Cha 15, Inspire Frenzy class feature, base attack bonus +12.
**Benefit:** When using the Inspire Frenzy class feature, you can affect allies up to 30 feet away instead of only 10 feet.

## High Acrobatics
**Prerequisite:** Acrobatic, Acrobatics class feature.
**Benefit:** When you spend chakra to improve your Acrobatics ability, the bonus it provides to all skills is doubled.

## Greater Specialist's Cunning
**Prerequisite:** Int 13, Specialist's Cunning class feature, base attack bonus +6.
**Benefit:** You can use the Specialist's Cunning class feature on weapons you are already proficient in. With a successful Intelligence check, you gain a +1 bonus to attack rolls with the weapon. If you have the Weapon Focus feat with that weapon, you gain a +1 bonus to damage rolls instead. This bonus does not stack with the Weapon Focus feat or the Weapon Specialization class feature.

## Maniacal Assault
**Prerequisite:** [Universal: Risky Strike], Rage or frenzy class feature, base attack bonus +6.
**Benefit:** When using the power attack feat, you can subtract your a number from your class bonus to Defense instead of base attack bonus to determine your bonus to melee attack damage. This number cannot exceed your class bonus to Defense.

## Master of Antidotes
**Prerequisite:** Craft (pharmaceutical) 9 ranks, Knowledge (earth and life science) 9 ranks, Prolific Poisoner class feature.
**Benefit:** You can create specific antitodes to poison by simply identifying the poison. To do so requires a Knowledge (earth and life science) with a DC equal to the poison's saving throw difficulty plus 5. If you have access to a sample of the poison, a successful Craft (pharmaceutical) check enables you to create 4 doses of antitode instead of 3.

## Master of Poisons
**Prerequisite:** Craft (chemical) 15 ranks, Master Poisoner class feature.
**Benefit:** When you use the master of poison class feature, you can choose up to 2 effects from the poison to benefit you, but the duration of the ability is halved. If the selected poison only has one effect (for example, only deals Strength damage), the benefits of this feat cannot be applied to it.

## Never Caught
**Prerequisite:** Escape Artist 8 ranks, Acrobatics class feature
**Benefit:** You can apply the benefits of the Acrobatics class feature to your Escape Artist checks as well.

## No Trace
**Prerequisite:** Disguise 8 ranks, Trackless Steps class feature
**Benefit:** You can apply the bonus from the Trackless Steps ability as an insight bonus to all Disguise checks.

## Stand Your Ground
**Prerequisite:** Adept marksman class feature, base attack bonus +11.
**Benefit:** When you fire continuously for more than one round without moving from your position with more than a 5-foot step, you gain a +1 morale bonus to attack rolls. If you have the high ground, you also gain a +1 morale bonus to Defense and a +4 bonus to attack rolls made to confirm critical hits. This bonus lasts until you take a move action, spend at least one round without attacking or are otherwise forced away from your position.

## The Gift of Giving
**Prerequisite:** Craft (chemical) 9 ranks, Diplomacy 6 ranks, Enhanced Poisons class feature.
**Benefit:** When you create an enhanced poison, the effects are increased regardless of who applies or delivers it. In addition, when you use an enhanced poison crafted by you, you apply a +1 competence bonus to the saving throw difficulty.

## Venomous Strike
**Prerequisite:** Enhance Poisons class feature, Sneak Attack class feature, base attack bonus +6.
**Benefit:** When you deal sneak attack damage with a poisoned weapon, you gain a +1 bonus to damage per sneak attack dice.
