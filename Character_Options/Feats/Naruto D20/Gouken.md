# Gouken

The Iron Fist style is very popular in the fire country. Most Ninja who specialize in Taijutsu and live in the fire country know at least a technique from, or a variation of, the Gouken style.

**Prerequisite**: Taijutsu 4 ranks.

**Benefit**: You suffer no penalty to Learn techniques that would penalize you for not having the Gouken feat and gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls to confirm critical hits with unarmed attacks.

**Normal**: A character without this feat takes a -4 penalty to learn checks for certain taijutsu techniques, such as Taijutsu: Gouken - Konoha Daisenpuu (see Techniques chapter for details).
