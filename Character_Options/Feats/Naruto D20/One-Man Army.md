# One-Man Army [Meta-Chakra]

**Prerequisite**: Any 1 meta-chakra feat, Kage Bunshin no Jutsu (Advanced Proficiency), Tajuu Kage Bunshin no Jutsu

**Benefit**: You may spend 1 meta-chakra charge to suffer 1 less damage per clone when performing Kage Bunshin or Tajuu Kage Bunshin no Jutsu to replicate yourself, minimum 1.
