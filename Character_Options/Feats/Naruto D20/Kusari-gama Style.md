# Kusari-gama Style

**Prerequisite**: Int 13, [Universal: Defensive Stance], proficient with the kusari-gama.

**Benefit**: You can use the kusari-gama as a two-handed weapon to attack enemies up to 10 feet away and no longer suffer an attack of opportunity to do so. However, you do not threaten a larger area than normal when wielding the weapon in this manner.

**Normal**: You can only throw the kusari-gama to make a single attack, and need a move-equivalent action to recover it. You also incur an attack of opportunity from any creature threatening you.
