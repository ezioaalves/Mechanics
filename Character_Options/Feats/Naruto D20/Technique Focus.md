# Technique Focus

You attain particular aptitude at a specific field of Genjutsu or Ninjutsu.

**Benefit**: You choose one subtype of Genjutsu or Ninjutsu techniques (such as Doujutsu or Katon) and are able to perform those techniques as though you were one level higher. In addition, you gain a +1 enhancement bonus to skill threshold to perform techniques of that subtype.

Enhancement bonus do not stack with one another. Universal focus applies to any Genjutsu or Ninjutsu without a subtype.

**Special**: You can chose this feat multiple times. Each time, it applies to a different subtype of techniques.
