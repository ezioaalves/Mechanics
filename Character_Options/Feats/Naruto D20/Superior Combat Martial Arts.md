# Superior Combat Martial Arts

**Prerequisite**: Combat Martial Arts, base attack bonus +3.

**Benefit**: Your unarmed strikes deal more damage as you gain levels, as shown on the table below.

| Character Level | Damage |
| :--- | :--- |
| 8 or lower | 1d6 |
| 9-16 | 1d8 |
| 17 or higher | 1d10 |

**Special**: If you have the Feral Combat beastmaster ability, Living Weapon martial artist ability, Sacred Fist Stance sacred fist ability or the Unarmed Strike taijutsu master ability, you deal damage as though four levels higher.
