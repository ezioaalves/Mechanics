# Craft Sealed Item [Item Creation]

You are capable of crafting objects such as paper bombs and chakra gems, as well as sealed items.

**Prerequisites**: Chakra Control 4 ranks, Craft (calligraphy) or Fuinjutsu 4 ranks.

**Benefit**: You may use the Craft (calligraphy) skill to create chakra inscriptions and explosives without increasing the craft time.

You are able to craft Sealed Items (as per enhancement seals).
