# Skillful Assault

**Prerequisite**: Wis 13, Heroic character.

**Benefit**: At the beginning of your turn, you can designate an opponent and each attack made against that opponent will not reduce it below 0, but suffer a -2 circumstance penalty to attack and damage rolls. This cannot be used on a creature at or below 0 hit points. You may select a new opponent at the beginning of any turn.
