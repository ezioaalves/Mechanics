# Elemental Specialization

**Prerequisite**: Chakra Control 12 ranks, Ninjutsu 15 ranks

**Benefit**: By selecting this feat, the character has to choose one of the elements: Doton, Fuuton, Hyouton, Katon, Raiton and Suiton. He may use any techniques of the chosen element ignoring any material focus the technique may require.

Any material component fades immediately after the technique ends or after its effects have been dealt.

**Special**: A character can gain this feat multiple times. Each time, the character must select another element.
