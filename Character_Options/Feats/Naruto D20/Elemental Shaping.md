# Elemental Shaping [Meta-Chakra]

You are able to practice far greater control over your elemental Ninjutsu techniques.

**Prerequisite**: Any 1 meta-chakra feats, Ninjutsu 12 ranks.

**Benefit**: This meta-chakra feat allows the character to add the Shapeable (S) component to a cone, line, emanation or burst area effect of a Doton, Fuuton, Hyouton, Katon, Raiton or Suiton Ninjutsu technique.

The technique’s perform requirements increase by 2. The chakra cost increases by 1 every 2 ranks (round up).
