# Sacred Flurry

**Prerequisite**: Sacred fist stance (1d6), buddhist palm (chakra), base attack bonus +6, Dex 13.

**Benefit**: The sacred fist may spend a swift action each round to enter a state of sacred flurry. During the sacred flurry, the character gains an additional sacred fist attack at his highest attack bonus when making a full-attack action, but all attacks made this round suffer a -2 penalty to attack rolls.

The sacred flurry does not stack with other similar abilities, such as flurry of blows or the Renzuki technique, and can only be used when the character is in the Sacred Fist stance.
