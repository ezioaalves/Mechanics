# Fuinjutsu Adept

You are a master of sealing techniques.

**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus to Fuinjutsu checks by selecting that feat and the DC of your Fuinjutsu techniques increase by 1. In addition, you may make 1 additional attempt when learning Fuinjutsu techniques.

You gain a +1 bonus to your effective skill threshold when performing Fuinjutsu techniques.
