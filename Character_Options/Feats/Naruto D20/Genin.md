# Genin

**Prerequisite**: Must know at least 4 techniques.

**Benefit**: Being a Genin grants a character automatically a “licensed” license in his Ninja village, a +1 bonus to their Wealth and +1 bonus to any one of the following skills: Chakra Control, Gather Information, Genjutsu, Hide, Investigate, Move Silently, Ninjutsu, and Taijutsu.

Alternatively, the character may chose one of the two following abilities in place of a skill bonus: Sense Chakra or Suppress Chakra.

See Ninja Ranks chapter for more details.

**Special**: This feat is a bonus feat available to all basic classes.
