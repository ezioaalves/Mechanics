# Technical Knowledge

Your advanced knowledge of the true workings of Chakra enables you to teach others and yourself with great efficiency.

**Prerequisite**: Educated, Knowledge (ninja lore) 9 ranks, any 1 Meta-Chakra Specialization (class ability).

**Benefit**: The character gains a +1 bonus to Learn checks made when developing or creating a Chakra Control, Genjutsu or Ninjutsu technique. The character also grants an additional +2 bonus to Learn checks when teaching a Chakra Control, Genjutsu or Ninjutsu technique that he knows to another. The Teaching bonus cannot be higher than +5 still even with the bonus granted by this feat.
