# Heighten Technique [Meta-Chakra]

You can imbue your techniques with greater power and finesse, making them harder to command but far more deadly.

**Prerequisite**: Any 1 meta-chakra feat.

**Benefit**: This meta-chakra feat, when applied to a technique, allows a character to increase the technique’s effective rank by 1, up to 5 (maximum rank 14). This also increases the perform requirements as well as the saving throw DC of the technique by the set amount. All effects dependent on the technique’s Rank are calculated according to the heightened technique.

The technique’s perform requirements increase by 3. The chakra cost increases by 3 plus 1 every 2 ranks (round up).

**Special**: If you apply this feat to a technique, it cannot be enhanced by another meta-chakra feat.
