# Flawless Form [Meta-Chakra]

You execute taijutsu moves to a perfection.

**Prerequisite**: Taijutsu 4 ranks

**Benefit**: This meta-chakra feat can only be applied to a Strike taijutsu technique.

Although it grants no benefits, you gain a +2 enhancement bonus to attack and damage rolls for 1 round after performing the technique successfully.

The technique’s perform requirements increase by 2. The chakra cost increases by 1 every 2 ranks (round up).
