# Concealed Technique [Meta-Chakra]

**Prerequisite**: Efficient Technique, Chakra Control 12 ranks.

**Benefit**: You may apply this meta-chakra feat to a Chakra Control, Fuinjutsu, Genjutsu or Ninjutsu technique. The chakra cost of the technique is completely concealed and the chakra cost is reduced by 1 every 4 ranks (minimum 1). The technique’s perform requirements increase by 6.
