# Illuminated Proficiency

**Prerequisite**: Sacred fist stance, buddhist palm (chakra), Wis 15.

**Benefit**: You may treat attacks from a quarterstaff, tonfa, nunchaku or kama as a sacred fist attack while in sacred fist stance. You use either the weapon's base damage or your sacred fist damage, whichever is higher. The buddhist palm ability is also conferred to the weapon.

Any other ability that affect unarmed attack, such as weapon focus or weapon specialization, does not apply to weapon attacks.
