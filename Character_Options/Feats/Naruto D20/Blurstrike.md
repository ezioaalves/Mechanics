# Blurstrike [Meta-Chakra]

**Prerequisite**: Quick Draw, Blinkstrike +1 class ability, fifth step of mastery in Kenjutsu: Iaido.

**Benefit**: The character is able to sheathe and draw his weapon as a free action in the Blinkstrike stance, without provoking an attack of opportunity.

The character may spend 1 meta-chakra charge to increase his Blinkstrike ability bonus by +2 for 1 round while making a full-attack action. This feat can be used up to three times per encounter.

This ability does not stack with the True Blinkstrike feat, and cannot be used in conjunction with it.
