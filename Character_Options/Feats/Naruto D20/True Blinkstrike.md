# True Blinkstrike

**Prerequisite**: Blinkstrike +3 class ability, Quick Draw, Dodge, Dodge, Agile Riposte.

**Benefit**: The character is able to use this ability as a free action, it lasts for 1 round. The character turns his Blinkstrike bonus to defense into a bonus to attack and damage rolls equal to half his Blinkstrike level, rounded up. While in the Blinkstrike stance, he may make a full-attack action and still move up to his speed.

The character is subject to attacks of opportunity while doing so, but may Tumble normally or use the Blink Step ability as part of his move. If he is prevented from completing his move, he is also prevented from finishing his full-attack action.

While using this ability, the character must make a minimum 5 feet movement between each attacks, including attacks of opportunity, and is unable to return to a square he previously occupied this round.
