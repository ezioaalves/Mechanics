# Widen Technique [Meta-Chakra]

**Benefit**: This meta-chakra feat allows the character to alter a burst, emanation, line, or spread shaped technique to increase its area. Any numeric measurements of the technique’s area increase by 50%, but techniques that do not have an area of one of these four sorts are not affected by this feat.

The technique’s perform requirements increase by 3. The chakra cost is increased by 2 plus 1 every 2 ranks (round up).
