# Multipuppet Fighting

You are adept at fighting with multiple puppets.

**Prerequisite**: Advanced Puppetry class ability, Dex 13.

**Benefits**: You can animate multiple puppets with an attack action. When animating or fighting with more than one puppet, you can increase the number of hit dice of puppets you can control by one-half (+50%) by lowering the maximum hit dice a puppet can be animated with up to your character level or hit dice -3.
