# Face Masking

**Prerequisite**: Disguise 2 ranks, heroic character, can only be taken at 1st level

**Benefit**: Selecting this feat grants the character a +2 bonus to disguise checks made to assume the identity of a specific person and allows him to learn the Hijutsu - Kao Utsushi no Jutsu technique, though he must still succeed a Learn check normally and find a source to learn a Kinjutsu from.
