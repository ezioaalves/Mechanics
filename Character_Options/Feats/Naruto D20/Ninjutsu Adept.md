# Ninjutsu Adept

You are very good at performing hand seals and concentrating to perform Ninjutsu, and identifying them.

**Benefit**: You gain a +2 bonus to Ninjutsu checks by selecting that feat and the DCs of your techniques in increased by 1. In addition, you may make 1 additional attempt when learning Ninjutsu techniques.

You gain a +1 bonus to your effective skill threshold when performing Ninjutsu techniques.
