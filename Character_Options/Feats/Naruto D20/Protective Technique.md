# Protective Technique [Meta-Chakra]

**Prerequisite**: Any 1 meta-chakra feat, Con 13.

**Benefit**: Applying this meta-chakra feat to a Chakra Control or Ninjutsu technique used on yourself grants you a +4 deflection bonus to Defense for 1 round.

The technique’s perform requirements increase by 2. The chakra cost is increased by 1 every 2 ranks (round up).
