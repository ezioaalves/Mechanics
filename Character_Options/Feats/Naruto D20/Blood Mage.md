# Blood Mage [Meta-Chakra]

You draw power from your blood and use it to perform various techniques and rituals.

**Prerequisite**: Fuinjutsu 6 ranks, Con 13.

**Benefit**: The character is able to learn techniques requiring the Blood Mage meta-chakra feat, without penalty, even if they are Sealing ninjutsu techniques (the user still suffers normal penalty to learn other sealing techniques until he meets the requirements).

He is also able to expend a meta-chakra charge to convert his blood into chakra, dealing himself 1 point of damage to reduce the chakra cost of a technique by 1, which can be increased to 5 points of damage to reduce a technique’s cost by 5.
