# Elemental Mastery [Meta-Chakra]

Your innate genius allows you to learn gain additional elemental affinities.

**Prerequisite**: Genius Ninja, any 1 meta-chakra feat, Ninjutsu 9 ranks.

**Benefit**: When selecting this feat, you gain an additional elemental nature transformation.

**Special**: This feat can be selected multiple times, each time it applies to a different affinity.
