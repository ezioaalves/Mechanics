# One-handed Seals [Meta-Chakra]

Your extreme ability and genius gives you the uncanny ability to perform hand seals one handed.

**Prerequisite**: Ninjutsu 9 ranks, Sleight of Hands 6 ranks, Genius Ninja (genjutsu or ninjutsu).

**Benefit**: A character selecting this feat may use a Fuinjutsu, Genjutsu or Ninjutsu technique with the Hand Seals component as though they required only Half Seals, meaning that he can perform techniques requiring Hand Seals with only one hand free. Changing the Hand Seals component into Half Seals increase the technique’s perform requirements by 2.

The character can spend a meta-chakra charge to ignore the complexity rating increment when performing that technique.

**Normal**: A character cannot perform hand seals one handed without this feat.

**Special**: The character can apply this feat to a technique that has already been enhanced by another meta-chakra feat.

Applying this meta-chakra feat to a technique does not always consume a meta-chakra charge.
