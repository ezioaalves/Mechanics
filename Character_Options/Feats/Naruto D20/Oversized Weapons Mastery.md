# Oversized Weapons Mastery

**Prerequisite**: Str 15, Monkey Grip, Oversized Weapons Proficiency, base attack bonus +11.

**Benefit**: You are able to wield oversized weapons with one hand. In addition, weapons one size category larger are now treated as light weapons. Weapons that cannot normally be wielded one-handed, such as a fullblade, still require two hands to use.
