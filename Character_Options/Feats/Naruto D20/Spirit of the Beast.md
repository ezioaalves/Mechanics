# Spirit of the Beast [Epic Feat]

*Source: NarutoD20 Redux v1.3, Chapter 10 – Bloodlines and Templates, Moujuu Aishou (p350).*

**Prerequisite**: Moujuu Aishou, Handle Animal 16 ranks.

**Benefit**: You improve your animal companion's Strength, Dexterity or natural armor bonus to Defense by 1, or you can teach it an additional trick.

**Special**: Can be selected multiple times.
