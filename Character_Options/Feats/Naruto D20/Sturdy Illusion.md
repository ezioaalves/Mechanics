# Sturdy Illusion [Meta-Chakra]

Your illusions are tougher to dispel.

**Prerequisite**: Any 1 meta-chakra feat, Genjutsu 12 ranks.

**Benefit**: Applying this meta-chakra feat to a Genjutsu technique gives it a 20% chance to resist being dispelled.

The technique’s perform requirements increase by 2. The chakra cost is increased by 1 every 2 ranks (round up).

**Special**: If you apply this feat to a technique, it cannot be enhanced by another meta-chakra feat.
