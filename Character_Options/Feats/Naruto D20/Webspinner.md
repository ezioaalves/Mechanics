# Webspinner

**Prerequisite**: Advanced Bloodline (Gumoshin), Gumokeitou.

**Benefit**: You gain the ability to spend 2 point of chakra to create 2 additional kumonosu up to three times per day as a free action, though you cannot exceed your maximum number of kumonosu by this mean. The rate at which you regenerate your kumonosu increases to 4 per hour.
