# Light Sleeper

You trained your body continuously to adapt to the lack of sleep, and you are able to recover just as you would for the period you slept.

**Prerequisite**: Con 13.

**Benefit**: You are able to sleep only 4 hours per night and recover one-half the amount of hit points, chakra pool and chakra reserve normally recovered during normal rest.

In this mode of sleep, ability damage is not recovered. The character lessens the penalty to Listen checks made while asleep by 2.

**Normal**: A character needs to rest for 8 hours a night to recover one hit points per level previously lost.
