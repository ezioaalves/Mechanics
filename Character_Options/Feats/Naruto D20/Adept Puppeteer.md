# Adept Puppeteer [Meta-Chakra]

You have great control over your puppets and can reanimate them in the blink of an eye.

**Prerequisite**: Ninjutsu 12 ranks, Int 15, Advanced Puppetry class ability.

**Benefit**: The character can reanimate a puppet that was deanimated from a failed Concentration check as a free action by spending a meta-chakra charge without paying chakra from chakra threads. This can be done even during another’s turn, but must still be done within 1 round of the puppet being deanimated.
