# Craft Weapon Seals [Item Creation]

**Prerequisite**: Craft (calligraphy) 4 ranks, Craft (mechanical) 6 ranks, Chakra Control 4 ranks, heroic character.

**Benefit**: You are able to add Weapon Seals to a properly outfitted weapon. The penalty you take to learn Sealing ninjutsu is lessened by -4, although the time required is still increased.
