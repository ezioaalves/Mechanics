# Empowered Resilience

You are proficient at using Chakra effectively to empower yourself.

**Prerequisite**: Chakra Control 6 ranks, Chakra Pool 16.

**Benefits**: You may spend an instant action and 1 point of chakra to gain a +4 resistance bonus to saving throws until your next turn. You may do so when it is not your turn, but must declare using the ability before making a saving throw.

**Note**: Using this ability expends your instant action this round, meaning that using techniques or ability requiring a swift action or an instant action, such as kawarimi no jutsu is impossible.
