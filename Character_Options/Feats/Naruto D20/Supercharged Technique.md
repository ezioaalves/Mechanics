# Supercharged Technique [Meta-Chakra]

You are able to supercharge a technique to alter its elemental properties.

**Prerequisite**: Any 1 meta-chakra feat.

**Benefit**: This meta-chakra feat can only be used with a Doton, Fuuton, Hyouton, Katon, Raiton or Suiton technique. Half the elemental damage of the appropriate type dealt by the technique is force damage.

The technique’s perform requirements increase by 3. The chakra cost is increased by 3 plus 1 every 2 ranks (round up).

**Special**: If you apply this feat to a technique, it cannot be enhanced by another meta-chakra feat.
