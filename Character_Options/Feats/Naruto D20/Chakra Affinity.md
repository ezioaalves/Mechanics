# Chakra Affinity

Your talent lies in a specific field of Chakra mastery, and you are at ease with technique falling in that category.

**Prerequisite**: Can only be taken at first level.

**Benefit**: Upon selection of this feat, the character must chose from one of the four technique type (Chakra Control, Genjutsu, Ninjutsu and Taijutsu) for which he will receive a +2 bonus to his Learn checks. He must also chose another type, which cannot be the same as his "good" type, for which he will suffer a -1 penalty to his Learn checks.
