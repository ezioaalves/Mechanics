Although you learned to read and write, reading can be difficult, and writing is commonly misspelled. This has affected you throughout your life. 

Prerequisite: You must be literate. 

Effect: You take a -4 penalty on all Craft (calligraphy) ,Craft (writing) , Decipher Script, Forgery, and Fuinjutsu checks. Learning a new language via the speak language skill requires double the amount of skill points invested.
