You are susceptible to fear. 

Prerequisite: Must not be immune to fear. 

Effect: You have a -6 penalty on all saves against fear effects. Even if you succeed on such a save, you are still shaken for 1 round. 

Special: If the effect would normally leave you shaken on a successful save, then you become frightened, instead. If the effect would normally leave you frightened on a successful save, you become panicked. 
This flaw overrides any class features, should they come into conflict.
