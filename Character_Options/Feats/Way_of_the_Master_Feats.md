# Way of the Master Feats

## Pressure Secrets
**Prerequisite:** Dex or Wis 15, [Universal / Finesse Category], Knowledge (earth and life science) 4 ranks.
**Benefit:** You may use your Dexterity or Wisdom modifier instead of your Strength modifier as your relevant ability score for Taijutsu techniques and learn Kyushojutsu techniques without a penalty.
