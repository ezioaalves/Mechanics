# Monster Feats

These feats are primarily intended for creatures, summons, and NPCs.

### Ability Focus
**Prerequisite:** Special attack.
**Benefit:** Add +2 to the DC of all saving throws for one extraordinary, supernatural, or spell-like special attack.
**Special:** Can be selected multiple times. Does not stack; applies to a new ability each time. Cannot be applied to a technique or item.

### Advanced Multiweapon Fighting [Epic]
**Prerequisite:** Dex 25, Multiweapon Fighting, Improved Multiweapon Fighting, BAB +15, three or more hands.
**Benefit:** You may make up to three attacks with each extra weapon (second at -5, third at -10).
**Special:** Replaces Advanced Two-Weapon Fighting for creatures with 3+ hands.

### Awesome Blow
**Prerequisite:** Str 25, [Universal: Risky Strike], Powerful Maneuvers, Large or larger.
**Benefit:** As an attack action, take -4 penalty to melee attack. If you hit a smaller corporeal opponent, they must save (Reflex DC = damage done) or be knocked back 10 ft and fall prone.

### Flyby Attack
**Prerequisite:** Fly speed.
**Benefit:** When flying, you can take a move action (including a dive) and another attack action at any point during the move.
**Normal:** Without this feat, the attack must be before or after the move.

### Improved Banish Resistance
**Prerequisite:** Banish Resistance +1, Cha 13.
**Benefit:** Your banish resistance increases by 1.
**Special:** Can be selected up to four times.

### Improved Multiweapon Fighting [Epic]
**Prerequisite:** Dex 19, Multiweapon Fighting, BAB +10, three or more hands.
**Benefit:** Gain an extra attack with each extra weapon at -5.
**Special:** Replaces Greater Two-Weapon Fighting for creatures with 3+ hands.

### Improved Natural Armor
**Prerequisite:** Natural armor bonus +1, Con 13.
**Benefit:** Your natural armor bonus increases by 1.
**Special:** Can be selected multiple times.

### Improved Natural Attack
**Prerequisite:** Natural weapon, BAB +4.
**Benefit:** Choose one natural weapon; its damage increases by one size category (e.g., 1d6 -> 1d8, 2d6 -> 3d6).
**Special:** Can be selected multiple times for different natural weapons.

### Multiattack
**Prerequisite:** Three or more natural weapons.
**Benefit:** Secondary attacks with natural weapons take only a –2 penalty.
**Normal:** Secondary attacks take –5 penalty.

### Multiweapon Fighting
**Prerequisite:** Dex 13, three or more hands.
**Benefit:** Penalties for fighting with more than one weapon are lessened by 2 for the main hand and 6 for off-hands.
**Special:** Replaces Two-Weapon Fighting for creatures with 3+ hands.

### Snatch
**Prerequisite:** Huge or larger.
**Benefit:** Start a grapple when hitting with a claw or bite. If you hold a creature 3+ sizes smaller, squeeze each round for automatic damage. Snatched opponents in mouth get no Reflex save vs. breath weapons. Can fling them aside for 1d6 damage per 10 ft traveled.

### Wingover
**Prerequisite:** Fly speed.
**Benefit:** Change direction quickly once per round as a free action. Can turn up to 180 degrees regardless of maneuverability. Cannot gain altitude during the round. 10 ft movement cost.
