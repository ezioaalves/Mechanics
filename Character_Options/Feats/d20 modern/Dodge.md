# Dodge (Combat)

Your training and reflexes allow you to react swiftly to avoid an opponent’s attacks and move safely through the battlefield.

**Benefit:** You gain a +1 dodge bonus to your AC. A condition that makes you lose your Dex bonus to AC also makes you lose the benefits of this feat.

Additionally, you gain a +4 dodge bonus to AC against attacks of opportunity provoked when you move out of or within a threatened square.

**Special:** This feat counts as both *Dodge* and *Dodge* for the purpose of prerequisites.
