# Fast Plus

Advancements in science coupled with your drive to excel enable you to progress faster toward becoming a truly dexterous hero.

**Prerequisite**: By the letter of the rules, a character must have at least three ranks in the Fast Hero class to take this feat.

**Benefit**: Character gains two talents from the Fast hero’s talent trees. The talents must be selected from the following list, a talent cannot be selected from more than one talent talent tree, and the character must meet all the prerequisites of a talent to select it. 

Defensive Talent Tree: Uncanny Dodge 1, Uncanny Dodge 2, Defensive Roll, or Opportunist, 

Increased Speed Talent Tree: Improved Increased Speed or Advanced Increased Speed.

**Special**: This feat may be selected multiple times. Each time it is selected, a different pair of talents must be choses than the previous times.
