# Deft Maneuvers (Combat)

You are skilled in a variety of dextrous combat maneuvers.

**Benefit:** You do not provoke an attack of opportunity when performing a **Trip, Disarm, Dirty Trick, Feint, Reposition, or Steal** combat maneuver. In addition, you receive a +2 bonus on checks with these combat maneuvers, and you can make a Bluff check to feint in combat as a move action.

You also receive a +2 bonus to your Combat Maneuver Defense whenever an opponent tries to perform any of these maneuvers against you.

**Normal:** These maneuvers provoke an attack of opportunity. Feinting is a standard action.
