# Weapon Specialization (Combat)

You deal more damage with a specific category of weaponry.

**Prerequisites:** Weapon Focus with the selected weapon group, Fighter level 4th.

**Benefit:** Choose a weapon group for which you have the Weapon Focus feat. You gain a +2 bonus on all damage rolls you make using weapons from the selected weapon group.

**Special:** You can gain this feat multiple times. Each time you take the feat, it applies to a new weapon group.

**EITR Note:** This feat now applies to entire **Weapon Groups** instead of individual weapons.
