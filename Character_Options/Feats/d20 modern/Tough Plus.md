# Tough Plus

Advancements in science coupled with your drive to excel enable you to progress faster toward becoming a truly tough hero.

**Prerequisite**: NOTE: Unlike other "Hero Plus" feats, there are enough talents without other talents as requirements and, thus, does not require any ranks in the Tough Hero class for the first two times it is selected. Any further selections after the first two would require at least one level in the class.

**Benefit**: Character gains two talents from the Tough hero’s talent trees. The talents must be selected from the following list, a talent cannot be selected from more than one talent talent tree, and the character must meet all the prerequisites of a talent to select it. 

Damage Reduction Talent Tree: Damage Reduction 2/—, Damage Reduction 3/—, 

Energy Resistance Talent Tree: Energy Resistance (choose one energy type: Acid Resistance, Cold Resistance, Electricity Resistance, Fire Resistance, Sonic/Concussion Resistance)
 
Unbreakable Talent Tree: Remain Conscious, Second Wind, or Stamina.

**Special**: This feat may be selected multiple times. Each time it is selected, a different pair of talents must be choses than the previous times.
