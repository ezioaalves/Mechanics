# Smart Plus

Advancements in science coupled with your drive to excel enable you to progress faster toward becoming a truly intelligent hero.

**Prerequisite**: By the letter of the rules, a character must have at least one level in the Smart Hero Class to take this feat.

**Benefit**: Character gains two talents from the Smart hero’s talent trees. The talents must be selected from the following list, a talent cannot be selected from more than one talent talent tree, and the character must meet all the prerequisites of a talent to select it. 

Research Talent Tree: Savant*, Linguist

Strategy Talent Tree: Exploit Weakness, Plan, or Trick.

**Special**: This feat may be selected multiple times. Each time it is selected, a different pair of talents must be choses than the previous times; the exception is the Savant talent, which may be selected multiple times. However, each time it is selected, a different skill must be selected (see the savant talent’s description).
