# Strafe

You can use an autofire weapon to effect a wider, narrower area.

**Prerequisite**: Personal Firearms Proficiency and Advanced Firearms Proficiency.

**Benefit**: When using a firearm on autofire, the character can affect any four squares in a straight line(that is, an area four 5-foot squares long and one square wide ).

**Normal**: A firearm on autofire normally affects a 10-foot-by-10-foot area.
