# Unarmed Combatant (Combat)

You are skilled at grappling and fighting while unarmed.

**Benefit:** You are considered to be armed even when unarmed—you do not provoke attacks of opportunity when you attack foes while unarmed. Your unarmed strikes deal 1d6 damage (Medium) or 1d4 (Small), and can deal lethal or nonlethal damage at your choice.

Additionally, you do not provoke an attack of opportunity when performing a **Grapple** combat maneuver. You receive a +2 bonus on checks made to grapple a foe and a +2 bonus to CMD against grapple attempts.

**Normal:** Attacking unarmed or starting a grapple provokes an attack of opportunity.
