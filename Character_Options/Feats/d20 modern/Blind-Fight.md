# Blind-Fight

You know how to fight in melee combat without being able to see your foes.

**Benefit**: In melee combat, every time the character misses because of concealment, the character can reroll the miss chance roll one time to see if the character actually hits. 

The character takes only half the usual penalty to speed for being unable to see. Darkness and poor visibility in general reduces the character's speed to three-quarters of normal.

**Normal**: Darkness and poor visibility in general reduces the character's speed to half of normal. See Movement in Darkness.
