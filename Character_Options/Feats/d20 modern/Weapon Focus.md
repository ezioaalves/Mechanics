# Weapon Focus (Combat)

You are exceptionally skilled with a specific category of weaponry.

**Prerequisites:** Base attack bonus +1, Proficient with at least one weapon in the group.

**Benefit:** Choose a weapon group (e.g., Light Blades, Heavy Blades, Thrown, etc.). You gain a +1 bonus on all attack rolls you make using weapons from the selected weapon group.

**Special:** You can gain this feat multiple times. Each time you take the feat, it applies to a new weapon group.

**EITR Note:** This feat now applies to entire **Weapon Groups** instead of individual weapons.
