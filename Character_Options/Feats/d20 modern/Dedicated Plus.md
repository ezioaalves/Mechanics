# Dedicated Plus

Advancements in science coupled with your drive to excel enable you to progress faster toward becoming a truly Dedicated Hero.

**Prerequisite**: By the letter of the rules, a character must have at least three levels in the Dedicated Hero Class to take this feat.

**Benefit**: Character gains two talents from the Dedicated hero’s talent trees. The talents must be selected from the following list, a talent cannot be selected from more than one talent talent tree, and the character must meet all the prerequisites of a talent to select it. 

Empathic Talent Tree: Improved Aid Another or Intuition. 

Healing Talent Tree: Healing Touch 1 or Healing Touch 2. 
 
Insightful Talent Tree: Aware, Faith, or Cool Under Pressure.

**Special**: This feat may be selected multiple times. Each time it is selected, a different pair of talents must be choses than the previous times.
