# Agile Riposte

You have learned to strike when your opponent is most vulnerable... at the instant your opponent strikes you.

**Prerequisite**: Base attack bonus 11, Dexterity 13, Dodge

**Benefit**: Once per round, if the opponent the character has designated as his or her dodge target (see the Dodge feat) makes a melee attack or melee touch attack against the character and misses, the character may make an attack of opportunity against that opponent. Resolve and apply the effects from both attacks simultaneously. Even a character with the Combat Reflexes feat can't use the Agile Riposte feat more than once per round. This feat does not grant more attacks of opportunity than the character is normally allowed in a round.
