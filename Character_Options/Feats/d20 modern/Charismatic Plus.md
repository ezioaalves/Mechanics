# Charismatic Plus

Advancements in science coupled with your drive to excel enable you to progress faster toward becoming a truly charismatic hero.

**Prerequisite**: By the letter of the rules, a character must have at least three ranks in the Fast Hero Class to take this feat.

**Benefit**: Character gains two talents from the Charismatic hero’s talent trees. The talents must be selected from the following list, a talent cannot be selected from more than one talent talent tree, and the character must meet all the prerequisites of a talent to select it. 

**Choose one from the following trees:**

Charm Talent Tree: Favor or Captivate

Fast-Talk Talent Tree: Dazzle or Taunt.
 
Leadership Talent Tree: Inspiration or Greater Inspiration

**Special**: This feat may be selected multiple times. Each time it is selected, a different pair of talents must be choses than the previous times.
