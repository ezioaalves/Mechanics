# Shot on the Run

You are highly trained in skirmish ranged weapons tactics.

**Prerequisite**: Dexterity 13, Dodge, Dodge, and Precise Shot.

**Benefit**: When using an attack action with a ranged weapon, the character can move both before and after the attack, provided that the character's total distance moved is not greater than his or her base movement speed. Moving in this way does not provoke an attack of opportunity from the defender the character is attacking (though it can provoke attacks of opportunity from others, as normal).
