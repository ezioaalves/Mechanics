# Strong Plus

Advancements in science coupled with your drive to excel enable you to progress faster toward becoming a truly Strong hero.

**Prerequisite**: By the letter of the rules, a character must have at least three ranks in the Strong Hero Class to take this feat.

**Benefit**: Character gains two talents from the Strong hero’s talent trees. The talents must be selected from the following list, a talent cannot be selected from more than one talent talent tree, and the character must meet all the prerequisites of a talent to select it. 

Extreme Effort Talent Tree: Improved Extreme Effort or Advanced Extreme Effort. 

Ignore Hardness Talent Tree: Improved Ignore Hardness or Advanced Ignore Hardness. 
 
Melee Smash Talent Tree: Improved Melee Smash or Advanced Melee Smash.

**Special**: This feat may be selected multiple times. Each time it is selected, a different pair of talents must be choses than the previous times.
