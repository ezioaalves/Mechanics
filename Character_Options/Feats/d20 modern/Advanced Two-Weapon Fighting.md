# Advanced Two-Weapon Fighting

You are a master of fighting with a weapon in each hand. Unlike the two-weapon fighting feat, this feat allows you to use mixed weapon types.

**Prerequisite**: Base attack bonus 11, Dexterity 13, Two-Weapon Fighting, Greater Two-Weapon Fighting

**Benefit**: The character gets a third attack with their offhand weapon, albeit at a -10 penalty. This feat also allows the character to use a melee weapon in one hand and a ranged weapon in the other.

**Normal**: See Attacking with Two Weapons for normal penalties.
