# Armor Proficiency (Heavy)

You are proficient with heavy armor.

**Prerequisite**: Armor Proficiency (Light), Armor Proficiency (Medium).

**Benefit**: When the character wears a type of armor with which the character is proficient, the character gets to add the armor's equipment bonus to his or her Defense. Also, the armor check penalty only applies to the following skills: Balance, Climb, Escape Artist, Hide, Jump, Move Silently, and Tumble.

**Normal**: A character who wears armor with which he or she is not proficient adds only the armor's nonproficient equipment bonus to her Defense. Also, she suffers its armor check penalty on attack rolls and on all skill checks that involve moving.
