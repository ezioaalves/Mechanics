# Reactive Shooter

You react instantly with a firearm.

**Benefit**: If you have a Medium-size or smaller ranged weapon in hand. you may act in the surprise round even if you are surprised. You roll initiative as though you were not surprised. The only action you may take in the surprise round, however, Is a single ranged attack with the weapon in your hand. You make this attack at your highest attack bonus, but you suffer an —5 penalty on the attack. You are still considered flat-footed during the surprise round.

**Normal**: If you are surprised at the beginning of combat, you cannot take any actions during the surprise round.
