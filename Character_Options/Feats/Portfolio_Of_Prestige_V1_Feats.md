# Portfolio of Prestige Volume I Feats

## Ace Assistant
**Prerequisite:** Craft 6 ranks, Master at work class feature, any item creation feat.
**Benefit:** You gain the ability to train one assistant to aid you in your work, a process which requires 5 days of harduous training. When working alongside the assistant, you can confer the benefits of any item creation feat you selected to that assistant as though they had taken the feat themselves as long as your assistant is working closeby. You may change which feat you confer the benefits of once per week. You may only have one assistant at a time, but you may train a new assistant by spending 5 days of training.

## Big Bully
**Prerequisite:** Bully class feature, sneak attack +1d6 class feature.
**Benefit:** You can sacrifice one sneak attack dice when making a sneak attack to attempt to Intimidate the creature. You must successfully hit the creature to do so, and make the check as a free action.

## Choke Hold
**Prerequisite:** Combat Martial Arts, Combat Throw, Deadly Hold +1d6 class feature.
**Benefit:** You have mastered the art of the choke hold. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to Grapple checks to strangle an opponent and the difficulty of the opponent's Constitution checks to avoid suffocating increases by 2.

## Improved Ace Assistant
**Prerequisite:** Craft 15 ranks, Master at work class feature, Specialty class feature, any 3 item creation feats.
**Benefit:** When you are able to confer a trained assistant the benefit of an item creation feat, you can confer the benefit of your chosen Master Artisan's specialty class feature as well.

## Intuitive Discovery
**Prerequisite:** Research 9 ranks, Research Technique class feature
**Benefit:** You can apply the bonus of the Research Technique class feature to skill checks made to identify techniques being performed.

## Maniacal Defense
**Prerequisite:** Great Fortitude, Rage class feature, base attack bonus +11.
**Benefit:** While wearing no armor and using no speed ranks, you gain your Constitution bonus as a bonus to Defense. Any condition that would cause you to lose your Dexterity bonus to Defense also denies you this bonus, and any condition that would improve your Constitution bonus also improves your Defense.

## Rampage
**Prerequisite:** Rage or frenzy class feature.
**Benefit:** While you are in a rage or frenzy, you gain a +4 bonus to any Strength check made to break unattended objects. This bonus increases to +6 if the check is made as part of a charge.
