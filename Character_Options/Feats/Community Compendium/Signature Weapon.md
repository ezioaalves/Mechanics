# Signature Weapon

You are so adept at using a particular model of a weapon that it's almost a part of you.

**Prerequisite**: Weapon Focus, Weapon Specialization.

**Benefit**: Choose a specific model of weapon like a weapon made of a unique material. When you're wielding that weapon, you gain a +2 bonus to attack and a +1 bonus to damage. This stacks with Weapon Focus and Weapon Specialization for the same weapons.
