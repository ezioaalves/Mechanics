# Chakra Regeneration

Your chakra fuels your body's healing.

**Benefit**: Your body uses chakra to heal itself. When resting to regain hit points, you may heal double your hit points but your chakra pool and chakra reserve recovers only half (round down) their normal amount.

**Normal**: You heal your hit points at a rate of character level + Con modifier per whole night of rest and recover your entire chakra pool with a night's rest.

**Special**: If you possess the Light Sleeper feat and use it, you may recover a normal full night's rest but only recover one quarter (rounded down) of your chakra pool and chakra reserve.
