## ADVANCED NATURE FEATS 

Advanced nature s are the combination of two chakra natures that result in a new nature, such as Water (Suiton) and Wind (Fuuton) creating Ice (Hyouton) . In order to take an Advanced Nature feat, you must possess one of the two comprising elements as your Nature Affinity, also these feats may only be taken at 1 st level though in incredibly rare cir cumstances a player might acquire something new, sometimes natures are seen as part of a bloodline but other times can be bestowed from other sources or simply by happenstance .

### Features of Advanced Natures 

A character with access to advanced natures are able to not only learn the two -comprising nature’s ninjutsu techniques but they are also able to learn the new nature of the two. As well the new element contains a new descriptor related to its name for purposes of Resistance and Damage Reduction, so that Bakuton (Explosion Release) has a descriptor of Explosion, just as Hyouton (Ice Release) has a descriptor of Cold.

## EMPOWERED NATURE FEATS 

A special group of natures that are the pinnacle of their source natures, empowered natures are the raw fury of their respective element. The five primary elements provide the source of power for empowered natures. 

### Obtaining an Empower Nature 

Typically, a character is born with this or is granted it via a bloodline it possesses and the user either obtains it naturally or must do som ething to trigger it. There may yet be undocumented methods of obtaining these abilities. GMs are encouraged to experiment and allow players to obtain these outside of level 1 to further expand growth options. 

### Benefits of Empowered Natures 

Having an empowered nature grants the user several advantages over their primary nature. As noted below each of these now applies to the user’s Empowered Nature. The character must pay per instance of using these benefits. 

The user’s techniques that deal damage now deal True Damage which mitigates the source nature’s elemental resistance and immunity , for example a Enton (Blaze) technique would bypass all fire resistance and fire immunity. The technique's perform requirements increase by 3. T he chakra cost is increa sed by 2 plus 1 every 3 ranks (round up). 

The user’s techniques which deal damage ignore hardness, unless the technique is used against another Empower Nature technique that creates something with a hardness value. Then the role of Nature Weakness plays a role, if the user’s nature is strong against the target’s technique that has hardness it ignores half, if it is weak, treat the hardness as double, if it is neither the attack functions normally and hardness subtracts from the total damage. 

Descriptors 

The descriptors for Empowered Nature techniques are as (Enton) [Fire], ( Purazumaton ) [Lightning], (Koton) [Earth], (Namiton) [Water], and (Taiton) [Wind]. 

# ENTON 

This advanced nature is also known as Blaze Release .

Prerequisite: Primary elemental affinity Fire, can only be taken at 1 st level. 

Benefit: The character gains access to the Empowered Nature Enton.

Benefit: The character gains access to the Empowered Nature Purazumaton .

Prerequisite: Primary elemental affinity Wind, can only be taken at 1 st level. 

Benefit: The character gains access to the Empowered Nature Taiton.
