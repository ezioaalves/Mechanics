# Chau Murky

You are skilled in the subtle measures used to dispose of rivals and enemies, and have few qualms against using hired killers and assassins.

**Prerequisite**: Poison Use.

**Benefit**: You gain a +2 competence bonus on Craft checks to prepare and identify poisons, and a +2 competence bonus to Treat Injury checks to treat poison. Additionally, if you are ever poisoned because of a botched roll, you may spend an action point to re-roll the failure.
