# Improved Dodge

You have become more accustomed to evading the attacks of your enemies.

**Prerequisite**: Dodge, Dex 15.

**Benefit**: You may apply the effects of the dodge feat to two targets simultaneously, or apply its effects to a single target twice (the effects stack).

A target that you have applied dodge to twice is still counted as having only one application for the purposes of feats and abilities such as Agile Riposte.

**Normal**: A character with the dodge feat can gain a +1 dodge bonus to defense against a single enemy.
