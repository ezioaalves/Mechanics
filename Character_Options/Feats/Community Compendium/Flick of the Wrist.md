# Flick of the Wrist

With a single motion, you can draw a light weapon and make a devastating attack.

**Prerequisite**: Dex 16, Sleight of Hand 5 ranks, Quick Draw.

**Benefit**: If you draw a light weapon and make an attack with it in the same round, you catch your opponent flat-footed (for the purpose of this attack only). You may use this feat only once per round and once per opponent during any single combat encounter.
