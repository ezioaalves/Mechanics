# Bloodline's Chosen

**Prerequisite**: Can only be taken at the 1st level, Advanced Bloodline.

**Benefit**: There is a level that not even a genius can reach and that is being chosen by fate. Upon selecting this feat, the potency of the chosen bloodline is decreased by one category. In example, a Major bloodline becomes an intermediate bloodline for the sake of determining the number of levels that must be taken to advance the bloodline.
