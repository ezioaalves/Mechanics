# Inventor

You are able to design and create items of exceptional quality and can do so with ease and confidence.

**Prerequisite**: Master Crafter.

**Benefit**: When using the Master Crafter feat, you are only required to pay 1/3 the ordinary item's cost in XP.

**Normal**: A character without this feat must pay 1/2 the ordinary item's cost in XP to make the mastercraft item.
