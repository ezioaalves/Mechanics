# Armor Focus

Trained with one type of armor until you are an expert, even in combat conditions — where it counts the most.

**Prerequisite**: Armor Proficiency (light or medium).

**Benefit**: Choose one type of armor that you are proficient with (battle armor, assault vest, etc.). While wearing that type of armor, your speed is reduced by 5 ft. less than usual (a speed penalty of -10 ft. becomes -5 ft., a speed penalty of -5 ft. is negated, etc.). Further, your armor check penalty while wearing the chosen type of armor is reduced to -1.

**Special**: This feat may be taken multiple times. Each time it is taken, you must specify a new type of armor.
