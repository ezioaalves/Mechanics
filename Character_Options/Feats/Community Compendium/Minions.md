# Minions

You have access to guards, thugs, or other subordinates who follow your orders.

**Prerequisite**: 6th level.

**Benefit**: You attract loyal minions, according to the following table. Note that you may take Leadership or Vile Leadership alongside this feat as this feat does not provide a Cohort, but the minions you take must be of a similar or the same alignment.

**Leader Score**: Your leader score is equal to the sum of your character level, your Reputation, and your Charisma modifier, modified by the factors listed below and any others your GM wishes to apply.

| Leader Attribute | Modifier |
|---|---|
| Great renown | +2 |
| Considered fair and generous | +1 |
| Displays special power | +1 |
| Has had well-publicized failure | -1 |
| Considered aloof or cruel | -1 |
| Known for unsurpassed megalomania | +3 |
| Has a secret base | +2 |
| Moves around a lot | -1 |
| Provides uniforms | +1 |

Minions are generally loyal, but aren't fanatical and won't do anything obviously suicidal. Minions who die or leave the character are replaced with new recruits within a month's time.
