**Prerequisite**: Primary elemental affinity Lightning or Water, can only be taken at 1st level.

**Benefit**: You gain an elemental affinity to Storm as well as Lightning or Water (whichever you didn't select as your primary elemental affinity). Your Storm affinity may replace your primary elemental affinity, which is then downgraded to a normal affinity. You do not gain a new nature until level 21 via level progression. This release has the descriptor of Storm.
