**Prerequisite**: Primary elemental affinity Earth or Lightning, can only be taken at 1st level.

**Benefit**: You gain an elemental affinity to Magnet as well as Earth or Lightning (whichever you didn't select as your primary elemental affinity). Your Magnet affinity may replace your primary elemental affinity, which is then downgraded to a normal affinity. You do not gain a new nature until level 21 via level progression. This release has the descriptor of Magnet.
