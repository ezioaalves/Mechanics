**Prerequisite**: Primary elemental affinity Earth or Water, can only be taken at 1st level.

**Benefit**: You gain an elemental affinity to Wood as well as Earth or Water (whichever you didn't select as your primary elemental affinity). Your Wood affinity may replace your primary elemental affinity, which is then downgraded to a normal affinity. You do not gain a new nature until level 21 via level progression. This release has the descriptor of Wood.
