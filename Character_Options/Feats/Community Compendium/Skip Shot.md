# Skip Shot

**Prerequisite**: Precise Shot, Precise Shot.

**Benefit**: If you have a solid, relatively smooth surface on which to skip a one-handed projectile (kunai, senbon, and shuriken), such as a concrete wall, and a target within 10 ft. of that surface, you may ignore cover between you and the target. However, you suffer a -2 penalty to your attack, and your attack deals -1 die of damage or in the case of a 1 die attack it does half damage (minimum of 1). Each time it ricochets it takes another -2 to the attack roll and half again damage. If the damage was 1 on the last attack, the projectile cannot ricochet any further.

**Special**: The surface does not have to be perfectly smooth and level — a brick wall or an asphalt road can be used. The target must be at least partially visible or you must have some other method of perceiving them.
