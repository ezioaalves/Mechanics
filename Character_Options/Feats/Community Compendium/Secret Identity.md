# Secret Identity

You have two identities.

**Benefit**: You have two Reputation scores, one for each identity. One identity (you choose which one) takes the Reputation score you've earned subsequent to taking this feat. The other identity has a Reputation score of 0. Most people won't connect your two identities unless confronted with incontrovertible evidence that you're one person with two personae. If you perform actions while under the identity of the second one (the one with the score of 0) you will assume any Reputation gain or loss due to you based on your actions. The same applies to your primary identity.

**Special**: If your public persona adopts a distinctive style of dress or moniker, increase your Reputation score by 2. You only get the Reputation increase once, even if you change your public persona's costume or nickname.
