# Champuru

Champuru consists of highly unorthodox swordplay and a fighting style based on breakdancing. This form takes bits and pieces from all schools of martial arts and relies on the user to fill up the rest as he fights his opponents, showing an opponent's movements that contradict their expectations.

**Prerequisite**: Taijutsu and Tumble 4 ranks.

**Benefit**: You can learn Champuru techniques without penalties to your learn check. In addition, the character is able to trip opponents with any medium weapon and does not provoke attacks of opportunity when tripping while unarmed.

**Normal**: A character trying to learn a technique with the Champuru style takes a -4 penalty to his Learn check if he has not taken this feat, and normal rules for tripping apply (see Basics chapter for details).
