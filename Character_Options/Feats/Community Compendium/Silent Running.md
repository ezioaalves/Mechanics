# Silent Running

This feat lets you run silently with ease.

**Prerequisite**: Character level 6th, Dexterity 12, Move Silently 10 ranks, Hide 10 ranks, Stealthy.

**Benefit**: You suffer no penalty for moving at your base speed when making Move Silently skill checks. In addition, you suffer only a -10 penalty for running or charging when making Move Silently skill checks.

**Normal**: You incur a -20 penalty for running and charging. You incur a -5 penalty for moving at your base speed.

**Special**: Attacking while moving silently still incurs a -20 penalty.
