# Combat Awareness

Your battle sense helps minimize the threat of flanking attacks.

**Prerequisite**: Wis 13, base attack bonus +1 or higher.

**Benefit**: Attackers do not gain the usual +2 bonus on their attack rolls when flanking you. This feat grants no effect when you are denied your Dexterity modifier to Defense, such as when you are flat-footed. You may still be sneak attacked when flanked.
