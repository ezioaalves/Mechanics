# Iron Stomach [Monstrous]

Even if your food struggles on the way down, there's little fear of it piercing your tough insides.

**Prerequisite**: Con 15, Swallow Whole.

**Benefit**: You apply your full natural armor to your AC for those attempting to cut themselves out after being swallowed whole.

Example: A creature that has natural armor +15 has an AC of 10 + 15 = 25 for those attempting to cut out of its stomach.

**Normal**: The AC for those who have been swallowed whole is 10 + half your natural armor. The example creature normally only has AC 17 (10 + 7 natural) inside its stomach.
