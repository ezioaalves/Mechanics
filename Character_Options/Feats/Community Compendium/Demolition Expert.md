# Demolition Expert [Meta-Chakra]

You are able to charge exploding tags to deal additional damage and hit a larger area with the explosion.

**Prerequisite**: Chakra Control 9 ranks, any other Meta-Chakra feat.

**Benefit**: The character is able to empower an exploding tag with extra power. To do so, he must spend 2 additional points of chakra per damage dice when charging the tag. When the tag explodes, it will deal 50% extra damage and the blast radius will be increased by 10 feet. Every use expends a meta-chakra charge. The DC to detonate said tag also increases by 5.
