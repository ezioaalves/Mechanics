# Agile Riposte

You know how to strike when your opponent is most vulnerable.

**Prerequisite**: Dex 13, Dodge.

**Benefit**: Once per round, if the opponent you have designated as your dodge target makes a melee attack or melee touch attack against you and misses, you may make an attack of opportunity against that opponent. Resolve and apply the effects from both attacks simultaneously.

Even with Combat Reflexes, Agile Riposte can't be used more than once per round. It does not grant more attacks of opportunity than the character is normally allowed in a round.
