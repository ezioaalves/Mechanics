# Hyper Fighting

**Prerequisite**: Advanced Combat Martial Arts, Weapon Focus (Unarmed), [Universal: Defensive Stance].

**Benefit**: You are a blur of punches and kicks! Every strike is so fast that opponents soon lose the ability to keep up. At the cost of an Action Point, all unarmed attacks made by the user for 1 round will have a 50% chance to deny the target their dexterity to defense. In addition, when using the full attack action, the user may make an additional attack at their highest attack bonus. All attacks made during this round suffer a -4 penalty.
