# Improved Heat Endurance

You can survive even in the most extreme natural heat conditions.

**Prerequisite**: Base Fortitude save +6, Heat Endurance.

**Benefit**: You gain fire resistance 5. If you already have fire resistance, it increases by 5. You can exist comfortably in high temperatures without having to make Fortitude saves (see Heat Dangers). Your protection against heat is level 3 (see Protection against Heat).
