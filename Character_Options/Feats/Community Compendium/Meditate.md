# Meditate

You can gather your mind to become more accurate and focused.

**Prerequisite**: Int 13, Wis 13, Focused.

**Benefit**: You can pay 1 action point to meditate for 2 rounds; this action allows attacks of opportunity because you need to stay in one spot for a certain amount of time. If you are distracted by a non-visual distraction like a hit or a noise, you must make a Concentration check (DC distraction specific) or lose focus and fail. If the check succeeds, the action continues. If the action succeeds, you gain +2 on Defense, +3 on all attack rolls, +1 on all unarmed damage rolls, deal lethal damage on unarmed attacks, and get a +1 Concentration bonus on all skills for 2d6 rounds.

**Special**: The following classes add Meditate to their Bonus Feat lists: Tough Hero, Smart Hero, Dedicated Hero, Strong Hero, Fast Hero, and Charismatic Hero.
