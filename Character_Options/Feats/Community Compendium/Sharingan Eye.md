# Sharingan Eye [Heritage]

**Prerequisite**: Uchiha Clan Occupation, Affinity Fire. Can only be taken at 1st level; may not be chosen if the user has already selected the Advanced Bloodline feat.

**Benefit**: Always spoken of as a cursed bloodline, those that carry the Uchiha blood grow ever rarer and those that can manifest the Sharingan Eye are even more so. Those that simply carry the trait, while not as dangerous as their full-blooded relatives, have found greater proficiency with Doujutsu techniques. The skill threshold of techniques with the Doujutsu descriptor are decreased by 2 and the DCs of those techniques are increased by 1.
