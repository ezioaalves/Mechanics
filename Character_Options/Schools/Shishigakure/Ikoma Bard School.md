# Ikoma Bard School

*The Ikoma are the historians and storytellers of the Lion, but their shinobi know that words can be weapons. They are masters of propaganda, morale, and psychological warfare, using their knowledge of history and their powerful voices to inspire their allies and demoralize their enemies.*

---

**Feat:** Creative

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **The Ancestor's Voice (Esagashi no Jutsu):** You can subtly weave hidden messages or verses into your speech, detectable only by those who know what to listen for.
*   **A Hero's Tale (Shougenzou no Jutsu):** You can create minor, yet inspiring or terrifying, illusions to accompany your stories and speeches, giving them a tangible presence.
*   **Rallying Cry (Lightning Raid):** You can let out a sudden, inspiring shout at the start of a conflict, spurring yourself and your allies to act faster.

**Starting Outfit:** Traveling clothes, wakizashi (short sword), yari (spear) or tessen (war fan), yumi (bow), quiver of arrows, traveling pack, any one musical instrument, journal.
