# Kitsu Medium School

*The Kitsu are the spiritual heart of the Lion Clan, shinobi with the rare ability to commune with the spirits of their ancestors. They are priests and mediums, calling upon the wisdom and power of the honored dead to guide and protect the living.*

---

**Feat:** Keen Sight

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Commune with the Fallen (Ancestry Unearthed):** You can enter a trance to ask questions of a target's ancestors, though the answers may be cryptic. *(This is a custom Genjutsu representing the Kitsu's unique abilities)*.
*   **Glimpse of the Other Side (Youso Fuuin no Jutsu):** You can create a seal that interacts with elemental spirits, a foundational technique for your spiritual duties.
*   **Hallowed Ground (Cleansing Rite):** You can perform a ritual to purify an area or a person from spiritual taint, making it safe for communion.

**Starting Outfit:** Sanctified robes, traveling clothes, wakizashi (short sword), knife, bō (staff), scroll satchel, traveling pack.
