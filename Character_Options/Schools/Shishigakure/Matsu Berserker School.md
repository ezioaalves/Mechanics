# Matsu Berserker School

*The Matsu are the ferocious heart of the Lion Clan, shinobi who throw themselves into battle with a passion and fury that terrifies their foes. They are berserkers, valuing bravery and aggression above all else, seeking to overwhelm their enemies with pure, untamed ferocity.*

---

**Feat:** Cleave

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Matsu's Fury (Taijutsu: Gouken - Konoha Reppuu):** You deliver a powerful, spinning kick, a signature move of the Matsu's aggressive and physical style.
*   **Unstoppable Charge (Chouyaku no Jutsu):** You can leap great distances, allowing you to close with the enemy in an instant and begin your assault.
*   **Ignore the Pain (Iryou Ninjutsu: Ryoji - Kekki):** Your fighting spirit is so strong that you can temporarily invigorate your body, granting yourself a burst of vitality.

**Starting Outfit:** Ashigaru armor, traveling clothes, daishō (katana and wakizashi), nodachi (greatsword) or tessen (war fan), knife, yumi (bow), quiver of arrows, traveling pack.
