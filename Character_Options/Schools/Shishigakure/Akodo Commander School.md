# Akodo Commander School

*The Akodo are the master strategists and tacticians of the Lion Clan. Their shinobi are not just warriors; they are leaders, trained in battlefield command and inspiring loyalty through their presence. They lead from the front, their every action calculated to achieve victory for the clan.*

---

**Feat:** Weapon Focus

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **The General's Roar (Ikaku no Jutsu):** You emit a powerful shout that instills fear and doubt in your enemies.
*   **For the Clan! (Dazzling Performance):** You perform a display of martial prowess or a stirring speech that captivates allies and enemies alike, making them more susceptible to your influence.
*   **Unyielding Assault (Taijutsu: Gouken):** You are trained in the Lion's signature direct and powerful fighting style, focused on overwhelming the enemy with pure force.

**Starting Outfit:** Ashigaru armor, traveling clothes, daishō (katana and wakizashi), yari (spear), any one weapon of rarity 6 or lower, two knives, yumi (bow), quiver of arrows, traveling pack.
