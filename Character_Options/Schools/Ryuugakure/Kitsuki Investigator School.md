# Kitsuki Investigator School

*Where others see lies, the Kitsuki see only the truth. Shinobi of this school are trained as peerless investigators, honing their perception to an almost supernatural degree. They are methodical and patient, using their unique insight to unravel conspiracies, solve mysteries, and expose any deception.*

---

**Feat:** Attentive

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Eye for Deceit (Weight of Duty):** You can subtly probe a target's mind to learn one of their major fears, a powerful tool for interrogation and reading intent.
*   **To Pierce the Veil (Genjutsu Kai):** Your sharp mind is trained to recognize and dispel illusions, allowing you to see through deceptions that would fool others.
*   **Unveiling Strike (Kenjutsu: Shamen-uchi):** Your kenjutsu is precise and analytical, designed to methodically dismantle an opponent's defense.

**Starting Outfit:** Traveling clothes, wakizashi (short sword), knife, calligraphy set, bō (staff) or jian (straight-edged sword), traveling pack, journal of observations.
