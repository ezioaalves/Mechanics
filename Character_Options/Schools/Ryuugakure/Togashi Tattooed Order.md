# Togashi Tattooed Order

*The Togashi are an order of ascetic monks who treat the body as a canvas for their chakra. Their shinobi channel power through the intricate tattoos that cover their skin, granting them supernatural physical abilities. They are enigmatic and introspective, seeking enlightenment through physical trial and spiritual discipline.*

---

**Feat:** Craft Sealed Item

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Fist of Stone (Ishi no Teashi):** You can harden your limbs to the consistency of stone, granting you powerful unarmed strikes and the ability to shatter objects.
*   **Voice of the Spirit (Gyoukou):** Through meditation and focus, you can call upon small moments of good fortune, subtly guided by the spirits of the world.
*   **Endure the World (Kawa no Yoroi):** Your skin becomes as tough as leather, granting you a natural armor against physical attacks.

**Starting Outfit:** Common clothing, bō (staff), traveling pack.
