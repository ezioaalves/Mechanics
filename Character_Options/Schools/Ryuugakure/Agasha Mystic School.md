# Agasha Mystic School

*The Agasha are the mystics of the Dragon Clan, shinobi who seek to understand the world not through pure elemental fury, but by communing with the spirits of the land itself. They are alchemists and ritualists, blending elemental ninjutsu with a deep understanding of the natural and spiritual worlds.*

---

**Feat:** Widen Technique

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Fist of the Elements:** Choose one elemental technique to master: `Hiakahou` (Blazing Red Cannon), `Tsuchi no Jutsu` (Earth Mallet Technique), or `Mizudama no Jutsu` (Water Sphere Technique).
*   **Spirit's Insight (Doku Hakken no Jutsu):** By attuning yourself to the spirits of nature, you can detect poisons and impurities in substances or creatures.
*   **Purifying Prayer (Cleansing Rite):** You perform a ritual to cleanse a person or object of spiritual corruption or taint.

**Starting Outfit:** Sanctified robes, wakizashi (short sword), bō (staff), knife, traveling pack, scroll satchel, set of glass vials, journal.
