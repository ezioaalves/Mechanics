# Mirumoto Two-Heavens Adept School

*The Mirumoto follow the unconventional path of Niten, the two-heavens style, wielding both katana and wakizashi in a whirlwind of steel. Their shinobi are adaptable and unpredictable warriors, trained to overwhelm their opponents with a fluid, continuous offense that leaves no openings.*

---

**Feat:** Two-Weapon Fighting

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Flowing Blades Dance:** You receive the **Two-Weapon Fighting** feat as your primary school technique.
*   **Wind and Water Strike (Kenjutsu: Agari-uchi & Kudari-uchi):** You master the fundamental upward and downward cuts, the building blocks of the Mirumoto's relentless assault.
*   **Roar of the Dragon (Ikaku no Jutsu):** You channel your fighting spirit into a powerful kiai, intimidating your foes and shaking their resolve.

**Starting Outfit:** Traveling clothes, ceremonial clothes, ashigaru armor, daishō (katana and wakizashi) or wakizashi (short sword) and any two weapons of rarity 6 or lower, traveling pack.
