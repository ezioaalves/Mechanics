# Yogo Wardmaster School

*The Yogo are the wardmasters of the Scorpion, tasked with protecting the clan's secrets through the art of Fuinjutsu. Their shinobi are experts in creating and breaking seals, laying traps, and binding spirits. They are the guardians of the Scorpion's darkest lore, and their seals are said to be unbreakable.*

---

**Feat:** Sealwaver

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Seal Against the Darkness (Cleansing Rite):** You can perform a fuinjutsu ritual to cleanse a person or object of spiritual corruption.
*   **Warding Glyph (Seal of Alertness):** You can place an invisible seal on an object or threshold that sends you a silent mental alert when it is crossed by an unauthorized person.
*   **Binding the Spirit (Youso Fuuin no Jutsu):** You can create a seal to bind a minor elemental spirit, a foundational technique for more advanced fuinjutsu.

**Starting Outfit:** Sanctified robes, concealed armor, wakizashi (short sword), bō (staff) or knife, three shuriken (throwing stars), calligraphy set, scroll satchel, traveling pack.
