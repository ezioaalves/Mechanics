# Soshi Illusionist School

*The Soshi are the shugenja-nin of the Scorpion, but their communion with the spirits is a dark and twisted one. They are masters of genjutsu, specializing in illusions of terror, pain, and madness. They bring their enemies' worst nightmares to life, breaking their minds and spirits long before their bodies.*

---

**Feat:** Genjutsu Adept

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Deceit of the Senses (Magen: Jigoku Tenshi no Jutsu):** You can make a target believe they are on fire, drowning, or being crushed, causing them to react to an illusionary element.
*   **Waking Dream (Shougenzou no Jutsu):** You can create a minor, static illusion, perfect for altering a document, creating a false wall, or hiding an object.
*   **Invitation to Madness (Shinheki no Jutsu):** You can directly assault a target's mind, causing a painful mental shock that can leave them reeling.

**Starting Outfit:** Sanctified robes, inconspicuous garb, wakizashi (short sword), knife, scroll satchel, traveling pack.
