# Shosuro Infiltrator School

*The Shosuro are the actors and spies of the Scorpion Clan. Their shinobi are masters of disguise, infiltration, and assassination. They can become anyone, go anywhere, and eliminate any target without leaving a trace. They are the unseen daggers that protect the heart of the clan.*

---

**Feat:** Death Blow (3.5)

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Silent Step, Hidden Blade (Kakureimino no Jutsu):** You can cloak yourself in shadows, becoming nearly invisible as long as you remain still.
*   **A Fleeting Shadow (Sensational Distraction):** You create a captivating illusion, such as a shower of petals or a beautiful song, to fascinate a target and slip by unnoticed.
*   **Poisonous Kiss (Fukumihari):** You can spit a volley of poisoned needles, a classic tool for a silent and deadly assassination.

**Starting Outfit:** Ceremonial clothes, common clothes, traveling clothes, daishō (katana and wakizashi), knife, yumi (bow), quiver of arrows, traveling pack.
