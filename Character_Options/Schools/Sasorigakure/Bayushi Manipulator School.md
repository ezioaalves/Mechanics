# Bayushi Manipulator School

*The Bayushi are the masters of lies, deceit, and manipulation, serving the Scorpion by turning their enemies against each other. Shinobi of this school are masters of the court, using blackmail, seduction, and poison in their words to achieve the clan's goals. They know that a well-placed lie is deadlier than any blade.*

---

**Feat:** Force of Personality

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Whispers in the Dark (Onwaku no Jutsu):** You can create illusory sounds, perfect for planting seeds of doubt or creating a distraction.
*   **A Mask for the Soul (Henge no Jutsu):** You can alter your appearance, allowing you to move through society as someone else entirely.
*   **The Perfect Lie (Well of Desire):** You can subtly influence a target's mind, making them far more susceptible to any bribe you offer.

**Starting Outfit:** Ceremonial clothes, common clothes, traveling clothes, wakizashi (short sword), any one weapon of rarity 6 or lower, calligraphy set, traveling pack.
