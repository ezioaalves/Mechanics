# Hida Taijutsu School

**Focus:** Defensive Taijutsu & Heavy Armor

Producing the most stalwart protectors of the Wall, the Hida Defender School teaches its disciples fortitude, courage, and practicality in equal measure. Their duty is to endure against all threats, honorable or not. From their heavily plated armor to their techniques for shattering carapaces with mighty hammer blows, Hida Defenders have developed a fighting style that gives them the utmost advantage and greatest chance of not just victory, but survival.

---

**Feat:** Armor Focus

**Starting Techniques:** 
*   Ishimaru Ryu: Shodan Dachi (Ishimaru Style: First Stance)
*   Taijutsu: Gouken (Hand-to-Hand: Iron Fist)
*   Toujun no Jutsu (Earth Shield Technique)

**Starting Outfit:** Lacquered armor, traveling clothes, daishō (katana and wakizashi), tetsubō or ōtsuchi (war club or hammer), club, knife, traveling pack.
