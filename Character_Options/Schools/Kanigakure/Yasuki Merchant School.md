# Yasuki Merchant School

**Focus:** Social Maneuvering, Appraisal, and Wealth

The Yasuki are the quartermasters and merchants of the Crab Clan, wielding wealth and resources as effectively as other shinobi wield a blade. They understand that wars are won not just on the battlefield, but through logistics, supply lines, and economic power. A Yasuki shinobi is a master of negotiation, appraisal, and finding value where others see none. They are cunning, resourceful, and always looking for an angle to exploit for the benefit of their clan.

---

**Feat:** Windfall

**Starting Techniques:** 
*   **Deceit of the Senses (Magen: Jigoku Tenshi no Jutsu):** You can make a target believe they are on fire, drowning, or being crushed, causing them to react to an illusionary element.
*   **Waking Dream (Shougenzou no Jutsu):** You can create a minor, static illusion, perfect for altering a document, creating a false wall, or hiding an object.
*   **Invitation to Madness (Shinheki no Jutsu):** You can directly assault a target's mind, causing a painful mental shock that can leave them reeling.

**Starting Outfit:** Traveling clothes, ceremonial clothes, wakizashi (short sword), knife, yumi (bow), quiver of arrows, calligraphy set, traveling pack.
