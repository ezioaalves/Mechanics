# Hiruma Ninpou School

**Focus:** Ranged Combat, Stealth, and Survival

The Hiruma are the eyes and ears of the Crab Clan, ranging far into hostile territory to gather information and eliminate threats before they reach the Wall. Their shinobi are masters of survival, archery, and moving unseen. A Hiruma Scout is expected to be self-sufficient, deadly, and ever-vigilant, embodying the pragmatism required to survive where others would perish.

---

**Feat:** Improved Initiative

**Starting Techniques:** 
*   Choose one: `Ninpou: Shurikenjutsu - Renken` (Ninja Art: Shuriken Skill - Linked Throw) or `Taijutsu: Dachi - Kakutou Waza` (Hand-to-Hand: Fighting Stance)
*   Choose one: `Kenjutsu: Kudari-uchi` (Sword Art: Downward Cut) or `Taijutsu: Jiki-uchi` (Hand-to-Hand: Opportunity Strike)
*   `Kakureimino no Jutsu` (Mythical Invisibility Cloaking Technique)

**Starting Outfit:** Traveling clothes, ashigaru armor, daishō (katana and wakizashi), yari (spear) or yumi (bow) and quiver of arrows, knife, traveling pack, finger of jade.
