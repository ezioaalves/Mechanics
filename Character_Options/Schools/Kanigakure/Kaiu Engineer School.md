# Kaiu Fuinjutsu School

**Focus:** Defensive Fortifications, Crafting, and Siege Warfare

The Kaiu are the master builders and architects of the Crab Clan, responsible for the design, construction, and maintenance of the great Kaiu Wall. Their shinobi are as much engineers as they are warriors, trained to see the battlefield as a series of structures to be created or dismantled. They are experts in demolition, fortification, and the use of complex siege weapons, using their knowledge to control the flow of battle.

---

**Feat:** Siege Engineer

**Starting Techniques:** 
*   `Kenjutsu: Urashamen-uchi` (Sword Art: Reversed Diagonal Cut)
*   `Shinheki no Jutsu` (Mind Rend Technique)
*   `Chakra no Hikari` (Chakra Light)

**Starting Outfit:** Traveling clothes, ceremonial clothes, ashigaru armor, daishō (katana and wakizashi), club, oyumi (crossbow), quiver of bolts, traveling pack.
