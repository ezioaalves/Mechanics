# Kuni Purifier School

**Focus:** Anti-Supernatural Combat, Fuinjutsu, and Survival

The Kuni family are the soul-wardens of the Crab Clan, shinobi-scholars who study the nature of their enemies to better destroy them. They are experts on the Shadowlands and the foul creatures that spawn from its Tainted influence. A Kuni Purifier's training is a grim ordeal, involving academic study of forbidden lore and brutal practical application against monstrous foes. They learn specialized fuinjutsu to cleanse Taint and combat the supernatural, alongside practical martial skills to survive the horrors they face.

---

**Feat:** Resist Taint

**Starting Techniques:** 
*   Choose one: `Taijutsu: Gouken` (Hand-to-Hand: Iron Fist) or `Kenjutsu: Agari-uchi` (Sword Art: Upward Cut)
*   `Toujun no Jutsu` (Earth Shield Technique)
*   `Cleansing Rite` (Jōka no Gishiki)
*   `Youso Fuuin no Jutsu` (Element Seal Technique)

**Starting Outfit:** Sanctified robes, wakizashi (short sword), knife, makeup kit, scroll satchel, traveling pack.
