# Shiba Guardian School

*The Shiba are the sworn protectors of the Isawa family. Shinobi of this school are trained as selfless bodyguards, blending martial prowess with a defensive, vigilant mindset. They are the shield of the Phoenix, taught to endure any hardship and to give their lives for those they protect.*

---

**Feat:** Defensive Martial Arts

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Body of Duty (Zentai Bougyo):** You take a total defensive stance, focusing all your energy on deflecting blows and protecting your ward.
*   **Strike with Purpose (Taijutsu: Jiki-uchi):** Your attacks are not aggressive, but opportunistic, designed to intercept and punish an enemy who overextends.
*   **Guardian's Awareness (Inuhana no Jutsu):** You heighten your sense of smell to that of a canine, making it nearly impossible for ambushes or hidden enemies to escape your notice.

**Starting Outfit:** Ashigaru armor, travel clothes, daishō (katana and wakizashi), naginata (polearm) or yari (spear), traveling pack.
