# Asako Loremaster School

*The Asako are the scholars and loremasters of the Phoenix Clan. Their shinobi are not warriors first, but seekers of knowledge, diplomats, and mystics. They use their deep understanding of the world, both mundane and spiritual, to solve problems and protect the clan's interests, preferring a well-chosen word to a well-thrown kunai.*

---

**Feat:** Educated

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Know the Soul (Weight of Duty):** By touching a target, you can probe their mind to learn their deepest fears, providing invaluable insight in social or interrogation scenarios.
*   **Recite the Passages (Shitsukentou no Jutsu):** You can recite a piece of lore or a philosophical koan so profound that it disorients a target, causing them to stumble.
*   **The Mind's Library (Artisan Appraisal):** You can focus your chakra on an object to perceive intricate details of its history and craftsmanship.

**Starting Outfit:** Ceremonial robes, sanctified robes, traveling clothes, wakizashi (short sword), knife, scroll satchel, calligraphy set, traveling pack.
