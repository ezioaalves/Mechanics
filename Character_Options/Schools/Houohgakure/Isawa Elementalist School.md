# Isawa Elementalist School

*The Isawa are the undisputed masters of elemental ninjutsu in Rokugan. Shinobi of this school are pure spellcasters, viewing the world as a tapestry of elemental forces to be commanded. They are the living artillery of the Phoenix Clan, capable of unleashing widespread devastation from a distance.*

---

**Feat:** Elemental Focus

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Mastery of the Elements:** Choose two of the following to begin your training: `Shoukakyuu no Jutsu` (Minor Fire Ball), `Genwakudoro no Jutsu` (Blinding Mud), `Kazegama no Jutsu` (Wind Scythe), or `Mizudama no Jutsu` (Water Sphere).
*   **Elemental Bulwark (Toujun no Jutsu):** You can raise a simple but effective shield of earth to protect yourself from harm.
*   **Unravel the Weave (Genjutsu Kai):** Your understanding of chakra is so profound that you can easily recognize and dispel disruptive illusions.

**Starting Outfit:** Sanctified robes, wakizashi (short sword), knife, scroll satchel, traveling pack.
