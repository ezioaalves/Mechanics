# Ide Trader School

*The Ide are the diplomats and merchants of the Unicorn Clan. Their shinobi are masters of negotiation, logistics, and communication, often serving as the clan's face in foreign lands. They understand that wealth is a weapon and that a well-negotiated treaty can win a war without a single drop of blood.*

---

**Feat:** Windfall

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **The Right Price (Well of Desire):** You can subtly influence a target's mind, making them more open to bribery and negotiation.
*   **A World of Goods (Artisan Appraisal):** You can focus your chakra to perceive intricate details about an object's craftsmanship and value.
*   **Swift Escape (Kaisoku no Jutsu):** Your time in foreign lands has taught you the importance of a quick getaway, allowing you to move with surprising speed when necessary.

**Starting Outfit:** Traveling clothes, wakizashi (short sword), yumi (bow), quiver of arrows, calligraphy set, traveling pack, Unicorn warhorse, journal.
