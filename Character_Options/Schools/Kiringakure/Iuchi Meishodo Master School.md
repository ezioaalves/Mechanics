# Iuchi Meishodo Master School

*The Iuchi are the mystics of the Unicorn, but their magic is strange and foreign to the people of Rokugan. They are masters of Meishōdō, a unique art that involves creating magical talismans and potions. Their shinobi are alchemists and healers, their techniques a blend of ninjutsu and strange, foreign alchemy.*

---

**Feat:** Craft Shinobi Drugs

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Blessing of the Kami (Inari's Blessing):** You can imbue a handful of berries with healing chakra, creating potent medical items that can restore vitality.
*   **Water from the Earth (Mizudama no Jutsu):** You can conjure a sphere of water, a versatile technique for both offense and providing for your needs in the wild.
*   **Wind to Speed the Way (Sarutobi no Jutsu):** You can use wind chakra to make yourself lighter, allowing you to jump further and move with greater agility.

**Starting Outfit:** Traveling clothes, ceremonial clothes, wakizashi (short sword), one weapon of rarity 6 or lower, calligraphy set, traveling pack, Unicorn warhorse.
