# Utaku Battle Maiden School

*The Utaku Battle Maidens are an elite force of all-female warriors, known for their peerless cavalry skills and their unbreakable honor. Their shinobi are paragons of virtue and strength, trained to be the ultimate heavy cavalry, capable of shattering enemy lines with a single, devastating charge.*

---

**Feat:** Mobility

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **The Unbreakable Mare (Stonewall Tactics):** You take an imposing defensive stance, drawing the attention of enemies and protecting your allies from harm.
*   **Purity of the Charge (Taijutsu: Jiki-uchi):** Your attacks are disciplined and opportunistic, designed to punish any foe who leaves an opening.
*   **Strength of the Utaku (Shodan Jouryoku):** Through intense training, you have developed your physical strength to its peak, allowing you to perform great feats of power.

**Starting Outfit:** Lacquered armor, ceremonial clothes, daishō (katana or scimitar, wakizashi), yari (spear), yumi (bow), quiver of arrows, knife, traveling pack, Utaku steed.
