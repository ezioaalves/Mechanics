# Shinjo Outrider School

*The Shinjo are the scouts and vanguards of the Unicorn Clan. Their shinobi are born in the saddle, masters of reconnaissance, and experts in mounted combat. They are swift, adaptable, and possess a deep bond with their steeds, moving as one with their animal companions.*

---

**Feat:** Animal Affinity

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **One with the Steed (Juuki no Jutsu):** You can share your senses with an animal you are bonded with, making your horse the perfect scouting partner.
*   **Strike like the Wind (Kenjutsu: Agari-uchi):** Your attacks are swift and decisive, designed for quick skirmishes and hit-and-run tactics.
*   **The Endless Sky (Kuuryuuken no Jutsu):** You can read the currents of the wind, allowing you to detect unseen enemies and predict weather changes.

**Starting Outfit:** Ashigaru armor, traveling clothes, daishō (katana or scimitar, wakizashi), yumi (bow), quiver of arrows, knife, traveling pack, Unicorn warhorse.
