# Moto Conqueror School

*The Moto are the grim warriors of the Unicorn, shinobi who embrace the thrill of battle and the glory of conquest. They are relentless and terrifying on the battlefield, riding down their foes with a savage intensity that is unsettling to the more traditional warriors of Rokugan.*

---

**Feat:** Training

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **Ride Down the Foe (Taijutsu: Gouken - Konoha Reppuu):** You deliver a powerful, sweeping kick, a technique perfect for striking from horseback or while charging.
*   **Conqueror's Gaze (Kyougaku no Jutsu):** You can channel your killing intent into a terrifying gaze that can frighten a target into submission.
*   **Endless Horizon (Chihou no Jutsu):** Your nomadic life has given you an innate sense of direction, allowing you to use the earth's magnetic field to always know which way is north.

**Starting Outfit:** Traveling clothes, concealed armor, daishō (scimitar and wakizashi), yumi (bow), quiver of arrows, two knives, traveling pack, Unicorn warhorse.
