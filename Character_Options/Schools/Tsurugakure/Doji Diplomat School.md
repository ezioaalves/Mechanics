# Doji Diplomat School

*The shinobi of the Doji Diplomat School are the eyes, ears, and voice of the Crane Clan in the courts of Rokugan. They are masters of etiquette, manipulation, and information gathering, using their silver tongues and subtle genjutsu to achieve victory without ever drawing a weapon. Their path is one of influence and control, ensuring the Crane's dominance in the political arena.*

---

**Feat:** Trustworthy

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **The Unquestionable Word (Kuchijouzu no Jutsu):** Your mastery of social graces is such that your words carry an almost supernatural weight, making others inclined to believe you.
*   **A Single Misstep (Shitsunen no Jutsu):** You can make a target momentarily forget a minor event, a perfect tool for erasing a social faux pas or a slip of the tongue.
*   **Graceful Avoidance (Kaisoku no Jutsu):** You move with a dancer's grace, allowing you to navigate crowded courts or withdraw from a tense situation with supernatural speed.

**Starting Outfit:** Ceremonial clothes, wakizashi (short sword), yumi (bow) or yari (spear), calligraphy set, traveling pack, an attendant or Rokugani pony.
