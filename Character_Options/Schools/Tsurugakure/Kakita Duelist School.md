# Kakita Duelist School

*The Kakita are the premier duelists of the Crane, and their shinobi are no exception. Students of this school are trained in the art of Iaijutsu, where a duel can be won with a single, impossibly fast strike. They are artists of the blade, blending deadly precision with a disarming, almost beautiful fighting style that is as captivating as it is lethal.*

---

**Feat:** Quick Draw

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **The Empty Blade (Kenjutsu: Iaido):** The foundational technique of the Kakita, allowing you to draw your weapon and strike as a single, fluid motion.
*   **Sudden Flurry (Reppushou):** You can create a sudden, sharp gale with a gesture, staggering an opponent for a critical moment before your strike.
*   **Blinding Steel Flash (Akari Henko no Jutsu):** You reflect light off your blade in a dazzling flash, momentarily disorienting your foe and leaving them open to your attack.

**Starting Outfit:** Traveling clothes, ceremonial clothes, daishō (katana and wakizashi), yari (spear) or yumi (bow), quiver of arrows, traveling pack, an attendant or Rokugani pony.
