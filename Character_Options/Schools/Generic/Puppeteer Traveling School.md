# Puppeteer Traveling School

**Focus:** Puppet-based Ninjutsu and utility.

A nomadic school that teaches the intricate art of puppetry for both performance and combat. Students learn to imbue puppets with chakra and control them from a distance, turning simple dolls into extensions of their own will.

---

**Feat:** Craft Puppets

**Starting Technique:** 
*   Ninpou: Chakra no Ito (Ninja Art: Chakra Threads)
*   Saihou no Jutsu (Sewing Technique)
*   Ninpou: Shurikenjutsu - Nageriki (Ninja Art: Shuriken Skill - Power Throw)

**Starting Outfit:** Traveling clothes, a basic blank puppet, a set of crafting tools, a scroll of `Ninpou: Chakra no Ito`, traveling pack.
