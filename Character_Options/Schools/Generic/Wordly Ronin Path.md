# Wordly Ronin Path

*Not all shinobi belong to a Great Clan. Ronin are masterless warriors, forging their own path in the world. They are survivors, mercenaries, and adventurers who rely on their wits and a diverse set of skills to stay alive. Their training is a hodgepodge of techniques learned on the road, making them unpredictable and adaptable.*

---

**Feat:** Skilled

**Starting Technique:** 
*   **A Thousand Faces:** Choose one technique that reflects your past: `Taijutsu: Keikai-uchi` (for a brawler), `Onwaku no Jutsu` (for a trickster), or `Nawanuke no Jutsu` (for an escape artist).
*   **Survivor's Instinct:** Choose one technique for survival: `Doku Hakken no Jutsu` (to avoid poison), `Hakken no Jutsu` (to detect ambushes), or `Inuhana no Jutsu` (to track prey).
*   **No Master's Command:** Choose one core shinobi technique: `Zentai Bougyo` (for defense), `Shitsunen no Jutsu` (for misdirection), or `Bunshin no Jutsu` (for deception).

**Starting Outfit:** Traveling clothes, any one weapon of rarity 6 or lower, katana (long sword) or yumi (bow), wakizashi (short sword), any two items of rarity 4 or lower.
