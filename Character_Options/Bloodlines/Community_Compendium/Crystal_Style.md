CRYSTAL STYLE


### REQUIREMENTS

Elemental Affinity: Earth Skills: Concentration 3 ranks Feats: Advanced Bloodline (Shoton) TABLE: CRYSTAL STYLE BLOODLINE TRAITS

### CRYSTAL AFFINITY

The character gains a bonus to learn, perform and convert Shoton techniques equal to the number shown. In addition, once per day, the user can add half their Crystal Affinity as a bonus to a Shoton technique DC while Crystal Skin is active.
