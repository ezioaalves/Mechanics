# Crystal Style (Shoton)

Shoton is a unique and rare elemental nature that allows the wielder to create and manipulate crystals at will.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Crystal Style bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Elemental Affinity**: Earth
*   **Skills**: Concentration 3 ranks
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Shoton)

## Bloodline Abilities

### Crystal Affinity (Ex)
The character gains a bonus to learn, perform, and convert Shoton techniques equal to the number shown. In addition, once per day, the user can add half their Crystal Affinity as a bonus to a Shoton technique DC while Crystal Skin is active.
