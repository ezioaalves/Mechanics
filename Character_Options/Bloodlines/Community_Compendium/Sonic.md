# Sonic

Those select few of the sound village that have this unique ability gain acute hearing and vibrations sensing, allowing them to fight without sight.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Sonic bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Elemental Affinity**: Wind
*   **Ability Scores**: Wisdom 16
*   **Skills**: Listen 4 ranks, Search or Spot 2 ranks
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Sonic)

## Bloodline Abilities

### Enhanced Hearing (Ex)
The wielder gains a bonus to Listen checks equal to half their character level and is able to make a Listen check in place of Spot checks, though spot penalties still apply. This ability costs 2 chakra every other round to maintain, can be activated as a move action.

### Blindsight (Ex)
Through vibrations, acute hearing, and echolocation, the character maneuvers and fights as well as a sighted creature. Invisibility and darkness are irrelevant. The ability’s range is specified in the table. The character does not need to make Spot or Listen checks to notice creatures within range of its Blindsight ability unless otherwise noted.

### Sound Affinity (Ex)
As the ninja's power increases, her ability to manipulate sound waves improves. Each one can only be chosen once unless otherwise stated:
*   **Sound Mastery**: Increases the Ninja's character level by 1 for all Ninjutsu with the Sound descriptor. 
*   **Increased Damage**: Increases any damage from a Ninjutsu with the Sound descriptor by 1 extra die of the appropriate type. 
*   **Intense Sound Waves**: Increases the DC by 1 for all Ninjutsu with the Sound descriptor. 
*   **Speed of Sound**: Grants the user an additional swift action the first time they choose this ability. The second time it is selected, they upgrade the additional action to a move action. 
*   **Keen Senses**: Increase the range of Blindsight by 5 ft. 
*   **Sonic Boom** (Requires Intense Sound Waves): You can change half your Sonic damage to Force damage. Selecting this a second time allows you to change all Sonic to Force instead.

### Perfect Hearing (Ex)
By spending an extra 5 chakra per round the character can increase the range of his Blindsight granted to him by this bloodline by 100%. This chakra cost is in place of the normal cost of Enhanced Hearing. Activating and deactivating this ability is a free action.
