### BLINDSIGHT

Through vibrations, acute hearing, and echolocation, the character maneuvers and fights as well as a sighted creature. Invisibility and darkness are irrelevant. The ability’s range is specified in the characters’ descriptive text. The character does not need to make Spot or Listen checks to notice creatures within range of its Blindsight ability unless otherwise noted.
