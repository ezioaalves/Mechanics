# Seiseigan

The Seiseigan is a special kekkei genkai that manifests itself in the eyes, and has been called the Golden Healing Eye in ninja lore due to its special properties. It is said that this doujutsu originated with the Sage of Six Path’s wife long before the days of ninja and hidden villages.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Seiseigan bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Skills**: Concentration 3 ranks, Spot and Treat Injury 4 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Seiseigan).

## Bloodline Abilities

### Seiseigan (Su)
The character can activate this ability as an attack action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. The ability manifests in the eyes of the user, changing them to a solid yellow color. The character gains an insight bonus to Concentration, Spot and Treat Injury checks.

The character can always See Chakra and can spend a move-equivalent action to See Through Chakra for as long as he concentrates, plus 5 rounds. In addition, while the character can See Through Chakra, he can make a Spot check (DC 15) to perceive a creature’s internal anatomy as though with x-ray vision. In doing so the character can determine the creature’s Tenketsu condition, current chakra pool, and hit points rounded to quarter units. He can see if the creature has suffered recent bone fractures or breaks or if it has been subjected to poison or disease and where in the body it resides or affects.

This ability costs 2 chakra per 3 rounds to maintain. Deactivating this ability is a free action.

### High Speed Sight (Ex)
While the character’s Seiseigan is active he gains a high speed sight quality as above.

### Purification Chakra (Su)
While the character’s Seiseigan is active, the user can manifest this ability as a full-round action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. The user’s eyes begin to glow yellow. This ability creates an orb of chakra around the user’s hand that can be applied to another creature via touch (willing) or a successful touch attack. This ability costs 3 chakra and the chakra orb lasts for 1 round per Seiseigan bonus. Upon touching the target, the user is able to perform different medical feats. They are as follows:

*   **Fatigue**: The user cures fatigue or improves the exhausted condition to fatigue.
*   **Stabilize**: The user will instantly stabilize the touched creature, assuming it was previously reduced to -1 hit points (or lower) and is dying.
*   **Extract Poison**: The user negates the effects of any poison. In addition, if the user can make a small incision on the creature’s body with a chakra scalpel or surgery kit, he can extract the poison entirely. Finally, the creature gains a +2 resistance bonus to Fortitude saves against the same poison for 24 hours after being affected by this ability.

If the user extracts poison entirely from the touched creature’s system, he may make up to 3 antidotes as though from an actual dose of poison. The poison residue must be used within 1 hour of being extracted. Using one of the aforementioned abilities does not deactivate the Purification Chakra ability, but each subsequent use of the ability costs an additional 3 chakra.
