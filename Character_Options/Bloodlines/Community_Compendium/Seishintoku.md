# Seishintoku

The Seishintoku bloodline, often associated with the Seishin Clan, grants its bearers remarkable mental fortresses. Their minds are steeled against external intrusions, particularly Genjutsu, and they can even reflect such mental attacks back at their source.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Seishintoku bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.

### Minor
*   **Skills**: Concentration 4 ranks, Chakra Control or Genjutsu 4 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Seishintoku).

### Intermediate
*   **Skills**: Concentration 4 ranks, Chakra Control or Genjutsu 4 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Seishintoku).

## Bloodline Abilities

### Innate Shields (Ex)
The user's mind has steeled itself against the predations of those who would trespass within. The user gains a passive bonus to Will saves against the effects of Genjutsu and other techniques that allow a Will save.

### Active Shields (Su)
Though the user's mind has its own defenses, she can train her mind to further resist the effects of Genjutsu. As an immediate action and at a cost of 3 chakra/round while active, the user gains an active bonus to Will saves against the effects of Genjutsu. The Active Shields can be released as an Immediate action. This bonus stacks with the bonus granted by Innate Shields.

### Rebound (Su)
When the user successfully resists the effects of a Genjutsu, he may choose to direct that Genjutsu back at the caster as though he or she were the original target of the spell. The Will save to negate the redirected Genjutsu gains a bonus equal to the user's Innate Shield bonus and, if active at the time of deflection, Active Shield bonus.
