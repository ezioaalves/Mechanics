### SPECIAL INFORMATION

A character that possesses this bloodline is unable to learn or perform techniques with an elemental descriptor because he inherently lacks an elemental affinity. In addition, the character has no defined chakra coil system and thus cannot suffer chakra coil or tenketsu damage. However, he must make a Chakra Control check equal to the perform DC of any Chakra Control, Genjutsu, or Ninjutsu technique in order to gather the chakra to perform it. Taijutsu techniques do not require this check. Anyone who can See Chakra can make a Spot check (DC 15) to see the character’s inner chakra source. It appears as a center mass of swirling energy that resides in the stomach area. Finally, the character gains the following bloodline traits.

### SHITATCHI (SU)

The character can activate this ability as a swift action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. Activating this ability causes the user’s inner chakra source to fill the character’s body, lining the edges of his insides pushing outwards. While active, the Shitatchi ability grants the character a bonus to defense and Chakra Control checks. In addition, a character with the Shitatchi ability active treats all Chakra Control techniques that deal damage as if they were empowered with the Empower Technique feat (See Meta-chakra feats for more details). This ability costs 3 points of chakra every 2 rounds to maintain. Deactivating this ability is a free action.
