## Bloodline Abilities

### Water Body (Su)
With this ability, the character uses a number of Suiton Units stored in his body. The user's body stores up to one unit plus one per two levels, and once used the Suiton Unit is wasted. These Suiton Units may be restored by drinking water (two liters per unit) and can be used to activate some of the character's abilities. 

Additionally, and at expenses of an Action Point, the character may sacrifice one or more Suiton Units in order to recover a number of Hit Points equal to 1d6 per Suiton Unit sacrificed. This may be done as a full round action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity by succeeding a Chakra Control Check (DC 12 + 2 per each Unit). 

Once every 24 hours one Suiton Unit is irremediably wasted to keep the character's organism working. If he happens to run out Suiton Units, he will receive one point of Constitution damage every hour until he is dead or he drinks enough water to acquire a Suiton Unit.

### Resist (Su)
The character gains Water Resistance by the specified amount.

### Molding (Sp)
The character can activate this ability by spending a Suiton Unit as a move equivalent action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity, and its effects will last for one round per character level (or until dismissed). While this ability is active, the character possesses Strength Rank 1 (Water Molding 1), Strength Rank 2 (Water Molding 2) or Strength Rank 3 (Water Molding 3).

### Resilient (Sp)
The character can activate this ability by spending a Suiton Unit as a move equivalent action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity, and its effects will last for one hour per character level (or until dismissed, as a swift action). While this ability is active, the character possesses Damage Reduction as stated for his progression and level. 

Every time the character takes damage (which is reduced by this ability) a Suiton Unit is expended. This ability cannot be used if the character has one or none Suiton Units, and stops working automatically when the character has only one Unit on him. 

This ability can also be used as a swift action in response to an enemy's attack, whenever the user is not flat footed or has his Dexterity negated somehow. This can be done by expending an action point, and if used in this way, the ability only lasts for 1 round per level.
