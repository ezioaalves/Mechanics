### SKIN CONTROL (EX)


### SKIN MANIPULATION (EX)

This ability allows the user to harden or soften their skin as a free action. This ability costs 1 point of chakra for every round it is active, depending on if the user hardens or softens their skin they get a variety of effects: Hardened Skin (Ex): When the user activates this ability, they gain 1 Natural Armor bonus and Damage Reduction (DR/Dark Iron), as well as a penalty to Dexterity Skill checks, and Speed Ranks equal to the rank of this ability. Cannot be active while Softened Skin is active. Softened Skin (Ex): When the user Activates this ability they gain two points of Damage Reduction (DR/Slashing) per rank in this ability. As well as gaining one point per rank in all dex based skill checks, Energy Resistance (Force) but takes a penalty to strength based skill checks, and Strength Ranks equal to the rank of this ability. Cannot be active while Hardened Skin is active.
The user makes a melee touch attack, against a target that provokes an attack of opportunity. If the attack is successful the target must succeed a Fort save (DC 10 + half HD + Con mod). If the save is failed the user can bestow the penalties, from one of the Skin Control abilities upon the target for 1d4 minutes.

### RAPID REGEN (EX)

While the Skin Control ability is active the user becomes able to change its form where wounds appear, creating smooth skin where once were wounds. The user gains fast healing equal to the rank in this ability. If it spends a full-round action and succeeds on a Concentration check (DC equal to the total damage it has sustained), it heals 10 points of damage for 5 chakra.

### CLAW (EX)

As a move action the user can turn his fingers into claw like appendages that deal damage as stated parenthetically above. These claws get a bonus to attack and damage equal to the users rank in Skin Control. The claws threaten a critical on a natural roll of 20 and do 3 times damage. This costs an additional 1 chakra every 2 rounds.
