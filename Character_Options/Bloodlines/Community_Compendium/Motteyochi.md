MOTTEYOCHI
This bloodline is very famous due to the monks of the
Temple of Stone, as many of them were seen using it to
defend against an invasion of the Earth Country. It is said
that these peaceful monks turned to ferocious warriors
of earth, clad in stone armor and an innate sense of
everything around them, and through this were able to
fend off entire waves of soldiers for days on end. Though
this rumor is true, most men and women with this
bloodline are a part of a large society of peaceful monks,
who worship the ideals of the first Earth Country
daimyo. Many feel that to become one with the universe
is to first become one with the earth and nature. They
detach themselves from being directly ruled over by any
person and fight purely for the earth, the simple truths
of nature, and peace throughout all lands - so that the
world may experience inner calmness and become one
with another.

### TREMORSENSE (SU)

As a move action the character may spend 5 points of chakra to gain Tremorsense 30 feet. The Tremorsense will last for 1 minute. At level 9 the distance becomes 45 feet and lasts 2 minutes, and at level 20 it becomes 60 feet and lasts 3 minutes.

### ONE WITH THE EARTH (SU)

Activating this ability is an attack action that requires only concentration and costs 2 points of Chakra every 2 rounds that can't be converted. The earth around the user moves and attaches itself to the character, forming an armor. The character's equipment bonus changes to +8 (+3 non-proficient) with a maximum dexterity bonus of +1, as if he were wearing enhanced Heavy Battle Armor. The user takes a -6 armor check penalty and has his speed reduced to 20 feet, these penalties do not apply while moving underground. The armor weighs 80 lbs., and thus the user needs at least that much earth, sand, rock or mud nearby. Deactivating this ability is a free action. At levels 5, 8, 14, and 17 the armor also allows the user to gain DR #/chakra as specified above and increases the chakra paid every two rounds by one.

### BONDING EARTH (PASSIVE)

As a free action, once per round the user may bond two stones together - increasing their size category by 1. No matter how large the stone may be, it may not passively effect other creatures - unless a technique or similar effect states otherwise - even if an opponent flies above the character when a colossal stone orbits overhead or they grapple him when 6 small stones float around his body. A stone may never grow larger than colossal size.

### RELEASE BOND (PASSIVE)

As a free action, once per round the user may release the bond between two or more stones, breaking them down as he so wishes. The stones may never be broken down to a size category below small - unless a technique or similar effect states otherwise.

### OFFENSIVE MANEUVER (ACTIVE)

As a swift action, the user may command one of his stones to fly forward and strike the opponent. The range of this attack is 10 feet plus five per two levels. It deals 1d6+1 earth damage, which increases by 1d6+1 for every additional size category of the stone. The attack is similar to that of a basic ranged attack, though the user may choose to use his intellect modifier rather than his dexterity.

### DEFENSIVE MANEUVER (ACTIVE)

As an instant action, the user may command one of his stones to fly forth and hit anyone who is initiating a melee or ranged attack, even a jutsu, within its attack range (maximum 20 feet for this action) in order to intervene. The attack and damage of this is the same as the Offensive Maneuver, though no matter how large the stone is it may do no more than 1d6+1 earth damage. In addition to damage though, it will also grant the foe a -2 penalty to hit or to perform the action being made per size category of the stone. This ability does not count towards the maximum number of Defensive Maneuvers per day, such as Kawarimi and similar techniques.

### EARTHEN SATELLITES

Your competence in maintaining control over your Earthen Orbit ability further increases, allowing you to increase the number of stones raised. Prerequisite: Earthen Orbit 5, Concentration 18 ranks, Chakra Control 18 ranks, Ninjutsu 18 ranks Benefit: You gain the ability to control additional stones though your Earthen Orbit ability. As such, your maximum number of stones increases by 2. Special: This feat may be taken multiple times, each time granting the character an additional 2 stones.

### EXTENDED TREMORSENSE

Your ability to sense movement through the Earth becomes innate. Prerequisite: Advanced Bloodline (Motteyochi), Chakra Control 8 ranks. Benefit: The character can activate his Tremorsense ability for 1 less chakra, and now their range for Tremorsense grows 15 additional feet on top of the base amount.

### EXTREME TREMORSENSE (EPIC)

Your ability to sense movement through the Earth becomes innate. Prerequisite: Advanced Bloodline (Motteyochi), Extended Tremorsense, Chakra Control 26 ranks. Benefit: The character can activate his Tremorsense ability for 1 less chakra, and now may sense out to 90 feet when doing so.

### INCREASED ARMOR (ACTIVE)

The character may add one orbiting stone to his Heavy Battle Armor as a swift action, granting himself an additional +1 equipment bonus to defense per size category. The character's armor check penalty and speed reduction increase by -1 and -5 respectively per additional equipment bonus added, making it possible to completely immobilize one's self. The character's total bonus through this ability may not surpass +4.
