SHARINGAN [DOUJUTSU]
SHARINGAN TECHNIQUES
AMATERASU (GODDESS OF THE SUN)
Ninjutsu (Enton) (Requires Mangekyou Sharingan (a))
[Fire, Uchiha Kinjutsu]
Rank: 10 (A); Learn DC: 25, 6 success; Perform: 13 (DC
27); Time: 1 attack action; Components: E, H, Mas;
Range: Close; Effect: Consumes 1 target or object in a
burst of flame/round; Duration: Concentration (up to 1
round/level) plus 1 round/level (see text); Save: Reflex
half, Reflex partial; CR: Yes (see text); Cost: 6.
This technique can only be learned if selected when
taking the Mangekyou Sharingan feat. Your
Mangekyou Sharingan needs to be active to use this
technique, but not to concentrate on it. Add your
Sharingan Eye bonus to Concentration checks made to
maintain this technique. You gain a +4 bonus to
overcome Chakra Resistance when using this technique.
You project intense black flames through your
Mangekyou Sharingan, targeting up to one creature or
unattended object (within line of sight) per round. Each
target suffers 2d6 fire damage, halved with a Reflex
save. On a failed save, the target will catch on fire for 1
round per level, or per damage die the burst of fire
dealt (whichever is lower). You cannot choose the same
target more than 3 times per use of this technique. If a
target that is caught on fire is hit again, the duration of
its catching fire will be refreshed, the effect is also
refreshed if the target is hit by another technique that
requires Amaterasu.
Creatures that catch fire due to this technique can
attempt to put it out as normal, but the save DC to do so
is increased to 25. For 1 minute after it is created,
Amaterasu flames cannot be doused by water, unless
done by a creature with at least 18 ranks of Ninjutsu
through the use of a Suiton technique, but it can be
sealed by the Fuuka Houin technique.
If a burst of flame was fired near a surface, a 5-ft.
square of the surface will catch on fire, dealing half the
damage of the original burst of flame to every creature
and object that comes in contact with it. The fire spreads
at a rate of 5 ft. every round continuing to deal the initial
half damage of the burst of flame that created it. The fire
will spread and burn until its duration ends.
As the technique ends, you suffer 1 Mangekyou
Sharingan Blindness, plus 1 for every 2 rounds you
keep concentrating on this technique after the first.
Empower – Spend 1 Chakra to increase the damage
by 1d6, up to 1d6 per level or 15d6. You must empower
each attack with this technique separately.
Empower – Spend 1 Chakra to increase the DC of the
Reflex Save for half damage by 1 (up to 3).
Mastery – Every step of mastery in this technique
allows the user to ignore 3 points of fire resistance
of any target caught in his Amaterasu. Fire immunity is
not affected from this mastery.
ENTON: ENTENKA (BLAZE RELEASE: FLAME WRAPPING FIRE)
Ninjutsu (Enton) (Requires Mangekyou Sharingan (a),
Amaterasu (t), Kagutsuchi (1)) [Fire, Uchiha Hijutsu]
Rank: 10 (A); Learn DC: 25, 5 successes; Perform: 11 (DC
23); Time: 1 attack action; Components: C, Mas; Range:
Personal; Duration: 1 round/level; Save: None; CR: No;
Cost: 12.
The user wraps their body in Amaterasu flames
providing a deadly defense. The flames visibly wrap
around the user and automatically react to attacks of any
kind. Even if the user is unaware of the attack, the flames
will intercept it.
For the duration of the technique the user gains a +4
Deflection bonus to Defense, a Damage Reduction of
15/- and a Fire and Cold Resistance of 30.
A creature dealing damage with an unarmed or natural
weapon attack suffers 1d6 points of fire damage each
time it successfully hits the target. A weapon used by a
creature attacking a character with this technique will
damage their weapon, weapons suffer from 1d6 points of
fire damage unless they are also made of Amaterasu
flames.
Fire damage from this technique, can be halved with a
successful Reflex save. On a failed save, the target also
catches on fire for 2 rounds. The DC to put out the
Amaterasu flames is a Reflex save (DC 25). It cannot be
doused by normal water unless it was created or
manipulated by a creature with at least 18 ranks in the
Ninjutsu skill for 1 minute (by means of a Suiton
technique), but it can be sealed by the Fuuka Houin
technique.
Mastery – Every odd step of mastery in this technique
adds another 5 Fire and Cold resistance.
At every even level of mastery, the duration for being
caught on fire by this technique increases by 1 round.
At fifth step of mastery the damage increased to
creatures and weapons striking the shield is increased to
1d8.
ENTON: HONOIKAZUCHI (BLAZE RELEASE: FIRE LIGHTNING)
Ninjutsu (Enton) (Requires Mangekyou Sharingan (a),
Amaterasu (t), Kagutsuchi (2)) [Fire, Uchiha Hijutsu]
Rank: 9 (A); Learn DC: 25, 5 successes; Perform: 12 (DC
25); Time: 1 attack action; Components: E, H, Mas;
Range: 5 ft. radius; Duration: (see text); Save: Reflex half
and Reflex partial; CR: Yes (see text); Cost: 8 + 3 per
additional spike.
The user creatures a number of spikes made from
Amaterasu flames within the immediate area around
himself.
Each spike deals 2d6 points of fire damage, which can
be halved with a successful Reflex save. On a failed save,
the target also catches on fire for 1 round/level or the
number of damage die dealt by a spike (whichever is
lower). The DC to put out the Amaterasu flames is a
Reflex save (DC 25). It cannot be doused by normal
water unless it was created or manipulated by a creature
with at least 18 ranks in the Ninjutsu skill for 1 minute
(by means of a Suiton technique), but it can be sealed by
the Fuuka Houin technique.
The user may create a number of spikes equal to 1
every 4 character levels.
Empower – The user may spend an additional 1 point
of chakra to increase the damage dealt by one spike by
1d6, up to a maximum of 1d6 per level or 12d6. This
process must be repeated for each spike.
Mastery – Every even level of mastery increases the
spikes damage by 1d6.
At fifth level of mastery the range increases to 10 ft.
radius.
ENTON: KAGUTSUCHI (BLAZE RELEASE: FLAME CONTROL)
Ninjutsu (Enton) (Requires Mangekyou Sharingan (a),
Amaterasu (t)) [Fire, Uchiha Kinjutsu]
Rank: 10 (A); Learn DC: 25, 6 successes; Perform: 13 (DC
27); Time: (see text); Components: C, Mas; Range: Close;
Duration: (see text); Save: (see text); CR: (see text); Cost:
(see text).
This incredible ability enables the user to manipulate
and control the legendary flames of Amaterasu.
Possession of this technique enables the user to
manipulate and interact with their Amaterasu flames
and remain unscathed. Without this technique any
Amaterasu flames that are currently active will burn
uncontrollably until their duration is over. Simply
possessing this technique makes the user immune to his
own Amaterasu flames unless they are later manipulated
by another user of Kagutsuchi.
Upon learning this technique, the user is able to
control Amaterasu flames in one of three ways.
Manipulate: The user may take control of any
currently manifested Amaterasu flames in any direction
within range of the technique. This functions as standard
action and costs the user an amount of chakra equal to
the number of squares they’re moving that are covered
by Amaterasu flames. For each 5 ft. square has a chakra
cost of 1.
Extinguish: The user may extinguish any Amaterasu
flames that are currently active. The cost for doing so is
1 chakra cost per additional 5 ft. area beyond the first 5
ft. which has no cost.
Counter: The user may use this technique to counter
another user’s Amaterasu, the chakra cost is equal to the
amount the user creating their Amaterasu paid. At
which point the techniques cancel each other out.
In any instance of this technique the user may not
control Amaterasu flames of others that are also
currently being manipulated, flames that are considered
to be manipulated are anything that is under the effect
of a technique that has a requirement of Kagutsuchi or
the Kagutsuchi technique itself. Kagutsuchi and any
technique requiring it are all also able to be sealed by the
Fuuka Houin technique.
This technique can only be learned if selected by the
user when taking the Mangekyou Sharingan feat as well
as also selecting the technique Amaterasu.
Mastery – Every step of mastery in this technique
allows the user to affect another 5 ft. for free when using
the Manipulate or Extinguish options.
ENTON: KASAI NO KENGEKI (BLAZE RELEASE: FIRE WEAPON)
Ninjutsu (Enton) (Requires Mangekyou Sharingan (a),
Amaterasu (t), Kagutsuchi (3)) [Fire, Uchiha Hijutsu]
Rank: 8 (A); Learn DC: 25, 5 successes; Perform: 11 (DC
23); Time: 1 attack action; Components: C, E, H, Mas;
Range: Personal; Duration: 1 round/level; CR: No; Cost:
(see text).
The user is able to create a weapon by manipulating
Amaterasu flames. The weapons the user may creates are
of any sort they are familiar with. If creating a weapon
that typically has reach they may increase the normal
length of the weapon by an additional 5 ft. for an
additional chakra cost of 2.
Melee based weapons also can hurl small shards of
Amaterasu flame each time they are swung. The shards
count as 1d6 fire damage and the attack is a ranged touch
attack. The user may create 2 shards per swing, each
shard has a range of 20 ft. and may target a separate or
the same creature. Fire from this source does not cause
the subject to catch on fire. These weapons cost 10
chakra for 2 handed weapons, 8 chakra for 1 handed
weapons, and 6 chakra for light weapons to create.
Thrown weapons can also be created and thrown.
These weapons deal their standard damage as fire
damage as well in addition to their 1d6 fire bonus
damage. The weapons have their standard range plus 10
ft. These weapons cost 2 chakra to create.
The weapon deals damage normal to its size for a
creature of the size wielding it but instead is dealt as fire
damage. In addition, the weapon deals 1d6 fire damage.
The additional fire damage can be halved with a
successful Reflex save. On a failed save, the target also
catches on fire for 2 rounds. The DC to put out the
Amaterasu flames is a Reflex save (DC 25). It cannot be
doused by normal water unless it was created or
manipulated by a creature with at least 18 ranks in the
Ninjutsu skill for 1 minute (by means of a Suiton
technique), but it can be sealed by the Fuuka Houin
technique.
A creature that is larger or smaller than medium size
will pay a different cost for melee weapons, for each size
larger the user pays an additional 6 chakra, for each size
smaller the user pays 2 less chakra.
Any technique that user knows that requires a weapon
as a focus may utilize this weapon in its place. The
weapon is considered chakra-enhanced for bypassing
Damage Reduction.
Mastery – At every odd level of mastery, the user may
pay an additional 2 chakra to increase the weapon with
reach by another 5 ft.
At every even level of mastery, the duration for being
caught on fire by this technique increases by 1 round.
At the fifth step of mastery the duration becomes 1
minute per level.
KAMUI (WRATH OF THE GODS)
Ninjutsu (Spacetime) (Requires Mangekyou Sharingan
(a)) [Uchiha Clan Kinjutsu]
Rank: 14 (SS); Learn DC: 31, 8 success; Perform: 19 ranks
(DC 39); Time: 1 attack action; Components: C, (See
Text); Range: (See Text); Target: (See Text); Duration:
Instant; Save: Fort partial; CR: Yes (See Text); Cost: (See
Text).
This technique is one of the most fearsome aspects of
the Mangekyou Sharingan. The technique uses an
enormous amount of chakra and has two incredible
abilities depending on which eye is in use. Each eye
constitutes one technique. When selecting both the user
makes one learn check.
Left Eye: Upon obtaining the Left Eye option the user
can distort space-time around a person and use the
distortion to destroy a person completely.
The user must have his Mangekyou Sharingan active
to use this ability. The user must hit the target with a
ranged touch attack that deals 2d4 points of damage per
level (maximum 40d4) unless the target succeeds a
Fortitude save. If the save is successful, the attack deals
2d6 points of damage plus 1 per level (maximum
2d6+20). If the target is killed by the technique, its body,
along with all of the objects it is carrying, are destroyed
and the remains sent to the user’s personal dimension (if
they have the Right Eye option, if not the remains are
lost forever). If the user fails to hit the target, the user
can make a Concentration check (DC 30) to maintain the
technique for an additional 1 round per Sharingan Eye
bonus. Each round after the first, the user may only
make one action and must pay an additional 5 chakra. If
the user makes another ranged touch attack, he suffers a
-1 penalty to his attack.
If the user is dazzled, the damage is halved. This
technique cannot be used if the user is blinded or if his
Mangekyou Sharingan is not currently active.
The base chakra cost for this variant of the technique
is 30. It increases by 10 chakra for each size category
above medium; however, this technique cannot
completely destroy a creature of size category Huge or
higher.
Right Eye: Upon obtaining the Right Eye option the user
gains their own personal dimension which acts as a finite
plane with limited access, a demiplane. Each demiplane
acts separately from any other created by another
character.
Demiplane: The plane grows at a rate of 10 ft. in radius
per day to an initial maximum of 200 ft. The
environment reflects whatever the character desire’s it
to appear, once this is set it cannot typically be undone.
The character determines the atmosphere, water,
temperature and general shape of the terrain (the
character cannot create terrain made from esoteric
materials, such as silver, uranium; you’re limited to stone
and dirt).
This cannot create life (including vegetation), nor can
it create constructs such as buildings, roads, wells or
dungeons, and so forth. The character must add them in
some other fashion if they so desire such as bringing
plants and animals with them. Time passes normally on
this plane.
Teleport: The user doesn’t need to have his
Mangekyou Sharingan active to use this ability. To
teleport, the user must make a ranged touch attack on a
target or their self that deals no damage. If the attack is
successful, the user is able to warp himself and or the
targets, to their personal dimension or any other
dimension the user has been to or has synchronized
with. The target will disappear, along with all of the
objects it was carrying, without a trace. If the target is
aware of the user before the attack, he gets a +2 Insight
bonus to Defense against the user’s touch attack. The
target can willingly allow the user to perform this
variant of the technique on him, in which case the user
automatically succeeds the touch attack.
When exiting another dimension to return to the
Material Plane, the user may appear anywhere they have
previously visited before.
The base chakra cost for this variant of the technique
is 10 if used to enter their own dimension but costs
nothing to leave and causes no Mangekyou Sharingan
Blindness for the user if they do not bring others with
them. Other dimensions are 30 chakra to enter and
leave, this option causes 1 Mangekyou Sharingan
Blindness per use if used to teleport the user and or
others who are all willing, if used on someone unwilling
then becomes 1 Blindness per use. The user may bring 1
other person.
Warp: The user doesn’t need to have his Mangekyou
Sharingan active to use this ability. The user can use this
technique to warp in and out of reality as if teleporting.
As a move action, the user may teleport in any direction
within twice his movement speed.
The base chakra cost for this variant of the technique
is 10.
Phase: The user doesn’t need to have his Mangekyou
Sharingan active to use this ability. The user can also
temporarily warp all or select parts of his body to their
personal dimension, making his body intangible for a
short time. The user must spend a full-round action to
perform this ability.
For 1 round per Sharingan Eye bonus, the user will
become intangible and cannot be hit by any attacks,
physical or chakra-based. However, the user cannot
make attacks or use additional techniques that directly
affect others until the technique is deactivated. If the
user was touching another creature or object (of size
category Medium or lower), the effects are applied to
that creature as well for as long as the user is touching it,
plus 1 round. It is a swift action to deactivate this ability.
The base chakra cost for this variant of the technique
is 25.
Avoiding an Attack: Minimum DC 30. The user
doesn’t need to have his Mangekyou Sharingan active to
use this ability. The user performs Kamui as an instant
action before an enemy’s attack roll (melee or ranged
attack, touch attack, or grapple attempt) targeting him
specifically, and warps the designated part of his body to
another dimension. To do so, the user must be aware of
the attack.
Avoiding an attack costs 6 points of chakra, and can be
done up to 1 time per Sharingan Eye bonus per day
(maximum 5). There is no maximum CR. Unlike
techniques like Kawarimi, the character can use this
technique to avoid attacks when immobilized as long as
he is not blinded.
To avoid the attack, the user must make a Perform
check opposed by the opponent's attack roll (add 5 if the
attack roll is a Chakra Control or Ninjutsu effect, a
natural 20 or a confirmed critical). Success means that
the user becomes intangible and takes no damage. The
effects last until the end of the round.
Avoiding an attack must be declared before knowing
the result of the attack roll. Failure means that the
chakra spent trying to avoid the attack is wasted, and the
technique fails. This option causes 1 Mangekyou
Sharingan Blindness every 2 uses.
After using any option of this technique, the user gains
1 point of Mangekyou Sharingan Blindness, unless
otherwise stated. See Mangekyou Sharingan for details
on blindness.
Both Eyes: When the user selects both of his Mangekyou
techniques one for each eye, the final power of Kamui is
unlocked.
Synchronize (Requires Left and Right Eyes): The user
must have his Mangekyou Sharingan active to use this
ability. The character may also synchronize with other
dimensions, in order to synchronize with a different
dimensions the user must either have been present
within the dimension or seen it at some point. This
ability has the Hand Seals component. If the user wishes
to synchronize with a dimension they have not visited
but are aware potentially exists, the user must make a
Perform check, DC equal to the table below. To see if a
user is aware of a Plane of existence without having first
or second hand information about it, the user can make
a Knowledge (Arcane Lore) check DC equal to the same
Perform DC listed for the Table: Synchronize
Dimensions.
The base chakra cost for this ability is 50 + the DC of
the plane you are trying to synchronize with. The user
suffers 1 Mangekyou Sharingan Blindness.

At each level of mastery, the user may teleport an

KOTOAMATSUKAMI (DISTINGUISHED HEAVENLY GODS)
Genjutsu (Doujutsu) (Mangekyou Sharingan (a))
[Uchiha Kinjutsu]
Rank: 20 (Epic); Learn DC: 45, 10 successes; Perform: 28
(DC 50); Time: 1 full-round action; Components: C, E;
Range: 30 feet; Target: One or more creatures; Duration:
1 minute/2 levels; Save: Will negates, Will partial; CR:
Yes; Cost: 30.
This technique allows the user to enter the opponent's
mind and manipulate them by giving them false
experiences, making it seem as if they were doing things
of their own free will. It is regarded as a Genjutsu of the
highest class, due to the victim being entirely unaware
that they are being manipulated.
The user must have his Mangekyou Sharingan
activated to use this technique. With a large expenditure
of chakra, the user can perform one of the most powerful
Genjutsu techniques known to man. The user makes a
gaze attack to initiate this technique. Any creature
looking at the user must succeed a Will save or else
become caught in the technique.
Any affected creature is put into a deep trance.
However, time will go on as usual in the subjects’ minds.
They will act and react to whatever the user creates for
the duration of the technique. The user may present false
experiences in the form of combat, dialogue or
environment.
Combat: If the subject was in combat when this
technique took effect, he remains in combat. During
combat, each point of chakra the subject spends is
converted into mental damage due to the taxing effort
on the brain. In addition, he also suffers mental damage
equal to half the damage he endured while under the
effects of the technique. This damage, however, is dealt
once the subject is free of the technique, and it can be
halved by succeeding a Will save (as per the technique).
Dialogue: The user can alter or continue conversations
between the subject and whomever they were speaking
with when the technique took effect. Similarly, he can
create additional personas to interact with the subject in
various ways. The user decides what his creations say
and how they react to the subject. The user may choose
this option to persuade the subject of something
important or lead them down a certain path. This option
deals no damage unless it provokes combat (see above).
Environment: The user can alter things within the
subject’s environment similarly to other illusion
techniques. For example, the user could make a ceiling
collapse to force the subject to escape or change which
way a road leads to get the user to a designated location.
Therefore, the user would choose this option to force the
subject down a specific path or create a feeling of distress
or urgency in the subject’s mind. The user suffers half of
any damage he takes while under the technique’s effects
once the Genjutsu deactivates, of which can be halved
with a successful Will save (as per the technique).
The user may also pay an additional 10 chakra at the
beginning of this technique to control the subjects’
movements while under the technique’s effects. With
this ability, the user can move the subject’s body to the
aforementioned locations or control his actions during
combat. The user cannot take any other actions and the
duration is cut in half when he uses the technique in this
fashion. He must make a concentration check (DC 20 +
the target’s CR) per subject, per round to control the
subjects. If the user fails a check, the subjects may make
a Will save as per the technique to negate the technique.
After using this technique, the user suffers 4 points of
Mangekyou Sharingan blindness. If this technique was
empowered, the user suffers an additional 1 point of
blindness. Refer to the Mangekyou Sharingan blindness
chart for details of blindness.
Empower – The user may spend 10 points of additional
chakra to increase the duration to 1 minute per level
(maximum 20 minutes).
Special: This technique can only be used once per eye,
per month, and never more than once in a single
encounter. If a character has Mangekyou eyes implanted
into them who do not possess the Sharingan Bloodline,
the time to use them increases to one eye every 6
months, if the user only has one of the Mangekyou eye
the time is further increased to 1 year per use. If the user
possesses the Hashirama Senju Cells template, then he
may use this technique once per eye, per week instead if
they also have the Sharingan Bloodline. If the user does
not possess the Sharingan Bloodline the time reduced for
having both eyes is 3 months. If the user has only one, it
is reduced to 6 months.
KUDAKE CHITTA KAGAMI (SHATTERED MIRRORS)
Genjutsu (Doujutsu) (Requires Mangekyou Sharingan
(a)) [Uchiha Kinjutsu]
Rank: 14 (Super S); Learn DC: 30, 8 successes; Perform:
17 (DC 37); Time: 1 full-round action; Components: C,
S, E, Mas; Range: Close; Duration: 1 round/4 levels; Save:
Will partial (see text); CR: Yes; Cost: 12.
This technique is among the strongest Genjutsu used
by the wielders of the Sharingan. It creates a powerful
illusion that breaks the battlefield into multiple pieces as
if a mirror was shattered into several pieces.
This high level Genjutsu creates the illusion that the
user is in multiple areas around the target at one time. It
is intended to disorientate and trick the target’s mind. To
use this technique, the user’s Mangekyou Sharingan
must be active.
The user makes a gaze attack against a single creature
to initiate this technique. The target may make a Will
save to avoid the gaze attack, but takes a penalty equal
to half the user’s Sharingan Eye bonus, round down. If
the target fails his save, he will see his world break like
a mirror into several pieces that will surround him in all
directions. These pieces will reflect parts of the previous
area in random locations, thus disorienting the target.
For the duration of this technique, the target suffers a -4
penalty to attack rolls and Reflex saves and is considered
flat-footed. He may not perform actions or techniques
that require concentration.
After using this technique, the user gains 2 points of
Mangekyou Sharingan Blindness. See Mangekyou
Sharingan for details on blindness.
Empower – The user may pay 4 chakra to increase the
duration of this technique by 1 round. (Up to a number
of rounds equal to the user’s Charisma modifier)
Mastery – Every odd level of mastery increases the
penalties induced by this technique by 1.

Training
Rank: 10 (A); Learn DC: 25, 4 successes; Components:

The user enhances his Katon techniques with the
black flames of Amaterasu, allowing him to burn
through considerable protections. The user ignores 3
points of fire resistance or hardness with his Katon
techniques while his Mangekyou Sharingan is active.
If a creature Catches on Fire as a result of a technique
enhanced by this ability, the burning damage also
benefits from the reduction in fire resistance.
Mastery – The first four steps of mastery allow the user
to ignore an additional 3 points of fire resistance with
his Katon techniques while his Mangekyou Sharingan is
active, and each requires one additional step of mastery
in Amaterasu.
The fourth rank of mastery therefore requires full
mastery of Amaterasu.
The fifth step of mastery allows the user to deal half
damage to fire immune creatures with Katon techniques
while Mangekyou Sharingan is active.
SUSANO’O (GOD OF SEA AND STORMS)
Ninjutsu (Requires Chakra Control 23 ranks, Ninjutsu 23
ranks, Mangekyou Sharingan (a), 2 Mangekyou
Sharingan techniques, and Epic technique (Susano'o)(f))
[Uchiha]
Rank: 20 (Epic); Learn DC: 45, 8 successes; Perform: 28
(DC 50); Time: 1 Full-Round Action; Components: C,
Mas; Range: Personal; Target: You; Duration: 1 round/2
levels (D); Save: None; CR: Yes; Cost: (See text)
The third technique said to be unlocked with those
who possess the Mangekyou Sharingan, this technique
summons a massive ethereal warrior to the user’s aid,
granting him incredible offensive and defensive
capabilities, at a taxing price on the user’s body and eyes.
The Susano’o can be summoned only impartially at
first, yet as the user’s prowess gradually increases, so too
does the size and strengths of the user’s Susano’o. It
manifests initially as a set of ribs, and a large bony arm,
but gradually becomes the large ethereal warrior of
legend.
Ribs: The user summons the initial form of the
Susano’o; a set of ribs around himself to protect him from
damage. The user can spend his move action to summon
a skeletal hand with 1 claw attack with a reach of 10 ft.,
and a threat range of 20. He may then use his attack
action to attack with the skeletal hand either by making
an attack or grappling an opponent. He may also use the
hand as you would use a normal hand.
Once the duration ends, or he dispels the technique,
the user must make a Fortitude save (DC 10 + 1 per round

TOKUTEN NO SHUNKAN (MOMENT OF REDEMPTION)
This powerful bloodline was possessed by members of
a branch family from clan Kaguya. The Kaguya, being a
warrior people, sought to use the power of the branch in
their warfare through manipulative and oppressive
means.
The Kaguya systematically denied the family their
rights as a clan in and of themselves, and at times denied
the existence of the people altogether. Those that
possessed the bloodline refused to fight alongside the
Kaguya as lesser members. They offered a deal to clan
head, they would continue to fight alongside their
brethren, but only as equals. The clan head must
acknowledge the branch as its own clan, with full rights.
The Kaguya responded with the wholesale slaughter of
every first born male child from the branch. Its
remaining members scattered themselves to protect
their children, and future children from the scorn of the
Kaguya. Seeking asylum in a variety of countries and
villages across the globe, they changed their names,
hiding in plain sight.

(a)) [Uchiha Kinjutsu]
Rank: 12 (Super S); Learn DC: 29, 8 successes; Perform:
15 (DC 32); Time: 1 full-round action; Components: S, E,

CR: No; Cost: 12.
Utilizing the Sharingan’s innate predictive capabilities
in conjunction with advance spacetime manipulation,
the user of this technique is able to seemingly reverse
time in order to alter the past.
This technique combines the predictive nature of the
Sharingan with spacetime properties to alter the past
with hopes of aiding the user in combat. To use this
technique, the user’s Mangekyou Sharingan must be
active.
Spending a full-round action, the user concentrates on
using his eye’s special ability. After performing this
technique, the user will essentially be sent back 1 round
in time. Any creatures on the battlefield, other than the
user, are placed back where they were before their
respective turns and must take the exact same actions
they took during the previous round. The user, however,
may alter his actions how he sees fit. Any damage the
user suffered or dealt is nullified, for he has travelled
back in time. Initiative remains the same as it was prior
to the use of this technique.
After using this technique, the user gains 2 points of
Mangekyou Sharingan Blindness. See Mangekyou
Sharingan for details on blindness.
Empower – By spending an additional 6 chakra, the
user may increase the number of rounds he is sent back
by 1. This technique may only be empowered one time.
