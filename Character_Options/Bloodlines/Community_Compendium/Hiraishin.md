### HIRAISHIN (SU)

You gain the indicated number of Speed Ranks. In addition, you can activate your Speed Rank ability without paying Chakra for a number of rounds per day equal to your Hiraishin ability plus your Dexterity modifier, which stacks with the free use gained from Mastering the Speed Rank Training techniques.

### BLINDING SPEED (EX)

Up to a number of times a day (see table), while his Hiraishin is active, the character may spend 2 chakra points to move both before and after making an attack, and give himself a +2 to his attack and damage rolls, for the duration of the attack. This ability must be declared before the character does any action besides a free action.

### UP THE WALLS (EX)

While you have Speed Rank 1 or greater active, you can run up 45-degree surfaces without penalty, and can run up vertical surfaces during a move action by making a Climb or Tumble check (DC 20). If you do not reach a horizontal surface by the end of the movement, you begin to fall.
