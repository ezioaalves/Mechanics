### PURPLE EYES (SU)

The character can activate this power as an attack action, and deactivate it as a free action. Maintaining the Purple Eyes active costs the user 2 points of chakra per round. The character gains an enhancement bonus to his Strength, Constitution, and Charisma scores. This does not apply to HP rolls or Attack rolls. This does apply to skill checks, carry weight, and Fortitude saves. While the Purple Eyes are active, the character becomes unable use the Concentration skill, and Intelligence or Wisdom based skills other than Spot and Search. He is also unable to perform techniques requiring the Concentration component. He remains able to Sense Chakra normally, and is treated as a creature 3 levels higher when it comes to sensing chakra.

### DEADLY INSIGHT (SU)

The character gains an insight bonus to attack rolls and Reflex saves while the Purple Eyes Ability is active.

### HIGH SPEED SIGHT (SU)

When the Purple Eyes Ability is active; the character gains a High Speed Sight of the specified amount for as long as he can See Chakra.
