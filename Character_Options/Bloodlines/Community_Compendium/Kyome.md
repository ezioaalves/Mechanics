# Kyome

Known as “Devil Eye”, this little-known bloodline from the Ryu clan from the Village Hidden in the Grove aids the user in performing genjutsu by allowing them to see into their opponent’s mind and see their fears. Some of the most powerful in this clan even unlock greater proficiency with it, manifesting the fear without so much as a thought on their part.

When not activated the eyes of the user are pure black, save for the iris which is white. Upon activating the Kyome the eye changes, the sclera (white part of the eye) and the iris turn completely black, while the pupil turns white. While gazing into these eyes you feel them searching the deepest part of your mind, finding the very thing you fear the most.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Kyome bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Skills**: Genjutsu 4 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Kyome).

## Bloodline Abilities

### Kyome (Su)
The character activates this ability as an attack action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. While Kyome is active the character gains an insight bonus to all Genjutsu checks and checks to dispel Genjutsu. The character can concentrate for a move-equivalent action to gain the ability to See Chakra for as long as he concentrates, plus 5 rounds. Concentrating this way is the same as if he was concentrating on a technique. Kyome costs 1 point of chakra per round to maintain. If the character is blinded, he/she gains none of the benefits of Kyome. Deactivating this ability is a free action.

### Kyofu no kokei (Su)
This ability is also known as “Fear Sight”, it is the signature ability of the Ryu clan. Once per encounter as an attack action that provokes an attack of opportunity while Kyome is active the user may probe his/her enemy’s mind for their fears. The target, if aware, may attempt to avert their eyes as per the standard rules. Should the target fail to avert their eyes, or is unaware of this attempt and meets the user’s gaze must pass a will save (DC: 10+ (Kyome bonus*1.5)+ the user’s Cha Mod), if successful the target pushes the user out of their mind, and is now aware that something is wrong if they weren’t before. Should they fail the user finds a “minor” fear of the subject, for the next three rounds the target suffers a -1 to their will save against Genjutsu cast by the user of this technique if it involves their minor fear (the GM decides, and lets the player know what this minor fear is).

At 7th level, the user’s skill grows and may now find an “average” fear. The target suffers a -2 instead of the -1 penalty against Genjutsu involving their “average” fear.

At 11th level, the user may spend 8 chakra while discerning an “average” fear the user may immediately create an illusion of the target’s “average” fear, forcing him/her to make a will save with the -4 penalty against a DC of 10+ Kyome bonus*1.5 + Cha Mod or be shaken for up to 1 round per level. The target may make another save every round to end the cowering early.

At 15th level the user may now find a “greater” fear. The target suffers a -3 penalty instead of -2 or -1 against Genjutsu involving their “greater” fear.

At 20th level this ability has fully developed, by spending 15 chakra when discerning one “greater” fear the user may immediately create an illusion of the target’s “greater” fear, forcing him/her to make a will save with the -6 penalty against a DC of 10+ Kyome bonus*1.5 + the user’s Cha Mod or fall prone trembling in fear and unable to take any actions for 1d4+1 rounds. (Note: the target is not helpless).

### Genjutsu Resistance (Ex)
Due to their natural skill in Genjutsu, the members of the Ryu clan start to develop a natural resistance to them. The character gains the specified bonus as an enhancement bonus to resist any and all Genjutsu; this ability is only active while their eye is activated.

## Related Feats

### Battle Ready (Kyome) [Meta-Chakra]
You are quick in activating your Kyome in battle.
*   **Prerequisite**: Advanced Bloodline (Kyome), Kyome +2.
*   **Benefit**: The character may spend 1 meta-chakra charge to activate the Kyome ability as a swift action.

### Captivating Eyes
Your eyes captivate everyone around you, whether through fear or awe.
*   **Prerequisite**: Advanced Bloodline (Kyome), Overcharge.
*   **Benefit**: You may, as a swift action, spend one of your uses of the Overcharge feat to instead grant you a +4 bonus to your charisma score for the remainder of the encounter or 10 minutes, whichever is shorter. This bonus does not stack with multiple uses of this ability.

### Overcharge
You can temporarily increase the power of your Kyome, but at a cost.
*   **Prerequisite**: Advanced Bloodline (Kyome), Kyome +3.
*   **Benefit**: Once per day per five character levels (maximum 4/day at level 20) you may spend a move action to “Overcharge” your Kyome. For 2 rounds the bonus gained from your Kyome ability and your Genjutsu Resistance ability increases by +2. For 2d8 rounds afterward, you are fatigued, and the bonuses from your Kyome and your Genjutsu Resistance abilities are reduced to +1, or +2 if the user possesses the Kyome +5 ability.

### Overcharge Proficiency
You become more adept at using your overcharge ability.
*   **Prerequisite**: Advanced Bloodline (Kyome), Kyome +4, Overcharge.
*   **Benefit**: You gain two more uses of the Overcharge ability (not limited by level), in addition you only suffer from the drawback for 2d6 rounds afterward, instead of 2d8.
