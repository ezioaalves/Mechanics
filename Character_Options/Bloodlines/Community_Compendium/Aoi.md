# Aoi

[Flavor text: The Aoi bloodline is known for its members' preternatural connection to animals, particularly wolves, and their enhanced senses.]

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Aoi bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Ability Scores**: Wisdom or Charisma 16
*   **Skills**: Handle Animal 4 ranks
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Aoi), Moujuu Aishou

## Bloodline Abilities

### Acute Sense of Smell (Ex)
In many ways, the sense of smell of an Aoi is a lot like the scent ability but without the automatic nature of scent. An Aoi nose allows it to locate and identify certain races and creatures by their smell, and to aid in tracking it. An Aoi automatically gets to attempt a DC 10 Wisdom check to detect an animal within 30 feet, regardless of concealment. If the animal is upwind, the range at which an Aoi can detect it doubles. If it is downwind, the range is halved.

An Aoi has a better ability to detect and distinguish the scents of creatures than a normal human. This gives them a +5 racial bonus on Spot checks made to oppose a known individual's Disguise check if the individual comes within 5 feet. An Aoi within 5 feet of an invisible or hidden creature is entitled to a DC 10 Wisdom check as a free action to pinpoint that creature.

An Aoi gains a +2 racial bonus on all Survival checks made to follow tracks. Aoi can't track by smell alone, but the olfactory clues they find aid their tracking techniques. Aoi take a -2 penalty on all saving throws against attacks based on odor.

### Track (Ex)
To find tracks or follow them for one mile requires a Survival check. The character must make another Survival check every time the tracks become difficult to follow. The character moves at half his or her normal speed (or at the character's normal speed with a -5 penalty on the check, or at up to twice the character's speed with a -20 penalty on the check).

### Keen Senses (Ex)
An Aoi is born with better senses than most. They gain a boost to Search, Spot, and Listen checks equal to the bonus in the table.

### Predators Instincts (Ex)
As an Aoi masters their bloodline, they are able to apply their Keen Senses modifier to Melee Attack rolls and Reflex saves.

### Adrenaline Surge (Ex)
A number of times per day specified, the character is able to gain an adrenaline surge as a free action. The adrenaline surge only remains available for the action it was declared for, and if left unused, is wasted. The adrenaline surge grants the character a +1 circumstance bonus every two levels (maximum +10) to a single attack roll, Reflex save or Defense against a single attack, or to any check made to use a technique as a defensive maneuver or while avoiding an attack. If the adrenaline surge is used to gain a bonus to a Reflex saving throw or to Defense, the surge consumes a daily use of both defensive maneuver and avoiding an attack.

### Scent (Ex)
This ability lets a creature detect approaching enemies, sniff out hidden foes, and track by sense of smell. A creature with the scent ability can detect opponents by sense of smell, generally within 30 feet. If the opponent is upwind, the range is 60 feet. If it is downwind, the range is 15 feet. Strong scents, such as smoke or rotting garbage, can be detected at twice the ranges noted above. Overpowering scents, such as skunk musk, can be detected at three times these ranges.

The creature detects another creature’s presence but not its specific location. Noting the direction of the scent is a Move action. If it moves within 5 feet of the scent’s source, the creature can pinpoint that source. A creature with the Track feat and the scent ability can follow tracks by smell, making a Wisdom check to find or follow a track. The typical DC for a fresh trail is 10.
