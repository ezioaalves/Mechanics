# Tokeidome

The Tokeidome is a bloodline that allows the user to supercharge their physical actions, though often at a cost to their own body.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Tokeidome bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Ability Scores**: Wisdom 14
*   **Skills**: Chakra Control 3 ranks
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Tokeidome)

### Demonic Reach

*   **Benefit**: You may add your Charisma Modifier to the attack and damage roll for your Leech Life ability.
