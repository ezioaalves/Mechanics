GUMOSHIN TECHNIQUES
KUMO NENKIN NO YOROI (SPIDER ARMOR OF STICKY GOLD)
Ninjutsu (Requires Gumokeitou (a))
Rank: 8 (B); Learn DC: 24, 3 successes; Perform: 11 (DC
23); Time: 1 attack action; Components: C, M, Mas;
Range: Personal; Target: Self; Duration: 1 round per level
or until discharged (See text); Save: None (harmless);
Cost: 12.
Kumo Nenkin no Yoroi is a ninjutsu technique utilized
by the Gumoshin clan. Taking advantage of his body's
ability to create the Kumonosu, the Shinobi is able to
create an armor shell to protect his body. When the
shinobi emits his Kumonosu from his mouth and his
sweat glands the liquid hits the air and hardens. This
form an almost imperceptible barrier around the user,
that lasts for 1/round per level or until it breaks. The
damage reduction granted is 5/- (Does not stack with
similar sources of Damage Reduction). The armor will
absorb 5 points of damage per level, when it breaks and
leaves the user defenseless. In addition, for as long as this
technique is active, the user is immune to Tenketsu
sealing from techniques like Jyuuken or the Tenketsu
Freeze ability of the Ninja Hunter class.
Mastery – Third level of mastery on this technique
allows it to be used as an instant action, once per round,
just before an opponent attacks. The Perform DC on this
case is equal to the attack roll, and the chakra cost can’t
be converted.
Material Focus – One Kumonosu.
KUMO SENKYUU - SUZAKU (SPIDER WARBOW - HORRIBLE
SPLIT)
Ninjutsu (Requires Gumokeitou (a))
Rank: 9 (B); Learn DC: 22, 4 successes; Perform: 12 (DC
24); Time: 1 attack action; Components: C, M; Range:
Personal; Effect: One large warbow; Duration: 1 round
per level (D); Save: None (harmless); Cost: 6 (See text).
This ninjutsu technique is unique to the Gumoshin
clan. By expelling the Kumonosu inside his body, the
user gathers it and forms a large warbow with on which
the user is automatically proficient. The range increment
for this special bow is doubled. The base chakra cost is 6
but can be increased depending on the arrows created.
For as long as this technique lasts, the user can, once per
round as a swift action, create one of the following
arrows that can be shot normally by the bow:
Normal Arrow: By spending 1 Kumonosu and 2 points
of chakra, this arrow will be formed by expelling golden
spider thread from his mouth into the shape of deadly
arrow. This particular arrow deals 2d8 points of piercing
damage on a successful ranged attack roll.
Spider String Arrow: This version requires 1
Kumonosu and 4 points of chakra. The spider string
arrow is almost equal to the previous arrow, but has a
spider web string attached to the end. When this version
is fired, the trailing spider thread will contain the user's
chakra, this can allow him to maneuver the arrow
wherever it needs to go to hit his opponent, granting a
bonus of +10 to his attack roll. The user can only create
a single arrow of this kind per use of the technique.
Spider String Special Arrow: This last and most
powerful arrow is an improvement of the previous
arrow. Still having the spider string that grants a bonus
of +10 to attack rolls, this one contains a screw-tip, this
design causes the arrow to spin and pierce any obstacle
it hits, ignoring up to 10 points of hardness and damage
reduction and adding twice the user's strength modifier
to damage. This arrow costs 1 Kumonosu and 8 points of
chakra. The user can create only a single arrow of this
kind per use of the technique, and may only use this
arrow once per encounter.
Material Focus – One Kumonosu to create the bow and
one more for each arrow fired.
