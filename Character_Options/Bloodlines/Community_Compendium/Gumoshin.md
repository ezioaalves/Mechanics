# Gumoshin

The fearsome ability of the Gumoshin clan is in fact a mutation much like the Aburame, except that this particular clan holds several glands and traits found on numerous spiders, namely the ability to produce silk threads or walk on walls with mere chakra manipulations. Some of the stronger individuals of the Gumoshin are known to grow several additional arms, sometimes even up to 4.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Gumoshin bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Skills**: Climb 2 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Gumoushin).

## Bloodline Abilities

### Spider Silk (Su)
The character's body produces spider silk by itself, though he cannot use it without techniques. His body holds 1 kumonosu per level. Once used, kumonosu are created at the rate of 1 per hour. If the character does not possess a bloodline level in this bloodline, he does not gain additional kumonosu after 6th level until a level is taken.

### Spider Climb (Su)
A number of times per day, the user can use this ability as an attack action. By spending 1 kumonosu or 2 points of chakra to walk on horizontal surfaces or upside down, including walls and ceiling, as per the spider climb spell (caster level is the character level). Deactivating this ability is a free action.

### Spider Affinity (Ex)
The character gains the specified bonus on Handle Animal checks made to influence spiders and vermin-like creatures.

### Blood Pact (Spider)
The character gains the Blood Pact (spider) feat at 7th level.

### Multi-Armed Subtype
A character with this bloodline may choose to grow more than a pair of arm at character creation. The character may gain up to 4 additional arms, taken in pairs.

#### Traits:
*   **2-Armed**: The character gains a +4 racial bonus to Climb and Grapple checks, and 2 additional arms. The character gains a level adjustment +2.
*   **4-Armed**: The character gains a +8 racial bonus to Climb and Grapple checks, and 4 additional arms. The character gains a level adjustment +4.
*   **Multi-Armed**: The multi-armed character's additional off-hands are not included in the Two-Weapon Fighting feats, and he therefore must select the Multiweapon Fighting feat. A character with multiple arms gained by this bloodline, regardless of proficiency, may never take the Advanced Multiweapon Fighting feat.

## Related Techniques

### Kumo Nenkin no Yoroi (Spider Armor of Sticky Gold)
**Rank**: 8 (B); **Learn DC**: 24, 3 successes; **Perform**: 11 (DC 23); **Time**: 1 attack action; **Components**: C, M, Mas; **Range**: Personal; **Target**: Self; **Duration**: 1 round per level or until discharged; **Save**: None (harmless); **Cost**: 12.

Kumo Nenkin no Yoroi is a ninjutsu technique utilized by the Gumoshin clan. Taking advantage of his body's ability to create the Kumonosu, the Shinobi is able to create an armor shell to protect his body. When the shinobi emits his Kumonosu from his mouth and his sweat glands the liquid hits the air and hardens. This form an almost imperceptible barrier around the user, that lasts for 1/round per level or until it breaks. 

The damage reduction granted is 5/- (Does not stack with similar sources of Damage Reduction). The armor will absorb 5 points of damage per level, when it breaks and leaves the user defenseless. In addition, for as long as this technique is active, the user is immune to Tenketsu sealing from techniques like Jyuuken or the Tenketsu Freeze ability of the Ninja Hunter class.

**Mastery**: Third level of mastery on this technique allows it to be used as an instant action, once per round, just before an opponent attacks. The Perform DC on this case is equal to the attack roll, and the chakra cost can’t be converted.
**Material Focus**: One Kumonosu.

### Kumo Senkyuu - Suzaku (Spider Warbow - Horrible Split)
**Rank**: 9 (B); **Learn DC**: 22, 4 successes; **Perform**: 12 (DC 24); **Time**: 1 attack action; **Components**: C, M; **Range**: Personal; **Effect**: One large warbow; **Duration**: 1 round per level (D); **Save**: None (harmless); **Cost**: 6 (See text).

This ninjutsu technique is unique to the Gumoshin clan. By expelling the Kumonosu inside his body, the user gathers it and forms a large warbow with on which the user is automatically proficient. The range increment for this special bow is doubled. The base chakra cost is 6 but can be increased depending on the arrows created. For as long as this technique lasts, the user can, once per round as a swift action, create one of the following arrows that can be shot normally by the bow:

*   **Normal Arrow**: By spending 1 Kumonosu and 2 points of chakra, this arrow will be formed by expelling golden spider thread from his mouth into the shape of deadly arrow. This particular arrow deals 2d8 points of piercing damage on a successful ranged attack roll.
*   **Spider String Arrow**: This version requires 1 Kumonosu and 4 points of chakra. The spider string arrow is almost equal to the previous arrow, but has a spider web string attached to the end. When this version is fired, the trailing spider thread will contain the user's chakra, this can allow him to maneuver the arrow wherever it needs to go to hit his opponent, granting a bonus of +10 to his attack roll. The user can only create a single arrow of this kind per use of the technique.
*   **Spider String Special Arrow**: This last and most powerful arrow is an improvement of the previous arrow. Still having the spider string that grants a bonus of +10 to attack rolls, this one contains a screw-tip, this design causes the arrow to spin and pierce any obstacle it hits, ignoring up to 10 points of hardness and damage reduction and adding twice the user's strength modifier to damage. This arrow costs 1 Kumonosu and 8 points of chakra. The user can create only a single arrow of this kind per use of the technique, and may only use this arrow once per encounter.

**Material Focus**: One Kumonosu to create the bow and one more for each arrow fired.
