# Chakra Resonance (Kyomei)

The wielder of this bloodline has immense control over his chakra system and is able to push it through his pores and manipulate it in different ways to enhance his abilities.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Chakra Resonance bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Ability Scores**: Constitution 16
*   **Skills**: Chakra Control 4 ranks, Concentration 2 ranks
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Chakra Resonance)

## Bloodline Abilities

### Chakra Resonance (Su)
This ability requires an attack action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity to activate. Once activated, the user may choose one of the following ability enhancements. The user of this ability may switch between enhancements as a swift action, once per round, that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. This ability costs 1 chakra per round to maintain. Deactivating this ability is a free action; however, the user will be dazed after deactivation for 1 round.

*   **Chakra Enhanced Strength**: The user’s limbs are coated in chakra and allow him to deal increased damage. All unarmed melee attacks deal an additional amount of damage equal to the number indicated in the chart.
*   **Chakra Shield**: The user can thicken his skin by pushing chakra out of his pores to strengthen his natural defense. The user gains a damage reduction against all damage types equal to the number indicated in the chart.
*   **Burst Speed**: The user focuses his chakra on the tips of his feet to significantly increase his overall speed. The character increases the number of squares (5 feet) he can move per round by the number indicated in the chart.
*   **Chakra Vision**: The user can focus his chakra into his eyes to significantly enhance his vision. While this ability is active, the user gains High Speed Sight equal to the indicated number above as well as the ability to see much further than the average person. He suffers a penalty to Spot checks after 2 squares (10 feet) plus the indicated number, instead of solely every 2 squares (10 feet).

### Sense/Suppress Chakra (Su)
By 2nd level, the character can sense and suppress chakra naturally.

### Full Effect (Su)
A number of times per day, the user may utilize all four of the Chakra Resonance abilities simultaneously for 3 rounds. The chakra cost is doubled per round while this ability is in effect.

### Supercharged Resonance (Su)
Once per day, the character can supercharge his Chakra Resonance ability to its very peak. For 1 round per Constitution modifier score, the character may use all four abilities simultaneously at double the indicated Chakra Resonance number. This ability costs triple the normal chakra cost. The character may not deactivate this ability freely and must instead spend a full-round action to deactivate it prematurely. Once this ability ends, the character must succeed a chakra control check (DC 20) or else instantly be drained of 2d6 chakra. He must also succeed a Fortitude save (DC 20) or become fatigued.

## Related Feats
*   **Chakra Restoration**: The character gains the Chakra Restoration feat at 6th level.
