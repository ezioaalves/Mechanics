CHAKRA RESONANCE
The members of the Kyomei clan were once regarded
as some of the strongest feudal warriors of their time.
Sadly, with the changing times and the countless wars,
their stronghold in the Grass Country was sacked and its
members scattered throughout the world. However,
ninja lore still spins tales of Kyomei clansmen and their
powerful bloodline.

Chakra Vision: The user can focus his chakra into his eyes to significantly enhance his vision. While this ability is active, the user gains High Speed Sight equal to the indicated number above as well as the ability to see much further than the average person. He suffers a penalty to Spot checks after 2 squares (10 feet) plus the indicated number, instead of solely every 2 squares (10 feet). The user of this ability may switch between enhancements as a swift action, once per round, that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. This ability costs 1 chakra per round to maintain. Deactivating this ability is a free action; however, the user will be dazed after deactivation for 1 round.
