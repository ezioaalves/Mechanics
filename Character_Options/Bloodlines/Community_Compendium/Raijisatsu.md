OUKOKOI (SU)
The user can activate this ability as a move-equivalent
action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity.
While active, the user’s body is surrounded by a layer of
raw chakra that protects him. For as long as he has this
ability active he gains a damage reduction equal to the
indicated number in the chart above. This damage
reduction applies to all types of damage. This layer of
protective chakra is invisible to anyone who cannot See
Chakra. A character who can Sense Chakra can sense a
strange activity of chakra surrounding the user. This
ability costs 1 chakra per round to maintain. At 10th
level the chakra cost increases to 2 per round, and at 18th
level it increases to 3 per round. Deactivating this ability
is a free action.
PROTECTIVE SPHERE (SU)
While the Oukokoi ability is active, the user can spend
1 point of chakra per Oukokoi progression to expand the
chakra layer outward to form a 5-foot radius dome that
surrounds him as a swift action that does not provoke an
attack of opportunity. The dome is considered a barrier
in which nothing can get in, including incorporeal
entities. The dome has a hardness of 5 and hit points
equal to 10 times the number indicated in the chart
above. While within the dense dome of chakra, the user
cannot move but may perform ranged attacks and
techniques that do not require movement. This ability
This Bloodline power comes from a side house of the
Dattoumaru clan called the Seijina clan. This bloodline
power uses lightning coursing through the body to attain
speeds quicker than the Hiraishin and make lightning
ninjutsu even deadlier. However, this power does not
come without a price. The lightning causes considerable
damage to the muscles over time and it is not uncommon
for those in the Seijina house to die at a young age.

### ENHANCED AFFINITY (SU)

The character gains an additional +1 enhancement bonus to Learn and Perform checks, and checks made to convert the cost of any techniques of that element to hit points. The DC for the saving through of any Raiton technique used by the character receives a +1 enhancement bonus. The character suffers a -4 penalty to Learn and Perform checks for any other element.

### HIRAISHIN KAIZEN (SU)

The character can activate this ability as a swift action, and gains a speed rank bonus equal to the specified amount for doing so. This bonus does not stack with other speed rank bonuses. While active, the ability costs 1 point of charka per 2 rounds, plus 1 per speed rank gained (minimum 2) that can’t be converted to hit points. This technique also causes 1d4 lightning damage per speed rank gained every 2 rounds starting on the first. The character may make a Fortitude save of DC 10+2 for every speed rank gained to half this damage which cannot be prevented by energy resistance. This ability can be downplayed and allows the character to use it at less than full power. While making contact with water while Hiraishin Kaizen is active, the character takes 1d6 points of damage per round he maintains contact, or 2d6 points for total immersion in water. Deactivating this ability is a free action.

### RAIJISATSU (SU)

The character may increase the damage dealt by Raiton Ninjutsu by the amount shown in the table. Using this ability raises the Chakra cost by 1 and Ninjutsu check required by 2 for each additional d6 of damage dealt. After performing the technique, regardless of if it hits or misses, the user immediately takes 1d4 damage per additional d6 dealt. Energy
resistance cannot prevent the damage to the user. If a technique would deal damage multiple times in the same usage, the extra damage only counts for the initial instance.
