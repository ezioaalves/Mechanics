# Raijisatsu

This Bloodline power comes from a side house of the Dattoumaru clan called the Seijina clan. This bloodline power uses lightning coursing through the body to attain speeds quicker than the Hiraishin and make lightning ninjutsu even deadlier. However, this power does not come without a price. The lightning causes considerable damage to the muscles over time and it is not uncommon for those in the Seijina house to die at a young age.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Raijisatsu bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Elemental Affinity**: Lightning
*   **Ability Scores**: Dexterity 13
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Raijisatsu)

## Bloodline Abilities

### Enhanced Affinity (Su)
The character gains an additional +1 enhancement bonus to Learn and Perform checks, and checks made to convert the cost of any techniques of that element to hit points. The DC for the saving through of any Raiton technique used by the character receives a +1 enhancement bonus. The character suffers a -4 penalty to Learn and Perform checks for any other element.

### Hiraishin Kaizen (Su)
The character can activate this ability as a swift action, and gains a speed rank bonus equal to the specified amount for doing so. This bonus does not stack with other speed rank bonuses. While active, the ability costs 1 point of charka per 2 rounds, plus 1 per speed rank gained (minimum 2) that can’t be converted to hit points. This technique also causes 1d4 lightning damage per speed rank gained every 2 rounds starting on the first. The character may make a Fortitude save of DC 10+2 for every speed rank gained to half this damage which cannot be prevented by energy resistance.

This ability can be downplayed and allows the character to use it at less than full power. While making contact with water while Hiraishin Kaizen is active, the character takes 1d6 points of damage per round he maintains contact, or 2d6 points for total immersion in water. Deactivating this ability is a free action.

### Raijisatsu (Su)
The character may increase the damage dealt by Raiton Ninjutsu by the amount shown in the table. Using this ability raises the Chakra cost by 1 and Ninjutsu check required by 2 for each additional d6 of damage dealt. After performing the technique, regardless of if it hits or misses, the user immediately takes 1d4 damage per additional d6 dealt. Energy resistance cannot prevent the damage to the user. If a technique would deal damage multiple times in the same usage, the extra damage only counts for the initial instance.
