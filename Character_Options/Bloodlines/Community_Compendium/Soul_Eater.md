SOUL EATER
The Kuiarasu Clan from Hidden Sealed Hills is one of
the most ancient clans in this hidden village. Its
members are feared because there are only two kinds of
people in this clan, those who are skilled enough to
handle the cursed power granted by their heritage and
those who are not, being that last group tormented with
visions from Yomi as payment for a long forgotten pact
sealed with some underworld entity.
All members of the clan are able to manifest the
powers from this bloodline, some of them at a higher
degree, but only those who learn the ways to control the
soul hunger are the ones who manage to keep their
sanity.
SOUL HUNGER (EX)
Soul Eater
Major
This condition shared by all the clan members gives
them the need to "feed" from human souls in exchange
to the capability to attain some special abilities. The
character needs to have a certain amount of Soul Units
stored in his body, otherwise the hunger will haunt him.
For every day the character spends without any Soul
Units stored in his body, he must make a Will save (DC
10, +1 for every day in this condition), or receive 1d3
points of temporary Wisdom damage. This condition
first manifests when the character uses for the first time
one of their Soul Units, until then Soul Hunger does not
manifest itself.

### SOUL EATER (EX)

The members of the Kuiarasu Clan have a special bond with Yomi, the Underworld, they use this special connection to store soul energy from other human beings to use it to fuel their dark powers. The character uses a number of Soul Units stored in his body. The user’s
Through the usage of the souls trapped within his body the Kuiarasu clan members can evoke the essence of the Underworld to torment their enemies. The character can activate this ability by expending 1 Soul Unit as an attack action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. All creatures caught in the area of effect must make a Will save (DC 10 + 1/2 character level + Charisma Modifier) or become shaken for the duration of this ability, if the save is failed by 10 or more, they are instead panicked. Kuiarasu Clan members with more hit dice are immune to this ability.

### SOUL VITALITY (SU)

At this level the Kuiarasu clan members learn how to revitalize their bodies using the soul energy previously absorbed. The character can activate this ability by expending 1 Soul Unit per d6 up to the max indicated on the table they wish to heal as a attack action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. This ability functions twice per encounter.

### LEECH LIFE (SU)

Some clan members use this ability as a last resort to absorb part of a living person's soul, and only under dire circumstances, others just use it. The character can activate this ability by expending 1 Action Point as a move action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. This ability lasts 1 round per two character levels, and while active, the user can make a touch attack against a humanoid creature to drain from it a certain amount of Chakra Points (the user does not gain said amount of chakra).
