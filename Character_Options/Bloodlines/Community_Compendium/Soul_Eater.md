# Soul Eater

The Kuiarasu Clan from Hidden Sealed Hills is one of the most ancient clans in this hidden village. Its members are feared because they possess a cursed power granted by their heritage, tormented with visions from Yomi as payment for a long forgotten pact sealed with some underworld entity.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Soul Eater bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.

### Intermediate
*   **Ability Scores**: Charisma 14
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Soul Eater)

### Major
*   **Ability Scores**: Charisma 16
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Soul Eater), Genius Ninja

## Bloodline Abilities

### Soul Hunger (Ex)
This condition shared by all the clan members gives them the need to "feed" from human souls. The character needs to have a certain amount of Soul Units stored in his body. For every day without any Soul Units, he must make a Will save (DC 10, +1 per day) or receive 1d3 temporary Wisdom damage.

### Soul Eater (Ex)
The members of the Kuiarasu Clan use a number of Soul Units stored in their bodies. The body stores up to 1 unit + 1 per two levels + Charisma modifier. Units are restored by the *Kumihosu no Jutsu* ritual.

### Fear Aura (Sp)
Through the usage of trapped souls, the user can evoke the essence of the Underworld. As an attack action (1 Soul Unit), all creatures in range must make a Will save (DC 10 + 1/2 level + Cha modifier) or become shaken (panicked if failed by 10+).

### Soul Vitality (Su)
Revitalize the body using soul energy. As an attack action (1 Soul Unit per d6), heal the amount indicated in the table. This ability functions twice per encounter.

### Leech Life (Su)
As a move action (1 Action Point), for 1 round per two levels, the user can make a touch attack to drain Chakra Points (deals damage per the chart, chakra enhanced). For every 4 points drained, gain 1 Soul Unit.
