LIVING WEAPON
Once believed to be a legend based solely on
superstition, this bloodline has recently re-emerged in
the shinobi world. The wielders of this powerful
bloodline are said to be able to manifest spiritual
weapons from marked points on their bodies. Although
these living weapons are beginning to resurface, they are
still extremely rare to see.
