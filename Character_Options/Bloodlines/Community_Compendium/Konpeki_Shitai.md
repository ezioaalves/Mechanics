# Konpeki Shitai

One of the oldest clans of the Land of Twilight, the Fuuryuu clan is best known as spies and assassins. Because of their bloodline, they have also become feared and mistrusted.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Konpeki Shitai bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Konpeki Shitai)

## Bloodline Abilities

### Shadow Arts (Ex)
Gained at 1st level as a bonus feat.

### Suppress Chakra (Ex)
At 3rd level, the user gains the Suppress Chakra ability. This allows the user to hide their chakra presence from others, making it harder to be detected by *Sense Chakra* or other chakra-sensing abilities.

### Skill Bonus (Ex)
The character gains the indicated bonus to Hide and Move Silently checks.

### Walk Through Darkness (Su)
At level 6, the bloodline user gains the ability to move through shadows. While using this ability, the user is able to walk through shadows as a move action. He can move a distance through shadows equal to the distance given in the table. He can move to any location that has a shadow and that he has seen at least once. This ability costs 2 chakra per use.

### Moon Demon (Su)
At level 12, the bloodline user gains the ability to become a literal shadow. He becomes infused with shadows while using this ability. While in this form, he gains two claw attacks that deal damage as per his size category. He also gains a +4 bonus to Natural Armor, and his Speed Rank increases by 2, which stacks with any permanent speed rank increases.

He must make a Fortitude save every 4 rounds, with a DC of (10 + 1 per every two rounds the power is active), or fall unconscious from the strain on his body. This ability costs 4 chakra per round of use, although the Speed Rank increase does not cost any chakra.

### Greater Moon Demon (Su)
At level 18, he no longer needs to make a Fortitude check every 4 rounds while in the Moon Demon form.
