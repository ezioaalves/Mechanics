KONPEKI SHITAI
One of the oldest clans of the Land of Twlight,
the Fuuryuu clan is best known as spies and assassins.
Because of their bloodline, they have also become feared
and mistrusted.


### REQUIREMENTS

Feats: Advanced Bloodline (Konpeki Shitai) TABLE: KONPEKI SHITAI BLOODLINE TRAITS

### SHADOW ARTS

Gained at 1st level as a bonus feat.

### SUPRESS CHAKRA

At 3rd level, the user gains the Supress Chakra ability.

### WALK THROUGH DARKNESS

At level 6, the bloodline user gains the ability to move through shadows. While using this ability, the user is able to walk through shadows as a move action. He can move a distance through shadows equal to the distance given in the table. He can move to any location that has a shadow and that he has seen at least once. This ability costs 2 chakra per use.

### MOON DEMON

At level 12, the bloodline user gains the ability to become a literal shadow. He becomes infused with shadows while using this ability. While in this form, he gains two claw attacks that deal damage as per his size category. He also gains a +4 bonus to Natural Armor, and his Speed Rank increases by 2, which stacks with any permanent speed rank increases. He must make a Fortitude save every 4 rounds, with a
DC of (10 + 1 per every two rounds the power is active), or fall unconscious from the strain on his body. This ability costs 4 chakra per round of use that while the Speed Rank increase does not cost any chakra. At level 18, he no long needs to make a Fortitude check every 4 rounds.
