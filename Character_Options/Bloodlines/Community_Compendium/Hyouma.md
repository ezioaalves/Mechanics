# Hyouma

The Yuki clan is a clan of the frozen wastes whose bloodline grants them exceptional resilience to cold, and the capability to manipulate it expertly.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Hyouma bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Special**: Elemental Affinity (Water or Wind).

## Bloodline Abilities

### Hyouton
The character gains the Hyouton feat at first level of this bloodline.

### Cold Blooded (Ex)
You gain a bonus to learn, identify, and perform Hyouton techniques.

### Hyouma (Su)
As an attack action, you gain a burrow speed that only functions in ice, snow, or fully frozen surfaces equal to half your normal movement speed. While burrowing, you may choose whether to leave tunnels as you move. This ability costs 3 Chakra per minute and can be deactivated as a free action. 

While Hyouma is active, you can spend a move action to freeze enough water to cover a 5 ft area, or concentrate for a minute to create up to 20 ft radius of ice. The ice forms in an area around you in a circle with you at the center. Areas covered in this ice count as a slipper surface for anyone besides the user, requiring DC 15 Balance check or the use of Yukigutsu (Snow Walking) technique. Failing means the creature falls prone. Activities such as running or jumping or anything else the GM includes raises the DC by 5. While Hyouma is active the user is able to provide all the ice needed for any ice-based techniques automatically.

### Hyouma (Full) (Su)
As Hyouma (half), but your burrow speed increases to your normal movement speed. You may add your Intelligence modifier to all Hyouton techniques. As well your ability to create rings of ice improve, you can now spend a move action to freeze enough water to create a circle with a radius of 20 ft. and spend a minute concentrating to create a ring 80 ft. in radius.

### Arctic Tracking (Su)
While Hyouma is active, you can spend a swift action to gain a Tremorsense for 1 + Constitution modifier in rounds. It has a range of 30 feet and only functions on ice or snow.

### Arctic Protection (Su)
The character gains Cold Resistance by the specified amount.

## Related Techniques

### Shuugyou: Aian'aisu (Training: Iron Ice)
**Rank**: 4 (C); **Learn DC**: 17, 2 successes.
Upon learning this technique, any ice created by the user (whether ability or technique) has an increased Hardness of 5 and double the normal HP.
