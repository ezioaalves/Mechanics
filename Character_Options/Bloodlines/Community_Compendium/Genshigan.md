# Genshigan

The members of the Bakuha clan are renowned for their incredible doujutsu. Even among their own clan, this ocular kekkei genkai is rare. Nonetheless, the visual prowess of the individuals who do awaken the Genshigan is on par with the Sharingan, but with deadlier effects.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Genshigan bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Skills**: Concentration 4 ranks or Spot 4 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Genshigan).
*   **Special**: Elemental Affinity (Fire).

## Bloodline Abilities

### Genshigan (Su)
**Prerequisite**: Stone Skin +3
The character activates this ability as an attack action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. He gains an insight bonus to Defense, Spot checks and Reflex saves against Ninjutsu or Chakra Control techniques equal to the indicated number in the chart above. 

The character also gains the Darkvision supernatural ability for up to 60 feet while the Genshigan is active. While the Genshigan ability is active, the character can concentrate as a move-equivalent action to See Chakra for as long as he can concentrate, plus 5 rounds. Concentrating this way is the same as if he were concentrating on a technique. 

This ability costs 1 chakra per round to maintain. If the character is blinded, he gains none of the benefits of the Genshigan. Deactivating this ability is a free action.
**Benefit**: The cost of maintaining any form of Stone Skin is lessened by 1 chakra.

### Trace Fire (Su)
While the character’s Genshigan are active, he may use this ability. The character can spend a full-attack action to concentrate on a 5-ft square within 30 feet of himself and create a small explosion that deals damage to any creature in the square equal to the indicated number in the chart above, half of which is fire damage and the other half force. 

The creature can attempt a Reflex save (DC 10 + character level + Genshigan bonus) to half the damage. Each round the character can make a Concentration check (DC 20) to continue making explosions in different 5 ft. squares for as long as he can concentrate. He must stand still and cannot take damage during this time or else lose control of the ability. 

This ability costs 1 point of chakra per round to perform. This increases by one chakra per d6 of the Trace Fire ability. The character may choose to spend less chakra to do less damage.

### Power from Mother Earth
**Prerequisite**: Stone Skin +4
**Benefit**: The cost of any Doton jutsu performed by the character is lessened by 1.
