# Yomi No Koujin

The Yomi no Koujin bloodline is from Hidden Sealed Hills, a place where the underworld (Yomi) is connected to the world. Members of the Ghost Clan can partially enter this realm, gaining ghostly traits and the ability to phase through objects.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Yomi no Koujin bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.

### Minor
*   **Ability Scores**: Wisdom 13

### Intermediate
*   **Ability Scores**: Constitution 13, Wisdom 15

## Bloodline Abilities

### Kawasu (Sidestep) (Su)
The character adapts his body and spirit to enter the realm of the dead. His appearance becomes death-like pale. For the duration, he gains a +2 circumstance bonus to all Intimidate checks, but a -4 penalty to other Charisma-based social checks (Genjutsu excluded). 

This ability grants a lesser fortification: 25% chance to ignore critical hits, sneak attacks, non-lethal damage, fatigue, or exhaustion. Drawback: unaffected by medical techniques or pills while active. 
*   **Cost**: 1 point temporary Con damage (1 round/level), 2 points (1 min/level), or 3 points (1 hour/level).

### Koudo Kawasu (Advanced Sidestep) (Su)
Same as Sidestep, but fortification increases to 50%.

### Dai Kawasu (Greater Sidestep) (Su)
Same as Sidestep, but gains immunity to the stated conditions.

### Resistances (Ex)
The character gains resistance to cold and electricity, and a bonus to saves against poisons and Genjutsu as indicated in the table.

### Phasing Defense Bonus (Ex)
The character can use his phasing ability to dodge attacks. Active while Sidestep is active. The bonus is an inherent bonus.

### Ghostly Phasing (Su)
The character becomes Incorporeal and assume a ghostly appearance. He can move at base speed in any direction (even through objects) without scent or need to breathe. 
*   **Will Save**: Upon return, make a Will save (DC 15 + 1 per 10 mins spent) or be shaken/frightened/panicked.
*   **Cost**: 1 point temporary Strength damage. Move action.
