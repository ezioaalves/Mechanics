YOMI NO KOUJIN
The Yomi no Koujin bloodline is from Hidden Sealed
Hills, a gray and gloomy place lost somewhere between
Earth and Wind Countries. This village is known to be
the place where the underworld (or Yomi the realm of
death) is connected to our world by a long-time-ago
sealed gateway.
One of the greatest clans in this village developed
centuries ago, a way to partially enter the underworld,
they are known as the Ghost Clan.

### KAWASU (SIDESTEP) (SU)

At this level the character begins to adapt his body and spirit to enter the realm of the dead, the underworld also known as Yomi, in the process the life within his body starts to fade, his appearance resembles that of an undead, with the skin turning death-like pale and his eyes dark as consumed by the abyss. For the duration of this ability, the character gains a +2 Circumstance Bonus to all intimidate Checks, but a -4 Penalty from the same source to all other Charisma based social related checks (Genjutsu being the exception).

### RESISTANCES (EX)

The character gains Resistance to cold and electric jutsus and effects, as well as a bonus to saves against poisons and genjutsu by the number stated in the table.

### PHASING DEFENSE BONUS (EX)

The character can use in a limited way his Phasing ability to dodge incoming attacks. This ability is active as long Sidestep is active, and has no additional cost, but the character can dismiss it freely as a swift action. The bonus granted is an Inherent bonus that depends on the character’s level (see table).

### GHOSTLY PHASING (SU)

At this stage the character can finally have limited access to the underworld, a realm that is superposed to ours but not tied to its physical laws, a world filled with unspeakable horrors, the destination point of all grieving souls that have left behind their physical shell. When this ability is activated the character becomes
Incorporeal and his body assumes a ghostly appearance to all viewers. While in this condition the character may only move at base speed, but he may do so in any desired direction (even through solid objects) without leaving any marks or his scent behind, and without the need of breathing. Every time the character comes back from Yomi, the underworld, he must make a Will save because of the countless horrors he witnessed in that place. The DC for this save is 15 + 1 for every 10 minutes the character spends in Yomi (minimum DC 15). If the check fails, the character is shaken for 1d4 rounds, if it is failed by 5 or more is frightened for 1d4 minutes, and if it is failed by 10 or more he is panicked for 1d4 hours. Activation of this ability takes a movement action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity, and the character receives 1 point of temporary Strength damage to pay the cost of his trip.
