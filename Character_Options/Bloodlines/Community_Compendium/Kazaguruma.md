# Kazaguruma

The Kisharin clan’s Kazaguruma is a bloodline that manifests itself in the eyes and allows the wielder to amplify their already awesome Genjutsu prowess. Its name derives from the appearance of the eye when the Kazaguruma activates; that of a wheel that constantly spins while active.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Kazaguruma bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Skills**: Genjutsu 4 ranks, Spot 2 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Kazaguruma).

## Bloodline Abilities

### Genjutsu Adept (Ex)
The character gains the Genjutsu Adept feat at first level of this bloodline.

### Kazaguruma Eye (Su)
The character activates this ability as a move-equivalent action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. While active the character gains an insight bonus to Spot checks, Will saves against Genjutsu techniques, and Genjutsu checks to perform or identify techniques, and detect illusions equal to the indicated number in the chart.

The character can concentrate as a move-equivalent action to See Chakra for as long as he can concentrate, plus 5 rounds. Concentrating this way is the same as concentrating on a technique.

The Kazaguruma Eye costs 2 chakra every 3 rounds to maintain. If the character is blinded, he gains none of the benefits of this ability.

### Gaze Ability (Su)
The character can only use his gaze abilities while his Kazaguruma Eye is active. Each progression in this ability offers a different ability as stated below.

#### Gaze Ability 1
Once per encounter, the character can make a gaze attack on a single creature within 30 feet. The creature can make a Will save (DC 10 + character level + Cha modifier) to negate the effects of this ability. If the creature fails the save, the creature becomes dazed for 1 round.

#### Gaze Ability 2
Once per encounter, the character can make a gaze attack on a single creature within 30 feet. The creature can make a Will save (DC 10 + character level + Cha modifier) to negate the effects of this ability. If the creature fails the save, they begin to see the things they fear form around them causing them to become shaken for 1d4 rounds.

#### Gaze Ability 3
Once per encounter, the character can make a gaze attack on a single creature within 30 feet. The creature can make a Will save (DC 10 + character level + Cha modifier) to negate the effects of this ability. If the creature fails the save, they begin to feel nauseated for 1d4 rounds.

#### Gaze Ability 4
Once per encounter, the character can make a gaze attack on a single creature within 30 feet. The creature can make a Will save (DC 10 + character level + Cha modifier) to negate the effects of this ability. If the creature fails the save, their eyes start to blacken to the point of blindness for 1d4 rounds.

#### Gaze Ability 5
Once per encounter, the character can make a gaze attack on a single creature within 30 feet. The creature can make a Will save (DC 10 + character level + Cha modifier) to negate the effects of this ability. If the creature fails the save, their body starts to stiffen and he becomes temporarily petrified. He falls prone and is considered paralyzed for 1 round.

The character cannot use this ability if he is blinded. If the character is dazzled, then the DC required to avoid the gaze attack is reduced by 2 and the duration of the ability is halved (Minimum 1 round). After using one of the gaze abilities above, the character takes a -1 to attack rolls and skill checks for 1 round. Each ability above has a chakra cost equal to the indicated number in the chart.
