KAZAGURUMA [DOUJUTSU]
The Kisharin clan’s Kazaguruma is a bloodline that
manifests itself in the eyes and allows the wielder to
amplify their already awesome Genjutsu prowess. Its
name derives from the appearance of the eye when the
Kazaguruma activates; that of a wheel that constantly
spins while active.


### REQUIREMENTS

Skills: Genjutsu 4 ranks, Spot 2 ranks. Feats: Advanced Bloodline (Kazaguruma). TABLE: KAZAGURUMA BLOODLINE TRAITS

### RESILIENT ART

Prerequisite: Monster Summoner II, Ninjutsu 10 ranks, Craft (calligraphy) or Craft (visual arts) 10 ranks Benefit: Pick either Fire or Water damage, your creatures no longer take one-half again as much damage from that source.

### VISIONS OF ART

Prerequisite: Ink Vision, Ninjutsu 8 ranks, Craft (calligraphy) or Craft (visual arts) 8 ranks Benefit: You are able to cast Genjutsu with the subtype of Doujutsu through the eyes of your summons. The doujutsu’s rank cannot be higher than your level - 6. In the case of having Genius Ninja (Genjutsu) the highest it may be is your level - 4. All other effects remain the same and the creature summoned pays for the cost of the jutsu. No jutsu used in this manner may be altered by class abilities or meta-chakra abilities or any other form of alteration from artifacts.
