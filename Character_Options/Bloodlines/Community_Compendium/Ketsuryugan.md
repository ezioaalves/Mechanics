# Ketsuryugan

The Ketsuryugan is a doujutsu kekkei genkai, which appears in certain individuals of the Chinoike clan. The Ketsuryugan is recognized by its reddish color, giving a special appearance for the users of this doujutsu, characterized as holders of "eyes as red as blood". The Ketsuryugan confers genjutsu casting abilities. Its power earned it comparison to the Three Great Doujutsu.

The Ketsuryugan also gave the users the ability to manipulate the user's blood, or more accurately the iron in the blood, allowing the user to not only manipulate their blood, but any liquid with high iron concentration.

To qualify to gain Ketsuryugan bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.

## Bloodline Abilities

### Ketsuryugan (Su)
The character activates this ability as an attack action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. He gains an insight bonus to Defense, Initiative and Spot checks and Genjutsu checks to detect illusions, as well as checks made to read lips or memorize a string of numbers, words or certain details (such as a crime scene) equal to the bonus in the table.

The character can concentrate for a move-equivalent action to gain the ability to See Chakra for as long as he concentrates, plus 5 rounds. Concentrating this way is the same as if he were concentrating on a technique.

The Ketsuryugan costs 1 point of chakra every other round to maintain. If the character is blinded, he gains none of the benefits of the Ketsuryugan. Unless caught flat-footed, immobilized or feinted against, the character cannot lose his Dexterity bonus to defense while the Ketsuryugan is active. Deactivating this ability is a free action.

### Blood Rebellion (Su)
Members of the Ketsuryugan bloodline have the ability to manipulate their own and other’s blood, by touch in the case of others. When the character touches an opponent or an opponent touches the character, the character gains the ability to manipulate the blood of that creature as an attack action. This ability does not affect creatures without blood.

This ability functions as a touch attack. If it hits, the character spends 1 point of chakra per d6 they wish to inflict up to the amount in parenthesis to the creature. The attack infects the target with the user’s own chakra which begins to invade their system, the victim may make a Fortitude save to resist this, DC 10 + 1/2 character’s level + character’s Wisdom modifier. The chakra remains in the person’s system for a number of days equal to the character’s Ketsuryugan bonus. To remove the chakra from the body of the victim before the duration ends, a medical specialist of at least level 5 must tend to them via *Iryou Ninjutsu: Ryoji Ryoukudou* and also be able to See Through Chakra or have someone who can use the ability to assist them. Using this ability does not provoke an attack of opportunity.

### Blood Companion (Ex)
The character may turn a body of water into a Blood Companion as a standard action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. In order to create a Blood Companion, the character needs a small source of water (16 liters or more) nearby and must sacrifice 5 hit points as they cut themselves and bleed into the body of water as a free action. A character may create a Blood Companion of a size equal to the size listed in the table above, but may elect to create a smaller than the max category. Each Blob size requires x4 more liters of water than the previous size (64 for Large, 256 for Huge and so on), as well as an additional 5 hit points sacrificed per larger size.

A Blood Companion may act on the round after it is created, and acts on its creator's initiative. A Blood Companion has HD equal to its creator's HD. A character may not create more than the character’s Wisdom modifier of Blood Companions at a time. When a character creates a Blood Companion past their limit then the oldest active Blood Companion is destroyed.

## Related Feats

### Blood Doctor
**Prerequisite**: Reverse Doctor, Expert Healer
**Benefit**: You can, as a standard action, use your Blood Rebellion ability to heal an ally. When you use this ability, you roll Blood Rebellion damage as if you were 4 levels lower than you actually are. Using this ability in this way costs 2 chakra per d6 you heal.
**Normal**: Your Blood Rebellion only deals damage.

### Explosive Blob
**Prerequisite**: Blood Companion (Small); Blood Rebellion (2d6)
**Benefit**: Any Blood Companion you create gains the Explosive Surprise ability.
**Normal**: Your Blood Companions lack the Explosive Surprise ability.

### Battle Ready (Blood Companion)
**Prerequisite**: Blood Companion (Medium)
**Benefit**: You may create a Blood Companion as a move action. It is only able to act the round after it is created.
**Normal**: You may only create a Blood Companion as a standard action.
