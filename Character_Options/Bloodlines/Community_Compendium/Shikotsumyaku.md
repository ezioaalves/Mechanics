# Shikotsumyaku

This strange bloodline carried by the Kaguya clan allows them to manipulate the growth and hardness of their skeletal structure, and more potent versions even allow their bearer to heal quickly and regenerate limbs.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Shikotsumyaku bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.

### Minor

*   **Skills**: Concentration and Taijutsu 2 ranks. 
*   **Feat**: Toughness

### Major

*   **Feat**: Toughness

## Bloodline Abilities

### Bone Weapon (Ex)
As a move-equivalent action that does not provoke attacks of opportunity, the user can extract a Bone Weapon of certain sizes from one of their limbs. If the weapon is Diminutive or Tiny, they are usable and do not render a limb disabled. If the user creates a Small, Medium or Large Bone Weapon, the limb becomes unusable for 10 rounds - 1 per level (minimum 1). 

*   **Unusable Arm**: Cannot be used to make skill checks, hand seals or wield weapons, and incurs a -2 penalty to Strength and Grapple checks. 
*   **Unusable Leg**: Halves your movement speed and makes you unable to run, incurring a -1 AC penalty and a -4 penalty to opposed Bull Rush, Trip or Overrun checks. 
*   **Table: Weapon Damage**
    | Size | Damage | Hardness/HP | Example |
    | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
    | Diminutive | 1 | 10/2 | Needle |
    | Tiny | 1d4 | 10/4 | Kunai |
    | Small | 1d8 | 10/6 | Ninja-to |
    | Medium | 1d10 | 10/8 | Katana |
    | Large | 2d6 | 10/10 | Large Katana |

The user may create an unlimited number of Diminutive and Tiny Bone Weapons at no cost, while creating larger Bone Weapons costs 1, 2, or 3 Chakra respectively for Small, Medium or Large. Weapons may deal bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing at the user’s choice. A Bone Weapon lasts for 6 hours before turning to dust. The user is considered proficient with all their Bone Weapons. 

### Bone Armor (Ex)
As an instant action, you can spend up to 5 Chakra to gain DR X/Dark Iron for a number of rounds equal to 1 + their Con modifier, where X is the amount of Chakra spent.

### Extended Life (Ex)
You add a number of years to all of your maximum age and to the limits of all age categories for your race, though you cannot be set back from the age category you are currently in.

### Shikotsumyaku (Ex)
Your Bone Weapons gain an enhancement bonus to attack and damage rolls. While your Bone Armor DR gains a bonus equal to the level of Shikotsumyaku, this bonus adds to the DR as well as the rounds the ability lasts (i.e. a +5 bonus would mean a user can spend 1 chakra and gain +6 DR/Dark Iron for 6 rounds).

### Deathless (Ex)
You gain a bonus to Fortitude saves against massive damage, and become capable of regenerating limbs you have lost. Tiny body parts like fingers and toes take 1d4+2 days to regrow, large parts like hands and feet take 3d4+6 days to regrow, and large limbs like an arm or leg takes 4d6 weeks. You are fatigued while the regeneration takes place. In addition, you recover from Strength, Dexterity and Constitution damage twice as fast while resting.

## Related Techniques

### Hari Kinkotsu (Bone Needles)
Taijutsu (Strike; Requires Bone Weapon (a)) [Strike, Kaguya Clan Hijutsu] 
Rank: 2 (C); Learn DC: 15, 3 successes; Perform DC: 15; Time: 1 attack action; Cost: 1 per bone needle. 
By forming a piece of thin very sharp bone in the user’s mouth they are able to then fire the needle shaped bone fragment utilizing some Chakra. Each needle can be fired as an attack action at your highest attack bonus (or multiple based upon your BAB). 
*   **Empower**: Spend 1 Chakra to fire an additional needle, up to 1 per level (max 5). 
*   **Mastery**: Every odd step increases the damage done by the needle by +1 (+4 max).

### Ishizukurijin Shikkan (Stoneman Disease)
Taijutsu (Strike; Requires Bone Weapon (a) Togeyomi no Jutsu) [Kaguya Clan Hijutsu] 
Rank: 10 (A); Learn DC: 25, 5 successes; Perform DC: 27; Time: 1 attack action; Save: Fortitude negates; Cost: 3. 
This deadly technique infuses the victim with a bit of the user's own DNA which forces the victim's own bones to reproduce uncontrollably. If the attack deals damage, the target makes a Fortitude save. Failure incurs 1d6 Con damage. After 1 minute, another save is required or incur another 2d6 Con damage. This continues each day for 1d6 Con damage until successful save. Can be removed by Medical Ninja (Treat Injury DC 25).

### Kamaratsu no Mai (Dance of the Larch)
