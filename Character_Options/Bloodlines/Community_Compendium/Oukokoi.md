# Oukokoi

The members of the Bōei clan have an interesting bloodline that allows the user to form a seemingly invisible defense created out of raw chakra. It is a strong and rare kekkei genkai that makes any wielder a tough adversary.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Oukokoi bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Ability Scores**: Con 16
*   **Skills**: Chakra Control 4 ranks, Concentration 4 ranks
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Oukokoi)

## Bloodline Abilities

### Calm Mind, Calm Spirit (Su)
At first level of this bloodline, the wielder gains a +2 to all Concentration and Chakra Control checks.

### Oukokoi (Su)
The user can activate this ability as a move-equivalent action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. While active, the user’s body is surrounded by a layer of raw chakra that protects him. For as long as he has this ability active he gains a damage reduction equal to the indicated number in the chart above. This damage reduction applies to all types of damage. This layer of protective chakra is invisible to anyone who cannot See Chakra. A character who can Sense Chakra can sense a strange activity of chakra surrounding the user. This ability costs 1 chakra per round to maintain. At 10th level the chakra cost increases to 2 per round, and at 18th level it increases to 3 per round. Deactivating this ability is a free action.

### Protective Sphere (Su)
While the Oukokoi ability is active, the user can spend 1 point of chakra per Oukokoi progression to expand the chakra layer outward to form a 5-foot radius dome that surrounds him as a swift action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. The dome is considered a barrier in which nothing can get in, including incorporeal entities. The dome has a hardness of 5 and hit points equal to 10 times the number indicated in the chart above. While within the dense dome of chakra, the user cannot move but may perform ranged attacks and techniques that do not require movement. This ability lasts as long as the user concentrates, plus 1 round per Oukokoi progression. Once this ability reaches its duration or the user willingly dismisses it, the user may attempt a Chakra Control check (DC 15 + number of rounds the Protective Sphere was active) to reform the Oukokoi chakra layer as a free action. Otherwise, Oukokoi is instantly deactivated and can only be reactivated following the rules above.

### Ultimate Protection (Su)
One time per day, the wielder of this bloodline may activate the Oukokoi ability as a swift action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. It grants double its normal effects and costs no chakra to maintain. The raw chakra layer becomes visible to the naked eye and seems to burst from the user’s pores in an instant. This ability lasts for 1 round per 4 levels, and can be deactivated as a free action. However, the user becomes dazed for 1 round after deactivation.
