# Ajna Bindu

When an Ajna Bindu wills it, they can open a third eye on their forehead which functions in every way like a physical eye. A Third Eye user’s chakra turns white.

## Requirements
To qualify to gain Ajna Bindu bloodline levels, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Skills**: Diplomacy 2 ranks (Minor) or 3 ranks (Major)
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Ajna Bindu)

## Bloodline Abilities

### Third Eye (Su)
The character manifests this ability as an attack action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. He or she gains an insight bonus to Defense, Chakra Control and Sense Motive checks, and Genjutsu checks to detect illusions. The bonus is equal to the value associated with ‘Third Eye’ in the chart.

In addition, the character can attempt to make a Sense Motive Check with a DC of 10 + the opponent’s Will save bonus to create an emotional link with one opponent as a free action once each turn. So long as the user maintains the connection the user gains a +1 to the DC of the character's Doujutsu techniques while his Third Eye is active. The number of characters a Third Eye user can connect with at one time is equal to the number associated with ‘Third Eye’ in the chart. In addition, the user’s illusions that do no damage get a bonus against being dispelled equal to the bonus.

The character can concentrate for a move-equivalent action to gain the ability to See Chakra for as long as he concentrates, plus 5 rounds. Concentrating this way is the same as if he were concentrating on a technique. The Ajna costs 1 point of chakra every round to maintain. Deactivating this ability and maintaining the emotional connection with an opponent is a free action.

### Fatal Intuition (Su)
The character gains an insight bonus to Genjutsu DCs and Will saves while the Third Eye is active. The bonus is equal to the value associated with ‘Fatal Intuition’ in the chart.

### High Speed Sight (Su)
When the Third Eye is active, the character gains a high speed sight of the specified amount for as long as he can See Chakra.

### Deep Meditation (Su)
Deep Meditation allows the character to train more effectively. It is not possible to use an implement to gain a bonus in training while in Deep Meditation but one can tutor or be tutored, providing the tutor is part of the meditation. Despite being a special kind of training, Deep Meditation suffers all of the usual penalties involved with training including chakra cost. In addition, those in deep meditation are completely unaware of their environment, though direct physical contact breaks the meditation.
