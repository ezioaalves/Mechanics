# Bearer of the Kappa

Somehow the spirit of a Kappa has been entombed in your own body. Whether this was sealed within you before your birth or even more unusual after your birth, you forever have the blessings of the water spirits, but the curse of the kappa within you. You are forever regarded with distrust just as the Kappa who are famous for their pranks and tricks that sometimes go too far, like murder. Faint scales can be seen across your face in unusual patterns, each individual displays different patterns on their scales and the scales are located in different places on each bearer’s face. One rule remains consistent, the scales are very obvious to those around them.

Using the power of the Kappa you are able to exert control over water, making you an unmatched force near rivers and oceans.

## Template Traits

*   **Type**: Same as base creature, but gains the Demonic subtype.
*   **Skills**: The character suffers a -4 to all Charisma-based skills except Genjutsu.
*   **Feats**: The base creature gains Improved Chakra Pool for free upon receiving this template, and said feat will always be available in any Bonus Feat list to the host.
*   **Level Adjustment**: +2
*   **Challenge Rating**: +1

## Special Attacks

### Water’s Embrace (Su)
Using the water around him, the bearer of Water Spirit is able to form gigantic arms or shapes of water to slam into his enemies. The bearer can use a mass of water equal to his body weight to deliver an attack within 30 feet as long as his chakra pool is 1 or higher. Although this attack is considered a natural attack, the bearer can still deliver multiple attacks during a full attack action, depending on his base attack bonus. The character still threatens only the squares adjacent to him despite the range of this attack.

The attack deals damage as a slam attack from a magical beast one size larger than the character with his Strength score, plus one-half his level (round down). The attack increases in size by one category every 5 levels. The water also gains a +4 bonus to grapple checks, but does not benefit from speed or strength ranks or non-permanent bonus to Strength or size.

At 10th level, the bearer can deal damage as a claw attack instead. The water's embrace can be shaped at the bearer's will (being mindful of the embrace's size, at the GM's discretion), but doing so does not increase or reduce the damage it can deal. Regardless of its shape, the water's embrace will always appear to obviously be a water construct unless it is somehow disguised.

The bearer can still attack airborne creatures without being hindered by the normal limitations of making water levitate (see below).

### Water Shuriken (Ex)
The Bearer of the Kappa is able to throw shuriken made from his own water. Each shuriken requires an attack roll, and he can throw 4 per round at his highest attack bonus. A Water Shuriken deals 1d6 plus his modified Strength score points of piercing damage and has a range increment of 50 feet.

## Special Qualities

### Water Manipulation (Su)
The bearer of the Water Spirit, regardless of his will, is always protected by water. Whenever he finds himself within 30 feet of water, the bearer gains a +5 deflection bonus to defense against the first attack per 5 levels (minimum 1, maximum 4) in a round, after which the deflection is negated until the next round, a ray attack counts towards this limit and are also blocked if they’re within the amount that can be blocked.

The water can be used to carry weight with its equivalent strength score (see Water's Embrace), so long as it remains within 30 feet of the bearer, separate from the bearer's own carrying capacity, though only as a creature of the same size as the user (typically Medium-sized, with no multiplier for being a larger creature).

Using the water to float higher than 5 feet above the ground requires a Concentration check (DC 20) and spending 2 points of chakra each round to avoid the mass of water losing shape and dropping whatever it is holding.

The user can command a mass of water equal to five times his own weight to move at the same speed he can, but may only command a mass equal to his own weight to follow him during transportations, such as Shunshin no Jutsu or Shunpo (counts as a weightless creature). The range of control the bearer has over the water is limited to 30 feet.
