# Ninja Chef

## Requirements

To qualify to become a ninja chef, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
To qualify to become a Ninja Chef, a character must meet the following prerequisites
*   **Base Attack Bonus**:  +2
*   **Skills**:  Craft (Cuisine) 6 Ranks, Fuinjutsu 6 ranks, Chakra Control 6 ranks
*   **Feats**:  Chakra Weaving, Sealweaver

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Ninja Chef prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**:  d6
*   **Action Points**:  6 + ½ character level, rounded down. CLASS SKILLS The Ninja Chef's class skills are Concentration (Con), Chakra Control (Wis), Craft (Cuisine) (Int), Fuinjutsu (Int), Gather Information (Cha), Investigate (Int), Listen (Wis), Perform (Cooking Demo) (Cha), Research (Int), Search (Int), Sleight of Hand (Dex), Spot (Wis), Survival (Wis), Treat Injury (Wis).
*   **Skill Points**:  6 + Intelligence Modifier.

The following features pertain to the Ninja Chef prestige class.

### Iron Gut (Ex)

The Ninja Chef's innards grow more hardened to outside interference. The Ninja Chef gains a bonus to saving throws vs. Poisons and Nausea Effects targeting Fortitude. This bonus increases with level to a maximum of +10.

### Soul Food (Su)

The Ninja Chef's culinary skills give her meals a special quality all their own. Those who consume a meal with a Chakra Infusion prepared by the Ninja Chef gain a morale bonus to all attack rolls and saves for 10 minutes afterward. This bonus increases with level to a maximum of +5.

### Sneak Attack

The Ninja Chef has learned to apply her knowledge of anatomy and choice cuts of meat to her attacks and gains a Sneak Attack.
