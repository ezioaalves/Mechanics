# Samurai

The samurai is a disciplined warrior who has devoted themselves to the way of a chosen weapon, blending martial tradition with battlefield presence.

*Source: NarutoD20 Redux v1.3, Chapter 8 – Advanced and Prestige Classes (p90–92). This is the Ch8 Samurai **advanced class**, distinct from the Ch4 Samurai starting occupation.*

## Requirements

To qualify to become a samurai, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Base Attack Bonus**: +3.
*   **Skills**: Knowledge (tactics) 6 ranks, Taijutsu 6 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Armor Proficiency (Light) and one of Archaic Weapons Proficiency, Exotic Melee Weapons Proficiency or Nin Weapons Proficiency.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Samurai advanced class.

*   **Hit Die**: The Samurai gains 1d10 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
*   **Action Points**: The Samurai gains a number of action points equal to 6 + one-half his character level, rounded down, every time he attains a new level in this class.
*   **Class Skills**: Chakra Control (Wis), Climb (Str), Jump (Str), Knowledge (tactics) (Int), Move Silently (Dex), Read Language (None), Profession (Wis), Ride (Dex), Sleight of Hand (Dex), Speak Language (None), Spot (Wis), Swim (Str), Taijutsu (Str) and Tumble (Dex).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 3 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Samurai advanced class.

### Traditional Path
At 1st level, you choose a path you will follow:
- **Kenjutsu (Swords):** You gain the Tactical Implements feat for all swords, and you immediately learn the *Kenjutsu: Gekisai* technique (p218).
- **Kyujutsu (Bows):** You do not provoke attacks of opportunity when firing projectile weapons within a threatened area. You immediately learn the *Kyujutsu: Sasshi* technique (p223).
- **Soujutsu (Polearms, Staves):** The DC for Tumble checks to tumble past or through you increases by 5, and you immediately learn the *Soujutsu: Retsugeki* technique (p229).
- **Tsuijutsu (Axes, hammers, maces):** You gain the Sunder feat, and immediately learn the *Tsuijutsu: Tokuretsu* technique (p232).

### Weapon Focus
You gain the Weapon Focus feat for a weapon that falls within your chosen path. This weapon becomes your chosen weapon.

### Bonus Feat
At 3rd, 6th and 9th level, choose a feat from among the following, for which you must meet any prerequisites: Acrobatic, Agile Riposte, Archaic Weapons Proficiency, Armor Proficiency (medium, heavy), Athletic, Blind-Fight, Chakra Presence, Cleave, Combat Reflexes, Dead Aim, Defensive Martial Arts, Dodge, Exotic Melee Weapon Proficiency, Far Shot, Great Cleave, Improved Two-Weapon Fighting, Mobility, Monkey Grip, Point Blank Shot, Power Attack, Power Critical, Rapid Shot, Shot on the Run, Spring Attack, Two-Weapon Fighting, Weapon Adaptation Training, Weapon Finesse.

### Combat Acumen
Up to once per day per Samurai level, you may use a technique granted by your Traditional Path, Sacred Path or Supreme Path as a spell-like ability (you do not have to meet the Perform Requirements, and you do not need to pay its Chakra cost, though you must still pay Chakra if you wish to empower such a technique). The techniques are performed at your character level.

### Sacred Path
At 5th level, you gain additional mastery over your chosen path:
- **Kenjutsu:** You gain the Quick Draw feat and learn the *Kenjutsu: Getsutai* technique (p218).
- **Kyujutsu:** You gain the Precise Shot feat and learn the *Kyujutsu: Rasensha* technique (p223).
- **Soujutsu:** You gain the Unbalance Opponent feat and learn the *Soujutsu: Shunsou* technique (p229).
- **Tsuijutsu:** You gain the Whirlwind Attack feat and learn the *Tsuijutsu: Yamatoukai* technique (p232).

### Armored to the Teeth
You suffer no movement speed penalty for wearing medium or heavy armor. The chance to fail to perform Mobility techniques due to armor check penalties is reduced by 20%.

### Weapon Specialization
You gain the Weapon Specialization ability for your chosen weapon, granting you a +2 bonus to damage rolls with it. If you already have this ability for your chosen weapon, you may apply it to a different weapon or select a bonus feat from the Samurai bonus feat list.

### Supreme Path
At 9th level, attaining near-perfect mastery over your selected path, you gain additional abilities based on your chosen path:
- **Kenjutsu:** When wielding any weapon in this category, you may choose to deal piercing, slashing or bludgeoning damage instead of the weapon's normal damage type. You learn the *Kenjutsu: Kessen Ougi – Hiryuusai* technique (p219).
- **Kyujutsu:** When attacking with weapons in this category, you ignore any concealment that is not total concealment, and enemies do not benefit from any cover except total cover. You learn the *Kyujutsu: Kessen Ougi – Sogeki* technique (p223).
- **Soujutsu:** You can attack adjacent opponents with reach weapons within this category. You learn the *Soujutsu: Kessen Ougi – Tensou Ranka* technique (p229).
- **Tsuijutsu:** When using the Whirlwind feat with a weapon in this category, you may use the Combat Acumen ability, applying the technique to all attacks made. These attacks cannot score critical hits. You learn the *Tsuijutsu: Kessen Ougi – Metsugeki* technique (p231).

### Frightful Presence
Your imposing visage and terrifying fighting spirit causes fear in nearby foes while your weapon is drawn. Creatures within 30 ft. of you must make a Will save (DC 10 + ½ your Samurai level + your Cha modifier) or become shaken for 1d6 + Cha modifier rounds. If an opponent succeeds at their save, they become immune to your Frightful Presence for 24 hours. (Other creatures must save again once they are no longer shaken.)
