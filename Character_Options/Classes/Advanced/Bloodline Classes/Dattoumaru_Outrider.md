# Dattoumaru Outrider

Dattoumaru Outriders are the clan's forward eyes: scouts who move ahead of the army, survive first contact, mark the enemy's position, and make pursuit or escape almost impossible. Where Wind Walkers turn Hiraishin into an assassin's weapon, Outriders turn it into battlefield awareness and pursuit doctrine.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a Dattoumaru Outrider, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Base Attack Bonus**: +3.
*   **Skills**: Hide 6 ranks, Move Silently 6 ranks, Survival 6 ranks, Tumble 6 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Hiraishin), Improved Initiative.
*   **Special**: Hiraishin 2.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Dattoumaru Outrider prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: d8
*   **Action Points**: 6 + 1/2 character level, rounded down.
*   **Class Skills**: Balance (Dex), Chakra Control (Wis), Climb (Str), Craft (mechanical, structural) (Int), Escape Artist (Dex), Hide (Dex), Jump (Str), Knowledge (ninja lore, tactics) (Int), Listen (Wis), Move Silently (Dex), Profession (Wis), Search (Int), Sleight of Hand (Dex), Spot (Wis), Survival (Wis), Taijutsu (Str), Tumble (Dex).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 5 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Dattoumaru Outrider prestige class.

### Forward Motion

While Hiraishin is active, the Dattoumaru Outrider takes no penalty to Hide or Move Silently checks for moving at full speed. He also gains a bonus to Initiative checks equal to his Dattoumaru Outrider level.

### Afterimage Trail

When the Dattoumaru Outrider moves at least 20 feet while Hiraishin is active, he gains a +1 dodge bonus to Defense until the start of his next turn. At 3rd level, this bonus increases to +2. This bonus is lost if he is flat-footed, immobilized, or carrying a heavy load.

### Bonus Feat

At 2nd level, the Dattoumaru Outrider gets a bonus feat. The bonus feat must be selected from the following list, and the Dattoumaru Outrider must meet all the prerequisites of the feat to select it.

Acrobatic, Alertness, Athletic, Dodge, Focused, Improved Chakra Pool, Mobility, Nin Weapons Proficiency, Run, Stealthy, Track, Weapon Finesse.

### Mark the Route

At 2nd level, the Dattoumaru Outrider can mark the path of a fleeing target or the pressure line of an advancing enemy. As a move action, he may mark one creature he can see within 60 feet. For 1 minute, he gains a +4 competence bonus to Survival checks made to track that creature and to Listen or Spot checks made to notice that creature.

While Hiraishin is active, the Dattoumaru Outrider ignores the first 10 feet of movement penalty from difficult terrain when moving toward or away from the marked creature. The Dattoumaru Outrider can maintain only one marked creature at a time; marking a new creature ends the previous mark.

### First Contact

At 3rd level, the Dattoumaru Outrider is almost impossible to catch unready. Once per encounter, when combat begins and the Dattoumaru Outrider is not surprised, he may activate Hiraishin without spending an action and move up to half his speed before initiative is resolved. He must still pay any chakra cost or expend any free Hiraishin activation time normally required. This movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity.

If the Dattoumaru Outrider uses this movement to end in cover or concealment, he may immediately make a Hide check as part of the same ability.
