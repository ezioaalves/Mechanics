# Frost Wraith

Frost Wraiths are Hyouma shinobi who stop treating ice as terrain and start using it as a hunting blind. They vanish beneath frozen ground, stalk by the tremors of their prey, and strike from angles ordinary senses cannot guard.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a frost wraith, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Base Attack Bonus**: +2.
*   **Skills**: Hide 6 ranks, Move Silently 6 ranks, Ninjutsu 6 ranks, Survival 3 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Hyouma).
*   **Techniques**: At least one Hyouton technique.
*   **Special**: Hyouma (half).

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Frost Wraith prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: d8
*   **Action Points**: 6 + 1/2 character level, rounded down.
*   **Class Skills**: Balance (Dex), Chakra Control (Wis), Concentration (Con), Escape Artist (Dex), Hide (Dex), Knowledge (earth and life sciences, ninja lore, physical sciences, tactics) (Int), Listen (Wis), Move Silently (Dex), Ninjutsu (Int), Profession (Wis), Search (Int), Spot (Wis), Survival (Wis), Swim (Str), Taijutsu (Str), Tumble (Dex).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 5 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Frost Wraith prestige class.

### Icebound Passage

At 1st level, the Frost Wraith learns to slip into frozen terrain with practiced ease. He may activate his Hyouma ability as a move action instead of an attack action.

While Hyouma is active, the Frost Wraith gains a +4 competence bonus to Hide and Move Silently checks made while standing on, moving across, or burrowing through ice, snow, or fully frozen earth. He leaves no tracks and no visible tunnel in such terrain unless he chooses to do so. Entering or leaving ice, snow, or fully frozen earth as part of Hyouma movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity.

### Bonus Feat

At 2nd level, the Frost Wraith gets a bonus feat. The bonus feat must be selected from the following list, and the Frost Wraith must meet all the prerequisites of the feat to select it.

Advanced Hyouma, Alertness, Cautious, Focused, Improved Arctic Tracking, Improved Chakra Pool, Nin Weapons Proficiency, Ninjutsu Adept, Stealthy, Technique Focus (Hyouton techniques only), Track.

### Whiteout Veil

At 2nd level, while Hyouma is active and the Frost Wraith is on or adjacent to ice, snow, or fully frozen earth, he may spend a swift action and 3 points of chakra to stir a burst of cutting frost around himself. Until the start of his next turn, he gains concealment and may make Hide checks even while observed. The veil ends immediately if he leaves ice, snow, or fully frozen earth.

If the Frost Wraith has Arctic Tracking active, creatures detected by that tremorsense do not gain concealment from his Whiteout Veil.

### Ghost Beneath the Ice

At 3rd level, the Frost Wraith can fight from within the frozen ground. Once per encounter, while Hyouma is active and he is burrowing through ice, snow, or fully frozen earth, he may spend 4 points of chakra to make one melee attack or perform one Hyouton technique from within the surface he occupies.

For this attack or technique, line of effect and the point of origin are traced from any square of ice, snow, or fully frozen earth adjacent to the Frost Wraith's space. Until the action is resolved, the Frost Wraith has cover against creatures outside the ice. After the action is resolved, he must either emerge into that square or move at least 10 feet with Hyouma. If he cannot do either, he is forced into the nearest open square. This movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity from the target of the attack or technique.
