# Weaponmaster

The weaponmaster devotes themselves to the absolute mastery of a single chosen weapon, becoming a peerless instrument of melee destruction.

*Source: NarutoD20 Redux v1.3, Chapter 8 – Advanced and Prestige Classes (p102–103).*

## Requirements

To qualify to become a weaponmaster, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.

*   **Base Attack Bonus**: +6.
*   **Skills**: Knowledge (tactics) 9 ranks, Jump or Tumble 9 ranks.
*   **Feats**: One of the following — Archaic Weapons Proficiency, Combat Martial Arts, Exotic Melee Weapons Proficiency or Nin Weapons Proficiency.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Weaponmaster prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: 1d10.
*   **Action Points**: 7 + ½ your character level, rounded down, every time you attain a new level in this class.
*   **Class Skills**: Climb (Str), Drive (Dex), Jump (Str), Listen (Wis), Knowledge (tactics) (Int), Read Language (None), Perform (Martial Arts) (Cha), Profession (Wis), Ride (Dex), Sleight of Hand (Dex), Speak Language (None), Spot (Wis), Swim (Str) and Tumble (Dex).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 3 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Weaponmaster prestige class.

### Weapon Focus

Choose a melee weapon that you're proficient with, which becomes your "chosen weapon". You gain the Weapon Focus feat for this weapon. If you already have the feat for this weapon, you may select a bonus feat from the Weaponmaster's bonus feat list instead.

### Bonus Feat

At 2nd and 5th level, choose a feat from among the following, for which you must meet any prerequisites: Advanced Two-Weapon Defense, Advanced Two-Weapon Fighting, Blind-Fight, Cleave, Combat Expertise, Combat Reflexes, Dodge, Improved Two-Weapon Defense, Improved Two-Weapon Fighting, Monkey Grip, Power Attack, Two-Weapon Defense, Two-Weapon Fighting, Weapon Adaptation Training.

### Weapon Specialization

You gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls with your chosen weapon. If you already have the ability for your chosen weapon, you may select a bonus feat from the Weaponmaster bonus feat list instead.

### Greater Weapon Focus

The bonus granted by the Weapon Focus feat for your chosen weapon improves to +2.

### Improved Critical

The critical threat range of your chosen weapon increases by one.

### Greater Weapon Specialization

The Weapon Specialization bonus to your chosen weapon increases to +4.
