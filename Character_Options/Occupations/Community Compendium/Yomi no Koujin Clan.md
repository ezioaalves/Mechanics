The Yomi no Koujin bloodline is from Hidden Sealed Hills, a gray and gloomy place lost somewhere between Earth and Wind Countries. This village is known to be the place where the underworld (or Yomi the realm of death) is connected to our world by a longtime ago sealed gateway. One of the gr eatest clans in this village developed centuries ago, a way to partially enter the underworld, they are known as the Ghost Clan.
