The ancestors of the Unicorn wandered the lands learning from the others and creating a culture uniquely their own, synthesizing elements of other cultures with their native heritage. In the years since they have left their mark on history as the best cavalry, with a powerful commitment to diplomacy and justice. 

**Phoenix Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any three skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Climb, Diplomacy, Handle Animal, Iaijutsu Focus, Knowledge (Tactics), Listen, Ride, Search, Sense Motive, Spot, Swim, and Taijutsu. 

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Alertness, Mounted Combat (Mounted Archery, Trample, Ride -By Attack, Spirited Charge), P oint Blank Shot (Far Shot, Precise Shot, Rapid Shot, Shot on the Run), Weapon Focus (spear, lance, bow, or katana). 

**Affiliation:** If a Unicorn Clan member takes levels in Samurai his skills listed above become part of his Class Skills for Samurai levels. Hi s Reflex save becomes Good, and his Will save becomes Good. All feats listed above become feats he may select from the Bonus Feats granted by the Samurai class. 

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1
