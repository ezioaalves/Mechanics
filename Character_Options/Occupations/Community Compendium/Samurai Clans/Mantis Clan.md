**Mantis Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any three skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Balance, Chakra Control, Climb, Iaijutsu Focus, Jump, Knowledge (Tactics), Listen, Search, Spot, Swim, Taijutsu, and Tumble.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Alertness, [Universal: Defensive Stance], (Improved Tri ck, Whirlwind Attack), Dodge (Dodge, Spring Attack), Improved Initiative, [Universal: Risky Strike] (Cleave, Great Cleave), Quick Draw, Weapon Focus (katana, kodachi, any polearm, wakizashi) 

**Affiliation:** If a Mantis Clan member takes levels in Samurai his skills lis ted above become part of his Class Skills for Samurai levels as well as his Class Skills. His Fortitude save becomes Average, his Reflex save becomes Good and his Will becomes Poor. All feats listed above become feats he may select from the Bonus Feats gra nted by the Samurai class. The Mantis who takes wields a spear gains the ability to use the Swing Around ability. 

Swing Around (Ex): On any unsuccessful attack with a *polearm* except on a charge, a Mantis may make a second attack, swinging the polearm arou nd to hit his enemy with the blunt side of the weapon, albeit a -4penalty and at a 5 feet range. This attack deals 1d6 bludgeoning damage, plus his strength modifier. Weapon enhancements and special weapon abilities do not apply to this attack.
