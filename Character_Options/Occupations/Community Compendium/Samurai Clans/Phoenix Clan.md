The Phoenix are the most pious clan and enjoy many blessings they feel are derived from their spiritual outlook and attunement to their ways is as complete as mortal minds can attain. 

**Phoenix Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any three skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Climb, Iaijutsu Focus, Knowledge (Tactics), Listen, Search, Spot, Swim, and Taijutsu. 

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Alertness, Blind-Fight, Combat Reflexes, [Universal: Defensive Stance] (Deft Maneuvers, Deft Maneuvers, Whirlwind Attack), Great Fortitude, Improved Initiative, Iron Will, Quick Draw, Weapon Focus (katana). 

**Affiliation:** If a Phoenix Clan member takes levels in Samurai his skills listed above become part of his Class Skills for Samurai levels. His Fortitude save becomes Average, while his Reflex save becomes Good, and his Will save becomes Good. All feats listed above become feats he may select from the Bonus Feats granted by the Samurai class. 

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
