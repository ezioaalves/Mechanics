More than any other clan, the Lion clan embodies the warrior ideal of bushido —the way of the warrior, the virtues and morals of the samurai. The samurai of the Lion are the paragons of warrior virtue. 

**Lion Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any three skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character sel ects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Climb, Diplomacy, Iaijutsu Focus, Intimidate, Jump, Knowledge (Tactics), Listen, Search, Spot, Swim, and Taijutsu.

**Feat:** Select one of the fo llowing: Endurance, Expertise (Deft Maneuvers, Deft Maneuvers, Whirlwind Attack), Improved Initiative, Iron Will, Ki Shout (Great Ki Shout), Remain Conscious, Toughness, Weapon Focus (katana). 

**Affiliation:** If a Lion Clan member takes levels in Samurai his skills listed above become part of his Class Skills for Samurai levels. His Reflex save becomes Poor, while his Will save becomes Good. All feats listed above become feats he may select from the Bonus Feats granted by the Samurai class. While wielding eith er their katana or wakizashi a Lion Samurai may use the Pounce ability. 

Pounce (Ex): When making a charge, the character can follow with a full attack. 

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
