The Myugan Monk Order is a small clan in the northern regions of the Fire Country. They are an exclusive bunch who tends to live isolated lives in their own small village. Although they are generally pacifists, they have an amazing grasp of Taijutsu and Chakra Control which they harness through an ancient style of martial art known as Dao Shi. 


**Miugan Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Balance, Bluff, Concentration, Chakra Control, Escape Artist, Knowledge (Ninja Lore/Earth & Life Science), Listen, Spot, Survival, Taijutsu, Tumble. 

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Shitatchi), Combat Martial Arts, Genius Ninja, Taijutsu Adept. 

**Affiliation:** You gain a +1 bonus and require 1 less success to learn Myugan Hijutsu techniques. 

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
