The Hitosashi Clan are famous for their dancing and unique styles of combat revolving around their art of dance. They primarily focus on T aijutsu and genjutsu styles of com bat and are a very unique clan. 

**Hitosashi Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Perform (dance) is a permanent class skill. Select any of two skills from the list as permanent class skill. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bon us on the checks using that skill: Balance, Chakra Control, Genjutsu, Hide, Knowledge (art, ninja lore), Jump, Move Silently, Ninjutsu, Taijutsu,  or Tumble. 

**Feat:** Select one of following: Acrobatic, Advanced Bloodline (Buruhada), Combat Martial Arts, Creative, Defensive Martial Arts, Genjutsu Adept, Genin, Genius Ninja, Nin Weapons Proficiency, Taijutsu Adept, Tenodori. 

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Tenodori techniques. 

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
