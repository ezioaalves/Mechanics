The members of this ancient clan are owners of the sacred burial places from the Hidden Sealed Hills Village, and because of this and other secret arrangements made with the High Council rulers, they are said to have great amounts of resources and relics stored in their hideouts. Besides that, the clan members from Kuiarasu clan seem to have little contact with the rest of the village, always in the shadows, taking distance from the political affairs of the village, apparently. 


**Uchiha Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Bluff, Chakra Control, Concentration, Craft (Chemical, Calligraphy), Diplomacy, Hide, Intimidate, Knowledge (Ninja lore, the Underworld), Move silently, Ninjutsu, Taijutsu, Treat Injury. 

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Kuiarasu Clan Bloodline, Soul Eater), Genin, Genius Nin, Improved Ch akra Pool, Iron Will, Medical Expert, Will over Flesh.
