The Nara clan is a product of the Fire Country, namely Konohagakure. They are a small clan of incredibly intelligent people who are often regarded as the most tactical sort in the village. Many of the Nara clansmen become tactical advisors or squad leaders due to their innate planning abilities. Many of them also possess the ability to manipulate shadows, most notably Shikamaru 
Nara and his father, Shikaku.


**Nara Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any three skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Bluff, Chakra Control, Concen tration, Craft (Chemical, Writing), Decipher Script, Disable Device, Escape Artist, Gather Information, Genjutsu, Hide, Investigate, Jump, Knowledge (Behavioral Science, Earth & Life Science, Ninja Lore, Tactics), Listen, Move Silently, Ninjutsu, Research, Search and Spot. 

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Study, Alertness, Attentive, Blind -Fight, Combat 
Expertise, Combat Tactics, [Universal: Risky Strike], Deceptive, Defensive Martial Arts, Dodge, Educated, Genius Ninja, Genjutsu Adept, Improved Initiative, Insightful Reflexes, Ninjutsu Adept, Nin Weapon Proficiency, Precise Shot, Precise Shot, Stealthy, Talented Shinobi, Technique Focus. 

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Shadow Ninjutsu techniques. 

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1

**Special:** The following feats always figure in the Nara’s list of bonus feats: Shadow Arts.
