The Yamanaka clan is a product of the Fire Country, namely Konohagakure. They are a small clan of incredibly skilled people who are known as some of the best interrogators in the village. Many of the Yamanaka clansmen become interrogators due to their hijutsu, which allows them to enter the minds of others and control them or peer into their thoughts. 

**Yamanaka Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any three skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class sk ill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Bluff, Concentration, Chakra Control, Craft (Writing), Disguise, Escape Artist, Genjutsu, Hide, Investigate, Knowledge (Behavioral Science, Ninja Lore), Listen, Move Silently, Ninjutsu, Res earch, Search, Sense Motive, Sleight of Hand, and Spot. 

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Study, Alertness, Attentive, Chakra Affinity, Combat Tactics, Deceptive, Genius Ninja, Genjutsu Adept, Harmony, Improved Initiative, Iron Will, Nimble, Ninjutsu Adept, Nin Weapon Proficiency, Precise Shot, and Technique Focus. 

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Yamanaka Hijutsu techniques.
