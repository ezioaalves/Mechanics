Being a shinobi from the Hidden Mist, your village has a long tradition of powerful swordsman. Taking this occupation means that you were trained as a swordsman either by a mentor or even by one of the seven legendary swordsmen from Mist. 

**Kirigakure Swordsman (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 10+.

**Skills:** Select any three skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Balance, Climb, Chakra Control, Concentration, Craft (Mechanical), Escape Artist, Gather Information, Hide, Intimidate, Jump, Knowledge (ninja lore), Listen, Move Silently, Ninjutsu, Search, Swim, Sleight of Hand, Spot, Survival, Taijutsu, and Tumble. 

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Archaic Weapon Proficiency, Armor Proficiency, [Universal: Defensive Stance], Genin, Genjutsu Adept, Monkey Grip, Nin Weapons Proficiency, Ninjutsu Adept, [Universal: Risky Strike] and Taijutsu Adept. 

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +2
