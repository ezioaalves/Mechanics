The people of this clan are infamous for their ability to manipulate their bones in a variety of ways, known as Shikotsumyaku. Many fear them because most are known to have a love of battle that borders on bloodlust. 


**Kaguya Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Balance, Chakra Control, Concentration, Climb, Intimidate, Jump, Knowledge (Ninja lore, Tactics, The Underworld), Ninjutsu, Spot, Swim, Taijutsu, or Tumble.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Shikotsumyaku ), Archaic Weapon Proficiency, Combat Martial Arts, Defensive Martial Arts, Genin, Genius Ninja, Nin Weapons Proficiency, Ninjutsu Adept, Taijutsu Adept, or Toughness. 

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Kaguya Hijutsu techniques. 

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
