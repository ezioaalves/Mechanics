A clan most commonly found in the freezing northern parts of the world, many of the clan members migrated southward to the Land of Water. Their skills in controlling ice are fierce to behold and deadly to experience. 

**Yuki Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Hide, In timidate, Knowledge (Ninja lore ), Move Silently, Ninjutsu, Survival , and Taijutsu .

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Hyouma ), Chakra Affinity, Chakra Restoration, Elemental Specialization, Genin, Genius Ninja, Hand Seals Proficiency, Harmony , and Ninjutsu Adept .

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
