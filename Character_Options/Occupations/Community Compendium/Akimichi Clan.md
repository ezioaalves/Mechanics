The Akimichi clan is a product of the Fire Country, namely Konohagakure. They are a small clan of immensely powerful people who are often regarded as a gluttonous sort. Many of the Akimichi clansmen become squad leaders due to their power . Most of them also possess th e ability to alter their body size and mass. 

AKIMICHI CLAN (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any three skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks usin g that skill: Concentration, Chakra Control, Knowledge (Ninja Lore and Tactics), Listen, Ninjutsu, Search, Spot, and Taijutsu. 

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Akimichi Toughness, Brawl, Chakra Restoration, Combat Martial Arts, Combat Tactics, Comb at Throw, Defensive Martial Arts, Endurance, Gouken, Great Fortitude, Harmony, Powerful Maneuvers, Improved Chakra Pool, Ninjutsu Adept, Nin Weapon Proficiency, Resist Poison, Taijutsu Adept, and Toughness. 

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Akimichi Hijutsu techniques. 

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
