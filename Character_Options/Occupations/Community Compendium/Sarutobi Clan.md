The Sarutobi clan is one of the most renowned clans in the ninja world. They have produced many strong shinobi, most notably the Third Hokage of Konoha gakure, Hiruzen Sarutobi. They do not possess a specific bloodline like some clans, and yet they are still a group that could compare with the strongest of ninja clans. 

**Sarutobi Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any three skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Balance, Concentration, Chakra Control, Escape Artist, Hide , Jump, Knowledge (Ninja Lore), Listen, Move Silently, Ninjutsu, Sleight of Hand, Spot, Taijutsu, Tumble. 

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Acrobatic, Advanced Study, Archaic Weapons Proficiency, Athletic, Combat Martial Arts, Defensive Martial Arts, Dodge, Genin, Genius Ninja, Dodge, Nin Weapon Proficiency, Ninjutsu Adept, and Taijutsu Adept. 

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Konoha specific techniques. 

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +2
