The Hozuki clan, hailing from Kirigakure, are masters of water-based ninjutsu. Their signature technique allows them to turn their bodies into a liquid form, making them incredibly difficult to harm with physical attacks and granting them unparalleled stealth and infiltration capabilities, especially in or near water. They are a proud and powerful clan of the Hidden Mist.

**Hozuki Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Hide, Move Silently, Ninjutsu, Swim.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Hozuki), Ninjutsu Adept, Stealthy, Genin, Archaic Weapon Proficiency.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Hozuki Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
