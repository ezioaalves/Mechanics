The Funato clan are a fierce, seafaring clan of shinobi, known for their ruthless piracy and mastery of Water Release ninjutsu. They are at home on the ocean, using the waves and their own unique water techniques to overwhelm their enemies. They are a major power in the waters around the Land of Water, feared by sailors and coastal villages alike.

**Funato Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Intimidate, Knowledge (geography), Ninjutsu, Survival, Swim.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Kaze), Ninjutsu Adept, Athletic, Genin, [Universal: Risky Strike].

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Funato Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
