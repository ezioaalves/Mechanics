The Hirasaka clan are masters of a unique and intense form of fire ninjutsu, distinguished by their "Aoi" (Blue) flames. This special fire burns hotter and is more difficult to control than normal fire, a testament to the clan's exceptional chakra control and raw power. They are a clan of proud and powerful fire-wielders.

**Hirasaka Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Concentration, Intimidate, Ninjutsu, Taijutsu.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Aoi), Elemental Specialization (Fire), Ninjutsu Adept, Genin, Empower Technique.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Hirasaka Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
