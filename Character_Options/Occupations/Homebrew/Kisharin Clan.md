The Kisharin are a clan of weapon specialists, particularly known for their mastery of shuriken, kunai, and other throwing weapons. Their "Kazaguruma" (Pinwheel) bloodline grants them unparalleled accuracy and the uncanny ability to manipulate the trajectory of their projectiles in mid-air, making their attacks almost impossible to dodge.

**Kisharin Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Craft (mechanical), Ninjutsu, Sleight of Hand, Spot, Taijutsu.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Kazaguruma), Precise Shot, Far Shot, Genin, Nin Weapons Proficiency.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Kisharin Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
