The Fuma clan is a renowned shinobi clan, known for their aggressive tactics and their signature weapon: the giant, four-bladed Fuma Shuriken. They are a proud and powerful clan with a long history of battle, respected and feared for their skill in both armed and unarmed combat.

**Fuma Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Athletics, Intimidate, Ninjutsu, Survival, Taijutsu.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Living Weapon), Archaic Weapon Proficiency, Nin Weapons Proficiency, Genin, [Universal: Risky Strike].

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Fuma Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
