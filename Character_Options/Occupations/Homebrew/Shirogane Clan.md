The Shirogane clan is a reclusive and often feared clan, known for their unique "Medusa Eye" bloodline. This powerful dojutsu allows them to temporarily petrify or dramatically slow their opponents with a mere gaze. Due to the dangerous nature of their ability, they tend to live in isolation, honing their control to prevent accidental harm.

**Shirogane Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Concentration, Genjutsu, Intimidate, Ninjutsu.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Medusa Eye), Genjutsu Adept, Technique Focus (Genjutsu), Genin, Iron Will.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Shirogane Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
