The Fuuryuu clan are masters of lightning-infused taijutsu, thanks to their "Konpeki Shitai" (Azure Body) bloodline. They can coat their bodies in a shimmering blue aura of lightning chakra, dramatically increasing their speed, reflexes, and the destructive power of their physical attacks, similar to the Raikage's Lightning Release Armour.

**Fuuryuu Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Jump, Ninjutsu, Taijutsu, Tumble.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Konpeki Shitai), Taijutsu Adept, Improved Initiative, Genin, Run.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Fuuryuu Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
