The Tsuchigumo clan is a clan known for their powerful and often forbidden jutsu. They were once a prominent force, though their numbers have dwindled. They possess a deep knowledge of secret techniques, making them both respected and feared.

**Tsuchigumo Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Fuinjutsu, Knowledge (ninja lore), Ninjutsu, Survival.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Study, Sealweaver, Genin, Genius Ninja, Ninjutsu Adept.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Tsuchigumo Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
