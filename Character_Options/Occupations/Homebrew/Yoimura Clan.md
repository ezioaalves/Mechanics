The Yoimura clan are renowned artists and shinobi who practice a unique and deadly art form: the manipulation of explosive clay. By infusing clay with their chakra through special mouths on their hands, they can sculpt it into various animated forms that can be detonated at will. They are masters of demolition, art, and long-range combat.

**Yoimura Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Craft (sculpting, chemical), Hide, Ninjutsu, Sleight of Hand, Spot.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Clay Artist), Ninjutsu Adept, Precise Shot, Genin, Deceptive.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Yoimura Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
