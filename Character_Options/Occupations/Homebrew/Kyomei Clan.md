The Kyomei clan possesses the unique ability of "Chakra Resonance," allowing them to synchronize with the chakra of allies to boost their techniques, or disrupt the chakra of enemies to weaken their jutsu. They are invaluable support shinobi and formidable anti-ninjutsu specialists, capable of turning the tide of battle without throwing a single punch.

**Kyomei Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Concentration, Diplomacy, Ninjutsu, Sense Motive.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Chakra Resonance), Chakra Presence, Harmony, Genin, Ninjutsu Adept.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Kyomei Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
