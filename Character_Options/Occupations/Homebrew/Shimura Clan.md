The Shimura clan is a pragmatic and highly disciplined clan with deep roots in Konohagakure. Known for their unwavering, often severe loyalty to the village, they operate in the shadows, prioritizing the village's security above all else. They are skilled in a variety of covert arts and are not afraid to use any means necessary to achieve their goals, making them a respected, if feared, pillar of Konoha's strength.

**Shimura Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Fuinjutsu, Intimidate, Knowledge (civics, ninja lore, tactics), Ninjutsu, Taijutsu.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Kyuushou Kousei), Genin, Iron Will, Ninjutsu Adept, Taijutsu Adept, Trustworthy.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Konoha specific techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
