The Lee clan is a family of taijutsu specialists, known for their incredible work ethic and dedication to physical conditioning. While they have little to no aptitude for ninjutsu or genjutsu, their mastery of hand-to-hand combat is second to none, allowing them to overcome any obstacle through sheer effort and willpower. They are the embodiment of "hard work beats talent."

**Lee Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Balance, Climb, Concentration, Jump, Taijutsu, Tumble.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Genin, Taijutsu Adept, Endurance, Great Fortitude, Run.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn non-hijutsu Taijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
