The Yato clan is a feared clan of ferocious warriors who possess the "Chi no Bousou" (Rampage of Blood) bloodline. This ability allows them to enter a berserker state, granting them immense physical power and resilience at the cost of their own life force and tactical reasoning. They are forces of nature on the battlefield, driven by pure combat instinct.

**Yato Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Climb, Intimidate, Jump, Survival, Taijutsu.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Chi no Bousou), [Universal: Risky Strike], Endurance, Genin, Great Fortitude.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Yato Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
