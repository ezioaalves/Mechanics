The Garou clan are masters of atmospheric manipulation, thanks to their "Kesshuu" (Pressure) bloodline. They can increase or decrease the air pressure in a given area, creating crushing force fields, powerful shockwaves, or vacuums to suffocate their opponents. This makes them incredibly versatile and dangerous foes.

**Garou Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Concentration, Knowledge (physical sciences), Ninjutsu, Survival.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Kesshuu), Ninjutsu Adept, Focused, Genin, Empower Technique.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Garou Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
