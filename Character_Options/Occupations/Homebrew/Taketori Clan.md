The Taketori clan are masters of sound, utilizing their "Reikyou" bloodline to manipulate sound waves for a variety of effects. They are known for their powerful sound-based genjutsu and destructive ninjutsu, often employing small, chakra-infused bells or other instruments as catalysts for their techniques. Their abilities can soothe, hypnotize, or shatter, making them versatile and dangerous shinobi.

**Taketori Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Genjutsu, Ninjutsu, Perform (any), Sleight of Hand.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Reikyou), Genjutsu Adept, Ninjutsu Adept, Genin, Skill Focus (Perform).

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Taketori Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
