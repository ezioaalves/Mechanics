The Iburi clan is a reclusive clan whose members possess a unique and often unstable bloodline that allows them to turn their bodies into smoke. This ability grants them exceptional powers of infiltration and evasion, but its volatility means many clan members live in seclusion to protect themselves. Those who master this power are truly untouchable spies and assassins.

**Iburi Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Escape Artist, Hide, Move Silently, Ninjutsu.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Akekochuu), Stealthy, Dodge, Genin, Dodge.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Iburi Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
