The Yota clan were a mysterious, nomadic clan with the unique and powerful ability to manipulate the weather. By channeling their emotions into their chakra, they could freely create rain, wind, and lightning. This power made them both a potential blessing and a curse to those they encountered, as their emotional state directly influenced the environment around them.

**Yota Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Concentration, Ninjutsu, Perform (singing), Survival.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Genin, Ninjutsu Adept, Elemental Specialization (any), Weather Forecast, Force of Personality.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Yota Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
