The Hatake clan is a small but highly respected clan in Konohagakure, known for producing exceptionally talented and versatile shinobi like the famed "White Fang of the Leaf." They are renowned for their combat prowess, advanced tracking skills, and unwavering loyalty to their comrades, making them some of the most reliable ninja in the village.

**Hatake Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Chakra Control, Ninjutsu, Survival, Taijutsu, Treat Injury.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Genin, Ninjutsu Adept, Taijutsu Adept, Track, Archaic Weapon Proficiency.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Konoha specific techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +2
