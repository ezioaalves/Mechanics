The Kagetsu clan possesses the "Motteyochi" (Full Moon) bloodline, a unique trait that ties their power to the lunar cycle. They gain enhanced strength, speed, and chakra recovery during the night, with their abilities peaking during the full moon. They are masters of nighttime combat, infiltration, and assassination.

**Kagetsu Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skills from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Hide, Move Silently, Ninjutsu, Survival, Taijutsu.

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Motteyochi), Stealthy, Alertness, Genin, Dodge.

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Kagetsu Hijutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
