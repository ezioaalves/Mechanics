The Ishimaru Clan are some of the finest practitioners of Ninjutsu and Taijutsu of the Hidden Stone. Some of its more talented members form an entire squad of the Tsuchikage's elite guard . Their combat style is as simplistic as it is brutal. 

**Ishimaru Clan (Occupation)**

**Prerequisite:** Age 8+.

**Skills:** Select any two skill from the following list as permanent class skills. If a skill the character selects is already a class skill, he receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that skill: Climb, Hide, Jump, Move Silently, Ninjutsu, Survival, and Taijutsu. 

**Feat:** Select one of the following: Advanced Bloodline (Fortify), Archaic Weapon Proficiency, Armor Proficiency (light, medium), Brawl, Combat Martial Arts, Defensive Martial Arts, Genin, Nin Weapons Proficiency, Ninjutsu Adept, and Taijutsu Adept. 

**Affiliation:** You require 1 less success to learn Ishimaru Hujutsu techniques.

**Wealth Bonus Increase:** +1

**Reputation Bonus Increase:** +1
