# Gigantic

These humanoids are larger than normal humans, growing up to become between 8 and 14 feet tall.

Size: Large.
Ability Modifiers: +8 Strength, +4 Constitution, −2 Dexterity.
Natural Armor: Gigantic humans gain a +2 natural armor bonus to Defense.
Challenge Rating: +1.
Level Adjustment: +2.

*Source: NarutoD20 Redux v1.3, Chapter 2 – Races (p19). See [[00_-_Races_Rules|Races Rules]] for size and level-adjustment rules.*
