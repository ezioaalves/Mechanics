# Monstrous

These creatures were born with extreme deformations due to some birth defect or unusual experimentation.

Type: Monstrous Humanoid.
Ability Modifiers: +4 Strength, +2 Constitution, −2 Dexterity, −2 Charisma.
Deformities: Choose two deformities from the list below, gaining the benefit of each. If you choose two natural attacks, specify which one will be your primary natural weapon.
Bonus Feat: You gain Archaic Weapons Proficiency or Simple Weapons Proficiency as a bonus feat.
Challenge Rating: +1.
Level Adjustment: +2.

## Deformities (choose two)

- **Advanced Immune System (Ex):** Gain a +8 bonus to Fortitude saves against poisons and diseases.
- **Amphibious (Ex):** You can breathe normally when underwater.
- **Lithe (Ex):** Your ability modifiers change to −2 Strength, +4 Dexterity, +2 Constitution and −2 Charisma, and you gain a +2 racial bonus to Balance, Escape Artist and Tumble checks.
- **Natural Weapon (Bite):** You have vicious fangs and gain a single bite attack that deals 1d4 Piercing damage.
- **Natural Weapon (Claws):** You gain 2 claw attacks, dealing 1d4 Slashing damage. Your hands become claw-like.
- **Natural Weapon (Gore):** You grow one or more horns and gain a gore attack that deals 1d6 Piercing damage.
- **Natural Weapon (Tail Whip):** You grow a tail and gain a tail whip attack that deals 1d6 Bludgeoning damage.
- **Nocturnal (Ex):** You gain low-light vision, and darkvision up to 60 feet.
- **Powerful Legs (Ex):** Your base speed increases by 10 feet. Your feet might be capable of grasping and manipulating objects as if they were hands (discuss with your GM).
- **Scales (Ex):** You have scales covering your entire body, granting a +4 natural armor bonus to Defense.

## Mutation (feat)

*The following feat is available to Monstrous Humans.*

**Prerequisite:** Monstrous human, must be level 1.
**Benefit:** You gain one additional deformity.
