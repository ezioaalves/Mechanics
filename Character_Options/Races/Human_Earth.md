# Human/Earth

Humans native to the Earth country. They tend to be tougher and heavier than other humans.

Ability Modifiers: +2 Constitution, −2 Wisdom.
Tremorsense (Ex): Once per day as a swift action that requires concentration, you gain Tremorsense (30 ft) that lasts for 1 minute or until the end of your current encounter, whichever is shorter. (See p365)
Skills: +2 racial bonus to Climb checks.
Saving Throws: +1 racial bonus to save against earth-based attacks.
Elemental Affinity: Earth.
Languages: Add Speak Earth.
Other Languages: Sign Earth Common, Sign Common, Fire, Lightning, Water, Wind.

*Source: NarutoD20 Redux v1.3, Chapter 2 – Races (p19). Non-Human: see [[00_-_Races_Rules|Races Rules]].*
