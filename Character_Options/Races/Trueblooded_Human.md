# Trueblooded Human

These humans are the product of selective breeding through generations of highly-trained, Chakra-capable shinobi.

Ability Modifiers: +2 Strength, +2 Constitution, +2 Charisma.
Superior Vision: You gain low-light vision and darkvision up to 60 feet.
Bonus Feat: You receive Archaic Weapons Proficiency or Simple Weapons Proficiency as a bonus feat.
Other Languages: Sign Common, Earth, Fire, Lightning, Water, Wind.
Level Adjustment: +1.

*Source: NarutoD20 Redux v1.3, Chapter 2 – Races (p21). Non-Human: see [[00_-_Races_Rules|Races Rules]].*
