# Human/Fire

Humans native to the Fire country. They tend to have somewhat lighter hair and skin.

Ability Modifiers: +2 Charisma, −2 Strength.
Inspire Courage (Sp): Once per day as a swift action, you can inspire yourself and allies within 30 feet, granting them a +1 morale bonus to attack rolls, saves and skill checks, as well as a +4 morale bonus to saves against fear effects. This bonus lasts for 1 minute or until the end of the encounter (whichever comes first). At 10th and 20th level, you can use this power an additional time per day.
Skills: +1 racial bonus to Diplomacy and Gather Information checks.
Saving Throws: +1 racial bonus to save against fire-based attacks.
Elemental Affinity: Fire.
Languages: Add Speak Fire.
Other Languages: Sign Fire Common, Sign Common, Earth, Lightning, Water, Wind.
