# Human/Wind

Humans native to the Wind country. They are leaner than normal humans, with golden skin and pale-blond hair.

Ability Modifiers: +2 Dexterity, −2 Wisdom.
Quickness (Su): You may activate this ability as an instant action when making a Reflex save for half damage, up to once per day. If you succeed at the save, you take no damage instead.
Skills: +1 racial bonus to Balance and Tumble checks.
Saving Throws: +1 racial bonus to save against wind-based attacks.
Elemental Affinity: Wind.
Languages: Add Speak Wind.
Other Languages: Sign Wind Common, Sign Common, Earth, Fire, Lightning, Water.
