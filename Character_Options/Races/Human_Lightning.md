# Human/Lightning

Humans native to the Lightning country. They tend to be shorter and feature darker hair and rougher skin.

Ability Modifiers: +2 Dexterity, −2 Charisma.
Grounded (Su): As an instant action once per day, you take half damage from a single electric attack, before damage resistance and saves are applied. Must be used while directly contacting the ground, before damage.
Skills: +2 racial bonus to Tumble checks.
Saving Throws: +1 racial bonus to save against lightning-based attacks.
Elemental Affinity: Lightning.
Languages: Add Speak Lightning.
Other Languages: Sign Lightning Common, Sign Common, Fire, Lightning, Water, Wind.
