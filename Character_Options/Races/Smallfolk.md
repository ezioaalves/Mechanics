# Smallfolk

As their name implies, these humans are much smaller than normal, but proportioned like a normal human. They tend to be between 3 and 4 and a half feet tall.

Size: Small.
Ability Modifiers: +2 Dexterity.

*Source: NarutoD20 Redux v1.3, Chapter 2 – Races (p21). Small-size and non-Human rules: see [[00_-_Races_Rules|Races Rules]].*
