# Human/Water

Humans native to the Water country. They tend to be leaner, with longer limbs and darker hair.

Ability Modifiers: +2 Strength, −2 Charisma.
Hold Breath (Ex): You can hold your breath twice as long as normal before suffocating or drowning.
Skills: +2 racial bonus to Swim checks.
Saving Throws: +1 racial bonus to save against water-based attacks.
Elemental Affinity: Water.
Languages: Add Speak Water.
Other Languages: Sign Water Common, Sign Common, Earth, Fire, Lightning, Wind.

*Source: NarutoD20 Redux v1.3, Chapter 2 – Races (p20). Non-Human: see [[00_-_Races_Rules|Races Rules]].*
