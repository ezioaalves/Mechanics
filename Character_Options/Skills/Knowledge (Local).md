# Knowledge (Local)

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** No (untrained checks limited to DC 10)
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Knowledge (Local) covers legends, personalities, inhabitants, laws, customs, traditions, humanoids. Per the [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md), it absorbs **three** d20 Modern Knowledge subcategories:

- **Knowledge (Current Events)** — recent news, politics, sports, entertainment, foreign affairs
- **Knowledge (Popular Culture)** — music, personalities, genre fiction, urban legends, gaming
- **Knowledge (Streetwise)** — street and urban culture, local underworld personalities and events

In Kaihou this is the **village-pulse** skill — knowing who matters in this district right now, what rumours are circulating, which yakuza run which territory, and which Iron-Wards bureaucrat to talk to about which permit. The companion to [Knowledge (History)](Knowledge%20(History).md)'s past-event scope.

*Naruto D20 has no entry for this skill in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section — base rules apply.*

---

## Description

A character with Knowledge (Local) is *plugged in* — they recognise faces, know which name to drop, follow the rumour mill, and read the city the way other skills read a battlefield. Sendo Hunter intel work, Kumanoi Fun House information-brokering, Hoshigake smuggling-route reconnaissance, and most "what's happening in the Iron Docks tonight" questions go here.

## Check

### General DCs *(Pathfinder)*

| Question | DC |
|---|---|
| Know local laws, rulers, popular locations | 10 |
| Know a common rumour or local tradition | 15 |
| Know hidden organisations, rulers, locations | 20 |
| Recognise a face from the underworld or political fringe | 20+ |
| Identify a rare local custom or obscure event | 25+ |

### Identify a Humanoid Creature *(Pathfinder)*

- **DC = 10 + creature's CR** for humanoid creature types (most NPCs, foreign shinobi, mundane criminals).
- Each 5 points over the DC reveals an additional useful detail.

### Identify a Combat Feat / Class Feature in Use *(Pathfinder)*

When you witness a humanoid using a notable feat or class feature, you may attempt Knowledge (Local) to identify it.

- **DC = 10 + character's level** to identify the feat / feature.

> *In Kaihou, this is the skill that recognises another shinobi's training: "that's a Sendo Hunter style", "that's a Yoimura wall-defence drill", "that's the Kumanoi sliding-trap motion".*

### Current Events & Popular Culture *(absorbed from d20 Modern)*

| Question | DC |
|---|---|
| Recall a recent major news event | 10 |
| Identify a popular figure (entertainer, sports figure, political personality) | 10–15 |
| Recall an underground or restricted rumour | 20 |
| Recall a piece of cultural lore (genre fiction, urban legend) | 15 |

### Streetwise *(absorbed from d20 Modern)*

| Question | DC |
|---|---|
| Identify a common underworld figure | 15 |
| Know the territory of a specific yakuza group / criminal organisation | 15 |
| Know the protocol for approaching a black-market broker | 20 |
| Recall a hidden meeting location or fence's address | 20+ |

## Action

Usually **no action** (passive recall).

---

## Try Again

**No.** The check represents what you know.

---

## Special

- **Untrained** Knowledge (Local) checks are capped at DC 10.
- You can **take 10**. You **cannot take 20**.
- With library access (less useful for current events but useful for popular culture), the cap is lifted; 1d4 hours per check.
- A character with the **Educated** feat (d20 Modern) gains **+2** on any two Knowledge subcategories.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original values were "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Knowledge (Local) gains a **+2 bonus** on **[Diplomacy](Diplomacy.md)** Gather Information checks. *(Naruto D20 — formerly "Knowledge (Streetwise) → Gather Information".)*
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Diplomacy](Diplomacy.md)** (formerly Gather Information per the conversion table) gains a **+2 bonus** on Knowledge (Local) checks about current events. *(Naruto D20 — formerly "Gather Information → Knowledge (Current Events)" — the inverse direction; both apply, they're complementary skills.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (Knowledge (Ninja Lore) (Int).md, vault):** Cross-skill synergies preserved from the original generic Knowledge subcategory synergy list. Source-skill names updated per the conversion table (Knowledge (Streetwise) / (Current Events) / (Business) → Knowledge (Local); Gather Information → Diplomacy). Rank thresholds "5" converted to **2** via the −3 rule.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge/)):** Backbone — the topical scope (legends, personalities, inhabitants, laws, customs, traditions, humanoids), the standard DC framework, the humanoid-identification formula, the identify-combat-feats-in-use mechanic (DC 10 + character level).
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 76 — Knowledge subcategories):** Knowledge (Current Events), Knowledge (Popular Culture), Knowledge (Streetwise) topical scopes absorbed.

**Conflict resolution:** Three d20 Modern Knowledge subcategories collapse into one Pathfinder Knowledge (Local). Note: Knowledge (Business) is **not** absorbed here — per the conversion table it maps to **[Profession](Profession.md)**, so the original d20M "Knowledge (Business) → Profession" synergy is fully self-contained inside Profession and is not re-exported to Knowledge (Local).
