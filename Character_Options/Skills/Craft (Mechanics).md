# Craft (Mechanics)

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** Yes
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Craft (Mechanics) is one of the most setting-distinctive Craft skills in Kaihou. It covers:

- **Puppet construction and modification** (the Sunagakure-style puppeteer tradition, the Living Puppeteer forbidden art)
- **Enhancement-seal slots** crafted onto weapons and armor
- **Traps** (per the user's decision, Pathfinder's Craft (Traps) folds into Craft (Mechanics) rather than getting its own file)
- **Repair of mechanical devices** (the entire d20 Modern Repair skill folds in here for mechanical objects per the conversion table)
- **Standard mechanical fabrication** (gears, springs, mechanisms, locks)

*Naruto D20 modifies this skill in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section with the full puppet-crafting and enhancement-seal-slot mechanics.*

---

## Description

A character with Craft (Mechanics) can build a kunai with a folding handle, install a hidden dart-launcher in a forearm guard, craft a battle puppet from scratch, add enhancement-seal slots to a weapon, set a tripwire-activated snare in a corridor, and repair the puppet after the fight breaks it. The Puppeteer prestige class and the Sunagakure Puppeteer occupation depend heavily on this skill.

## Check

### Standard Mechanical Fabrication *(Pathfinder + d20 Modern Craft (Mechanical) baseline)*

For ordinary mechanical objects (locks, clockwork, simple devices), use the Pathfinder Craft framework: DC depends on item complexity; time ~1 hour per 5 DC.

### Enhancement Seal Slots *(Naruto D20, preserved verbatim)*

You can craft a seal slot onto a piece of equipment, allowing an enhancement seal to be added later.

| Seal Slot | Craft DC | Chakra Cost | Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| Minor | 15 | 5 | 4 hr |
| Superior | 20 | 10 | 8 hr |
| Greater | 25 | 15 | 16 hr |

**Failure** reduces the maximum number of seal slots the equipment can carry by **1** (minimum 0).

**Item limits:**
- **Armor equipment** (armor, cloak, ear, waist, wrists, feet, finger): max **1 seal slot each**.
- **Weapons:** up to **2 seal slots**, with no more than one being a Greater slot. Each head of a double weapon has its own seal slot (not shared with the other head).

*See Chapter 13 (p. 407) of NarutoD20 Redux v1.3.pdf for enhancement-seal mechanics.*

### Puppet Crafting *(Naruto D20, requires the Craft Puppet feat)*

You craft puppet cores and puppet components from various mechanical components.

| Puppet Core | Purchase DC | Craft DC | Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Combat Puppet Core**, Small | 15 | 20 | 8 hr |
| **Combat Puppet Core**, Medium | 20 | 23 | 12 hr |
| **Combat Puppet Core**, Large | 25 | 26 | 16 hr |
| **Utility Puppet Core**, Small | 13 | 16 | 8 hr |
| **Utility Puppet Core**, Medium | 18 | 19 | 12 hr |
| **Utility Puppet Core**, Large | 23 | 22 | 16 hr |
| **Living Puppet Core** (Animal or Human) | Varies | Varies | Varies |

**Normal-quality components** have Craft DC = Purchase DC + 5; material cost = Purchase DC − 2; crafting time = **1 hour per 5 points of DC**. To craft **S-class** components, see the epic uses (Chapter 14 of NarutoD20 Redux).

**Mastercraft Quality** requires the **Mastercrafter** feat. Mastercrafted components improve any numerical aspect except ability score bonuses or penalties; the bonus does not transfer to applied poisons. Normal Mastercrafting costs apply.

**Advanced Quality:** Apply **+10 Purchase DC** and **+15 Craft DC** modifiers to the normal-quality stats; advanced-quality components take one fewer point when installed (minimum 1).

**Modifications** to puppet cores:

| Modification | Purchase DC | Craft DC | Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| Modify Capacity | varies | varies | varies |
| Undo Modification (where allowed) | 5 | 20 | 1 hr |
| Free point | 20 | 25 | 4 hr |
| Utility point | 10 | 15 | 2 hr |
| Weapon point | 10 | 15 | 2 hr |
| Upgrade core | varies | varies | varies |

*See NarutoD20 Redux Chapter 13 (p. 402) for puppet-component lists and core-upgrade mechanics.*

### Living Puppets *(Naruto D20 — forbidden art)*

Crafting puppets from organic beings requires the **Craft Living Puppets** feat. This is the **Sasori-style forbidden art** — practising it openly is a kinjutsu-level offence in most villages. *In Kaihou, the Living Puppeteer prestige class is the canonical path; the practice is heavily restricted.*

### Trap Construction *(Pathfinder Craft (Traps), absorbed per user decision)*

Build mechanical traps — tripwires, pressure plates, deadfalls, spike pits, the snares the Dattoumaru clan train ambush specialists on.

| Trap Complexity | Craft DC | Time |
|---|---|---|
| Simple (tripwire alarm, basic snare) | 10 | 1 hour |
| Average (concealed pit, spring trap) | 15 | 4 hours |
| Complex (multi-stage, chakra-triggered conventional mechanism) | 20 | 1 day |
| Master-grade (Yoimura Wall trap, Foundry sabotage device) | 25+ | days to weeks |

> *Chakra-trigger components for traps are Craft (Mechanics) for the mechanism; if the trigger uses a sealing-tag, that part is Craft (Calligraphy).*

### Repair Mechanical Devices *(absorbs d20 Modern Repair)*

Repairing any mechanical device — vehicles, locks, puppets, weapons-with-mechanical-components — uses Craft (Mechanics).

**Puppet repair (Naruto D20, preserved verbatim from `Repair (Int).md`):**

- Removing **−1** from the defeat penalty: DC 20, takes 1 hour (half time if you beat DC by 5+). Cost: Wealth check DC 8.
- Repairing a **destroyed puppet**: DC 35, each attempt = 32 hours of work (4 days; half time if beat DC by 5+). Cost: Wealth check DC 17.
- **Installing a puppet component**: DC equal to the component's Purchase DC. Time: 1 hour per 5 points of Purchase DC.
- **Uninstalling a puppet component**: DC 15. Time: 10 minutes.
- A character without a puppet crafter's kit takes **−4** on these checks.

## Action

Per the times listed in each table.

---

## Try Again

Yes for repair. For crafting, failure consumes materials; jury-rig repairs (full-round action) only last until end of encounter.

---

## Special

- You can **take 10** on Craft (Mechanics). You can **take 20** on most uses except where chakra is spent on failure (e.g. enhancement-seal-slot crafting consumes the chakra cost regardless of success).
- Without the appropriate kit (puppet crafter's kit, inscriber's kit, mechanic's tools), take **−4** on the check.
- The **Craft Puppet** feat is required for puppet construction.
- The **Craft Living Puppets** feat is required for the forbidden Living Puppet variant.
- The **Mastercrafter** feat enables mastercraft components.
- The **Gearhead** feat (d20 Modern) and at least 1 rank grants **+2** on Craft (Mechanics) checks.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original values were "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Knowledge (Engineering)](Knowledge%20(Engineering).md)** (formerly Knowledge (Physical Sciences)) gains a **+2 bonus** on Craft (Mechanics) checks. *(Naruto D20.)*
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Craft (Mechanics) gains a **+2 bonus** on **[Disable Device](Disable%20Device.md)** checks against mechanical devices. *(Naruto D20.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (NarutoD20 Redux v1.3.pdf "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section, p. ~36):** Entire enhancement-seal-slot mechanics; puppet cores (Combat / Utility / Living, Small / Medium / Large), normal/mastercraft/advanced quality framework, modification table, the Craft Puppet and Craft Living Puppets feat requirements. Preserved verbatim.
- **Naruto D20 (Repair (Int).md, vault):** Puppet repair rules (DC 20 for −1 removal, DC 35 for destroyed), install/uninstall puppet components, puppet crafter's kit −4 penalty. Preserved verbatim and absorbed here since Repair as a standalone skill is dissolved into Craft per the conversion table.
- **Pathfinder (Craft skill framework, Craft (Traps) subcategory):** Trap construction folded in per the user's batch-7 decision. PF's Craft (Locks), Craft (Clockwork) functionally absorbed here as well — they're rare enough in a shinobi setting not to warrant separate files.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 67 — Craft (Mechanical); p. 80 — Repair):** Provides the d20 Modern baseline for both crafting mechanical objects and repairing them. Both fold into this single Craft (Mechanics) skill.

**Conflict resolution:** **Big consolidation in this file** — Naruto D20 Craft (Mechanical), the d20 Modern Repair skill, and Pathfinder Craft (Traps) all merge into one skill per the user's directive. The Repair vault file (`Repair (Int).md`) is replaced by this skill's puppet-repair section. Per the user's decision, **no separate Craft (Traps).md exists**; trap construction is a use of Craft (Mechanics).
