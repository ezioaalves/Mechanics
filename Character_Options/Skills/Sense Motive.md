# Sense Motive

**Ability:** Wisdom (Wis)
**Trained Only:** No
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Sense Motive is the skill of reading other people — detecting lies, intuiting hidden motives, recognising when someone is acting under outside influence, and intercepting secret messages encoded in conversation. In Kaihou it also absorbs the topical scope of the d20 Modern **Knowledge (Behavioral Sciences)** skill per the conversion table — psychology, sociology, and criminology fall under Sense Motive rather than under any Knowledge subcategory.

---

## Description

Where Perception asks whether you noticed *the world*, Sense Motive asks whether you noticed *the person*. The skill is the social shinobi's lie-detector — and in a setting where intent-reading dojutsu like the Kurugan exist, an ordinary character's Sense Motive is what stands in for that perception on every other shinobi.

---

## Check

### Hunch *(Pathfinder)*

- **DC 20** to get a "feeling" that something is wrong about a situation or that a person is trustworthy / untrustworthy.

### Sense Enchantment *(Pathfinder; absorbs Naruto D20 "Analyze Behavior")*

Recognise when a character's behaviour is being influenced by an enchantment, genjutsu, illusion, compulsion, or other outside source — even if the affected character is not aware of it.

- **DC 25** in the general case.
- **DC 15** if the target is dominated (or under a similarly absolute compulsion).

> The Naruto D20 vault file phrased this same use as **"Analyze Behavior"** at DC 25. Kaihou uses the Pathfinder formulation (clearer about the dominated-DC-15 case) and treats the two names as synonymous; the Naruto term "Analyze Behavior" is preserved as a vault-canonical alias for searchability.

### Discern Secret Message *(Pathfinder + d20 Modern, merged)*

When a Bluff user is conveying a hidden message in conversation, you may attempt Sense Motive to intercept it. The check is **opposed by the sender's Bluff**.

- **Success by 5+:** You intercept and understand the message.
- **Success by 4 or less:** You realise a message is being sent, but cannot decipher its content.
- **Failure by 4 or less:** You detect nothing.
- **Failure by 5+:** You misinterpret the message and may infer false information.

### Avoid Being Bluffed *(d20 Modern)*

When another character makes a Bluff check against you, your Sense Motive opposes it. The GM may roll your Sense Motive in secret so you do not know an attempt was made.

### Behavioral-Sciences Knowledge *(d20 Modern Knowledge (Behavioral Sciences), absorbed)*

Sense Motive in Kaihou also covers the topical knowledge of psychology, sociology, and criminology that the d20 Modern Knowledge (Behavioral Sciences) skill provided. Use Sense Motive for questions about *why* someone might be acting a certain way, characteristic behavioural patterns of a population, criminological inference about a perpetrator from a scene, etc.

---

## Action

- **Reactive** when used to oppose a Bluff (no action).
- **At least 1 minute** to assess a person's trustworthiness for a Hunch.
- **An entire evening** for a comprehensive social-situation read (Pathfinder).
- Naruto D20's vault file specified **1 minute** to analyse a target's behaviour for outside influence — Kaihou keeps this for the Sense-Enchantment use.

---

## Try Again

Generally **no**. You may make a fresh Sense Motive check for every distinct Bluff attempt made on you. For analysing behaviour, you usually cannot retry unless the person begins to act in a way that would raise new suspicion.

---

## Special

- You can take 10 on Sense Motive checks. You **cannot take 20** (d20 Modern preserved).
- A character with the **Alertness** feat (Pathfinder) gains **+2** on Sense Motive — and **+4** at 10+ ranks in Sense Motive. *(Pathfinder source — no −3 rank conversion applies since the threshold comes from PF directly.)*
- A character with the **Attentive** feat (d20 Modern) gains **+2** on Sense Motive checks.
- **Rangers** gain bonuses against favoured enemies (Pathfinder).

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original value was "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Sense Motive gains a **+2 bonus** on Diplomacy checks. *(Naruto D20 + d20 Modern.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (Sense Motive (Wis).md, vault):** "Analyze Behavior" use (DC 25 to detect outside influence over a target) and the Diplomacy synergy. The Analyze Behavior use is preserved as an alias for Pathfinder's Sense Enchantment (same mechanic, different name).
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/sense-motive](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/sense-motive/)):** Hunch DC, Sense Enchantment DC table (including the dominated-DC-15 case), Discern Secret Message graduation, Alertness scaling at 10+ ranks.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 81):** Bluff-opposing rule, secret-message detection, Attentive feat. Also: **Knowledge (Behavioral Sciences)** topic scope is absorbed here per the [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md).

**Conflict resolution:** Naruto D20's "Analyze Behavior (DC 25)" is mechanically identical to Pathfinder's "Sense Enchantment (DC 25)" — Pathfinder's formulation wins for clarity (it explicitly handles the dominated case at DC 15). Both names are preserved as aliases. The Alertness feat's 10+ ranks scaling is a Pathfinder-native threshold and is NOT subject to the −3 rule.
