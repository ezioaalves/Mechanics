# Acrobatics

**Ability:** Dexterity (Dex)
**Trained Only:** No
**Armor Check Penalty:** Yes

Acrobatics is the unified Pathfinder skill for nimble physical movement — balancing on precarious surfaces, jumping, tumbling past or through opponents, and breaking falls safely. In Kaihou it replaces the three separate d20 Modern skills **Balance**, **Jump**, and **Tumble**.

---

## Description

A shinobi using Acrobatics is moving in a way that ordinary footwork can't manage — across a tightrope, over a chasm, around an opponent's swing. Where Climb governs vertical surfaces and Swim governs water, Acrobatics governs everything else where balance, agility, and timing matter more than raw power.

---

## Check

### Balance on a Narrow or Precarious Surface *(Pathfinder; absorbs d20 Modern Balance)*

| Surface Width | Acrobatics DC |
|---|---|
| Greater than 3 ft. wide | 0 (no check) |
| 1–3 ft. wide | 5 |
| 7–11 in. wide | 10 |
| 2–6 in. wide | 15 |
| Less than 2 in. wide | 20 |

A successful check lets you move at half speed along the surface as a move action. Failure means you spend the move keeping your footing; failure by 5+ means you fall.

### Jumping *(Naruto D20 modifications take priority)*

Acrobatics is now the jump skill. Naruto D20's "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section explicitly modifies Jump — those modifications win per the Naruto > PF > d20M priority rule.

**Long Jump (horizontal):**
- Requires only a **10-ft running start** (Naruto D20 modification; d20 Modern required 20 ft).
- **DC = distance jumped in feet** (Pathfinder formula — simplest of the three and uncontested by Naruto D20).
- **DC = 2 × distance** (no running start).
- Long jumps cannot exceed your speed in a single jump.

**High Jump (vertical):**
- Requires only a **10-ft running start** (Naruto D20 modification).
- **DC = 2 + (2 × height in feet)** *(Naruto D20 formula — significantly easier than PF's `4 × height` or d20 Modern's `2 + 4 × height`).* For example, a 4 ft. jump is DC 10.
- **Double the DC** with no running start.
- **Maximum jumping height is doubled** compared to the base rules (Naruto D20 modification).

**Jumping Down:**
- **DC 15** to reduce the effective fall distance by **20 ft.** *(Naruto D20 — doubles d20 Modern's 10 ft. reduction. Same DC as Pathfinder's fall-break.)*

### Tumble Past Opponents *(Naruto D20 modifications take priority)*

- **DC 15** (Naruto D20) to move at **half your speed** through squares adjacent to opponents, provoking no attacks of opportunity. Make one check per opponent you move past; **+2 to DC for each opponent after the first**.
- Failure means you provoke attacks of opportunity as usual.

> *d20 Modern's Tumble had this at DC 20; Naruto D20 made it DC 15. Naruto D20 wins per priority.*

### Move Through a Threatened Square *(Pathfinder; alternative for non-Naruto-style movement)*

For cases that don't fit the Naruto D20 Tumble framing — moving without specifically tumbling — Pathfinder's formula applies:
- **DC = opponent's CMD** at half speed.
- **DC = opponent's CMD + 10** at full speed.
- **+2 to DC** for each additional opponent.

### Tumble Through Opponents *(Naruto D20 modification)*

- **DC 25** (Naruto D20; d20 Modern had this at DC 20) to roll, jump, or dive through squares **occupied by** opponents, moving as if they weren't there.
- **Half speed**; **+2 DC per opponent after the first**; check separately for each.
- You may **take a −10 penalty** on this check to move at your **full speed** instead (Naruto D20 addition).
- Failure means you stop before entering the opponent's square and provoke an AOO from them.

### Stand from Prone *(Naruto D20 addition)*

- **DC 20** to stand from prone as a **free action** immediately before taking a move action. Does not provoke attacks of opportunity. Failure wastes the move action.

### Break a Fall / Slow Fall *(Naruto D20 modification)*

- **DC 15** to treat a fall as if it were **20 ft. shorter** when calculating damage. *(Naruto D20 doubles d20 Modern's 10 ft. reduction. Same DC as the standard fall-break check.)*

### Tumble Surface Modifiers *(Naruto D20 addition)*

The surface you Tumble across affects the DC of the Tumble check:

| Surface type | DC modifier |
|---|---|
| Lightly obstructed (scree, some rubble, shallow bog, undergrowth) | +2 |
| Severely obstructed (cavern floor, dense rubble, dense undergrowth) | +5 |
| Sloped or angled | +2 |
| Lightly slippery (wet floor) | +2 |
| Severely slippery (ice sheet) | +5 |

*You cannot Tumble in a deep bog.*

---

## Action

- An Acrobatics check is part of another action (movement, an attack, falling) or a reaction (catching a fall). No separate action is required.
- Balancing while moving across a surface uses your move action.

---

## Try Again

Generally yes for new attempts in new situations. For jumping, you may try again on subsequent rounds. Falling-softly checks are once per fall.

---

## Special

- You can take 10 on an Acrobatics check in non-stressful conditions. You can take 20 only when there is no danger associated with failure (e.g. practising a jump in safety).
- The **Athletic / Acrobatic** family of feats grants +2 bonuses (see the specific feat).

### d20 Modern Tumble defensive-fighting bonus *(preserved)*

A character with **2 or more ranks** in Acrobatics gains a **+3 dodge bonus to Defense** (instead of the normal +2) when **fighting defensively**, and a **+6 dodge bonus** (instead of the normal +4) when engaging in **total defense**. *(d20 Modern Tumble rule; rank threshold converted from "5 ranks" via the −3 rank-prereq rule.)*

## Synergy

No specific cross-skill synergies in the merged file. Several Acrobatic feats interact with the skill (see feat descriptions).

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (NarutoD20 Redux v1.3.pdf, "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section, p. ~36):** **Jump** modifications — 10-ft running-start requirement, High Jump DC = 2 + (2 × height), doubled jump-down distance reduction, doubled max jumping height. **Tumble** modifications — DC 15 to tumble past opponents (vs d20M DC 20), DC 25 to tumble through opponents (vs d20M DC 20) with the −10-penalty-for-full-speed option, surface-modifier table, Stand-from-Prone DC 20, Slow Fall doubled to 20 ft. reduction. Defensive-fighting bonus (5+ ranks → +3/+6 dodge) is also from Naruto D20.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/acrobatics](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/acrobatics/)):** Balance DCs by surface width, the Move-Through-Threatened-Square (vs CMD) alternative formulation, Long Jump base formula (DC = feet jumped, uncontested by Naruto D20).
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 63–64 — Balance; p. 76 — Jump; p. 83–84 — Tumble):** Used as the d20 Modern reference; superseded by Naruto D20 wherever the two conflict (Jump and Tumble values).

**Conflict resolution:** d20 Modern split this into three skills (Balance, Jump, Tumble); Pathfinder unified them. Kaihou uses the Pathfinder unified model. For **Jump** and **Tumble** specifically, Naruto D20's "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section explicitly modifies the base rules — those modifications take priority over both PF and d20M (PF's High Jump `4 × height` and d20M's `2 + 4 × height` are both replaced by Naruto D20's `2 + 2 × height`). The defensive-fighting bonus rank threshold is converted from "5 ranks" to **2 ranks** via the −3 rule.
