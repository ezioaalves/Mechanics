# Ninjutsu

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** Yes
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Ninjutsu is the discipline of performing and analysing ninjutsu techniques — the broadest of the chakra-skill categories, covering everything from elemental manipulation to utility and combat applications that don't fall under Genjutsu, Taijutsu, Fuinjutsu, or Chakra Control.

This skill has no Pathfinder or d20 Modern equivalent; it is Naruto D20 native.

---

## Description

When a shinobi performs a ninjutsu technique they have learned, the Ninjutsu skill determines whether the performance succeeds. When a shinobi observes a ninjutsu being performed, this same skill determines whether they can identify what it was.

---

## Check

### Perform a Ninjutsu Technique

The DC to perform a ninjutsu is specified by the technique itself. Roll Ninjutsu against that DC.

### Identify a Witnessed Ninjutsu

When you witness a ninjutsu, you may attempt a Ninjutsu check to identify it, provided the technique is one known to you.

- **DC = 10 + the technique's Complexity Rating + the technique's Rank**

A success identifies the technique by name and reveals its basic effect.

---

## Action

- Identifying a witnessed ninjutsu is a **free action**.
- Performing a ninjutsu takes the action specified by the technique.

---

## Try Again

You are not allowed a retry to perform or identify a ninjutsu. (You may attempt to perform the technique again with another action of its required type.)

---

## Special

- A character with the **Ninjutsu Adept** feat gains a **+2 bonus on Ninjutsu checks**.
- A character can take 10 but **cannot take 20** when performing a ninjutsu technique.

---

## Synergy

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Ninjutsu gains a **+2 bonus on Learn checks** for Ninjutsu techniques. *(Naruto D20 originally "5 ranks"; converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule.)*
- A character with **7 or more ranks** in Ninjutsu can create Ninjutsu *hijutsu* or *kinjutsu* techniques without suffering the standard additional penalty. *(Naruto D20 originally "10 ranks"; converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule.)*
