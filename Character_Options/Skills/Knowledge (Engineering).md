# Knowledge (Engineering)

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** No (untrained checks limited to DC 10)
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Knowledge (Engineering) is the **applied-sciences scholarship skill of Kaihou**. It covers buildings, fortifications, machinery, mechanical and electrical systems, and the technical knowledge that operates and maintains them. Per the [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md), it absorbs two d20 Modern skills:

- **Knowledge (Physical Sciences)** — astronomy, chemistry, mathematics, physics, engineering
- **Use Computer** — operating, programming, and breaking into computer systems

In the Naruto-D20 setting, the Yoimura clan and the Foundry District are this skill's natural home — anything from "how does this wall hold up" to "how does this Yoimura demolition charge work" to "how do I disable this sensor" lives here.

*Naruto D20 has no entry for this skill in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section — the unmodified Pathfinder rules apply, augmented with d20 Modern's detailed Computer Use mechanics.*

---

## Description

A character with Knowledge (Engineering) knows how built things work — walls, bridges, traps, weapons, vehicles, computers, sealed mechanisms whose triggers are physical rather than chakra-based. It pairs with [Craft](Craft.md) (which builds those things) and [Disable Device](Disable%20Device.md) (which breaks or bypasses them).

## Check

### General DCs *(Pathfinder)*

| Question | DC |
|---|---|
| Identify a dangerous construction feature | 10 |
| Determine a structure's style or age | 15 |
| Determine a structure's weakness (where it would fail under stress) | 20 |
| Recognise a rare construction technique or obscure machinery | 25+ |

### Identify a Construct *(Pathfinder)*

- **DC = 10 + creature's CR** to identify a construct (golem, animated armour, modern robot, Yoimura sealed-explosive automaton).
- Each 5 points by which you exceed the DC reveals an additional useful detail (weakness, special ability, control method).

### Computer / Sealed-Device Operation *(d20 Modern, absorbed)*

Routine computer use does not require a check. Make a Knowledge (Engineering) check when **finding files** on an unfamiliar system, **breaking through security**, **writing programs**, or **operating a remote device** for which you don't have direct authorization.

**Find a file on an unfamiliar system:**

| Size of Site | DC | Time |
|---|---|---|
| Personal computer | 10 | 1 round |
| Small office network | 15 | 2 rounds |
| Large office network | 20 | 1 minute |
| Massive corporate network | 25 | 10 minutes |

**Defeat computer security** *(trained-only use)*:

| Level of Security | DC |
|---|---|
| Minimum | 20 |
| Average | 25 |
| Exceptional | 35 |
| Maximum | 40 |

- Failing by **5 or more** alerts the system administrator.
- Beating the DC by **10 or more** automatically succeeds at all subsequent security checks at that site for the duration of the session.

**Cover Tracks** (optional, DC 20): impose a **−5 penalty** on attempts to identify you if your activity is detected.

**Write a program** (DC 20, 1 hour): grants **+2 circumstance bonus** to a specific later task.

**Operate a remote device:** GM sets DC by complexity. Failure by 5+ alerts the administrator.

> *In a primarily shinobi-flavoured Kaihou campaign, the Computer Use side of this skill is **rare** — most of the village's information systems are scrolls, archives, and ledgers (handled by [Linguistics](Linguistics.md) Research), not digital. Foundry-District workshops and certain Sendo-Hunter forensic tools may use this side; treat the listed DCs as guidance.*

---

## Action

- Most Knowledge checks are no action (passive recall).
- Computer-Use checks take the listed time per task.
- Construct-identification is no action.

---

## Try Again

No for general Knowledge recall. Yes for most Computer Use tasks (a hacker can keep trying), though each failure may compound the risk of detection.

---

## Special

- **Untrained** Knowledge (Engineering) checks are capped at DC 10. Defeating computer security cannot be done untrained.
- You can **take 10** on Knowledge (Engineering). You can **take 20** on Computer Use tasks where no detection penalty applies.
- A character with the **Educated** feat (d20 Modern) gains **+2** on any two Knowledge subcategories of choice.
- A character with the **Technologist** feat (Pathfinder) automatically identifies objects of Craft DC 15 or less without a check.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original values were all "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Knowledge (Engineering) gains a **+2 bonus** on the appropriate **Craft** check (formerly Knowledge (Physical Sciences) → Craft (chemical, electronic, mechanical, structural); now [Craft (Chemical)](Craft%20(Chemical).md), [Craft (Mechanics)](Craft%20(Mechanics).md), [Craft (Explosives)](Craft%20(Explosives).md), [Craft (Weapons)](Craft%20(Weapons).md), [Craft (Armor)](Craft%20(Armor).md) — apply contextually). *(Naruto D20.)*
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Knowledge (Engineering) gains a **+2 bonus** on **[Linguistics](Linguistics.md) Research** checks made using a computer or digital archive. *(Naruto D20 — formerly Computer Use → Research.)*
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Knowledge (Engineering) gains a **+2 bonus** on **[Survival](Survival.md)** checks made using a computer or GPS-equivalent device. *(Naruto D20 — formerly Computer Use → Navigate; see also [Survival.md](Survival.md) Source Notes.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (Computer Use (Int).md, vault):** Cross-skill synergies (Computer Use → Navigate and Research using a computer). Rank threshold "5" converted to **2** via the −3 rule. Naruto D20's PDF has no "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" entry for Knowledge (Physical Sciences) or Computer Use — base rules apply. *(The original Knowledge (Physical Sciences) → Craft (chemical) synergy is now Knowledge (Engineering) → [Craft (Chemical)](Craft%20(Chemical).md); not merged into a Pathfinder "Alchemy".)*
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge/)):** Backbone — the topical scope (buildings, aqueducts, bridges, fortifications, technology), the standard DC framework, the construct-identification formula, Technologist feat.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 65 — Computer Use; p. 77 — Knowledge (Physical Sciences) topic scope):** The detailed Computer Use mechanics (Find File, Defeat Computer Security, Cover Tracks, Write Program, Operate Remote Device) preserved as a sub-skill.

**Conflict resolution:** Two d20 Modern skills (Knowledge (Physical Sciences) and Computer Use) collapse into one Pathfinder Knowledge (Engineering) skill. Both topical scopes and the Computer Use detailed mechanics are preserved. The Naruto D20 cross-skill synergies are re-mapped to the new skill names (Craft subcategories instead of Craft (chemical), Linguistics instead of Research, Survival instead of Navigate).
