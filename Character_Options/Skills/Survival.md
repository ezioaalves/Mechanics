# Survival

**Ability:** Wisdom (Wis)
**Trained Only:** No
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Survival is the wilderness skill — staying alive away from the village, following tracks, predicting weather, and navigating overland without a guide. In Kaihou it absorbs the three separate d20 Modern skills **Survival**, **Navigate**, and **Use Rope** (the last not separately defined in the local d20 Modern SRD).

---

## Description

A shinobi with Survival can keep themselves and others fed and intact in hostile terrain, follow trails across varied ground, and find their way across great distances without maps. The Tracking-feat-gated tracking use is the most distinctive: ordinary characters with high Survival can read terrain, but the **Track** feat unlocks the ability to actively follow trails.

---

## Check

### Tracking *(Pathfinder; requires the Track feat or equivalent to follow trails)*

Following a trail uses Survival against a DC that varies with the surface:

| Surface | Base DC |
|---|---|
| Very soft ground (snow, mud) | 5 |
| Soft ground (dirt, sand, grass) | 10 |
| Firm ground (most outdoor terrain, indoor floors) | 15 |
| Hard ground (rock, indoor stone) | 20 |

**Movement while tracking:**
- Half speed: no penalty.
- Normal speed: **−5** to the Survival check.
- Double speed: **−20** to the Survival check.

**Modifiers:**

| Condition | DC Modifier |
|---|---|
| Every three creatures in the tracked group | −1 |
| Tracked creature size — Fine | +8 |
| Tracked creature size — Diminutive | +4 |
| Tracked creature size — Tiny | +2 |
| Tracked creature size — Small | +1 |
| Tracked creature size — Medium | 0 |
| Tracked creature size — Large | −1 |
| Tracked creature size — Huge | −2 |
| Tracked creature size — Gargantuan | −4 |
| Tracked creature size — Colossal | −8 |
| Every 24 hours since the trail was made | +1 |
| Each hour of rain since the trail was made | +1 |
| Fresh snow since the trail was made | +10 |
| Poor visibility (overcast, moonless night) | +3 to +6 |

### Get Along in the Wild

**DC 10** to move up to **half your overland speed** while hunting and foraging (no food or water supplies needed). You can provide for one additional person per **2 points** by which your check exceeds 10.

### Weather Resistance

**DC 15** to grant yourself a circumstance bonus on Fortitude saves against severe weather: **+2** while moving up to half speed, **+4** while stationary. You can extend the bonus to one additional person per point by which your check exceeds 15.

### Predict Weather

**DC 15** to predict the weather for the next 24 hours. Extend the prediction by one day per 5 points your check exceeds the DC.

### Avoid Getting Lost & Avoid Natural Hazards

**DC 15** (Pathfinder) / **DC 18** (d20 Modern) to avoid getting lost or to avoid natural hazards like quicksand.

> *Kaihou uses DC 15 per Pathfinder priority.*

### Overland Navigation *(d20 Modern Navigate, absorbed)*

When travelling a great distance without a map, make a Survival check at the trip's start:

| Trip Length | DC |
|---|---|
| Short (a few hours) | 20 |
| Moderate (a day or two) | 22 |
| Long (up to a week) | 25 |
| Extreme (more than a week) | 28 |

- **Success:** You move via the best reasonable course. You arrive in the expected time.
- **Failure:** You arrive but it takes twice as long.
- **Failure by 5+:** You travel the expected time but only get halfway, then become lost.

A second check (**DC 20**) regains your path if you became lost; failure costs half a day before you can try again.

**Branching path or multi-way junction:** **DC 20** to intuit the correct branch toward a known destination.

**Determine position from natural landmarks (constellations etc.):** **DC 15** — requires a clear view of the night sky.

### Rope Use *(absorbed; no separate d20 Modern section in the local SRD)*

Survival in Kaihou is also the skill for general rope-handling tasks: tying knots that hold under load, lashing items together, securing climbers. The d20 Modern SRD in the vault does not define Use Rope as a standalone skill; per the [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md), any rope use falls under Survival. (Specific applications: escaping a binding falls under **[Escape Artist](Escape%20Artist.md)**; rigging a structure falls under **[Craft](Craft.md)**.)

## Action

- A tracking check is rolled when first picking up a trail and whenever conditions change (terrain, weather, trail-age threshold crossed).
- Wilderness survival checks (Get Along, Weather Resistance) are made daily.
- Overland navigation checks are made at the start of the trip and at branching points.

---

## Try Again

- **Tracking:** Yes — after at least 1 hour outdoors or 10 minutes indoors.
- **Wilderness survival, weather:** Yes, on subsequent attempts.
- **Overland navigation:** Yes (DC 20 to recover from being lost; costs half a day per failed recovery).

---

## Special

- You can **take 10** on a Survival check. You can **take 20** when tracking (if there's time), when determining position from landmarks, or in any other no-danger situation — but **not** on periodic checks to get along in the wild.
- The **Track** feat is required to follow a trail in most editions; without it, Survival reveals tracks only on a DC 25 check (Pathfinder rule).
- A trained character automatically determines true north (Pathfinder).
- The **Guide** feat (d20 Modern) grants **+2** on Survival.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original value was "5 ranks in Search".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Perception](Perception.md)** (formerly Search per the conversion table) gains a **+2 bonus** on Survival checks made **to find or follow tracks**. *(Naruto D20.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (Survival (Wis).md, vault):** The Search → Survival tracking synergy. The skill name "Search" is replaced with "Perception" per the [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md); the rank threshold "5 ranks" is converted to **2 ranks** via the −3 rule.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/survival](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/survival/)):** Backbone — tracking DCs by surface, modifiers, get-along-in-the-wild, weather resistance, predict weather, the DC 15 avoid-lost check.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 78 — Navigate; p. 82 — Survival):** Overland Navigation DCs (the trip-length table), the regain-path DC 20, the position-by-constellations DC 15, the Guide feat. d20 Modern's "DC 18 avoid lost" is superseded by Pathfinder's DC 15. Use Rope is not defined in the local SRD; absorbed conceptually via the conversion table.
- **Naruto D20 (Navigate (Int).md, vault):** Computer Use → Navigate synergy. **Dropped** in Kaihou — Navigate is now Survival (Wis), not a separate Int-based skill, and the computer-aided navigation context is rare enough in a shinobi setting that the synergy was deemed not worth preserving. *(If this is wrong and computer-aided navigation is a relevant scenario in your campaign, the converted bonus would be: "2+ ranks in Knowledge (Engineering) → +2 on Survival checks made using a computer or GPS-equivalent device".)*

**Conflict resolution:** Three d20 Modern skills (Survival, Navigate, Use Rope) collapse into one Pathfinder Survival skill. Pathfinder's DC values win where they differ (e.g. avoid-lost DC 15 vs d20M's DC 18). The Naruto D20 tracking-synergy bonus is preserved with the rank threshold converted from 5 to **2**. The Navigate (Int).md Computer-Use synergy is **dropped** as setting-irrelevant; flagged for GM review.
