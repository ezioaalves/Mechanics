# Linguistics

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** Yes
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Linguistics is Pathfinder's unified writing-and-scholarship skill. In Kaihou it absorbs **three** d20 Modern skills: **Decipher Script**, **Forgery**, and **Research**.

The Speak/Read-Language mechanics are **not used** in Kaihou — the Naruto universe is treated as monolingual at the table, so ranks in Linguistics do not buy languages.

---

## Description

A character with Linguistics can forge or detect forged documents, decipher unfamiliar scripts and codes, and conduct research through written sources. Where Diplomacy gathers information by talking to people, Linguistics gathers it by reading.

---

## Check

### Decipher Writing *(Pathfinder + d20 Modern Decipher Script, merged)*

Decipher writing in an unknown script, code, or fragmented text:

| Complexity | DC |
|---|---|
| Simplest messages | 20 |
| Standard codes and scripts | 25 |
| Intricate, exotic, or very old writing | 30+ |

**Failure** triggers a secret DC 5 Wisdom check by the GM to determine if you draw a false conclusion. (Pathfinder formulation; d20 Modern used DC 10 — Pathfinder's wins per priority.)

**Helpful texts** or applicable code-breaking aids: typically **+2 circumstance bonus** to the check.

### Create Forgeries *(d20 Modern Forgery, preserved with extra detail)*

Pathfinder Linguistics has a forgery rule but d20 Modern's table is significantly more detailed. Kaihou keeps the d20M tables as the canonical model.

**Time to forge:**

| Document Type | Check Modifier | Time |
|---|---|---|
| Simple (typed letter, business card) | +0 | 10 min |
| Moderate (letterhead, business form) | −2 | 20 min |
| Complex (stock certificate, driver's licence) | −4 | 1 hr |
| Difficult (passport) | −8 | 4 hr |
| Extreme (military/law-enforcement ID) | −16 | 24 hr |

**Familiarity modifiers** (apply to the forger's check):

| Familiarity | Modifier |
|---|---|
| Unfamiliar (seen once for less than a minute) | −4 |
| Fairly familiar (seen for several minutes) | +0 |
| Quite familiar (on hand, or studied at leisure) | +4 |
| Forger has produced other documents of the same type | +4 |
| Document includes a specific signature to reproduce | −4 |

The GM makes the forger's check **secretly**, so the forger does not know how good their work is.

### Detect Forgeries *(d20 Modern Forgery preserved)*

The original forger's Linguistics check (when creating the document) is opposed by a Linguistics check from any examiner. If the examiner's result equals or exceeds the forger's, the forgery is detected.

**Examiner modifiers:**

| Examiner's Condition | Modifier |
|---|---|
| Document type unknown to examiner | −4 |
| Document type somewhat known | −2 |
| Document type well known | +0 |
| Document put through additional tests¹ | +4 |
| Examiner only casually reviews the document¹ | −2 |

¹ Cumulative with one of the first three knowledge conditions.

A document that contradicts procedure, prior knowledge, or requires the examiner to give up something can increase suspicion (favourable circumstances for the examiner).

### Research a Topic *(d20 Modern Research, absorbed)*

Research uses Linguistics to extract information from written sources (libraries, archives, scroll repositories, the Kani Library and similar).

- **Base DC** depends on topic obscurity (GM-set).
- **General info DC 10** → **Protected info DC 25** (mirroring the Diplomacy Gather-Information ladder, but through *written* sources rather than people).
- **Time:** Approximately **1d4 hours** per check.
- Specific facts, dates, maps, etc. add **+5 to +15** to the DC.

---

## Action

- **Decipher writing:** at least 1 minute (about 1 page of text per minute on success).
- **Forging:** 10 minutes to 24 hours per page (see Document Type table).
- **Detecting a forgery:** 1 round per page.
- **Research:** 1d4 hours.

---

## Try Again

- **Decipher:** No, unless conditions change or new information is uncovered.
- **Forge:** Yes (with fresh materials).
- **Detect forgery:** Each examiner gets one check per document examination.
- **Research:** Yes.

---

## Special

- You can **take 10** on most Linguistics uses. You can **take 20** on Research and on Decipher Writing (if time permits and there's no danger), but **not** on forgery checks (since the GM is keeping the check secret).
- A character with the **Studious** feat (d20 Modern) gets **+2** on Decipher Writing and Research uses of Linguistics.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original value was "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Knowledge (Engineering)](Knowledge%20(Engineering).md)** (formerly **Computer Use** per the conversion table) gains a **+2 bonus** on Linguistics checks made for **Research using a computer or digital archive**. *(Naruto D20, from `Research (Int).md`.)*
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Linguistics gains a **+2 bonus** on **Fuinjutsu checks** made to double-check or identify seal arrays. *(Naruto D20, originally "Decipher Script" synergy; see [Fuinjutsu](Fuinjutsu.md).)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (Research (Int).md):** Computer Use → Research synergy. Skill names updated to current convention (Computer Use → Knowledge (Engineering); Research → Linguistics). The Fuinjutsu seal-array synergy already documented in [Fuinjutsu.md](Fuinjutsu.md) is cross-referenced here.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/linguistics](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/linguistics/)):** Backbone — unified scholarship-and-writing skill model, Decipher DCs (20/25/30+), DC 5 Wisdom save on decipher failure, forgery/detect-forgery framework, take-10/take-20 defaults.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 69 — Decipher Script; p. 73 — Forgery; p. 81 — Research):** The detailed Forgery tables (document type, familiarity, examiner condition) preserved as canonical. The Studious feat preserved as an addition.

**Conflict resolution:**

- **Languages:** Both d20 Modern and Pathfinder include language-learning rules under their related skills. **Kaihou drops the languages mechanic entirely** — the Naruto universe is treated as monolingual at the table, so ranks in Linguistics do not buy languages and there is no Speak/Read-Language mechanic.
- **Decipher false-conclusion save:** d20M DC 10 Wisdom; PF DC 5. PF wins.
- **Forgery tables:** PF Linguistics has thinner tables; d20M has more detailed ones. The detailed d20M tables are preserved as they aren't *conflicting* with PF — they're a more granular implementation of the same mechanic.
