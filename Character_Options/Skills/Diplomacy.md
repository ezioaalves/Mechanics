# Diplomacy

**Ability:** Charisma (Cha)
**Trained Only:** No
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Diplomacy is the skill of persuasion, negotiation, and shifting attitudes through reasoned discourse. In Kaihou it replaces the d20 Modern skills **Diplomacy** and **Gather Information**.

---

## Description

A successful Diplomacy check can change another character's attitude, make a request, win a negotiation, or — through the gather-information use — collect rumours, gossip, and discreet intelligence from a populated area. Diplomacy is the social shinobi's primary skill; even in a village whose culture prizes silence, the right words to the right person at the right time can move a clan.

---

## Check

### Influence NPC Attitude *(Pathfinder)*

The DC depends on the creature's starting attitude. The GM sets the initial attitude based on circumstance.

| Starting Attitude | Diplomacy DC |
|---|---|
| Hostile | 25 + creature's Cha modifier |
| Unfriendly | 20 + creature's Cha modifier |
| Indifferent | 15 + creature's Cha modifier |
| Friendly | 10 + creature's Cha modifier |
| Helpful | 0 + creature's Cha modifier |

- **Success:** Attitude improves one step. Each additional 5 points above the DC improves by another step (max +2 steps in a single check).
- **Failure by 4 or less:** No change.
- **Failure by 5+:** Attitude worsens by one step.

You cannot use this on the same creature more than **once every 24 hours**.

### Opposed Negotiations *(d20 Modern preserved)*

When two characters are negotiating (or advocating opposing cases before a third party), they roll opposed Diplomacy checks. The higher result prevails.

### Gather Information *(absorbed from d20 Modern Gather Information)*

Spend time in taverns, markets, or other populated venues passing out small bribes or buying drinks to collect news, rumours, and intelligence.

| Type of Information | DC | Approximate Cost (Purchase DC) |
|---|---|---|
| General (local rumour, gossip) | 10 | 5 |
| Specific (focused question) | 15 | 10 |
| Restricted (not widely known; need a knowledgeable contact) | 20 | 15 |
| Protected (dangerous to ask about; may attract attention) | 25 | 20 |

- A Gather-Information check takes **1d4+1 hours** (d20 Modern) or "1d4 hours" (Pathfinder) — Kaihou uses **1d4+1 hours** per the d20 Modern value (closer to lived experience at the table).
- You can increase the bribe to gain a circumstance bonus: **+2 to Purchase DC per +1 bonus** on the skill check.
- Asking about restricted or protected information may attract notice.

---

## Action

- **Influence attitude:** 1 minute of continuous interaction (Pathfinder).
- **Make a request:** 1 or more rounds depending on complexity.
- **Opposed negotiation:** as the encounter takes.
- **Gather information:** 1d4+1 hours.

---

## Try Again

- **Influence attitude:** Not within 24 hours on the same creature.
- **Opposed negotiation:** No retry on the same round; the encounter may produce new opportunities.
- **Gather information:** Yes, but every attempt takes 1d4+1 hours, and repeated questioning about a topic may draw attention.

---

## Special

- You can take 10 on a Gather-Information check but **cannot take 20** (d20 Modern preserved).
- A character with the **Trustworthy** feat (d20 Modern) gets a +2 bonus on Gather-Information uses of Diplomacy.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original values were all "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **Bluff** gains a **+2 bonus** on Diplomacy checks. *(Naruto D20 + d20 Modern.)*
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **Sense Motive** gains a **+2 bonus** on Diplomacy checks. *(Naruto D20 + d20 Modern.)*
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **Knowledge (Nobility)** (formerly Knowledge (Civics) — see the [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md)) gains a **+2 bonus** on Diplomacy checks **when pleading a case**. *(Naruto D20.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (Diplomacy (Cha).md, vault):** Cross-skill synergies (Bluff, Sense Motive, Knowledge (Civics→Nobility) — all converted via the −3 rule).
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/diplomacy](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/diplomacy/)):** Attitude-shifting table (the canonical 5-tier model), once-per-24-hours restriction, action timing.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 70):** Opposed-negotiation rules, Gather-Information table (DCs + Purchase DCs + time), Trustworthy feat. The d20 Modern attitude table is superseded by Pathfinder's clearer formulation.

**Conflict resolution:** d20 Modern and Pathfinder both have attitude-influence rules but with slightly different table structures. Per Naruto > PF > d20M, the Pathfinder model wins (it scales with the creature's Cha mod, which is a more interesting design). Where the Naruto D20 supplemental file used "5 ranks" as a synergy threshold, the −3 rank-prereq rule converts those to **2 ranks** to match Pathfinder's narrower rank progression.
