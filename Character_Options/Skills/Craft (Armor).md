# Craft (Armor)

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** Yes
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Craft (Armor) is the skill for forging, modifying, and repairing armor — leather harnesses, mail, scaled plate, reinforced shinobi-pattern armor, the Ishimaru clan's heavy ceremonial plate. Per the [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md), it also absorbs the **armor-side of the d20 Modern Repair skill**.

*Naruto D20 has no entry for "Craft (Armor)" in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section — base Pathfinder rules apply, augmented with the Repair-for-armor mechanics absorbed from d20 Modern.*

---

## Description

A character with Craft (Armor) forges plate, lames, scales, leather, and the fittings that hold them together; modifies armor for fit and chakra channeling; mounts decorative or functional fittings; and repairs damage from the field. The Ishimaru ceremonial-armor tradition and the Yoimura reinforced-pattern lines are both this skill's natural homes.

---

## Check

The Pathfinder Craft framework applies. Specific item DCs are listed in the [Equipment Shop](../../Equipment/Equipment_Shop.md) and the clan-ground inventories.

### Common Armor Craft DCs *(Pathfinder baseline)*

| Armor Type | Craft DC |
|---|---|
| Light armor (padded, leather, studded leather) | 12 |
| Medium armor (hide, scale, chainmail) | 15 |
| Heavy armor (banded, splint, plate) | 18 |
| Masterwork armor (any) | +5 to base DC |
| Shield (small / large / tower) | 10 / 12 / 15 |

### Repair Damaged Armor *(absorbs d20 Modern Repair for armor)*

| Damage | Repair DC | Time |
|---|---|---|
| Cosmetic / minor (dented plate, loose strap) | 10 | 10 min |
| Moderate (split lame, broken buckle, torn lining) | 15 | 1 hr |
| Major (sundered plate, structural failure) | 20 | full crafting time / 2 |
| Beyond repair (shattered, melted, riddled) | 25+ | full crafting time |

A character without an armorsmith's kit takes **−4** on the check.

### Modifications

Custom modifications (reinforced sections, chakra-conductive plating for enhancement-seal slots, hidden compartments) add to the base Craft DC. Adding **enhancement-seal slots** to armor is **[Craft (Mechanics)](Craft%20(Mechanics).md)** work (the seal-slot crafting itself), not Craft (Armor); but mounting the seal-slot reinforcement plates is Craft (Armor).

---

## Action

- Crafting from scratch: hours to weeks depending on complexity.
- Field repair: 10 min to 1 hr.

---

## Try Again

Yes for repairs. For crafting, failure typically wastes some material; failure by 5+ may ruin half.

---

## Special

- You can **take 10** on Craft (Armor); **take 20** when conditions allow.
- Without an armorsmith's kit, **−4** on the check.
- The **Mastercrafter** feat enables mastercraft armor.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 rank thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original values were "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Knowledge (Engineering)](Knowledge%20(Engineering).md)** (formerly Knowledge (Physical Sciences)) gains a **+2 bonus** on Craft (Armor) checks. *(Naruto D20.)*
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Craft (Mechanics)](Craft%20(Mechanics).md)** gains a **+2 bonus** on Craft (Armor) checks involving armor with mechanical components (articulated joints, mechanical visor lifts, integrated holstering). *(Naruto D20.)*
