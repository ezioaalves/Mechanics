# Skill Registry — Kaihou

The full Kaihou skill list, post-conversion. Every file in `Mechanics/Character_Options/Skills/` is linked here, grouped by function. The conversion mapping that produced this list lives at [Skill - Conversion](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md); the priority for resolving any conflict is **Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern**.

For the **Tactics** sub-system (individual and group tactics characters can learn and execute together), see [Tactics/Individual and Group Tactics](Tactics/Individual%20and%20Group%20Tactics.md). Tactics are not a skill — they sit next to the skill list and have their own catalog under `Tactics/List/`.

## Chakra Core *(Naruto-specific)*

The five skills that make a character a *shinobi*. Every one of these uses the Naruto D20 "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" / "NEW SKILLS" rules as its backbone.

- **[Chakra Control](Chakra%20Control.md)** *(Wis, Trained Only)* — perform Chakra Control techniques, tap reserves, conceal chakra, identify chakra-control techniques in use. Absorbs **Concentration** (d20 Modern) and the Pathfinder Concentration check.
- **[Ninjutsu](Ninjutsu.md)** *(Int, Trained Only)* — perform Ninjutsu techniques; identify Ninjutsu techniques in use.
- **[Genjutsu](Genjutsu.md)** *(Cha, Trained Only)* — perform Genjutsu techniques; resist and disrupt Genjutsu in others.
- **[Taijutsu](Taijutsu.md)** *(Str, Trained Only)* — perform Taijutsu techniques; sustain extended physical exertion.
- **[Fuinjutsu](Fuinjutsu.md)** *(Int, Trained Only)* — perform sealing techniques; analyse and dispel hostile seals.

> Companion skill for chakra economy: **[Craft (Calligraphy)](Craft%20(Calligraphy).md)** — for inscribing the physical tags and scrolls that hold the seals Fuinjutsu *performs*.

## Craft

Each Craft subcategory is its own skill in Kaihou. The d20 Modern **Repair** skill is dissolved: Repair-for-weapons → Craft (Weapons), Repair-for-armor → Craft (Armor), Repair-for-mechanical/puppets → Craft (Mechanics).

- **[Craft (Armor)](Craft%20(Armor).md)** — forging and repairing armor. Absorbs the armor side of d20M Repair.
- **[Craft (Calligraphy)](Craft%20(Calligraphy).md)** — inscribing seal tags, scrolls, paper-talismans. Naruto D20 core skill.
- **[Craft (Chemical)](Craft%20(Chemical).md)** — bombs, acids, smoke compositions, accelerants, propellants. *(Was part of Craft (Alchemy); split per house rule.)*
- **[Craft (Explosives)](Craft%20(Explosives).md)** — assembled explosive devices, demolition charges. Absorbs d20M **Demolitions**.
- **[Craft (Mechanics)](Craft%20(Mechanics).md)** — puppet construction, enhancement-seal slots, traps (incl. PF Craft (Traps)), general mechanical fabrication and repair. Absorbs the mechanical side of d20M Repair.
- **[Craft (Pharmaceutical)](Craft%20(Pharmaceutical).md)** — poisons, antidotes, soldier pills, combat drugs, medical preparations. *(Was part of Craft (Alchemy); split per house rule.)*
- **[Craft (Weapons)](Craft%20(Weapons).md)** — forging and repairing weapons. Absorbs the weapon side of d20M Repair.

## Knowledge

Each Knowledge subcategory is its own skill in Kaihou. Untrained Knowledge checks are capped at DC 10.

- **[Knowledge (Arcana)](Knowledge%20(Arcana).md)** — chakra theory, ninjutsu schools, doujutsu lore. Absorbs **Knowledge (Ninja Lore), (Arcane Lore), (Tactics)** (d20 Modern).
- **[Knowledge (Dungeoneering)](Knowledge%20(Dungeoneering).md)** — caverns, ruins, aberrations, rust-chakra corruption. Absorbs **Knowledge (Shadowlands)** (L5R-style d20M extension).
- **[Knowledge (Engineering)](Knowledge%20(Engineering).md)** — physics, mechanical engineering, construction, materials science. Absorbs **Knowledge (Physical Sciences)** and **Use Computer** (d20 Modern).
- **[Knowledge (History)](Knowledge%20(History).md)** — past events, lineages-as-history, art-history. Absorbs **Knowledge (Art)** (d20 Modern).
- **[Knowledge (Local)](Knowledge%20(Local).md)** — current events, street culture, popular culture, the village pulse. Absorbs **Knowledge (Current Events), (Popular Culture), (Streetwise)** (d20 Modern).
- **[Knowledge (Nature)](Knowledge%20(Nature).md)** — biology, geology, botany, ecology, weather. Absorbs **Knowledge (Earth and Life Sciences)** (d20 Modern).
- **[Knowledge (Nobility)](Knowledge%20(Nobility).md)** — court politics, lineages-as-genealogy, heraldry, law. Absorbs **Knowledge (Civics)** (d20 Modern).
- **[Knowledge (Planes)](Knowledge%20(Planes).md)** — summon realms, dimensional jutsu, Pure Land / Edo Tensei cosmology. Pure Pathfinder import.
- **[Knowledge (Religion)](Knowledge%20(Religion).md)** — gods, kami, rites, theology, ethics, philosophy. Folds in **Knowledge (Theology and Philosophy)** scope from d20 Modern.

## Notes on the conversion

- **Conversion table:** [Skill - Conversion](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md). Treat it as the canonical map.
- **Rank prerequisites:** Pathfinder rank prereqs stay as-is. Naruto D20 and d20 Modern rank prereqs are **reduced by 3** (the "−3 rule") — a "5 ranks" Naruto D20 prereq becomes "2 ranks" in Kaihou, reflecting the slower rank curve of Pathfinder.
- **Naruto Rank vs PF spell level in formulas:** When a Pathfinder formula uses *spell level*, halve the multiplier when converting to Naruto Rank (Naruto Rank scales twice as fast as PF spell level). For Kaihou's defensive performance baseline this means **15 + Rank** rather than 15 + Rank × 2.
- **Source notes in each skill file** record per-section provenance with the Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern priority order applied.

## Where to put new skill content

- A **new use of an existing skill** → add a section to that skill's file under `## Check` with a Source Notes line crediting the source.
- A **new skill** → write a new file in this folder following the established template and add a row to this registry.
- A **technique or jutsu that uses one of these skills** → the technique lives in `Mechanics/Techniques/`, not here; this folder only holds skill definitions.
