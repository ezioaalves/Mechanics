# Disguise

**Ability:** Charisma (Cha)
**Trained Only:** No
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Disguise is the skill of altering one's appearance to look like someone else — different gender, race, age, even a specific named individual. *Naruto D20 has no entry for this skill in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section — the unmodified Pathfinder rules apply.*

In a shinobi setting, Disguise overlaps with the Henge no Jutsu (Transformation Technique), which is a chakra-based Ninjutsu effect rather than a mundane disguise. Use Disguise for cosmetic alteration, costume, and acting; use Henge for chakra-shape-changing.

---

## Description

A character's Disguise check determines how convincing the disguise looks. It is **opposed by Perception** checks from anyone who scrutinises the character. Unsuspecting onlookers don't roll Perception unless something draws their attention; suspicious viewers do.

---

## Check

The base Disguise check is straightforward; modifiers reflect *what* you're trying to look like:

### Disguise Modifiers

| Disguise Type | Modifier |
|---|---|
| Minor details only (different hair, scar, ageing yourself a few years) | +5 |
| Different gender | −2 |
| Different race | −2 |
| Different age category (per step away) | −2 per step |
| Different size category | −10 |

### Familiarity Bonuses *(viewer's bonus to Perception when scrutinising)*

The Perception check by an onlooker who *knows the person you're impersonating* gets a bonus:

| Onlooker's Familiarity with the Real Person | Perception Bonus |
|---|---|
| Recognises on sight | +4 |
| Friends / associates | +6 |
| Close friends | +8 |
| Intimate (lover, sibling raised together, lifelong partner) | +10 |

### When Perception Checks Happen

- **Immediately upon meeting you**, and
- **Every hour thereafter** if you stay in the encounter.

If you don't draw attention, no Perception check is rolled. If you do attract attention (act suspiciously, get challenged, are looked at twice), the observer rolls.

### Magic-Aided Disguise *(Pathfinder)*

Magical alterations (*alter self*, *disguise self*, *polymorph*, *shapechange*) grant a **+10 bonus** to the Disguise check. In Kaihou, **Henge no Jutsu and equivalent Ninjutsu transformations** grant the same bonus (or a higher one at the GM's discretion, depending on technique Rank).

---

## Action

- **Creating a disguise:** **1d3 × 10 minutes** of work (Pathfinder).
- **Magic alteration:** the casting time of the relevant technique.

---

## Try Again

You may try to redo a failed disguise, but **once others know that a disguise was attempted, they'll be more suspicious** — practically, the GM may impose a circumstance penalty or rule that a retry against the same observer is impossible.

---

## Special

- You can take 10 on a Disguise check in non-stressful conditions. You can take 20 if you have the leisure to perfect the disguise without pressure.
- A character with the **Deceitful** feat (Pathfinder) gains **+2** on Disguise; **+4 at 10+ ranks**. *(Pathfinder threshold — no −3 conversion.)*
- The GM makes the Disguise check **secretly** so the character does not know how well the disguise holds up.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original value was "5 ranks in Bluff".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Bluff](Bluff.md)** gains a **+2 bonus** on Disguise checks **when the character knows they are being observed and tries to act in character**. *(Naruto D20.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (Disguise (Cha).md, vault):** The Bluff-while-observed synergy. Rank threshold "5" converted to **2** via the −3 rule. Naruto D20's PDF has no "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" entry for Disguise — the base d20 Modern rules apply unmodified except for the synergy note.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/disguise](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/disguise/)):** Backbone — the modifier table, familiarity-bonus table for impersonation, magic-aid +10 bonus, 1d3 × 10 minutes preparation time, Deceitful feat scaling at 10+ ranks.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 71):** Largely matches Pathfinder; the secret-GM-roll convention is preserved. d20M's "spot vs disguise" terminology is updated to "Perception vs disguise" per the conversion table.

**Conflict resolution:** No mechanical conflict between PF and d20M for Disguise. Naming aligns once "Spot" is read as "Perception" per the conversion table. The Bluff synergy is the only Naruto-specific addition.
