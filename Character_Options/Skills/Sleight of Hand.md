# Sleight of Hand

**Ability:** Dexterity (Dex)
**Trained Only:** Yes
**Armor Check Penalty:** Yes

Sleight of Hand is the skill of fine manual deception — palming a coin, pickpocketing a key, drawing a hidden weapon unobtrusively, performing stage magic. In Kaihou it also carries an unusual Naruto-specific application: **manual dexterity carries over into chakra hand-seal performance** (see Synergy).

*Naruto D20 has no entry for this skill in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section — the unmodified Pathfinder rules apply, with the Naruto-specific synergies preserved from the existing vault file.*

---

## Description

A character with Sleight of Hand can make small, controlled motions that observers miss. The skill is opposed by [Perception](Perception.md) for the observer; in Kaihou it also reflects fine-motor control that translates into faster, more reliable seal-sequences.

---

## Check

### Basic Sleight Tasks

| DC | Task |
|---|---|
| 10 | Palm a coin-sized object; minor sleight (make a coin "disappear"); make a small unattended object vanish |
| 20 | Lift a small object from a person; draw a hidden weapon unobtrusively; take something from a creature unnoticed |
| 20+ | More elaborate feats — opposed by Perception in many cases |

### Pickpocketing & Lifting

When you try to lift an object from a person, your check is **opposed by the target's Perception**. To successfully obtain the object, your **check result must be 20 or higher AND beat the opponent's check** — both conditions, per the d20 Modern formulation preserved as a stricter standard than PF's opposed-check-only rule.

The opponent **detects the attempt** if their Perception result beats your check, whether you got the object or not.

### Concealment Modifiers *(Pathfinder)*

| Item | Modifier on the check (favoring the concealer) |
|---|---|
| Dagger or comparable small weapon | +2 |
| Extraordinarily small object (single shuriken, a needle) | +4 |
| Heavy or baggy clothing | +2 |
| Searcher actively frisking you | searcher gains +4 |

---

## Action

- **Standard action** by default.
- You can attempt as a **move action** at **−20 penalty**.

---

## Try Again

Yes, but subsequent attempts against the same target increase the DC by **10**. If the first attempt was noticed, retry against the same observer is at +10 DC.

---

## Special

- You can **take 10** on a Sleight of Hand check. You **cannot take 20**.
- **Untrained:** Sleight of Hand cannot exceed DC 10 untrained, *except* for concealing an object on your body — you may make an untrained check for that use, but you must take 10.
- A character with the **Deft Hands** feat (Pathfinder) gains **+2** on Sleight of Hand; **+4 at 10+ ranks**. *(Pathfinder threshold — no −3 conversion.)*
- A character with the **Nimble** feat (d20 Modern) and at least 1 rank gains **+2** on Sleight of Hand.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original value was "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Bluff](Bluff.md)** gains a **+2 bonus** on Sleight of Hand checks. *(Naruto D20.)*
- **(Naruto D20 unique)** A character with **2 or more ranks** in Sleight of Hand gains a **+1 enhancement bonus** to meet the **perform requirements and Perform checks** for techniques requiring **hand seals or half-seals**. *(Naruto D20 — the manual dexterity that lets you palm a kunai also smooths out a Tora-Mi-Saru sequence.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (Sleight of Hand (Dex).md, vault):** The unique hand-seal enhancement-bonus synergy and the Bluff cross-skill synergy. Both rank thresholds "5" converted to **2** via the −3 rule. The hand-seal bonus is one of the few Naruto D20 mechanics that ties a non-chakra skill directly into chakra technique performance — preserve carefully when balancing.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/sleight-of-hand](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/sleight-of-hand/)):** Backbone — DC table (palm/lift/draw), concealment modifiers, standard-action default with move-action option at −20, DC +10 on retry, Deft Hands feat scaling at 10+ ranks, untrained DC 10 limit with concealment exception.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 81):** The stricter pickpocketing rule (need both result ≥ 20 AND beat the opposed check) preserved. The Nimble feat preserved.

**Conflict resolution:** **Pickpocketing standard:** PF says opposed check by itself; d20M adds an absolute DC 20 floor on top of the opposed check. **d20M wins** here as a slight tightening that fits Kaihou's "shinobi notice things" tone better. Other rules align. The Naruto-specific hand-seal synergy is unique — preserved without alteration beyond the rank-threshold conversion.
