# Knowledge (Planes)

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** No (untrained checks limited to DC 10)
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Knowledge (Planes) covers the spirit realms, summoning contracts, the Pure Land / Impure World axis, and the cosmology that connects them — the **summon-realm geography** of the Naruto setting: the Toad Realm of Myoubokuzan, the Snake Realm of Ryuuchi, the Slug Realm of Shikkotsu Forest, and the lesser realms the various clans contract with. By extension it covers the **Pure Land afterlife**, the **Edo Tensei boundary**, and the **dimensional jutsu** that punch holes between worlds (Kamui, the Summoning Jutsu itself, intra-realm space-time techniques).

The [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md) has no explicit d20 Modern row mapping to Knowledge (Planes); the skill is a pure Pathfinder import. Knowledge (Tactics) in d20M maps to "Knowledge (Arcane)" per the table — that is read as **[Knowledge (Arcana)](Knowledge%20(Arcana).md)**, not here. This file's scope is **non-overlapping** with Arcana: Arcana covers chakra theory and ninjutsu schools as a discipline; Planes covers the *realms* and the *entities native to them*.

*Naruto D20 has no entry for this skill in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section — base rules apply.*

---

## Description

A character with Knowledge (Planes) can identify a summoned creature's realm of origin, recall the summoning contract requirements for a specific clan, understand the cosmological geography that lets the Toad summons co-exist with the Snake summons, and reason about what a dimensional technique *actually does to the fabric of where-it-puts-you*. The skill of the **Summoner / Sage Mode practitioner / Edo Tensei researcher / sealing-theorist** who works at the boundaries.

---

## Check

### General DCs *(Pathfinder)*

| Question | DC |
|---|---|
| Recall a basic fact about a major summon realm (Toad, Snake, Slug) | 10 |
| Identify a summoned creature's realm of origin | 15 |
| Know the contract requirements or political structure of a summon realm | 20 |
| Recall obscure cosmological theory or the mechanism of a specific dimensional technique | 25+ |

### Identify an Outsider / Summoned Creature *(Pathfinder)*

- **DC = 10 + creature's CR** for creatures of the Outsider type (summoned beings, Edo Tensei vessels, planar entities).
- Each 5 over the DC reveals an additional useful detail (typical abilities, summon-realm allegiance, vulnerabilities).

### Recognise a Dimensional Technique in Use *(Kaihou-specific)*

- **DC = 15 + technique's Rank** (recall: in Kaihou the rank-vs-PF-spell-level conversion halves the multiplier, so this matches the standard "15 + Rank" pattern).
- On success, identify the technique's effect, realm of operation, and (on a check that beats the DC by 5+) any standard defence against it.

---

## Action

Usually **no action** (passive recall). Library or scriptorium research combines with [Linguistics](Linguistics.md) and takes 1d4 hours.

---

## Try Again

**No.** The check represents what you know.

---

## Special

- **Untrained** Knowledge (Planes) checks are capped at DC 10.
- You can **take 10**. You **cannot take 20**.
- With library access (a clan-archive that holds summoning lore, or a sealing-research library), the cap is lifted; each check takes 1d4 hours.
- The **Educated** feat (d20 Modern) grants **+2** on any two Knowledge subcategories.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original "5 ranks" → **2 ranks**.)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Knowledge (Planes) gains a **+2 bonus** on **[Fuinjutsu](Fuinjutsu.md)** checks involving **summoning-contract seals** or **dimensional / space-time seals**. *(Kaihou — sealing theory and planar theory share a foundation.)*
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Knowledge (Arcana)](Knowledge%20(Arcana).md)** gains a **+2 bonus** on Knowledge (Planes) checks involving chakra-as-cosmology questions. *(Naruto D20 — arcane theory feeds planar theory; both apply, they're complementary.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20:** No "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" entry and no exact analogue (the Naruto D20 base used the d20 Modern Knowledge subcategory list, which has no Planes equivalent). The vault adopts Pathfinder's scope as the natural home for summon-realm and dimensional-jutsu knowledge.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge/)):** Backbone — planes, inhabitants, magic related to planes (here read as: realms, summons, dimensional jutsu). Standard DC framework; identify-outsider (DC 10 + CR) mechanic.
- **d20 Modern:** No equivalent subcategory. The skill is a pure Pathfinder import.

**Conflict resolution:** None — the skill has no d20M analogue to merge. The boundary with [Knowledge (Arcana)](Knowledge%20(Arcana).md) is the only thing worth flagging: **Arcana** covers chakra theory, technique families, and ninjutsu *as a discipline*; **Planes** covers the *realms* and *entities native to them*. Summon-jutsu sits on the boundary; either skill can recall who you just summoned, but only Planes recalls *where it lives when you aren't summoning it*.
