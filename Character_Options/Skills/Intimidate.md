# Intimidate

**Ability:** Charisma (Cha)
**Trained Only:** No
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Intimidate is the skill of forcing cooperation through threat, presence, or fear — coercing a witness to talk, demoralising an opponent in combat, frightening a guard out of doing his job. *Naruto D20 has no entry for this skill in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section — the unmodified Pathfinder rules apply.*

---

## Description

Intimidate is the threatening counterpart to Diplomacy. Where Diplomacy persuades through reasoned discourse, Intimidate persuades through fear. The two skills are not interchangeable — an intimidated target complies in your presence but may turn against you the moment you leave; a Diplomacy-shifted target has actually changed their attitude.

---

## Check

### Demoralise Opponent *(Pathfinder, replaces d20 Modern's level-check formula)*

In combat, you can make a target **shaken** through intimidating display.

- **DC = 10 + target's Hit Dice + target's Wisdom modifier**
- **Size modifier:** **+4** if you are larger than the target, **−4** if smaller.

**Success:** Target becomes **shaken** for 1 round, plus 1 additional round per 5 points by which your check exceeds the DC.

**Action:** Standard action.

### Change Attitude / Force Cooperation *(Pathfinder + d20 Modern)*

Outside combat, you can use Intimidate to force a target to cooperate for a short time.

- **DC:** Same formula — **10 + target's HD + target's Wisdom modifier**.
- **Success:** Target acts friendly for **1d6 × 10 minutes** (PF) or treats you as friendly for **10 minutes** (d20M — within the more restrictive scope of "only for actions taken while in the character's presence"). Kaihou uses the **Pathfinder duration** per priority, but the **d20 Modern scope-of-cooperation caveat** is preserved: the target chats, advises, and offers limited help, but does not change their underlying attitude.
- **Action:** 1 minute of conversation.
- **Failure by 5+:** The target may **deceive or hinder** you (PF), or actively do the **opposite** of what you wished (d20M).

There are **limits**: you cannot force someone to obey your every command, betray a deeply-held loyalty, or do something that endangers their life.

---

## Action

- **Demoralise:** standard action.
- **Change attitude:** 1 minute of continuous interaction.

---

## Try Again

- **Demoralise:** Yes, but **each additional check increases the DC by +5**. The increase resets after **1 hour**.
- **Change attitude:** No. Once the initial check fails, the target has hardened against intimidation.

---

## Special

- You can **take 10** on Intimidate. You **cannot take 20** (d20 Modern preserved).
- A character **immune to fear effects** cannot be intimidated (d20 Modern preserved as an important Kaihou clarification — Kanikage Kisada and others with fear-immunity from Hida's bond, certain Yato berserkers in full Chi-no-Bousou state, etc.).
- A character with the **Persuasive** feat (Pathfinder) gains **+2** on Intimidate; **+4 at 10+ ranks**. *(Pathfinder threshold — no −3 conversion.)*
- A character with the **Confident** feat (d20 Modern) gains **+2** on Intimidate and on level checks to resist intimidation.
- **Half-orcs** (and similarly imposing races, if used in Kaihou) gain a **+2 racial bonus** (Pathfinder).

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original value was "5 ranks in Bluff".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Bluff](Bluff.md)** gains a **+2 bonus** on Intimidate checks. *(Naruto D20 — the same talent for reading and pushing a target's responses cuts both ways.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (Intimidate (Cha).md, vault):** The Bluff → Intimidate synergy. Rank threshold "5" converted to **2** via the −3 rule. Naruto D20's PDF has no "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" entry for Intimidate — the base d20 Modern rules apply unmodified except for the synergy note.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/intimidate](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/intimidate/)):** Backbone — the DC formula (10 + HD + Wis mod), size modifier ±4, demoralise → shaken outcome with scaling duration, change-attitude duration (1d6×10 min), repeated-attempt DC penalty (+5, resets after 1 hour), Persuasive feat scaling at 10+ ranks, racial bonuses.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 76):** The "level check vs Intimidate" opposed-roll formulation (replaced by PF's DC formula per priority), the "10 minutes friendly in your presence only" scope clarification (preserved as a Kaihou caveat on the PF outcome), the fear-immunity rule, the Confident feat.

**Conflict resolution:** **DC formula:** PF uses 10 + HD + Wis mod (a flat DC); d20M uses an opposed level check. PF's flat-DC formula wins per priority — much faster at the table. **Duration:** PF gives 1d6 × 10 minutes of friendly behaviour; d20M gives 10 minutes. **PF wins on duration**, but **d20M wins on scope** (only while in the character's presence; the target retains their actual attitude). Both flavours preserved. **Repeated-attempt DC penalty:** PF +5 per repeat with 1-hour reset (preserved); d20M "no retry at all" (superseded).
