# Genjutsu

**Ability:** Charisma (Cha)
**Trained Only:** Yes
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Genjutsu is the discipline of performing, detecting, and analysing illusory techniques — the chakra-borne manipulation of perception. It covers the projection of false stimuli, the disruption of an opponent's senses, and the recognition of those manipulations when used against you or others.

This skill has no Pathfinder or d20 Modern equivalent; it is Naruto D20 native.

---

## Description

Where Ninjutsu shapes the world and Taijutsu shapes the body, Genjutsu shapes the *witness*. A genjutsu's success depends not on its raw power but on the practitioner's ability to project a convincing falsehood directly into the target's chakra-perceptive system.

---

## Check

### Perform a Genjutsu Technique

The DC to perform a genjutsu is specified by the technique itself. Roll Genjutsu against that DC.

### Detect & Analyse a Non-Harmful Genjutsu

To detect a non-harmful genjutsu — an unnoticed noise, a modification of your surroundings, or a quiet illusion — you must first notice an anomaly. Once you suspect one, attempt a Genjutsu check.

- **DC = 15 + the technique's Rank**

On success, you become certain that a genjutsu is present (but not which one) and gain the ability to make a Will save to disbelieve it.

### Identify a Witnessed Genjutsu

When you witness a genjutsu, you may attempt a Genjutsu check to identify it, provided the technique is one known to you.

- **DC = 10 + the technique's Complexity Rating + the technique's Rank**

A success identifies the genjutsu by name and reveals its basic effect.

---

## Action

- **Analysing a genjutsu** is a **move-equivalent action**.
- **Identifying a genjutsu** is a **free action**.
- **Performing a genjutsu** takes the action specified by the technique.

---

## Try Again

You are not allowed a retry to analyse, perform, or identify a genjutsu.

---

## Special

- A character with the **Genjutsu Adept** feat gains a **+2 bonus on Genjutsu checks**.
- A character can take 10 but **cannot take 20** when performing a genjutsu technique.

---

## Synergy

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Genjutsu gains a **+2 bonus on Learn checks** for Genjutsu techniques. *(Naruto D20 originally "5 ranks"; converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule.)*
- A character with **7 or more ranks** in Genjutsu can create Genjutsu *hijutsu* or *kinjutsu* techniques without suffering the standard additional penalty. *(Naruto D20 originally "10 ranks"; converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule.)*
