# Knowledge (Arcana)

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** No (untrained checks limited to DC 10)
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Knowledge (Arcana) is the **chakra-scholarship skill of Kaihou**. It absorbs three d20 Modern Knowledge subcategories per the [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md):

- **Knowledge (Ninja Lore)** — Naruto D20 native (chakra theory, jutsu classification, summons, shinobi tradition)
- **Knowledge (Arcane Lore)** — d20 Modern (the occult, magic, supernatural, astrology, numerology)
- **Knowledge (Tactics)** — d20 Modern (combat strategies, force maneuvering)

In a setting where Pathfinder's "arcane magic" is replaced by the chakra system, this is the **broadest single Knowledge subcategory** — covering everything from chakra theory and bloodline genetics to occult lore and tactical doctrine.

---

## Description

A character with Knowledge (Arcana) understands the *theory* behind chakra, techniques, summons, and the supernatural — the "why does this work" question that complements the chakra skills' "how do I make this work". They can identify a witnessed technique's nature from observation, recall the history of a summon contract, recognise the signature of a particular village's tactical doctrine, and judge whether a phenomenon is mundane, occult, or chakra-borne.

## Check

### General Difficulty *(Pathfinder)*

| Question Difficulty | DC |
|---|---|
| Really easy | 10 |
| Basic question within the field | 15 |
| Tough or obscure question | 20 |
| Very tough / specialised question | 25 |
| Extremely difficult / lost-knowledge question | 30+ |

### Identify a Summon *(Naruto D20 — preserved verbatim from the source)*

You can accurately gauge the overall strength of a summoned creature.

- **DC = the summon's Level + a modifier based on its Rank** (see table).

| Summon Rank | DC Modifier |
|---|---|
| Soldier (Bushi) | +5 |
| Protector (Hogosha) | +10 |
| Guardian (Shugorei) | +15 |
| Noble (Seihei) | +20 |
| Champion (Shingen) | +25 |

Success identifies the summon's Level and Rank. (See [Mechanics/Summoning/Summoning_Rules.md](../../Summoning/Summoning_Rules.md) for the broader summon system.)

### Identify a Creature *(Pathfinder)*

- **DC = 10 + the creature's CR** to recall basic information about a known creature type (common creatures may be easier; rare or exotic creatures may be DC 15+ + CR).
- Each **5 points** you exceed the DC reveals **one additional useful piece of information** (a special ability, a weakness, a behavioural pattern).

In Kaihou, this covers identifying clan bloodline manifestations, recognising oni and yokai species, distinguishing a summoned creature from a naturally-occurring one, and so on. *Hozuki-style chakra-vampires, Aburame symbionts, Kaguya bone-manifestations, and similar bloodline-flavoured creatures are all in Knowledge (Arcana)'s scope.*

### Tactical Knowledge *(absorbs d20 Modern Knowledge (Tactics))*

The skill also covers **combat strategy and force-maneuver knowledge** — formations, doctrine, the tactical signatures of specific villages or clans, the principles of squad-level engagement.

> *Knowledge (Tactics) was a separate d20 Modern Knowledge subcategory. Per the [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md) it converts to Knowledge (Arcana) in Kaihou (the table has a slight typo — "Knowledge (Arcane)" — read as Arcana). Pathfinder typically routes military knowledge to Knowledge (Nobility) or Knowledge (History), but the conversion table is explicit and Kaihou follows it.*

### Occult / Arcane Lore *(absorbs d20 Modern Knowledge (Arcane Lore))*

The skill also covers **occult lore** — astrology, numerology, divination traditions, supernatural entities (kami, oni, jinchuriki theory, the Bijuu, the Fu-Leng seal, the Forgotten Resistance). Anything that is "magic-adjacent" in a non-chakra sense falls here.

---

## Action

Usually **no action** (a passive recall). Researching a topic in a library combines this skill with [Linguistics](Linguistics.md) and takes 1d4 hours.

---

## Try Again

**No.** The check represents what you know. Thinking about it again does not let you know something you never knew. (Exception: if you gain new information that reframes the question, a fresh check may apply.)

---

## Special

- **Untrained Knowledge (Arcana)** is simply an Intelligence check, **capped at DC 10**. You can know common knowledge about chakra and the supernatural untrained, but no specialist information.
- You can **take 10** on Knowledge (Arcana). You **cannot take 20**.
- With library access (the [Kani Library](../../../Lore/World_of_Rokugan/Kanigakure/04%20-%20Iron%20Wards/Kani%20Library.md) or similar), the untrained DC 10 cap is **lifted**, though each check takes 1d4 hours.
- A character with the **Educated** feat (d20 Modern) gains **+2** on any two Knowledge subcategories of their choice.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 rank thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original value was "5 ranks in Knowledge (Ninja Lore)".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Knowledge (Arcana) gains a **+2 bonus on Learn checks** when **mastering, developing, or creating a technique** (any chakra discipline — Ninjutsu, Genjutsu, Taijutsu, Fuinjutsu, or Chakra Control). *(Naruto D20 — the original "Knowledge (Ninja Lore) → Learn bonus" preserved and elevated to all chakra skills.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (NarutoD20 Redux v1.3.pdf p. ~40 — Knowledge (Ninja Lore); Knowledge (Ninja Lore) (Int).md, vault):** The Identify-Summon DC table by Rank, the Learn-check synergy. The Naruto D20 PDF defines Knowledge (Ninja Lore) as a new skill (not in the "NEW USES" section but in the "NEW SKILLS" section); its content is the Kaihou-canonical *use* of Knowledge (Arcana). Rank threshold "5" converted to **2** via the −3 rule.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge/)):** Backbone — general DC framework (10/15/20+), monster-knowledge formula (10 + CR), untrained DC 10 cap, library-access exception, take-10 rule, Knowledge subcategory model.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 76):** Knowledge (Arcane Lore) — occult, magic, astrology, numerology. Knowledge (Tactics) — combat strategy. Both subcategories absorbed into Knowledge (Arcana) per the conversion table. The Educated feat preserved.

**Conflict resolution:** **Subcategory naming:** The conversion table has "Knowledge (Tactics) → Knowledge (Arcane)" — a slight typo. Pathfinder's canonical name is **Arcana** (with the -a), and Kaihou follows that. **Subcategory consolidation:** Where d20 Modern had three separate skills (Ninja Lore, Arcane Lore, Tactics), Kaihou folds them into one (Arcana) per the conversion table. This makes Knowledge (Arcana) the *broadest* Knowledge subcategory in the system — three d20 Modern skills' worth of topic scope on one rank progression. Flag as a meaningful generosity if it shows up in character-build optimisation. **Learn-check synergy:** The Naruto D20 source explicitly gives the bonus for "mastering, developing, or creating a technique" — Kaihou interprets this as applying to all five chakra skills uniformly (the alternative — limiting it to "ninja-flavoured" learning only — is a no-op since all techniques are ninja-flavoured in this setting).
