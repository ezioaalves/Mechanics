# Craft (Calligraphy)

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** Yes
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Craft (Calligraphy) is the **chakra-inscription craft** of Kaihou — the skill that physically inks the seal arrays, scrolls, and chakra-paper-based explosives that the entire Fuinjutsu tradition depends on. Naruto D20 modifies this skill in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section, building an extensive inscription, scroll, and chakra-tag catalog atop the base d20 Modern Craft (Writing) skill.

This is one of the most setting-specific Craft skills in Kaihou — the **Yoimura Seals** facility, **Katsuyama** music-arrays, **Sendo** ward-tags, and every shinobi who has ever stuck a paper bomb to a wall all touch this skill.

---

## Description

A character with Craft (Calligraphy) **physically inscribes** what Fuinjutsu *designs*. The two skills work in tandem: Fuinjutsu reads, identifies, and uses seal arrays; Craft (Calligraphy) makes them. A shinobi can use Fuinjutsu to craft inscriptions instead, at a **−5 penalty** (see [Fuinjutsu](Fuinjutsu.md) for the inverse).

---

## Check

### Craft Chakra Inscriptions *(Naruto D20, preserved verbatim)*

You craft chakra inscriptions with various inks and materials. **CP** indicates the chakra used with each crafting attempt. **XP** cost (if any) is paid on completion.

**Using this skill without the Craft Sealed Items feat doubles the time** required to craft any inscription or chakra-explosive listed below.

**A character without a calligraphy kit takes a −4 penalty.**

#### Chakra-Storing Gems

| Type | Cost | Craft DC | CP | Time | XP Cost |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Advanced Seal (2) | 5 | 16 | 2 | 5 min | — |
| Least | 13 | 15 | 2 | 30 min | 10 |
| Lesser | 17 | 20 | 6 | 1 hr | 25 |

- **Least** requires the *Least Chakra Storing Gem* training technique; character level 3+.
- **Lesser** requires the *Lesser Chakra Storing Gem* training technique; character level 6+.
- **Greater** requires the *Greater Chakra Storing Gem* training technique; character level 10+.

#### Scrolls

- **Elemental Scroll:** You can only craft a scroll with an element you have affinity for.
- **Technique Scroll (Scroll of Seals):** To include a technique you must know it. The check DC equals the technique's Perform DC. The bonus granted by the scroll equals your steps of mastery in each scribed technique (note when creating: *e.g., Lesser Scroll of Seals (Katon Shuriken no Jutsu +3, Daichouyaku no Jutsu +2)*).
  - **Lesser** scroll: up to 3 techniques, combined Rank ≤ 6. Craft time: **1 hour per Rank** (combined).
  - **Greater** scroll: up to 3 techniques, combined Rank ≤ 14. Craft time: **4 hours per Rank** (combined).

## Action

Each item has its own crafting time. Most range from 5 minutes (Advanced Seal) to 4 hours (Sealing Tag) per attempt; long scrolls scale with technique Rank.

---

## Try Again

Yes, with fresh materials and chakra expenditure. Failure consumes the materials AND the chakra cost (the chakra is spent regardless of success).

---

## Special

- You **cannot take 20** on Craft (Calligraphy) when crafting chakra-based items (the chakra spend is irrecoverable on failure).
- Without a **calligraphy kit**, take **−4** on the check.
- The **Craft Sealed Items** feat halves the crafting time for inscriptions and explosives. Without it, doubled time applies.
- The **Craft Shinobi Drugs** feat is not required for Calligraphy work — that's a Craft (Alchemy) concern.

---

## Synergy

- **[Fuinjutsu](Fuinjutsu.md)** is the inverse: a Fuinjutsu user can substitute Fuinjutsu for Craft (Calligraphy) when crafting inscriptions, at a **−5 penalty** on the Craft check. This is *not* a synergy bonus — it's a substitution rule.

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (NarutoD20 Redux v1.3.pdf "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section, p. ~39):** Entire content — the chakra-storing gem table, the scroll table (elemental, technique, sealing, summoning), the paper-bomb / chakra-tag table, the Craft Sealed Items feat interaction, the Fuinjutsu substitution rule, the kit and time mechanics. All values preserved verbatim.
- **Pathfinder (Craft skill, Calligraphy subcategory):** Provides the broader Craft framework (Int, trained-only, kit-required, the standard Craft mechanics). The Naruto D20 fixed-DC tables supersede Pathfinder's progression-formula approach for any of the items listed here.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 68 — Craft (Writing)):** Pre-Naruto baseline (writing as a craft); fully superseded by Naruto D20's chakra-inscription content. d20 Modern Craft (Writing) had no chakra-active items.

**Conflict resolution:** Naruto D20's content here is **definitional** for the Kaihou setting — Pathfinder and d20 Modern have nothing comparable. The Craft Sealed Items feat is preserved as the canonical time-halving mechanic. The Fuinjutsu substitution rule is preserved verbatim and cross-referenced in [Fuinjutsu](Fuinjutsu.md).
