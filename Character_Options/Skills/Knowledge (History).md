# Knowledge (History)

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** No (untrained checks limited to DC 10)
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Knowledge (History) covers events, personalities, and cultures of the past. Per the [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md), it also absorbs the d20 Modern skill **Knowledge (Art)** — fine arts, graphic arts, art history, antiques, performance arts as scholarly subjects.

*Naruto D20 has no entry for this skill in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section — base rules apply.*

---

## Description

A character with Knowledge (History) recalls events from past generations — the wars that founded the villages, the migrations that shaped the clans, the personalities whose decisions made the present what it is. In Kaihou this also includes the **Three Man Alliance** (Kamizuru-Kuruma-Katsuyama), the events of the **Forward Bastion** disaster, the politics of the Founding Kages, and any other narrative-historical material the GM is willing to make a skill check resolvable.

The skill also covers **art history** — recognising styles, periods, and the work of specific artists; valuing antiques; understanding the historical context of a performance form.

## Check

### General DCs *(Pathfinder)*

| Question | DC |
|---|---|
| Recall a recent or historically significant event | 10 |
| Determine the approximate date of a specific event | 15 |
| Recall an obscure or ancient historical event | 20 |
| Identify a rare artifact's period and origin | 20 |
| Recall a fact known only to specialist scholars | 25+ |

### Art-History DCs *(d20 Modern Knowledge (Art), absorbed)*

| Question | DC |
|---|---|
| Identify the medium and rough period of a piece | 10 |
| Identify the style, school, or movement | 15 |
| Identify the specific artist | 20+ |
| Appraise an antique's value | 15 + age modifier |

---

## Action

Usually **no action** (passive recall). Researching with library access combines this skill with [Linguistics](Linguistics.md) Research and takes 1d4 hours.

---

## Try Again

**No.** The check represents what you know.

---

## Special

- **Untrained** Knowledge (History) checks are capped at DC 10.
- You can **take 10** on Knowledge (History). You **cannot take 20** (it's recall, not exploration).
- With library access (the [Kani Library](../../../Lore/World_of_Rokugan/Kanigakure/04%20-%20Iron%20Wards/Kani%20Library.md) or similar), the untrained DC 10 cap is **lifted**, though each check takes 1d4 hours.
- A character with the **Educated** feat (d20 Modern) gains **+2** on any two Knowledge subcategories.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original values were "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Knowledge (History) gains a **+2 bonus** on **[Linguistics](Linguistics.md)** checks made to **decipher ancient writings**. *(Naruto D20 — formerly "Knowledge (History) → Decipher Script (ancient writings)".)*
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Knowledge (History) gains a **+2 bonus** on **[Perform](Perform.md)** checks. *(Naruto D20 — formerly "Knowledge (Art) → Perform".)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (Knowledge (Ninja Lore) (Int).md, vault):** Cross-skill synergies preserved from the original generic Knowledge subcategory synergy list (Knowledge (History) → Decipher Script for ancient writings; Knowledge (Art) → Perform). Both source-skill names updated per the conversion table (Decipher Script → Linguistics; Knowledge (Art) → Knowledge (History)). Rank thresholds "5" converted to **2** via the −3 rule. Naruto D20's PDF has no "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" entry for Knowledge (History).
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge/)):** Backbone — the topical scope (wars, colonies, migrations, founding of cities), the standard DC framework.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 76 — Knowledge subcategories):** Knowledge (History) topical scope and Knowledge (Art) topical scope absorbed.

**Conflict resolution:** Two d20 Modern Knowledge subcategories (History and Art) collapse into one Pathfinder Knowledge (History) per the conversion table. The art-history scope is preserved as a sub-section. The Perform synergy that originally came from Knowledge (Art) is preserved as Knowledge (History) → Perform.
