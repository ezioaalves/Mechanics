# Craft (Explosives)

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** Yes
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Craft (Explosives) is the skill for designing and building conventional explosive devices, demolition charges, fuses, and detonators. In Kaihou it absorbs the d20 Modern skill **Demolitions** per the [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md).

> **Note on paper bombs and chakra-tags:** The Yoimura clan's signature *Kibaku Fuda* (paper bombs), Sealing Tags, and Sunburst Tags are **chakra-based** explosives and belong to **[Craft (Calligraphy)](Craft%20(Calligraphy).md)**, not here. This file covers *conventional* (non-chakra) explosives — gunpowder charges, shaped demolition devices, fuse-and-detonator assemblies, the bombs the Yoimura Wall corps uses to bring down structures in a controlled collapse.

*Naruto D20 has no entry for "Craft (Explosives)" as a separate skill — the closest content is the d20 Modern Demolitions skill, which Naruto D20 left unmodified.*

---

## Description

A character with Craft (Explosives) builds explosive devices from raw materials, sets them where they will do the most damage, places fuses and detonators, and disarms what other people have built. *Setting* a simple charge needs no check; *building* one does, and *placing it for maximum effect* against a specific target does.

---

## Check

### Setting a Detonator *(d20 Modern Demolitions, preserved)*

Most explosives need a detonator. Connecting one requires a check.

- **DC 10** to attach a standard detonator.
- **Failure:** the explosive fails to detonate.
- **Failure by 10 or more:** the explosive detonates *during installation*.

**Making an explosive difficult to disarm:** You can choose the disarm DC before making your set-detonator check (must be higher than 10). Your DC to set the detonator equals the chosen disarm DC.

### Placing for Maximum Effect *(d20 Modern Demolitions)*

Carefully placing an explosive against a fixed structure (a stationary, unattended object) exploits weak points to multiply damage.

- The GM makes the check secretly so the character does not know how well they have placed it.
- **Result 15+:** the explosive deals **double damage** to the structure.
- **Result 25+:** the explosive deals **triple damage** to the structure.

Normal damage still applies to all other targets within the burst radius.

### Disarming an Explosive *(d20 Modern Demolitions)*

- **DC 10** by default, unless the setter chose a higher disarm DC.
- **Failure:** the explosive is not disarmed.
- **Failure by 5+:** the explosive detonates.

### Building an Explosive *(uses Craft (Alchemy) for the chemical compound, Craft (Explosives) for the assembly)*

The chemical compound itself (gunpowder, explosive paste, incendiary mix) is made with **[Craft (Chemical)](Craft%20(Chemical).md)** — see that skill's consumables table. The assembly of the chemical compound into a deployable device (shaped charge, fragmentation casing, fuse-and-detonator) is **Craft (Explosives)**.

> See NarutoD20 Redux v1.3.pdf Chapter 7 for the catalog of conventional explosives (purchase DC, damage, burst radius) and [Yoimura Seals](../../../Lore/World_of_Rokugan/Kanigakure/02%20-%20Foundry%20District/Yoimura%20Seals.md) for the hybrid sealed-explosive ordnance program.

## Action

- **Setting a detonator:** full-round action.
- **Placing an explosive device:** 1 minute or more, depending on the job's scope.
- **Disarming an explosive:** full-round action.

---

## Try Again

Yes — though the consequences of certain failures (the device detonating during installation, the disarming attempt setting it off) make retries impossible in practice.

---

## Special

- You **cannot take 20** on a Craft (Explosives) check — the failure modes are too dangerous.
- A character with the **Cautious** feat (d20 Modern) and at least 1 rank gains **+2** on Craft (Explosives) checks.
- A character **without a demolitions kit** takes **−4** on Craft (Explosives) checks.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original values were "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Craft (Weapons)](Craft%20(Weapons).md)** (formerly Craft (Structural) per the conversion table — the closest mechanical mapping for "siege/structural craft") gains a **+2 bonus** on Craft (Explosives) checks made **to place an explosive device against a structure for maximum effect**. *(Naruto D20.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (Demolitions (Int).md, vault):** Craft (Structural) → Demolitions placement synergy. Rank threshold "5" converted to **2** via the −3 rule. Naruto D20's PDF has no "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" entry for Demolitions; the base d20 Modern rules apply unmodified.
- **Pathfinder (Craft skill, Explosives subcategory — not explicitly itemised on d20pfsrd, treated as a general Craft application):** Provides the broader Craft framework. The detailed Demolitions mechanics below come from d20 Modern.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 70):** Entire mechanics body — Set Detonator (DC 10, failure-by-10 detonates), choose-higher-disarm-DC option, Place Explosive Device (DC 15 / DC 25 for multipliers), Disarm Explosive (DC 10, failure-by-5 detonates), kit requirement (−4 without), Cautious feat.

**Conflict resolution:** Demolitions in d20 Modern is a standalone skill; in Pathfinder it's an application of Craft. Kaihou treats it as a Craft subcategory (Craft (Explosives)) per the conversion table. The detailed d20 Modern mechanics are preserved without alteration since Pathfinder has nothing more specific to override them. The structural-craft synergy is remapped to Craft (Weapons) as the closest available subcategory in Kaihou's Craft list; if a Craft (Structural) subcategory is added later for siege engineering, the synergy should be redirected.
