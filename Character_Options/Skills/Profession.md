# Profession

**Ability:** Wisdom (Wis)
**Trained Only:** Yes
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Profession is the parent skill covering vocational expertise — knowing how to perform a specific job competently, earn income from it, and handle the everyday problems that arise within it. Specific professions are handled as subcategories.

> **Note on economy.** Kaihou's vault uses **Purchase DC / Wealth bonus** mechanics from d20 Modern (visible across the clan-ground inventory tables). For consistency, Profession in Kaihou keeps the d20 Modern **Wealth-bonus-on-level-up** mechanic rather than Pathfinder's gold-per-week formula — using PF Profession would require converting the entire economy. Flag if you want this reversed.

---

## Description

A character with a Profession knows the routines of a specific job: how to use its tools, how to perform its daily tasks, how to supervise helpers, and how to handle common problems. A shinobi may pick up Profession (Ninja) as standard professional knowledge of village procedure, mission protocols, and bureaucratic forms — distinct from the actual *practice* of being a shinobi (which uses the chakra skills).

Multiple Professions can be developed independently — being an excellent sailor doesn't mean you can run an inn.

---

## Check

### Earn Wealth on Level Up *(d20 Modern, canonical in Kaihou)*

When you gain a new character level, make a Profession check.

- **DC = your current Wealth bonus.**
- **Success:** Your Wealth bonus increases by **+1**.
- For every **5 by which you exceed the DC**, your Wealth bonus increases by an additional **+1**.
- You **cannot take 10 or take 20** on this check.

**Rank-based Wealth bonus** (also added at level-up, separately from the check result):

| Ranks in Profession | Additional Wealth Bonus |
|---|---|
| 1–5 | +1 |
| 6–10 | +2 |
| 11–15 | +3 |
| 16–20 | +4 |
| 21+ | +5 |

> *The d20 Modern rank brackets above are pre-Pathfinder rank scaling — they are NOT subject to the −3 rank-prereq conversion rule because they are part of an integrated table, not a single threshold. Per the −3 rule's spirit, GMs may shift the brackets down by 3 (i.e. 1–2 / 3–7 / 8–12 / 13–17 / 18+); the table above preserves the d20 Modern original as the safer default.*

### Answer Questions about Your Profession *(Pathfinder)*

| Question Difficulty | DC |
|---|---|
| Basic question within your profession | 10 |
| Complex / non-obvious question | 15+ |

### Reputation Use *(d20 Modern preserved)*

At the GM's discretion, you may add your Profession modifier when making a Reputation check related to your professional field.

## Action

The Wealth-bonus check is a once-per-level event; not an in-game action.

Answering a professional question is typically no action.

---

## Try Again

- **Earn-Wealth check:** No — the level-up check is one-shot per level.
- **Professional questions:** GM discretion; sometimes yes with new context.

---

## Special

- **Untrained** characters can attempt basic professional knowledge questions at DC 10 only; they earn approximately **1 sp per day** at any honest labour (Pathfinder fallback).
- A character with the **Windfall** feat (d20 Modern) gains a **cumulative +1** on all Profession checks each time the feat is taken.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original value was "5 ranks in Knowledge (business)".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in the appropriate **Knowledge** subcategory (typically Knowledge (Local) — formerly Knowledge (Business) per the conversion table) gains a **+2 bonus** on Profession checks related to commerce. *(Naruto D20.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern. **Exception:** Profession's economy mechanic uses d20 Modern Wealth bonus to remain consistent with the vault's Purchase DC system.*

- **Naruto D20 (Profession (Wis).md, vault):** Knowledge (Business) → Profession synergy. Rank threshold "5" converted to **2** via the −3 rule; source skill renamed to Knowledge (Local) per the conversion table. Naruto D20's PDF has no "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" entry for Profession — the base d20 Modern rules apply unmodified except for this synergy note.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/profession](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/profession/)):** Provides the "answer questions about your profession" mechanic (DC 10 / DC 15+), the untrained 1 sp/day fallback, and the list of common professions. PF's primary mechanic (½ check result in gold per week) is **superseded** by d20M's Wealth bonus mechanic to keep Kaihou's economy coherent.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 79):** Canonical for the Wealth-bonus-on-level-up mechanic, the rank-bracket bonus table, the Reputation interaction, the Windfall feat.

**Conflict resolution:**

- **Income mechanic:** PF gives gold per week; d20M gives Wealth bonus increase on level up. Despite the priority rule, **d20M wins** here as a vault-coherence exception — the rest of Kaihou's economic infrastructure (clan-ground inventory tables, shop tables, NPC item lists) is all denominated in Purchase DCs. Switching to PF would force a full economy rewrite. Flag with GM if Pathfinder gold-per-week is desired.
- **Rank prerequisites in the Wealth-bonus table:** preserved at d20M originals (1–5, 6–10, etc.) rather than applying −3 across the table, since the table is an integrated structure rather than a single threshold. Alternative −3-shifted version noted inline.
