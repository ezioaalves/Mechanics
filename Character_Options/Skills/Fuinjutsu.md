# Fuinjutsu

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** Yes
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Fuinjutsu is the discipline of performing fuinjutsu techniques and crafting the chakra-inscribed seals, tags, and arrays they depend on. It is the chakra system's writing skill — sealing techniques, storage seals, barrier tags, binding arrays.

This skill has no Pathfinder or d20 Modern equivalent; it is Naruto D20 native. It overlaps with **Craft (Calligraphy)** in Pathfinder for inscribing the physical glyphs, and with **Decipher Script / Linguistics** for double-checking unknown seal arrays.

---

## Description

A fuinjutsu user does not merely *use* chakra — they *write* with it. Where every other chakra skill manipulates a transient effect, Fuinjutsu produces *persistent* artefacts: tags that endure, arrays that wait, seals that bind across decades. The skill governs both the moment-of-use performance (drawing, slapping, releasing) and the inscription of the seal arrays themselves.

---

## Check

### Perform a Fuinjutsu Technique

The DC to perform a fuinjutsu is specified by the technique itself. Roll Fuinjutsu against that DC.

### Identify a Witnessed Fuinjutsu

When you witness a fuinjutsu, you may attempt a Fuinjutsu check to identify it, provided the technique is one known to you.

- **DC = 10 + the technique's Complexity Rating + the technique's Rank**

A success identifies the fuinjutsu by name and reveals its basic effect.

### Craft Chakra Inscriptions

Fuinjutsu can substitute for **Craft (Calligraphy)** when crafting chakra inscriptions, at a **−5 penalty** to checks. See the Craft (Calligraphy) skill and the Chakra Inscriptions table for details.

> *In Kaihou's Pathfinder-converted skill list, Craft (Calligraphy) is the canonical home for the inscription craft itself; a Fuinjutsu user with no Calligraphy ranks can fall back on Fuinjutsu at −5.*

---

## Action

- Identifying a fuinjutsu is a **free action**.
- Performing a fuinjutsu takes the action specified by the technique.
- Crafting an inscription follows the Craft (Calligraphy) action rules.

---

## Try Again

You are not allowed a retry to perform or identify a fuinjutsu. (You may attempt to perform the technique again with another action of its required type.) Inscription crafting follows standard Craft retry rules.

---

## Special

- A character with the **Fuinjutsu Adept** feat gains a **+2 bonus on Fuinjutsu checks**.
- A character can take 10 but **cannot take 20** when performing a fuinjutsu technique.

---

## Synergy

*(All thresholds converted from Naruto D20 via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original values were 5 / 5 / 10.)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Fuinjutsu gains a **+2 bonus on Learn checks** for Fuinjutsu techniques.
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **Linguistics** (formerly Decipher Script per the conversion table) gains a **+2 bonus on Fuinjutsu checks** made to double-check and identify seal arrays.
- A character with **7 or more ranks** in Fuinjutsu can create Fuinjutsu *hijutsu* or *kinjutsu* techniques without suffering the standard additional penalty. *(Note: the Naruto D20 source text does not explicitly state this for Fuinjutsu but it is consistent with every other chakra skill at "10 ranks" originally — `10 − 3 = 7` ranks in the Kaihou-converted system; applied uniformly.)*
