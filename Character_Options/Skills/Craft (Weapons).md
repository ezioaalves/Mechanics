# Craft (Weapons)

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** Yes
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Craft (Weapons) is the skill for forging, modifying, and repairing weapons — kunai, shuriken, ninjato, kama, the Yato clan's brutal frontline arms. Per the [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md), it also absorbs the **weapon-side of the d20 Modern Repair skill**.

*Naruto D20 has no entry for "Craft (Weapons)" in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section — base Pathfinder rules apply, augmented with the Repair-for-weapons mechanics absorbed from d20 Modern.*

---

## Description

A character with Craft (Weapons) forges a blade from raw steel, modifies an existing weapon for better balance or a new edge profile, mounts custom grips, fits secondary mechanisms (folding kunai, dart-launchers, breakdown ninjato), and repairs weapon damage in the field. The Yato forge at [Yato's Weapons](../../../Lore/World_of_Rokugan/Kanigakure/02%20-%20Foundry%20District/Yato%27s%20Weapons.md) is this skill's natural home, alongside any Yoimura clan-pattern manufacturing.

---

## Check

The Pathfinder Craft framework applies: each weapon has a base Craft DC and a material cost (typically ⅓ the item's purchase price in standard PF; Kaihou uses Purchase DC). Specific item DCs are listed in the [Equipment Shop](../../Equipment/Equipment_Shop.md) and the clan-ground inventories.

### Common Weapon Craft DCs *(Pathfinder baseline)*

| Weapon Type | Craft DC |
|---|---|
| Simple weapon (kunai, club, dagger) | 12 |
| Martial weapon (ninjato, kama, longsword) | 15 |
| Exotic weapon (kusarigama, war fan, complex spike-weapons) | 18 |
| Masterwork weapon (any) | +5 to base DC |

### Repair a Damaged Weapon *(absorbs d20 Modern Repair for weapons)*

Repairing a damaged weapon uses the same Craft (Weapons) skill. The DC depends on the damage extent:

| Damage | Repair DC | Time |
|---|---|---|
| Cosmetic / minor (chipped edge, loose grip) | 10 | 10 min |
| Moderate (cracked blade, broken haft) | 15 | 1 hr |
| Major (snapped blade, structural failure) | 20 | full crafting time / 2 |
| Beyond repair (shattered, melted) | 25+ | full crafting time |

A character without a weaponsmith's kit takes **−4** on the check.

### Modifications

Custom modifications (folding mechanisms, secondary edges, balance tweaks, chakra-conductive plating) add to the base Craft DC depending on complexity. Mastercraft modifications require the **Mastercrafter** feat (see Naruto D20 reference).

---

## Action

- Crafting from scratch: hours to weeks depending on weapon complexity (use the Naruto D20 Craft (Mechanical) baseline: ~1 hour per 5 points of DC).
- Field repair: 10 min to 1 hr depending on damage extent.

---

## Try Again

Yes for repairs (with fresh time investment). For crafting, failure typically wastes some material; a failure by 5+ may ruin half the materials.

---

## Special

- You can **take 10** on Craft (Weapons). You can **take 20** when conditions allow (no time pressure, full workshop).
- Without a weaponsmith's kit, take **−4** on the check.
- A character with the **Mastercrafter** feat can produce mastercraft weapons (see Naruto D20 PDF for full feat).

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 rank thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original values were "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Knowledge (Engineering)](Knowledge%20(Engineering).md)** (formerly Knowledge (Physical Sciences)) gains a **+2 bonus** on Craft (Weapons) checks. *(Naruto D20.)*
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Craft (Mechanics)](Craft%20(Mechanics).md)** (formerly Craft (Mechanical) — for weapons with mechanical components like folding kunai, dart launchers) gains a **+2 bonus** on Craft (Weapons) checks involving such components. *(Naruto D20.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20:** No "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" entry for Craft (Weapons) — base Craft framework applies. Synergies preserved from the vault's general Craft framework with the −3 rank conversion.
- **Pathfinder (Craft skill framework):** Backbone — the fixed-DC-per-weapon-class approach, masterwork +5 DC, Mastercrafter feat.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 80 — Repair (Int)):** Repair mechanics absorbed for the weapon-repair side; the canonical Repair skill is dissolved into the relevant Craft subcategories per the conversion table.

**Conflict resolution:** No major conflicts. The weapon-craft framework is consistent across PF and d20M; the d20M Repair skill is the only meaningful merge target, and it folds cleanly into Craft (Weapons) for any "fix a damaged weapon" task.
