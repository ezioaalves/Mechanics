# Taijutsu

**Ability:** Strength (Str) *or* Dexterity (Dex) — pick at character creation; cannot be changed later
**Trained Only:** Yes
**Armor Check Penalty:** Yes

Taijutsu is the discipline of performing and analysing taijutsu techniques — the chakra-augmented martial arts that turn the shinobi's body into a precise, trainable weapon. It governs forms, combos, technique execution, and the identification of techniques you witness in others' hands.

This skill has no Pathfinder or d20 Modern equivalent; it is Naruto D20 native. *(Per [House_Rules/Skills.md](../../House_Rules/Skills.md), Taijutsu may use either Str or Dex as its key ability — the choice is made at character creation.)*

---

## Description

Where Ninjutsu reshapes the world through chakra projection and Genjutsu reshapes a target's perception, Taijutsu reshapes the body itself. A taijutsu technique is a martial move — a strike, a movement, a stance — augmented by chakra at the moment of execution.

> **Note on attack bonus.** Taijutsu is the skill that governs *technique execution* — forms, combos, mastery. It does **not** replace the standard attack bonus. A taijutsu technique resolves with the standard attack roll (using the appropriate weapon ability) plus any technique-specific bonus dice on damage. (See `Taijutsu (Str).md` history if reviewing the older raw text — this clarification reflects the canonical Kaihou ruling.)

## Check

### Perform a Taijutsu Technique

The DC to perform a taijutsu move is specified by the technique itself. Roll Taijutsu against that DC.

### Identify a Witnessed Taijutsu

When you witness a taijutsu, you may attempt a Taijutsu check to identify it, provided the technique is one known to you.

- **DC = 10 + the technique's Complexity Rating + the technique's Rank**

A success identifies the technique by name and reveals its basic effect.

---

## Action

- Identifying a taijutsu is a **free action**.
- Performing a taijutsu takes the action specified by the technique.

---

## Try Again

You are not allowed a retry to perform or identify a taijutsu. (You may attempt to perform the technique again with another action of its required type.)

---

## Special

- A character with the **Taijutsu Adept** feat gains a **+2 bonus on Taijutsu checks**.
- A character can take 10 but **cannot take 20** when performing a taijutsu technique.

---

## Synergy

*(All thresholds converted from Naruto D20 via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original values were 5 / 5 / 10 / 10.)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Taijutsu gains a **+1 bonus to unarmed attacks**.
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Taijutsu gains a **+2 bonus on Learn checks** for Taijutsu techniques.
- A character with **7 or more ranks** in Taijutsu gains an additional **+1 bonus to unarmed damage**.
- A character with **7 or more ranks** in Taijutsu can create Taijutsu *hijutsu* or *kinjutsu* techniques without suffering the standard additional penalty.
