# Knowledge (Religion)

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** No (untrained checks limited to DC 10)
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Knowledge (Religion) covers gods and goddesses, mythic history, ecclesiastic tradition, holy symbols, the rites and observances of every faith — and, by Kaihou-house-rule extension, the broader **theology and philosophy** of what humans believe about chakra, the spirit realm, and the afterlife.

There is no explicit row for "Knowledge (Theology and Philosophy)" in the [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md), but the d20 Modern subcategory's scope (*liberal arts, ethics, philosophical concepts, study of religious faith, practice, and experience*) is the natural Religion-scope read, and so it is folded in here.

*Naruto D20 has no entry for this skill in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section — base rules apply.*

---

## Description

A character with Knowledge (Religion) recognises holy symbols, knows which shrine in which district honours which kami, recalls the founding myths of the **Three Man Alliance** in their religious dimension, identifies the rituals of the **Iron Pulse** observances, knows the theology of the Rokugani-style ancestor-veneration that pervades Kanigakure, and can debate the philosophical underpinnings of any of it.

In Kaihou this is the **shrinekeeper / ascetic / philosophical-monk** skill — equally useful for sniffing out a heretical cult, identifying a relic, or holding your own in a temple-courtyard ethics argument.

---

## Check

### General DCs *(Pathfinder)*

| Question | DC |
|---|---|
| Identify a common deity's symbol or basic tenet | 10 |
| Know the rites and observances of a major shrine or sect | 15 |
| Identify a relic, recognise an obscure deity, recall the details of a specific religious tradition | 20 |
| Recall esoteric theological doctrine or the founding theology of an extinct sect | 25+ |

### Philosophy & Ethics *(absorbed from d20 Modern Knowledge (Theology and Philosophy))*

| Question | DC |
|---|---|
| Recall the basic position of a major philosophical school on a question | 10 |
| Identify a specific philosopher's argument or treatise | 15 |
| Reconcile two competing ethical frameworks for an argument or court case | 20 |
| Recall an obscure or heterodox philosophical position | 25+ |

### Identify a Humanoid with Religious Significance *(Pathfinder)*

- **DC = 10 + creature's HD** for outsiders, undead-religious figures, sanctified individuals, members of distinctive religious orders (priests, monks of a recognised sect).
- Each 5 over the DC reveals an additional useful detail.

> *In Kaihou this is the skill that recognises a Sendo Hunter shrine-warden, an Iron Pulse ascetic, or a Mibu medical-monk by their bearing and iconography.*

---

## Action

Usually **no action** (passive recall). Library or scriptorium research combines with [Linguistics](Linguistics.md) and takes 1d4 hours.

---

## Try Again

**No.** The check represents what you know.

---

## Special

- **Untrained** Knowledge (Religion) checks are capped at DC 10.
- You can **take 10**. You **cannot take 20**.
- With temple-library or scriptorium access, the cap is lifted; each check takes 1d4 hours.
- The **Educated** feat (d20 Modern) grants **+2** on any two Knowledge subcategories.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original "5 ranks" → **2 ranks**.)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Knowledge (Religion) gains a **+2 bonus** on **[Knowledge (Arcana)](Knowledge%20(Arcana).md)** checks involving spirit-realm, divine, or doujutsu-mythic topics. *(Naruto D20 — generic Knowledge-subcategory synergy adapted; theology feeds arcane recognition for spirit topics.)*
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Knowledge (Religion) gains a **+2 bonus** on **[Diplomacy](Diplomacy.md)** checks made within a religious context (temple negotiation, sect dispute, ascetic council). *(Naruto D20 — generic synergy adapted.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20:** No "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" entry. Synergies preserved with the −3 rank conversion.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge/)):** Backbone — gods and goddesses, mythic history, ecclesiastic tradition, holy symbols and rites scope; the standard DC framework; identify-creature mechanic for religiously-significant humanoids.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 76 — Knowledge subcategories):** Knowledge (Theology and Philosophy) — *liberal arts, ethics, philosophical concepts, and the study of religious faith, practice, and experience*. Folded in here as the natural Pathfinder home, despite the lack of an explicit conversion-table row.

**Conflict resolution:** The conversion table does not explicitly map Knowledge (Theology and Philosophy). It is folded into Knowledge (Religion) here because (a) the d20M subcategory's scope is dominantly theological, (b) Pathfinder has no Knowledge (Philosophy) subcategory, and (c) Knowledge (History) already absorbed Knowledge (Art), so an "ethics and philosophy" content area without a home would otherwise dangle. The "ethics / liberal arts" sliver does *not* overlap with Knowledge (Nobility) (legal/political) or Knowledge (History) (past events) in a way that demands re-routing.
