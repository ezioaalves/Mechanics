# Escape Artist

**Ability:** Dexterity (Dex)
**Trained Only:** No
**Armor Check Penalty:** Yes

Escape Artist is the skill of slipping bindings, squeezing through tight spaces, and breaking out of grapples. *Naruto D20 has no entry for this skill in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section — the unmodified Pathfinder rules apply.*

---

## Description

A character with Escape Artist can wriggle out of ropes, manacles, and nets; squeeze a body through a space barely large enough for the head; and use loose-jointed evasion to escape a grappler's hold or reduce a pin to a regular grapple.

---

## Check

| Restraint | DC | Action | Try Again |
|---|---|---|---|
| Rope bindings | Binder's **CMB + 20** | 1 minute | Only if your bonus is high enough that the DC is ≤ 20 + your bonus |
| Net, *animate rope*, *command plants*, *control plants*, *entangle* | 20 | Full-round action | Yes |
| *Snare* spell | 23 | Full-round action | Yes |
| Normal manacles | 30 | 1 minute | Yes |
| Masterwork manacles | 35 | 1 minute | Yes |
| Grapple / pinned condition | Grappler's **CMD** | Standard action | Yes |
| Tight space | 30 | At least 1 minute | Yes |

### Rope Bindings *(d20 Modern formulation preserved as alternative)*

The d20 Modern formulation was an **opposed check**: Escape Artist vs. the binder's **Dexterity check +20** (since it's easier to tie someone up than escape). Pathfinder's CMB-based formula is the canonical Kaihou version (per priority), but at the GM's discretion the d20 Modern opposed check can substitute if neither party has a defined CMB.

### Tight Space

A check is only called for if your **head fits but your shoulders do not**. If the space is long (an airshaft, a chimney), the GM may call for multiple checks. You **cannot fit through a space your head does not fit through**.

### Grapples and Pins

You may substitute an Escape Artist check (vs. grappler's CMD) for a combat-maneuver check to escape a grapple or to reduce a pinned condition to merely grappled. Doing so is a **standard action**; if you escape, you can also move that round.

---

## Action

See the table above. Escaping ropes takes 1 minute; nets are full-round; grapples are standard actions.

---

## Try Again

- **Ropes:** You can keep trying only if the DC is within reach (≤ 20 + your skill bonus).
- **Tight space:** Yes — multiple checks may be required for a long space.
- **Grapples:** Yes, on subsequent rounds.

---

## Special

- You can **take 10** on Escape Artist checks. You can **take 20** when you are not being actively opposed (including being tied up, since the binder is no longer actively resisting — you have the leisure to keep trying).
- A character with the **Stealthy** feat (Pathfinder) gains **+2** on Escape Artist; **+4 at 10+ ranks**. *(Pathfinder threshold — no −3 conversion.)*
- A character with the **Nimble** feat (d20 Modern) gains **+2** on Escape Artist.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original value was "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Escape Artist gains a **+2 bonus** on **Dexterity checks made to bind someone using rope**. *(Naruto D20 — the "you know how to escape, so you know how to tie a knot that resists escape" inverse.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (Escape Artist (Dex).md, vault):** The Escape-Artist-→-Dex-binding synergy. Rank threshold "5" converted to **2** via the −3 rule. Naruto D20's PDF has no "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" entry for Escape Artist — the base d20 Modern rules apply unmodified except for the synergy note.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/escape-artist](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/escape-artist/)):** Backbone — the restraint DC table (CMB-based for ropes, fixed for nets/spells/manacles, CMD-based for grapples), the grapple-substitution rule, Stealthy feat scaling at 10+ ranks.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 71):** Ropes opposed-check formula (binder's Dex check +20 — preserved as alternative when no CMB defined), Nimble feat, the take-20-while-tied-up clarification.

**Conflict resolution:** **Rope DC:** PF uses CMB-based formula; d20M uses opposed Dex check. Both preserved (PF as default; d20M as fallback when CMB isn't defined for the binder, common with NPCs). **Manacles DC:** d20M had a flat 35; PF distinguishes normal (30) and masterwork (35). PF wins. Nimble (d20M) and Stealthy (PF) feats both grant +2, but stack only if both are taken — they're distinct feats covering the same skill.
