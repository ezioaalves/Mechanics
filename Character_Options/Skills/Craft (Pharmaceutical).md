# Craft (Pharmaceutical)

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** Yes
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Craft (Pharmaceutical) is the Naruto D20 skill for crafting **antidotes, performance-enhancing drugs, and medical compounds** — Tosha Pills, Turtle's Dexterity, Heartroot Powder, Akimichi Food Pills, the entire Chakra-Growth class of enhancement drugs. It is **distinct from [Craft (Chemical)](Craft%20(Chemical).md)** — the two domains require different study and produce different effects.

*Naruto D20 modifies this skill in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section with its full item catalog.*

---

## Description

A shinobi with Craft (Pharmaceutical) compounds the rare, often dangerous, and sometimes life-changing substances that push a body past its normal limits or pull it back from the edge. The Akimichi clan's food-pill regimen, the Kaguya pharmacy's centuries of medical knowledge, and most enhancement-drug regimens go through this skill.

---

## Check

The base mechanic is the **fixed-DC-per-item** approach Naruto D20 uses: each item has a Cost, a Craft DC, and a Time.

**A character without a chemical / alchemical kit takes a −4 penalty** on Craft (Pharmaceutical) checks.

### Antidotes *(Naruto D20, preserved)*

You can craft **3 doses of antidote from 1 dose of poison**. Antidotes must be administered the same way the original poison would be (injury, contact, inhalation). On administration, the recipient immediately saves against one dose of poison; on success they are immune to any further effects and recover from current damage twice as fast.

For every **5 points** you exceed the DC, your antidote grants an additional **+1 bonus** to the save.

**General Antidote** (works against all poisons within a save-DC range):

| Poison Save DC range | Material Cost | Craft DC | Prep Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| 14 or lower | 8 | 13 | 1 hr |
| 15–18 | 12 | 15 | 3 hr |

A General Antidote grants **+2** circumstance bonus to the additional save.

**Specific Antidote** (made from one specific poison):

| Poison Save DC range | Material Cost | Craft DC | Prep Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| 14 or lower | 6 | 15 | 1 hr |
| 15–18 | 10 | 17 | 3 hr |
| 19–22 | 15 | 20 | 6 hr |
| 23 or higher | 20 | 25 | 12 hr |

A Specific Antidote grants **+4** circumstance bonus to the additional save.

### Performance-Enhancing Drugs *(Naruto D20 — requires Craft Shinobi Drugs feat)*

Performance-enhancing drugs (Akimichi Food Pills, Blackweed Elixir, Chakra Growth, Fireseed Extract, Lotus Extract, Nevoa Infusion, Swiftwind Elixir, Tengu Elixir, Tosha Liquor, Muscle Growth I/II, Ability-Enhancing Drug B/A/S-Class) require the **Craft Shinobi Drugs** feat and have additional cost columns (XP, Prep time, Brewing time) on top of the standard Cost / Craft DC.

> Full table and rules in NarutoD20 Redux v1.3.pdf Chapter 7 (p. 72) — too long to reproduce here, and dependent on the Craft Shinobi Drugs feat being active in your campaign.

## Action

Each item lists its own time. Antidotes 1–12 hours; medical compounds 1–16 hours; performance drugs require both prep (24–72 hr) and brewing (1–16 weeks).

---

## Try Again

Yes, with fresh materials. Failure consumes the materials AND any XP cost paid.

---

## Special

- You **cannot take 20** when crafting a pharmaceutical compound where failure damages the materials.
- Without the appropriate kit, take **−4** on the check.
- The **Craft Shinobi Drugs** feat is required for the performance-enhancing-drugs sub-table.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 rank thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original values were "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Knowledge (Nature)](Knowledge%20(Nature).md)** (formerly Knowledge (Earth and Life Sciences)) gains a **+2 bonus** on Craft (Pharmaceutical) checks. *(Naruto D20.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (NarutoD20 Redux v1.3.pdf "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section, p. ~37):** Entire content — antidotes, medical compounds, performance-enhancing drugs. Preserved. Kaihou keeps this as a **separate** Craft subcategory from Craft (Chemical) — the user explicitly decided against the Pathfinder "Alchemy" consolidation.
- **Pathfinder (Craft skill framework):** Provides the Craft framework. PF has no direct equivalent skill; pharmaceutical work in PF would be Craft (Alchemy), which Kaihou rejects.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 68 — Craft (Pharmaceutical)):** d20 Modern baseline Naruto D20 built atop. Superseded by Naruto D20's detailed item tables.
