# Bluff

**Ability:** Charisma (Cha)
**Trained Only:** No
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Bluff is the skill of strategic deception — convincing falsehoods, combat feints, hidden messages encoded in plain conversation, and the calculated social misdirection that lets a shinobi do their work in front of unsuspecting eyes.

In Kaihou, Bluff is also the foundation of **Compulsion-style Genjutsu performance** (see Synergy) — a shinobi who can lie convincingly carries that talent into the deception of the senses.

---

## Description

A Bluff check is **opposed by the target's Sense Motive**. The check resolves whether the target reacts as you wish for a short time (usually 1 round or less), or believes a falsehood you assert. The target must be aware of you and able to interact with you — you cannot bluff someone who does not perceive you.

---

## Check

### Circumstance Modifiers

| Circumstance | Modifier on the Bluff check |
|---|---|
| Target wants to believe you | +5 |
| Lie is believable | 0 |
| Lie is a little unlikely / minor risk to target | −5 |
| Lie is hard to believe / large risk for target | −10 |
| Lie is far-fetched | −10 |
| Lie is "way out there" — almost too incredible | −20 |
| Lie is impossible | −20 |
| Target is drunk or impaired | +5 |
| You possess convincing proof | up to +10 |

If the target's Sense Motive succeeds by **10 or less** against a bluff that asked for something risky, the target didn't so much see through the bluff as **prove reluctant to go along with it**. If Sense Motive succeeds by **11 or more**, the target has actually seen through the deception.

### Common Uses

#### Deceive or Lie

The default Bluff use. A successful check means the target reacts as you wish or believes the falsehood for the short term. **Minimum 1 round** of interaction.

#### Feint in Combat

Cause your opponent to be **denied his Dexterity bonus to AC against your next attack**.

- **DC = 10 + opponent's base attack bonus + opponent's Wisdom modifier** (or **Sense Motive bonus**, if higher).
- **Action:** Standard action.

#### Convey a Secret Message

Pass a coded message inside ordinary conversation so that uninvolved listeners do not catch it.

- **DC 15** for a simple message.
- **DC 20** for a complex message.
- **Action:** Twice normal speaking time.
- Other listeners may attempt an opposed **Sense Motive** check vs your Bluff result to decode it.

#### Create a Diversion to Hide

Use Bluff to momentarily distract observers so you can attempt **[Stealth](Stealth.md)** while being observed. See the Stealth skill for the −10 penalty and reach-a-hiding-place rules.

#### Feign Harmlessness

Convince enemies (or guards, or marks) that you pose no threat.

- **Action:** Full-round action minimum.

#### Suggest a Course of Action

Manipulate the target into believing a suggestion originates with themselves.

- **Action:** Minimum 1 minute of continuous interaction.

#### Perform an Inconspicuous Action

Render a normally-conspicuous action unnoticeable through social misdirection. GM adjudicates DC by context.

---

## Action

- **Convey Secret Message:** twice normal speaking time.
- **Deceive:** minimum 1 round.
- **Feign Harmlessness:** full-round action minimum.
- **Feint in Combat:** standard action.
- **Suggest Course of Action:** minimum 1 minute of continuous interaction.

---

## Try Again

Failed deception attempts impose a **−10 penalty** on subsequent attempts against the same target. The GM may rule that re-bluffing the same target is *impossible* once they have caught you out (especially for major deceptions).

---

## Special

- You can take 10 on a Bluff check in non-stressful situations.
- A character with the **Persuasive** feat (Pathfinder) or a comparable feat gains a bonus on Bluff (see the feat itself for the magnitude).

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original values were "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Bluff gains a **+2 bonus** on **Diplomacy**, **Intimidate**, and **Sleight of Hand** checks, as well as on **Disguise** checks when the character knows they are being observed and tries to act in character. *(Naruto D20.)*
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Bluff gains a **+1 enhancement bonus to meet the perform requirements of any Compulsion technique** (Genjutsu Compulsion family). *(Naruto D20 — unique to the Kaihou setting.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (Bluff (Cha).md, vault):** Genjutsu / Compulsion-technique synergy ("Being good at deceiving your foes has always benefited you when using Genjutsu"), the multi-skill synergy package (Diplomacy, Intimidate, Sleight of Hand, Disguise-while-observed). All Naruto rank thresholds converted via the −3 rule.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/bluff](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/bluff/)):** Circumstance Modifier table, Feint-in-Combat DC (BAB + Wis mod or Sense Motive, whichever is higher), Convey-Secret-Message DCs, Feign Harmlessness, Suggest Course of Action, action costs.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 63):** Sense-Motive-by-10-or-by-11 reluctant-vs-saw-through clarification, the canonical "demands risky action gives +10 to target" formulation.

**Conflict resolution:** Pathfinder and d20 Modern present the same skill differently — PF leads with a clean modifier table, d20M leads with a narrative + Sense-Motive-margin clarification. Kaihou keeps PF as the structural backbone (clean tables read faster at the table) and preserves the d20M margin clarification as a narrative note. The Naruto D20 Compulsion-Genjutsu tie is unique to this campaign and has no equivalent in PF or d20M.
