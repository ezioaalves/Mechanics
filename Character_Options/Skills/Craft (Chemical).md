# Craft (Chemical)

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** Yes
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Craft (Chemical) is the Naruto D20 skill for crafting **shinobi consumables and poisons** — Soldier Pills, Adrenaline Boost, Ice Bombs, Perception Oil, Chakra Restoration Amplifier, and other tactical chemical compounds. Kaihou preserves the Naruto D20 split between Chemical and Pharmaceutical (they are *not* merged into a generic "Alchemy" skill — the two domains require different study and produce different effects).

*Naruto D20 modifies this skill in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section with its full item catalog.*

---

## Description

A shinobi with Craft (Chemical) brews the field consumables that turn a long deployment from a marginal mission into a survivable one. The Sendo Hunters' anti-Taint kits, the Yato's forge-poisons, and most field-medical bandolier items pass through this skill.

---

## Check

The base mechanic is the **fixed-DC-per-item** approach Naruto D20 uses: each item has a Cost (Purchase DC for materials), a Craft DC, and a Time. You make the check, succeed or fail, and produce a fixed quantity.

**A character without a chemical kit takes a −4 penalty** on Craft (Chemical) checks.

### Consumables & Poisons *(Naruto D20, preserved verbatim)*

A successful check produces **4 doses** of poison or **5 uses** worth of consumables.

---

## Action

Each item lists its own time (1–8 hours). Continuous attention required during the listed time.

---

## Try Again

Yes, with fresh materials. Failure consumes the materials.

---

## Special

- You **cannot take 20** when crafting a chemical compound where failure damages the materials.
- Without the appropriate kit, take **−4** on the check.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 rank thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original values were "5 ranks".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Knowledge (Engineering)](Knowledge%20(Engineering).md)** (formerly Knowledge (Physical Sciences)) gains a **+2 bonus** on Craft (Chemical) checks. *(Naruto D20.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (NarutoD20 Redux v1.3.pdf "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section, p. ~37):** Entire content — the Craft (Chemical) consumables table. Preserved verbatim. Kaihou keeps this as a **separate** Craft subcategory from Craft (Pharmaceutical) — the user explicitly decided against the Pathfinder "Alchemy" consolidation.
- **Pathfinder (Craft skill framework):** Provides the broader Craft framework (Int, trained-only, kit-required). PF's Craft (Alchemy) is the closest single-skill mapping but is rejected in Kaihou for setting fit.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 67 — Craft (Chemical)):** Provides the d20 Modern baseline Naruto D20 built atop. Largely superseded by Naruto D20's detailed item tables.
