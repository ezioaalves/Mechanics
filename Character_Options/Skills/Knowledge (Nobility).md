# Knowledge (Nobility)

**Ability:** Intelligence (Int)
**Trained Only:** No (untrained checks limited to DC 10)
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Knowledge (Nobility) covers lineages, heraldry, royalty, courtly etiquette, and the politics of *the people who actually run things*. Per the [Skill Conversion Table](../../House_Rules/Skill%20-%20Conversion.md), it absorbs the d20 Modern skill **Knowledge (Civics)** — law, legislation, litigation, legal rights and obligations, political and governmental institutions and processes.

In Kaihou this is the **Iron Wards / court / Daimyo's-office** skill — knowing which of the Five Great Clans' patriarchs hates which, what the Kage's standing committee is currently debating, what tribunal procedure governs a missing-nin warrant, which protocol opens a door at the Iron Wards versus the Daimyo's chamber. The natural skill of the **Diplomat, Jounin Council aide, and Kanigakure bureaucrat.**

*Naruto D20 has no entry for this skill in the "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" section — base rules apply.*

---

## Description

A character with Knowledge (Nobility) knows the lineages — which Yoimura branch produced the current First Wall Captain, which Ishimaru is in line to head the clan, whose marriage thirty years ago realigned the Three Man Alliance. They know **legal procedure** — how to file a clan-elevation petition, what tribunal handles disputes between districts, the legal weight of a Kage's seal versus a Daimyo's chamberlain. They know **court etiquette** — which way to bow, which honorific, which fan to carry.

This is the *companion* to [Knowledge (Local)](Knowledge%20(Local).md): Local covers the streets and the rumour mill; Nobility covers the court and the bureaucracy.

## Check

### General DCs *(Pathfinder)*

| Question | DC |
|---|---|
| Know basic rules of etiquette, heraldic conventions, or the current ruling line of a major village | 10 |
| Identify a common patrician family, recall famous noble lines | 15 |
| Know a specific noble's heraldry, lineage details, or political stance | 20 |
| Recall obscure legal precedent or the politics of a long-dead lineage | 25+ |

### Legal / Civics *(absorbed from d20 Modern Knowledge (Civics))*

| Question | DC |
|---|---|
| Recall a basic legal right or village ordinance | 10 |
| Know the proper court / tribunal for a specific dispute | 15 |
| Recall a specific clause of clan-conflict precedent | 20 |
| Find a legal loophole in a treaty or charter | 25+ |

### Identify a Humanoid Lineage *(Pathfinder)*

- **DC = 10 + creature's HD** to recognise that a humanoid is a member of a specific noble line by their bearing, heraldry, or attributed mannerisms.
- Every 5 over the DC reveals an additional useful detail (alliances, marriage politics, current standing).

---

## Action

Usually **no action** (passive recall). Researching genealogies or court records combines with [Linguistics](Linguistics.md) and takes 1d4 hours.

---

## Try Again

**No.** The check represents what you know.

---

## Special

- **Untrained** Knowledge (Nobility) checks are capped at DC 10.
- You can **take 10**. You **cannot take 20**.
- With library access (the [Kani Library](../../../Lore/World_of_Rokugan/Kanigakure/04%20-%20Iron%20Wards/Kani%20Library.md) or any clan-archive), the cap is lifted; each check takes 1d4 hours.
- The **Educated** feat (d20 Modern) grants **+2** on any two Knowledge subcategories.

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original "5 ranks" → **2 ranks**.)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in Knowledge (Nobility) gains a **+2 bonus** on **[Diplomacy](Diplomacy.md)** checks in courtly or formal settings (clan council, Iron Wards audience, Daimyo's chamber). *(Naruto D20 — formerly attributed to Knowledge (Civics) → Diplomacy in courtly settings.)*
- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Knowledge (History)](Knowledge%20(History).md)** gains a **+2 bonus** on Knowledge (Nobility) checks involving dynastic or lineage questions. *(Naruto D20 — dynastic history feeds noble-line recognition; both apply, they're complementary.)*

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20:** No "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" entry. Synergies preserved with the −3 rank conversion; the courtly-Diplomacy synergy is the Naruto D20 vault's generic Knowledge-subcategory synergy adapted for the converted skill name.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/knowledge/)):** Backbone — heraldry, lineage, royalty, etiquette scope; the standard DC framework; the humanoid-lineage identification mechanic (DC 10 + HD).
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 76 — Knowledge (Civics)):** Absorbed — law, legislation, litigation, legal rights and obligations, political and governmental institutions and processes. This is the **bureaucracy / Iron Wards** side of the skill.

**Conflict resolution:** d20M Knowledge (Civics) folds cleanly into Knowledge (Nobility) per the conversion table — both cover "the formal apparatus by which power is organised", which is the natural Pathfinder scope.
