# Perform

**Ability:** Charisma (Cha)
**Trained Only:** No
**Armor Check Penalty:** No

Perform is the parent skill of stage and venue performance. In Pathfinder it has many sub-disciplines; Kaihou's [House_Rules/Skills.md](../../House_Rules/Skills.md) collapses them into **five** canonical subcategories: **Act**, **Dance**, **Music**, **Oratory**, and **Martial Arts**.

This parent file documents the generic Perform mechanics shared across all subcategories. Per-subcategory files cover discipline-specific notes.

---

## Description

A Perform check measures the quality of an artistic or technical performance for an audience. Each subcategory uses its own ranks — being a great singer doesn't make you a great dancer — but the **mechanics are identical** across them, so this file is the canonical reference for "how Perform works"; the subcategory files only add what's specific.

---

## Check

### Performance Quality DCs *(Pathfinder)*

| DC | Result |
|---|---|
| 10 | Routine performance. Audience may appreciate it but isn't impressed. (Earn ~1d10 cp/day.) |
| 15 | Enjoyable performance. Audience enjoys it; it isn't exceptional. (Earn ~1d10 sp/day in prosperous venues.) |
| 20 | Great performance. Audience highly impressed. (Earn ~3d10 sp/day; possible regional reputation.) |
| 25 | Memorable performance. Audience enthusiastic. (Earn ~1d6 gp/day; possible national reputation.) |
| 30 | Masterful / extraordinary performance. Audience awed. (Earn ~3d6 gp/day; may draw extraplanar attention.) |

*Income figures are Pathfinder-flavoured; in Kaihou's [Purchase DC](../../Equipment/Equipment_Shop.md) economy, treat each tier as granting a corresponding Reputation or Wealth gain at the GM's discretion.*

### Subcategories *(Kaihou's 5-category structure per [Skills.md](../../House_Rules/Skills.md))*

| Subcategory | Covers | File |
|---|---|---|
| **Act** | Drama, comedy, pantomime, stand-up routines | *Perform (Act).md — TBD* |
| **Dance** | Ballet, war-dance, ceremonial, rhythmic body movement | *Perform (Dance).md — TBD* |
| **Music** | All instrumental and vocal music — keyboards, percussion, strings, wind, sing (collapsed from PF's separate categories) | *Perform (Music).md — TBD* |
| **Oratory** | Epic recitation, storytelling, formal speech, sermon | *Perform (Oratory).md — TBD* |
| **Martial Arts** | War-dance demonstrations, kata, stylised combat displays — *Naruto D20 native subcategory* | [Perform (Martial Arts).md](Perform%20(Martial%20Arts).md) |

> Each subcategory's ranks are tracked separately. Perform (Music) covers all instruments and singing — Kaihou collapses PF's five music sub-skills (Keyboards, Percussion, Sing, Strings, Wind) into one, simplifying both character builds and Foundry VTT integration.

---

## Action

A Perform check usually requires at least several minutes to an hour or more — a discrete performance, not a continuous activity.

---

## Try Again

Allowed, though previous failures may increase future DCs (PF: +2 per failure due to audience prejudice; d20M: not for the same performance and audience).

---

## Special

- You can **take 10** on a Perform check in non-stressful conditions. You **cannot take 20**.
- A **masterwork instrument** grants **+2 circumstance bonus** on Perform (Music) checks (Pathfinder).
- A character with the **Creative** feat (d20 Modern) gains **+2** on two Perform subcategories they designate; the feat may be taken multiple times for different subcategory pairs.
- A character without an appropriate instrument *automatically fails* Perform (Music) checks for instrumental music. With an *improvised* instrument the GM may allow the check at **−4** (d20 Modern).

---

## Synergy

*(Naruto D20 / d20 Modern synergy thresholds converted via the −3 rank-prereq rule. Original value was "5 ranks in Knowledge (art)".)*

- A character with **2 or more ranks** in **[Knowledge (History)](Knowledge%20(History).md)** (formerly Knowledge (Art) per the conversion table) gains a **+2 bonus** on Perform checks. *(Naruto D20.)*

Subcategory-specific synergies (e.g. Perform (Martial Arts) gaining half of base attack bonus from BAB) live in the corresponding subcategory file.

## Source Notes

*Priority: Naruto D20 > Pathfinder > d20 Modern.*

- **Naruto D20 (NarutoD20 Redux PDF, "NEW USES FOR EXISTING SKILLS" → PERFORM (MARTIAL ARTS)):** The Perform (Martial Arts) subcategory is a Naruto D20 native addition; its content lives in [Perform (Martial Arts).md](Perform%20(Martial%20Arts).md). The Knowledge (Art) → Perform synergy is preserved here, with rank threshold "5" converted to **2** and the source skill renamed to Knowledge (History) per the conversion table.
- **Pathfinder ([d20pfsrd.com/skills/perform](https://www.d20pfsrd.com/skills/perform/)):** Backbone — the DC quality table with income tiers, masterwork-instrument +2 bonus, the action-time guidance.
- **d20 Modern (modernsrd.pdf p. 79):** Subcategory breakdown (Act, Dance, Keyboards, Percussion, Sing, Stand-Up, Stringed, Wind — collapsed in Kaihou per Skills.md), Creative feat, the no-instrument-auto-fail and −4 improvised-instrument rules.

**Conflict resolution:** Pathfinder and d20 Modern both define Perform with subcategories; Kaihou's [Skills.md](../../House_Rules/Skills.md) collapses them into 5 (Act, Dance, Music, Oratory, Martial Arts) — Music absorbs all instrumental + sing categories from both systems, and Stand-Up is folded into Act. This is a Kaihou house decision over both PF and d20M.
