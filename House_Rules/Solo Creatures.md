# Solo Creature Rules (Homebrew)

A **Solo Creature** is designed to challenge an entire party of adventurers by itself. It possesses immense durability, speed, and resilience to ensure it isn't overwhelmed by the action economy of a full squad.

## Core Traits

### Hit Dice & Health
*   **Hit Points:** A solo creature gains **4x the maximum hit points** per Hit Die (HD).
*   **Constitution Bonus:** Add the Constitution modifier for every HD (not multiplied).
*   **Toughness Feat:** Grants +10 HP instead of the usual +3.

### Defenses
*   **Defense (AC):** Gains a **+3 Luck Bonus** to Defense.
*   **Saving Throws:** Gains a **+2 Luck Bonus** to all Saving Throws.
*   **Immunities:** 
    *   Immune to **Death Effects**.
    *   Immune to effects that cause the **loss of a turn**.
    *   Immune to up to **5** of the following (GM Choice): Ability Damage, Ability Drain, Daze, Fear, Nausea, Negative Levels, Paralysis, Stunning.

### Action Economy (The Equalizer)
A solo creature must act frequently to threaten a group. Choose **one** of the following methods:

#### Method A: Extra Actions
The creature gains an additional set of actions *during its single turn*:
*   **Bonus Actions:** +1 Swift Action, +1 Move Action, +1 Attack Action.
    *   *Restriction:* These cannot be used for Spells, Supernatural Abilities, Psionics, or Techniques.
*   **Limit Break (2/Encounter):** The creature may ignore the restrictions above OR trade the extra actions to make:
    *   One additional attack at its highest bonus during a Full-Attack.
    *   A second additional attack at a -5 penalty.

#### Method B: Multiple Turns
The creature acts multiple times per round.
*   **Initiative:** Roll Initiative twice (or more). Pick the highest result.
    *   **Turn 1:** Acts on the highest roll.
    *   **Turn 2:** Acts on (Highest Roll / 2).
*   **Duration Rules:**
    *   Effects starting/ending at the *start* of a turn apply on **Turn 1**.
    *   Effects starting/ending at the *end* of a turn apply on **Turn 2**.
    *   "Once per round" effects trigger only once (usually Turn 1).

### Challenge Rating & XP
*   **XP Reward:** Worth **3x XP** of a standard creature of its CR.
*   **Encounter Level:** Counts as **3 Creatures** of its CR for difficulty calculations.
