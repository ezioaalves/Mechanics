# Elephant in the Room: Feat Tax Overhaul

This document codifies the "Elephant in the Room" (EITR) rule changes for the Naruto d20 campaign. These rules aim to reduce feat taxes and make combat maneuvers more accessible.

## Base Rule Changes

### Combat Maneuvers
*   **CMB Calculation:** `BAB + Str or Dex + Size Modifier`. (Agile Maneuvers is now universal).
*   **CMD Calculation:** `10 + BAB + Str + Dex + Size Modifier`.
*   **Hamstring:** A new combat maneuver that replaces *Stand Still* and *Scorpion Style*. Target is entangled for 1 round (plus 1 round per 5 over CMD).

### Universal Combat Options
The following abilities are now available to **everyone with BAB +1** without requiring a feat:

*   **Risky Strike (Power Attack / Deadly Aim):** 
    *   Take –1 penalty on attack rolls and CMB checks to gain +2 bonus on damage rolls.
    *   Damage increases by +50% (to +3) for two-handed weapons, or primary natural attacks.
    *   Damage is halved (to +1) for off-hand weapons.
    *   Increases: Every +4 BAB, penalty increases by –1 and damage bonus by +2.
    *   Applies to melee and ranged attacks.

*   **Defensive Stance (Combat Expertise / Fighting Defensively):**
    *   Take –1 penalty on attack rolls and CMB checks to gain +1 dodge bonus to AC.
    *   Increases: Every +4 BAB, penalty increases by –1 and AC bonus by +1.

*   **Weapon Finesse:**
    *   All light melee weapons and specific finesse weapons (rapier, whip, etc.) can automatically use Dexterity instead of Strength for attack rolls.

### Martial Mastery
*   Feats or abilities that apply to a specific weapon (e.g., Weapon Focus) now apply to its entire **Weapon Group** (Light Blades, Heavy Blades, Thrown, etc.).

## Revised Feat Tree

### 1. Merged Feats

| New Feat | Combined Effects | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Deft Maneuvers** | Improved Disarm, Improved Trip, Improved Feint, Improved Dirty Trick. | +2 bonus, no AOO. Feint as a move action. |
| **Powerful Maneuvers** | Improved Bull Rush, Improved Overrun, Improved Sunder, Improved Drag. | +2 bonus, no AOO. |
| **Dodge** | Dodge + Mobility. | +1 dodge bonus to AC. +4 vs AOOs from movement. |
| **Unarmed Combatant** | Improved Unarmed Strike + Improved Grapple. | Always armed. +2 on Grapple checks, no AOO. |
| **Precise Shot** | Point-Blank Shot + Precise Shot. | Point-Blank Shot removed as a prerequisite. Entry feat for archery. |
| **Greater Two-Weapon Fighting** | Improved + Greater TWF. | Gain iterative off-hand attacks at BAB +6 and +11. |

### 2. Altered Feats

*   **Agile Combatant:** Use Dex instead of Str for CMB checks for *all* maneuvers (Bull Rush, Grapple, etc.).
*   **Deflect Arrows:** Characters with *Shield Focus* may use this feat to deflect arrows with their shield.
*   **Shield Focus:** Grants an additional +4 bonus to AC against ranged attacks.

### 3. Removed Feats (Integrated into System)
*   **Combat Expertise** (Universal Option)
*   **Deadly Aim** (Universal Option)
*   **Power Attack** (Universal Option)
*   **Weapon Finesse** (Universal Option)
*   **Agile Maneuvers** (Integrated into CMB)
*   **Improved Bull Rush/Disarm/Overrun/Sunder/Trip/Feint** (Merged into Deft/Powerful)

---

## NPC & Bestiary Adjustment
When encountering NPCs with old feats:
1.  **Method I:** Use the stats as written, but allow use of *Risky Strike* and *Defensive Stance*.
2.  **Method II:** Swap defunct feats for the new versions (e.g., replace *Power Attack* with *Powerful Maneuvers* or a different feat).
