Throughout the game, you will find instances where Class Features are given to players that cover the gamut of Bonus Feats to things like Uncanny Dodge. This variant rule takes inspiration from 3.5 in that whenever a player receives a class feature multiple times, it improves in some fashion. This list is just the begin ning, feel free to suggest more that you come up with! 

**Bonus Feats:** Whenever a player receives a bonus feat from a class feature that they already possess, they may pick another from that same class’ Bonus Feat list, if they have all the feats in that list or there is no list, the player simply receives a free feat they may spend anywhere but must still meet the prerequisites and they must exhaust their options in the order presented above first. 

**Evasion:** When a player who has Evasion, gains Evasion again from another class feature, they gain Improved Evasion/Evasion X. 

**Sense Chakra:** Each time a character gains this feature either from a feat such as Genin, Chuunin, and Jounin or through classes like Ninja Hunter, the character increases their effective char acter level by 2 when using Sense Chakra and adds +1 to the duration. 

**Uncanny Dodge:** Whenever a character has Uncanny Dodge and then gains the same feature from another class, they instead gain Improved Uncanny Dodge/Uncanny Dodge 2.
